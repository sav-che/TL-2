### Author

Drude, Carl Georg Oscar (1852-1933), German botanist at Göttingen, Dresden and Breslau, professor of botany, director of the botanic garden and Geheimrath. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Drude* \[standard form in IPNI: *Drude*\]
