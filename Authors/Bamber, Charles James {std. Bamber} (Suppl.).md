### Author

\* Bamber, Charles James (1855-1941), British colonial physician and botanist, with the Indian medical service 1878; inspector-general of civil hospitals, Punjab 1910-1915. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Bamber*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
