### Author

Abeleven, Theodoor Hendrik Arnoldus Jacobus (1822-1904), Dutch botanist, apothecary and high-school teacher at Nijmegen. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Abeleven* \[standard form in IPNI: *Abeleven*\]

#### Herbarium and types
