### Author

Sismonda, Eugenio (1815-1870), Italian palaeontologist; Dr. med. Torino 1839. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Sismonda* \[standard form in IPNI: *Sismonda*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Andrews p. 333; Barnhart 3: 283; BM 4: 1933; CSP 5: 710, 8: 962; Jackson p. 60, 180, 187; LS 24925-24927; PR 8699; Quenstedt p. 400 (b. 29 Apr 1815).
