### Author

Schultze, Christian Friedrich (1730-1755), German paleontologist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *C. Schultze* \[standard form in IPNI: *C.Schultze*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Note

Christian Friedrich Schulze \[sic\] published *Flora Dresdens und Umgebung* in S.B. Isis, Dresden 1880: 62-77. 1881.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 245; BM 4: 1869-1870; Herder p. 352, 353, 411; Jackson p. 176; NI 1816; PR 8386-8487, ed. 1: 9434-9435.
