### Author

Andrzeiovski, Antoni Lukianovich (1785-1868), Lithuanian botanist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Andrzeiovski*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection KW|KW]] (9000).
*Ref*.: IH 2: 37.
- Candolle, Phytographie 392. 1880 (herb. at KRAM?).
