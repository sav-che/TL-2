### Author

Colla, Silvia (1902-x), Italian botanist, mentor of the Soc. Bot. Ital. 1926, Dr. Sc. Torino, studied 1938 in Argentina and the U.S.A. (*S. Colla*).

#### Herbarium and types

In fasc. 16, *Laboulbeniales*, 157 p., 30 Jun 1934, *in Flora italica cryptogama*, fasc 16. (Pfc. 3(2): XI). For text see below.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 366; Kew 1: 588; LS suppl. 5404-5410.
