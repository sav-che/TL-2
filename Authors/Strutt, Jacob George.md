### Author

Strutt, Jacob George (*fl*. 1820-1850), British landscape painter and etcher; at Lausanne and Rome 1830-1851. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Strutt* \[standard form in IPNI: *Strutt*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
