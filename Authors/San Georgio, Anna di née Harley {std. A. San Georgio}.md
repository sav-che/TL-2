### Author

San Georgio, Anna di née Harley (1803-1874), British-born Italian botanist at Florence. 
**Abbreviated name**: *A. San Georgio* \[standard form in IPNI: *A.San Georgio*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Note

According to Carolina Lemon, daughter of Anna i San Georgio, the latter was born as a daughter of "Lord Edoardo Oxford e da Giovanna Scott", obviously British parents. Her father's name must have been Harley before his ennoblement. Her husband was Giovanni Battista Rabitti, later comte di San-Georgio (see Saccardo 1: 226-227 and 2: 96).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 209 (b. 31 Jul 1803, d. 18 Mai 1874); BB p. 269; BM 4: 1797; Jackson p. 9; Rehder 1: 72; Saccardo 1: 145, 226-227, 2: 90.
