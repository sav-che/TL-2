### Author

Perktold, Josef Anton (1804-1870), Austrian cryptogamist and clergyman; entered the Praemonstratenser Convent of Wilten 1825 (baptismal name Anton); ordained 1829; clergyman at Tarrenz 1829-1830, Ellbögen nr. Innsbruck 1830-1840, Tulfes 1834-1836, Vill 1836-1842, Igels 1842-1852, ultimately again at Wilten as canon of its Convent. (*Perktold*).
