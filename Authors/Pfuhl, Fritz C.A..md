### Author

Pfuhl, Fritz C.A. (1853-1913), German (Pommeranian/Prussian) botanist and high school teacher at the Marien Gymnasium at Posen (now Poznan, Poland). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Pfuhl* \[standard form in IPNI: *Pfuhl*\]
