### Author

Parkinson, James (1755-1824), English surgeon, apothecary and palaeontologist at Hoxton, East London. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Js. Parkinson*

#### Collections

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Andrews p. 311; Barnhart 3: 50; BB p. 236; BM 4: 1523; CSP 4: 760; Desmond p. 479; DNB 43: 314-315; HU 764; Jackson p. 176; PR 6932 (ed. 1: 7747); Quenstedt p. 328.

#### Note

For a major treatise (bibliography, dates, notes) of the *Organic remains of a former world* 1804-1811 (3 vols.; botany in vol. 1) see Thackray (1976). In view of Thackray's extensive treatment we refrain from treating this work in any detail.
