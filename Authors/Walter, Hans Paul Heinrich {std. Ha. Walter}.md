### Author

Walter, Hans Paul Heinrich (1882-x), German (Prussian) botanist; studied at Halle 1902-1904, Freiburg i.Br. 1904, Halle 1904-1906; Dr. phil. Erlangen 1906; collaborator of A. Engler 1906-1909. (*Ha. Walter*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection B|B]] (mainly destr.).

#### Bibliography and biography

Backer p. 663; Biol.-Dokum. 18: 9729; BJI 2: 186; BM 5: 2260; Hortus 3: 1206; IH 1 (ed. 6): 365, (ed. 7): 345; MW p. 520; TL-2/1713; Zander ed. 10, p. 729, ed. 11, p. 832.

#### Biofile

- Bullock, A.A., Bibl. S. Afr. bot. 126. 1978 (cites also ecological publ. S. Africa 1936-1942 which are by Heinrich W. (b. 1898)).
