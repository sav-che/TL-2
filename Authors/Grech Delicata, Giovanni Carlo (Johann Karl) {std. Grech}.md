### Author

Grech Delicata, Giovanni Carlo (Johann Karl) (1811-1882), author on the Maltese flora. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Grech* \[standard form in IPNI: *Grech*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection FI|FI]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 237.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 79; BM 1: 729; PR 3537-3538.
