### Author

Piccioli, Antonio (1741-1842), Italian botanist; curator of the Firenze botanical garden. 
**Abbreviated name**: *A. Piccioli* \[standard form in IPNI: *Piccioli*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – Letters at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 4: 1571-1572; GF p. 70; Jackson p. 433; Kew 4: 303; NI 1527n; Plesch p. 361; PR 7134 (ed. 1: 8002-8005); Rehder 5: 671; Saccardo 1: 127 (b. 22 Apr 1794, d. 10 Jun 1842), 2: 84, cron. xxix; Sotheby 599; Tucker 1: 556.
