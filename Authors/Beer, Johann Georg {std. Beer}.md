### Author

Beer, Johann Georg (1803-1873), Austrian municipal administrator ("Stadtrath") and botanist in Vienna. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Beer*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection IBF|IBF]] (*orig*.?)
*Ref*.: IH 2: 65.

#### Bibliography and biography

- Anon., Oest. bot. Z. 23: 135. 1873.
