### Author

Manoury, Charles Ambroise (*fl*. 1869), French algologist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Manoury* \[standard form in IPNI: *Manoury*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. Issued *Algues de France* (nos. 1-1500) with Jean Antoine Mougeot, q.v.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 444; BM 3: 1234; De Toni 1: lxxxi, 2: lxxviii; IH 2: 564 (sub Mougeot); Nordstedt p. 22.
- Uhlworm, O., Bot. Centralbl. 15: 159. 1883 (announcement Mougeot, Manoury, Roumeguère, "Les algues fluviatiles et terrestres de France.").

#### Composite works

Contributed to H. E. Baillon, Dict. bot., vols. 2, 3, 4, see TL-2/253.
