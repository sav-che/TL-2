### Author

Schipp, William August (1891-1967), Australian-born explorer and plant collector; collected in N. Australia, New Guinea and Java; via San Francisco to British Honduras 1929; collected in British Honduras 1929-1935; returned to Australia because of failing health, living ultimately in New South Wales. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Schipp*

#### Herbarium and types

Collections from British Honduras in many herbaria, for full details see R.M. Lowden (1970). The most complete set (1934 specimens) is at [[Collection F|F]], other large sets at [[Collection BM|BM]] (1498), [[Collection BRH|BRH]] (1462), [[Collection K|K]] (1166), [[Collection UC|UC]] (1066); other sets at [[Collection A|A]], [[Collection AMES|AMES]], [[Collection B|B]], [[Collection BKL|BKL]], [[Collection BM|BM]], [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection G|G]], [[Collection GH|GH]], [[Collection K|K]], [[Collection L|L]], [[Collection LCU|LCU]], [[Collection MICH|MICH]], [[Collection MO|MO]], [[Collection NY|NY]], [[Collection PH|PH]], [[Collection S|S]], [[Collection UC|UC]], [[Collection US|US]], [[Collection Z|Z]].
