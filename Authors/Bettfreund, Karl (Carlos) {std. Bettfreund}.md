### Author

Bettfreund, Karl \[Carlos\] (*fl*. 1887-1901), botanist of German origin in Argentina. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Bettfreund* \[standard form in IPNI: *Bettfr.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection B|B]], [[Collection SI|SI]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 72.
