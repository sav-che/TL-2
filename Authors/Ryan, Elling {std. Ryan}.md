### Author

Ryan, Elling (1849-1905), Norwegian botanist and pharmacist; pharmac. exam. 1871; later manager of a chemical factory at Graesvik nr Frederikstad; amateur bryologist. (*Ryan*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection O|O]] (original bryophyte herb.), [[Collection TRH|TRH]] (phan.); other material at [[Collection GB|GB]], [[Collection H|H]], [[Collection L|L]].

#### Eponymy

*Ryanaea* A.P. de Candolle (1824, *orth. var.*) and *Ryania* M. Vahl (1796, *nom. cons.*) are both named for J. Ryan (*fl*. 1770-1790), plant collector.
