### Author

Uexküll-Gyllenbrand, Margarete von (*fl*. 1901), Baltic-German (Latvian) botanist; Dr. phil. Zürich 1901; student of C. Schröter. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Uexküll* \[standard form in IPNI: *Uexküll*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
