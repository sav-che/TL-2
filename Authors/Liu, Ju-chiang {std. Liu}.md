### Author

Liu, Ju-ch'iang (1895-x), Chinese botanist 
**Abbreviated name**: *Liu* \[standard form in IPNI: *J.C.Liu*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Mainly at [[Collection PE|PE]]; other material at [[Collection A|A]], [[Collection BH|BH]], [[Collection FM|FM]], [[Collection K|K]], [[Collection NY|NY]].

Barnhart 2: 391; IH 2: 452; MW p. 280-281.
