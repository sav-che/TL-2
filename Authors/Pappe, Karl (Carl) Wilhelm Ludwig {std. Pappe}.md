### Author

Pappe, Karl \[Carl\] Wilhelm Ludwig (1803-1862), German-born (Hamburg) South African botanist; Dr. med. Leipzig 1827; to South Africa 1835 as practicing physician; colonial botanist from 1848. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Pappe*
