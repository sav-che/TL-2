### Author

Derbès, Auguste Alphonse (1818-1894), French algologist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Derbés* \[standard form in IPNI: *Derbès*\]

#### Herbarium and types

material at [[Collection BP|BP]], [[Collection G|G]], [[Collection PC|PC]].
