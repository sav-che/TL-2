### Author

Schramm, \[Johannes\] Augustin (1773-1849), German (Silesian) botanist and high school teacher at the royal roman-catholic gymnasium of Leobschütz. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Aug. Schramm*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown; a single specimen collected by him is mentioned by Lindemann (see TL-2/3: 41).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 240 (d. 9 Aug 1849); Biol.-Dokum. 16: 8283; BM 4:1860; Futak-Domin p. 530; PR 8378-8379, ed. 1: 9302-9303; Rehder 1: 380.

#### Biofile

- Fiek, E., Fl. Schlesien 117. 1881.
- Wimmer, F., Fl. Schlesien 149. 1844.

#### Note

Karl Traugott Schramm, at Dresden, about whom no further details are available to us, was the editor of the short lived botanical journal "*Mittheilungen über Flora*, Gesellschaft für Botanik und Gartenbau in Dresden", 3 Hefte, Dresden 1841-1843.
