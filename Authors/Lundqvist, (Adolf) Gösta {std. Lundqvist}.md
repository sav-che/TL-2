### Author

Lundqvist, \[Adolf\] Gösta (1894-1967), Swedish palaeobotanist; Fil. lic. Stockh. högskola 1919; with the Swedish Geological Survey (for 40 years) in various capacities. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lundqvist*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection S|S]]-PA (fossil ferns).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 413; BL 2: 551, 685; BM 7: 760; GR p. 494; KR p. 474 (b. 24 Mar 1894).
- Fries, R. E., Short hist. bot. Sweden 68, 76. 1950.
- Lundqvist, J., Geol. För. Stockholm Förh. 90: 153-174. 1968 (obit., portr. bibl., b. 24 Mai 1894, d. 30 Jun 1967).
