### Author

Macreight, Daniel Chambers (1799-1868), Irish physician and botanist; BA Dublin 1820, MD 1827; lecturer at Middlesex Hospital, 1837 
**Abbreviated name**: *Macreight* \[standard form in IPNI: *Macreight*\]
