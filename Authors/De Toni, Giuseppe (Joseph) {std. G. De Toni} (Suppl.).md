### Author

\* De Toni, Giuseppe \[Joseph\] (fl. 1934-1939), Italian algologist, son of Giovanni Battista De Toni. (*G. De Toni*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 448; Kew 2: 68; TL-2/1: 638.
