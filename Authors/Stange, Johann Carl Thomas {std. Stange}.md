### Author

Stange, Johann Carl Thomas (1792-1854), German (Prussian-Saxony) botanist and high school teacher; studied theology and philology at Halle a. S.; teacher at the Friedrichs Gymnasium, Frankfurt a. Oder. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Stange*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. Stange is known to have made a herbarium (O. Stange 1862);

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 316 (b. 30 Jun 1792, d. 30 Oct 1954); PR 8901, ed. 1: 9866; Rehder 1: 380.

#### Biofile

- Ascherson, P., Fl. Provinz Brandenburg 1: 14. 1864.
- Lowden, R.M., Taxon, index pers. names vols. 1-30: 695. 1985.
- Stange, O., Verh. bot. Ver. Brandenburg 3/4: 370-372. 1862 (d. 3 Oct 1854).

#### Eponymy

*Stangea* Graebner (1906) honors the family of Graebner's wife.

### Publications
