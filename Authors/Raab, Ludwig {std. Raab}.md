### Author

Raab, Ludwig (*fl*. 1900), German (Bavarian) botanist and high school teacher at Straubing (Bavaria). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Raab* \[standard form in IPNI: *L.Raab*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection M|M]].
