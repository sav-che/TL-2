### Author

Seiland, Sjur Knutsen (1867-1920), Norwegian botanist and school teacher at Granvin; Hardanger from 1888-1919; lecturer at the teacher's college at Stord 1919-1920. (*Selland*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BG|BG]]; other material at [[Collection A|A]], [[Collection C|C]], [[Collection GB|GB]], [[Collection NMW|NMW]], [[Collection S|S]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 258 (b. 1 Dec 1867, d. 13 Jul 1920); BL 2: 449, 715; BM 4: 1897, 8: 1177; Bossert p. 360; Kleppa p. 331 (index).

#### Biofile

- Anon., Bot. Not. 1902: 158 (stipend), 1921: 14 (d.); Hedwigia 63: (97). 1921 (d.).
- Holmboe, J., Naturen, Bergen 44: 260-264. 1920 (obit., portr.).
