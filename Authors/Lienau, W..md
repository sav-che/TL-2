### Author

Lienau, W., (*fl*. 1863), German pharmacist and botanist at Eutin, later court chemist at Rendsburg. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lienau* \[standard form in IPNI: *Lienau*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
