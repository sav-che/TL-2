### Author

Becker, Gustav (1815-1880), German pharmacist and botanist at Bonn. 
**Abbreviated name**: *G. Becker*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BHU|BHU]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 150 (b. 22 Mai 1815, Höxter a. d. Weser, d. 28 Jul 1880).
