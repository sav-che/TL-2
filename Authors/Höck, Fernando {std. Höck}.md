### Author

Höck, Fernando (1858-1915), German botanist, born in Tating, Eiderstedt, later high school teacher at Lackenwalde (Neumark). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Höck* \[standard form in IPNI: *Höck*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection B|B]].
