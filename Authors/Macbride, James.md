### Author

Macbride, James (1784-1817), American botanist and physician; practicing in Pineville and Charleston, South Carolina. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. Macbr.* \[standard form in IPNI: *J.Macbr.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
