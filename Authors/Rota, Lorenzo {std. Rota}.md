### Author

Rota, Lorenzo (1819-1855), Italian botanist and physician; sometime botanical assistant in the University of Pavia; ultimately practicing physician at Bergamo; died of cholera. (*Rota*).

#### Herbarium and types

In the municipal Museum of Bergamo (A. de Candolle 1880); it is not clear whether this herbarium is still extant. Further material is at [[Collection FI|FI]] (inf. C. Steinberg).

#### Bibliography and biography
