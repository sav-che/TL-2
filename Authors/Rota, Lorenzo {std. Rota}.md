### Author

Rota, Lorenzo (1819-1855), Italian botanist and physician; sometime botanical assistant in the University of Pavia; ultimately practicing physician at Bergamo; died of cholera. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Rota* \[standard form in IPNI: *Rota*\]

#### Herbarium and types

In the municipal Museum of Bergamo (A. de Candolle 1880); it is not clear whether this herbarium is still extant. Further material is at [[Collection FI|FI]] (inf. C. Steinberg).

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 2(1): 270, 12(1): 288; Barnhart 3: 182 (d. 6 Aug 1855); BL 2: 369, 710; BM 4: 1737; Bossert p. 339; CSP 5: 301; DTS 1: 248, 6(4): 43; Herder p. 179; Hortus 3: 1203; Kew 4: 522; PR 7794-7795, ed. 1: 8748; Rehder 1: 427, 2: 144; Saccardo 1: 142, 2: 94, cron. p. xxxi; SBC p. 130; Tucker 1: 608.
