### Author

Sporleder, Friedrich Wilhelm (1787-1875), German (Prussian-Saxony) botanist; studied law and botany at Göttingen; government administrator at Wernigerode from 1815. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Sporleder* \[standard form in IPNI: *Sporl.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection HAL|HAL]]; further material at [[Collection JE|JE]], [[Collection M|M]] (lichens) and [[Collection WRSL|WRSL]] (lichens). – *Letters* to D.F.L. von Schlechtendal at [[Collection HAL|HAL]].

#### Bibliography and biography

ADB 35: 275-277 (E.A. Jacobs; b. 10 Apr 1787, d. 28 Mar 1875); Frank 3 (Anh.): 99; GR p. 177; IH 1 (ed. 7): 343; SK 1: 497, 8: xciv.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Bonplandia 2: 255. 1854.
- Rothmaler, W., Taxon 2: 142-143. 1953 (herb. at HAL).
- Seeland, H., Mitt. Herm. Roemer. Mus. Hildesheim 40: 24, 25, 29, 46. 1936.

#### Eponymy

*Sporledera* Hampe (1837); *Sporledera* Bernhardi (1842). *Note*: The derivation of *Sporlederia* A.W. Stiehler (1860) could not be determined.
