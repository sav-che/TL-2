### Author

Maurer, Heinrich \[Ludwig\] (1818-1885), German horticulturist at Jena; Grossh. Sächsischer Hofgärtner. 
**Abbreviated name**: *H. Maurer* \[standard form in IPNI: *Maurer*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 5(1): 636, 6(2): 165; Barnhart 2: 465 (b. 15 Dec 1818, d. 7 Sep 1885, err.); BM 3: 1270, 4: 1509; Herder p. 361; Morren ed. 2, p. 11, ed. 10, p. 24; Rehder 5: 556.
- Maurer, L. et K. \[Funeral card, Jena 6 Sep 1885\] (d. 6 Sep 1885; as Heinrich Maurer).
