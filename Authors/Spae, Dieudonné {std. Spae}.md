### Author

Spae, Dieudonné (1819-1858), Belgian horticulturist; assistant secretary of the Société royale d'agriculture et de botanique at Gent. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Spae*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 306 (b. 27 Sep 1819, d. 28 Oct 1858); BM 5: 1984; CSP 5: 767; Herder p. 73, 283; Hortus 3: 1204 (d. "1879?"); Jackson p. 135; MW p. 463; Rehder 5: 814-815; Zander ed. 10, p. 719, ed. 11, p. 821 (d. 1879).
