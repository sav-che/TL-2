### Author

Sordelli, Ferdinando (1837-1916), Italian naturalist at the municipal natural history museum, natural history teacher at the G.B. Piatti technical school in Milano. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Sordelli* \[standard form in IPNI: *Sordelli*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection B|B]] (mainly destr.). – *Letters* at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Andrews p. 333; Barnhart 3: 304 (d. 17 Jan 1916); BJI 1: 55; BM 5: 1972; Bossert p. 377; LS 9696, 25441-25442; Morren ed. 10, p. 84; NI 1871; Quenstedt p. 406; Rehder 2: 44; Saccardo 1: 156, 2: 102; Urban-Berl. p. 314.
