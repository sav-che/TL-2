### Author

Lavalle, Jean (1820-?), French botanist; professor of medical natural history at the medical college and director of the botanical garden of Dijon.
**Abbreviated name**: *Lavalle* \[standard form in IPNI: *Lavalle*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 3: 1068; CSP 8: 175; Jackson p. 88, 168; Kew 3: 378; LS 15008-15010; PR 5094; Rehder 5: 493; Tucker 1: 415.

#### Composite works

Editor of *Revue horticole* (de Dijon).
