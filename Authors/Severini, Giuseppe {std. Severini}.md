### Author

Severini, Giuseppe (1878-1918), Italian mycologist and phytopathologist; studied agricultural sciences at the Regio Istituto superiore, Perugia; Laur. sci. 1902; botanical assistant ib. 1902-1906; at the University of Roma 1906-1908; Dr. phil. 1908; again at Perugia as botanical assistant 1908-1914; on active service 1914-1918. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Severini*
