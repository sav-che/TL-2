### Author

Chalubinski, Tytus (1820-1889), Polish botanist, born 1820, Chociwek near Radom, died 4 Nov 1889, Zakopane, Galicia. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Chal.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection H|H]] (musci).
