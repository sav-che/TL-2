### Author

Rasmussen, Rasmus (1871-1962), Danish botanist of the Faroe Islands, born in Midvåg, carpenter, teacher, writer. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Rasmussen* \[standard form in IPNI: *Rasm.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Natural History Museum Tórshavn, Faroe Isl.; other material at [[Collection C|C]].

#### Note

Rasmussen used the pseudonym Regin i Lid for his novel *Bábilstornid* (1909). – Information on Rasmussen was kindly provided by Alfred Hansen.

#### Biofile

- Hansen, A., Bot. Tidsskr. 58(4): 327-328. 1963.
