### Author

Geilinger, Gottlieb (1881-1955), Swiss plant-geographer, born 21 Dec 1881, doctorate 1908 Univ. of Zurich, Prof. in the Kantonsschule Winterthur where he died. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Geil.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Geil.*\]

#### Note

Not to be confused with Gottlieb Geilinger (1853-1927), a Swiss industrialist, nor with Walter Geilinger, who collected plants in Africa in 1933.

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
