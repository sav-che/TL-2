### Author

Skepper, Edmund (1825-1867), British botanist and druggist at Harwich and Bury St. Edmunds. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Skepper* \[standard form in IPNI: *Skepper*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BM|BM]]. – *Letters* also at [[Collection BM|BM]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 284; BB p. 277; BL 2: 266, 717; BM 4: 1936; Desmond p. 562 (b. 20 Oct 1825, d. 2 Jun 1867); Jackson p. 360; PR 3980; TL-2/2660; Tucker 1: 653.
