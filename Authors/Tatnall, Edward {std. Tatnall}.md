### Author

Tatnall, Edward (1818-1898), American farmer, industrialist and botanist at Wilmington, Delaware; pioneer fruitcanner and match-maker. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Tatnall* \[standard form in IPNI: *Tatnall*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection COCO|COCO]]; further material at [[Collection DOV|DOV]] (impt. set), [[Collection DWC|DWC]], [[Collection GH|GH]], [[Collection PH|PH]]. – *Letters* at [[Collection GH|GH]], [[Collection NY|NY]].

#### Note

Robert Richardson Tatnall (1870-1956), a nephew of E. Tatnall also collected in Newcastle County, Delaware, after 1919; specimens are at DOV and PH. Published: *Flora of Delaware* and the Eastern Shore; an annotated list xxvi, 313 p., map, Wilmington (Society of Natural History of Delaware) 1946.
