### Author

Fothergill, John (1712-1780), British physician and botanist 
**Abbreviated name**: *Fothergill*

#### Herbarium and types

Material from his garden at Upton, West Ham, at [[Collection BM|BM]] and [[Collection LINN|LINN]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 205.
