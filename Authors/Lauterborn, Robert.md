### Author

Lauterborn, Robert (1869-1952), German algologist and protozoologist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lauterborn* \[standard form in IPNI: *Lauterborn*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 351; BFM 600, 601; BM 3: 1068; CSP 16: 630-631; De Toni 4: xxvi; Kew 3: 378; Moebius p. 79; Nordstedt suppl. p. 9; Rehder 5: 493.
- Anon., Allg. Forst. Jadgz. 124: 61-64. 1953 (n.v.).
- Cupp, E. E., Mar. plankton diatoms W. coast N. Amer. 217. 1943.

#### Eponymy
