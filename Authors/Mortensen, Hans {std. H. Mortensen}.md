### Author

Mortensen, Hans (1825-1908), Danish botanist; teacher at Naesgaard Agricultural College, 1849-1853; at Jonstrup training school from 1856-1896. 
**Abbreviated name**: *H. Mortensen*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection C|C]]; further material at [[Collection CGE|CGE]], [[Collection DAO|DAO]], [[Collection DBN|DBN]], [[Collection T|T]], [[Collection GB|GB]], [[Collection L|L]], [[Collection MW|MW]].
