### Author

Morris, Patrick Francis (1896-1974), Australian botanist; from 1913-1961 at the National Herbarium of Victoria at Melbourne. (*P. Morris*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MEL|MEL]]; some further material at [[Collection DAO|DAO]].
