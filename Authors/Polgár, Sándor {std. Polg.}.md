### Author

Polgár, Sándor (1876-1944), Hungarian lichenologist; Dr. phil. Budapest 1913. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Polg.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection DE|DE]] (12.000); further material at [[Collection BHU|BHU]], [[Collection BP|BP]], [[Collection GB|GB]] and [[Collection GH|GH]]. – Letters at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Backer p. 659; GR p. 660 (b. 13 Sep 1876, d. Jul 1944, Auschwitz); Hirsch p. 234; Kew 4: 322; SBC p. 129.
