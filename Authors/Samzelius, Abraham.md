### Author

Samzelius, Abraham (1723-1773), Swedish clergyman and botanist; studied at Uppsala 1745; ordained 1752; stationed at Örebro and Bodarne, Närke. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Samzelius* \[standard form in IPNI: *Samzelius*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
