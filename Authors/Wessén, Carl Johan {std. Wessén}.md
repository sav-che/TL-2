### Author

Wessén, Carl Johan (1812-1843), Swedish botanist; Dr. phil. Uppsala 1836; library assistant ib. 1839. (*Wessén*).

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection GB|GB]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 479; BL 2: 538, 731; BM 5: 2296; Herder p. 196; KR p. 754 (b. 12 Jun 1812, d. 26 Nov 1843); PR 10197, ed. 1: 11177.
