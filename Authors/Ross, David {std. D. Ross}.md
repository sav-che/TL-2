### Author

Ross, David (ca. 1810-1881), British (Scottish) classics teacher in Edinburgh. 
**Abbreviated name**: *D. Ross*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 4: 1734; Desmond p. 531 (d. 21 Feb 1881); Jackson p. 43, 280; Kew 4: 519; Rehder 1: 412; Tucker 1: 607.
