### Author

Fries, Elias Petrus (1834-1858), Swedish botanist, son of E. M. Fries. 
**Abbreviated name**: *E. P. Fr.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection UPS|UPS]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 11; KR p. 215-216; PR 3114.
- Anon., Bot. Not. 1858: 205-206.

#### Eponymy
