### Author

Norén, Carl Otto Gustaf (1876-1914), Swedish botanist; Dr. phil. Uppsala 1907; high school teacher in Stockholm (1910), Karlskrona (1911), and Vänersborg (1914). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Norén* \[standard form in IPNI: *Norén*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
