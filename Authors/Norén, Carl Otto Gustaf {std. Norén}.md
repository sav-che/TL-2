### Author

Norén, Carl Otto Gustaf (1876-1914), Swedish botanist; Dr. phil. Uppsala 1907; high school teacher in Stockholm (1910), Karlskrona (1911), and Vänersborg (1914). (*Norén*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
