### Author

Sébire, Albert (1863-1936), French botanist and clergyman (Pères du Saint-Esprit); had an experimental garden at Thiés, Sénégal from 1887-ca. 1925(?); in retirement in Belgium. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Sébire*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection P|P]].
