### Author

Bosshard, Walter (fl. 1927-1928), German explorer on the German Central Asian Expedition of 1927-1928. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Bossh.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection W|W]], [[Collection Z|Z]].
