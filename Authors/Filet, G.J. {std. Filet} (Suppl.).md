### Author

\* Filet, G.J. (1825-1891), Dutch army physician, born in Utrecht, studied medicine, went to the East Indies (Indonesia) 1847, retired 1872, died 1891 in Utrecht. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Filet*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
