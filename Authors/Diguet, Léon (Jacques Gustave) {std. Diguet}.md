### Author

Diguet, Léon \[Jacques Gustave\] (1859-1926), French botanist, born at Le Havre 25 Jul 1859, died 31 Aug 1926, stationed at Santa Rosalia and Isla Santa Catalina. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Diguet*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection P|P]] (1500).

#### Bibliography and biography
