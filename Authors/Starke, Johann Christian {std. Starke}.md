### Author

Starke, Johann Christian (1744-1808), German (Prussian/Neumark) botanist; pastor at Gr. Tschirnau nr Guhrau, Silesia 1775-1808; floristic botanist and bryologist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Starke* \[standard form in IPNI: *Starke*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Starke left his herbarium to the Schlesische Gesellschaft für Vaterländische Cultur, now possibly at [[Collection WRSL|WRSL]].

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 4: 117; Barnhart 3: 318 (d. 1 Jun 1808); Frank 3 (Anh.): 100; SBC p. 132.
