### Author

Kremer, Jean Pierre (1812-1867), French (Lorraine) botanist at Metz. (*Kremer*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection P|P]], [[Collection P|P]]-CO.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 320; BM 2: 1027; GR p. 331; IH 2: 387; Jackson p. 307; PR 4875-4877 (ed. 1: 5407); Rehder 5: 474.
- Anon., Bull. Soc. bot. France 14(c.r.): 190. 1867, 15: xv. 1868.
- Gosson, E. et Durieu, M. C, Exp. Sci. Algérie Bot. 2: xxxii-xxxiii. 1868.
- Maire, R., Progr. connaiss. bot. Algérie 84-85. 1931.

#### Eponymy

*Kremeria* Durieu de Maisonneuve (1846); *Kremeria* Cosson et Durieu de Maisonneuve ex Cosson (1856); *Kremeriella* Maire (1932).
