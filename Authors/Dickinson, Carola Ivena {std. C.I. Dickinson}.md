### Author

Dickinson, Carola Ivena (fl. 1934), English botanist, lichenologist, at Newham MA 1925, worked at the Herbarium, Royal Botanic Gardens, Kew. 
**Abbreviated name**: *C.I. Dickinson* \[standard form in IPNI: *C.I.Dickinson*\]

#### Herbarium and types
