### Author

Barros, Manuel (1880-1973), Argentine botanist and physician, honorary collaborator with the Museo Ciencias naturales Buenos Aires, Bot. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Barros* \[standard form in IPNI: *Barros*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
