### Author

Soppitt, Henry Thomas (1858-1899), British (Yorkshire) mycologist and drysalter, in Bradford until 1894; in Halifax 1894-1899. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Soppitt* \[standard form in IPNI: *Soppitt*\]
