### Author

Suard, V. (*fl*. 1843), French pharmacist and botanist at Nancy. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Suard* \[standard form in IPNI: *Suard*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection CGE|CGE]], [[Collection E|E]] and [[Collection NA|NA]].

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 4: 658; BL 2: 180, 720; Frank 3 (Anh.): 101; IH 2: (files); PR 9031; Rehder 1: 409; Tucker 1: 672; TL-2/11.292.

#### Biofile

- Hedge, I.C. & J.M. Lamond, Index coll. Edinb. herb. 134. 1970 (herb. mat. at E).

#### Eponymy

*Suardia* Schrank (1820) honors Peter Suardo who wrote in 1528 the book *Thesaurus aromatariorum medicis et aromatariis acque utilis*.
