### Author

Lunell, Joël (1851-1920), Swedish-born American physician and botanist; studied at Uppsala; physician at Willow City and Leeds, North Dakota, 1889-1920. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lunell* \[standard form in IPNI: *Lunell*\]

#### Herbarium and types
