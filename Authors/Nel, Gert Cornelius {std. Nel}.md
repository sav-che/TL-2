### Author

Nel, Gert Cornelius (1885-1950), South African botanist; Dr. phil. Berlin 1914 (under Engler); professor of botany at Stellenbosch, South Africa 1921-1950. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Nel* \[standard form in IPNI: *Nel*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection STE|STE]] and [[Collection STE|STE]]-U; further material at [[Collection PRE|PRE]].
