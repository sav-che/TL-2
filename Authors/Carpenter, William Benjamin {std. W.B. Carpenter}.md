### Author

Carpenter, William Benjamin (1813-1885), British physician, naturalist and microscopist. (*W. B. Carpenter*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Eponymy

*Carpenteria* J. Torrey (1853) is dedicated to William Marbury Carpenter (1811-1848), American botanist.
