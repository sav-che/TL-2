### Author

Reinsch, \[Edgar\] Hugo \[Emil\] (1809-1884), German (Bavarian) high school teacher and naturalist at Erlangen. 
**Abbreviated name**: *H. Reinsch* \[standard form in IPNI: *H.Reinsch*\]

#### Herbarium and types
