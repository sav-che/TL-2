### Author

Schneider, Josef (x-1885), Austrian botanist; assistant at the botanical museum and garden of the University of Wien. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Jos. Schneid.* \[standard form in IPNI: *J.Schneid.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
