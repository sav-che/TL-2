### Author

Sandwith, Mrs. Cecil Ivry (1871-1961), English botanist; collected plants in Northern Rhodesia 1929; floristically active in the Bristol area from 1909. 
**Abbreviated name**: *C.I. Sandw.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BRIST|BRIST]] (British adventives, [[Collection K|K]] (Rhodesia 1929), [[Collection SRGH|SRGH]] (Rhodesia); other material at [[Collection BM|BM]] (Rhodesia), [[Collection BRISTM|BRISTM]], [[Collection NMW|NMW]] (England), [[Collection U|U]] (England).
