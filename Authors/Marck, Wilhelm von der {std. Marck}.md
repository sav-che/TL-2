### Author

Marck, Wilhelm von der (1814-1900), German pharmacist and naturalist at Hamm; from 1866 living as "Privatgelehrter". 
**Abbreviated name**: *Marck* \[standard form in IPNI: *Marck*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BONN|BONN]], [[Collection MSTR|MSTR]].
