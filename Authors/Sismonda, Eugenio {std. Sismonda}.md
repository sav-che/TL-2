### Author

Sismonda, Eugenio (1815-1870), Italian palaeontologist; Dr. med. Torino 1839. (*Sismonda*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Andrews p. 333; Barnhart 3: 283; BM 4: 1933; CSP 5: 710, 8: 962; Jackson p. 60, 180, 187; LS 24925-24927; PR 8699; Quenstedt p. 400 (b. 29 Apr 1815).

#### Biofile

- Anon., Boll. Comit. geol. Italia 1870(1): 144-145 (obit. not.); Bonplandia 3: 34. 1855 (cogn. Leopoldina: Moro).
- Sobrero, Atti Acad. Sci. Torino 6: 327-357. 1871 (obit.; b. 29 Apr 1815).
- Zittel, K.A. van, Gesch. Geol. Paläont. 543, 705, 808, 837. 1899.

#### Eponymy

*Sismondaea* Delponte (1854).
