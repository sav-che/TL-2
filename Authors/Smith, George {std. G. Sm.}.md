### Author

Smith, George (1895-1967), British chemist and self-taught mycologist; M.S. Manchester 1918; with a firm of cotton manufaturers 1919; assistant London School of Hygiene and Tropical Medicine 1930-1961. (*G. Sm.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection CMI|CMI]], [[Collection K|K]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Ainsworth p. 112, 221, 308, 330, 347; Desmond p. 567; Hawksworth p. 186; Roon p. 105.

#### Biofile

- Ainsworth, G.C., Mycologia 60(5): 991-993. 1968 (obit., portr., b. 15 Dec 1895, d. 29 Mar 1967).
- Bisby, G.R., Trans. Brit. mycol. Soc., index 1896-1946, p. 22. 1952.
- Onions, A.H.S., Nature 215: 109. 1967 (obit. not.), Trans. Brit. mycol. Soc. 50(3): 339-340. 1967 (obit., portr.).

#### Eponymy
