### Author

Ruijs (Ruys), Johannes Marinus (*fl*. 1884), Dutch botanist and zoologist; participated in the Dutch Polar Expedition of 1882-1883; Dr. phil. Amsterdam 1884. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Ruijs* \[standard form in IPNI: *Ruijs*\]
