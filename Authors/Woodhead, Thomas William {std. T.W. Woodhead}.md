### Author

Woodhead, Thomas William (1863-1940), British ecologist; Dr. phil. Zürich 1906; lecturer Huddersfield Technical College 1891; later head of the Biological Dept. ib. 
**Abbreviated name**: *T.W. Woodhead*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown; some material in [[Collection EGHB|EGHB]], [[Collection HDD|HDD]], [[Collection QMC|QMC]] (see Kent & Allen 1984).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 517; BL 2: 275, 733; Desmond p. 674; TL-2/11.174.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Brit. Mus. gen. cat. print. books 260: 706-707. 1965.
- Kent, D.H. & D.E. Allen, Brit. Irish herb. 278. 1984.
- Pearsall, W.H., Naturalist 779: 157-159. 1940 (appreciation, portr.); Proc. Linn. Soc. 152: 383-385. 1941 (appreciation, list publ.).
