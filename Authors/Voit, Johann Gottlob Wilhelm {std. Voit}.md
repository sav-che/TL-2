### Author

Voit, Johann Gottlob Wilhelm (1787-1813), German (Bavarian) physician and botanist at Schweinfurt. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Voit* \[standard form in IPNI: *Voit*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection M|M]] (through Ignatz Döllinger, 1770-1841). – *Letter* to Sturm at [[Collection UPS|UPS]]-UB.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 442 (d. 12 Jun 1813); BM 5: 2235; Frank 3 (Anh.): 109; Herder p. 267; PR 9846, ed. 1: 10825; SBC p. 134; SY p. 44; TL-1/1275, 1371.

#### Eponymy
