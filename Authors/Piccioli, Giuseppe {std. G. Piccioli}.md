### Author

Piccioli, Giuseppe (*fl*. 1783-1818), Italian botanist; curator of a botanical garden at Firenze. 
**Abbreviated name**: *G. Piccioli* \[standard form in IPNI: *G.Piccioli*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – Letters at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Jackson p. 434; PR 7135 (ed. 1: 8006); Rehder 5: 671; Saccardo 1: 127.
