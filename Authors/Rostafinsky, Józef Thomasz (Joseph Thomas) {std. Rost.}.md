### Author

Rostafinsky, Józef Thomasz (Joseph Thomas) (1850-1928), Polish botanist, born in Warsaw; studied in Jena 1869-1870, Halle 1870-1872 and Strassburg 1872-1873; Dr. phil. Strassburg 1872-1873; Dr. phil. Strassburg (with A. de Bary) 1873; assistant ib. 1873-1876; lecturer at the University of Cracow 1876, e.o. professor of botany 1878, regular professor and director of the botanic garden 1881-1912. (*Rost.*).
