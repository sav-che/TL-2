### Author

Stelfox, Arthur Wilson (1883-1972), Irish architect and naturalist; at the National Museum Dublin 1920-1948. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Stelfox*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BEL|BEL]] (including correspondence and notes; over 400 specimens); further material at [[Collection BM|BM]] (over 2400 specimens, Cyperac), [[Collection DBN|DBN]], [[Collection GALW|GALW]].

#### Bibliography and biography

BL 2: 282, 284, 289, 720; BM 5: 2013; Desmond p. 582 (d. 19 Mai 1972).
