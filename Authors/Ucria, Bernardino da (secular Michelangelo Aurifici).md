### Author

Ucria, Bernardino da (secular: Michelangelo Aurifici) (1739-1796), Italian clergyman (order of St. Francis) and botanist at Palermo, "demonstrator at the Royal Palermo botanical Academy". 
**Abbreviated name**: *Ucria* \[standard form in IPNI: *Ucria*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 91 (Aurifici, Michelangelo, d. 29 Jan 1796), 3: 412; BM 5: 2159; Herder p. 149; Hortus 3: 1205; Jackson p. 436; PR 9594, ed. 1: 10567; Rehder 1: 61; Saccardo 1: 61 (da Ucria), cron. p. xxxv; Tucker 1: 705; Zander ed. 11, p. 828.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Malpighia 13: 97. 1899 (on portr.).

#### Eponymy

*Ucriana* Willdenow (1798).
