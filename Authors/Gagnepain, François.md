### Author

Gagnepain, François (1866-1952), French botanist at Paris, specialist on the flora of south-eastern Asia 
**Abbreviated name**: *Gagnep.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Gagnep.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection P|P]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 214.

#### Bibliography and biography

#### Composite works

Gagnepain was one of the major contributors to Lecomte, *Flore Indochine* for which he wrote the *tome préliminaire* and many family treatments which are listed under Lecomte.

#### Eponymy

*Gagnepainia* K. M. Schumann (1904).
