### Author

Stedman, John Gabriel (1744-1797), Scottish-born soldier in the Dutch colonial army; in Suriname 1772-1777. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Stedman* \[standard form in IPNI: *Stedman*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
