### Author

Strohmeyer, Otto \[August Karl\] (1869-?), German (Prussian) botanist; Dr. phil. Erlangen 1896; assistant at the Institute for Hygiene in Hamburg. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Strohmeyer* \[standard form in IPNI: *Strohm.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 340 (b. 26 Jan 1869); Christiansen p. 320; LS 26052-26052a; Nordstedt suppl. p. 15.
