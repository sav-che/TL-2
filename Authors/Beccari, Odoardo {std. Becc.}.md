### Author

Beccari, Odoardo (1843-1920), Italian botanical explorer and palm specialist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Becc.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Becc.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection FI|FI]] (13.000).
*Ref*.: IH 1 (ed. 6): 355, 2: 63.
- Steenis, Webbia 8(2): 427-436. 1952.
