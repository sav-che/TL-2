### Author

Fritsch, Karl \[Carl\] Wilhelm Georg (Freiherr) von (1838-1906), German vulcanologist and paleontologist, born 11 Nov 1838 in Weimar, visited Teneriffe 1862, died 9 Jan 1906 in Halle (on the Saale River). (*K.W.G. Fritsch*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 12; BM 2: 626-627; NDB 5: 623 (d. "Goddula b. Merseburg"); Sarjeant p. 1047.

#### Biofile

- Boettger, O., 1906. Ber. Senckenberg. Naturf. Ges. Frankfurt 1906: 27\*-29\*.
- Luedecke, O., 1906. Leopoldina 42: 44-53. (portr.) (n.v.)

#### Composite works

Edited the serial *Leopoldina* (Jena).
