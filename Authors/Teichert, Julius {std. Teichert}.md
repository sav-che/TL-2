### Author

Teichert, Julius (x-1873), German (Prussian) high school teacher and botanist; con-rector at the gymnasium at Freienwalde a. Oder. (*Teichert*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 5: 2081; PR 9129.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Bot. Zeit. 31: 543. 1873 (d. 27 Jul 1873).

#### Eponymy

*Teichertodinium* S.A.J. Pocock et W.A.S. Sarjeant (1972) honors prof. Curt Teichert, palaeontologist and stratigrapher.
