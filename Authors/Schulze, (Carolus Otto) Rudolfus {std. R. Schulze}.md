### Author

Schulze, \[Carolus Otto\] Rudolfus (1870-?), German (Berlin) botanist; studied at the Berlin University; Dr. phil. ib. 1893 as student of Adolf Engler. 
**Abbreviated name**: *R. Schulze* \[standard form in IPNI: *R.Schulze*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
