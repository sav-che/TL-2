### Author

Schrüfer, Theodor (1836-1908), German (Bavarian) priest and palaeobotanist; high school teacher ("Lyceal-Professor") for chemistry and natural history at Bamberg 1865-1904. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Schrüfer*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
