### Author

Schumacher, Albert (1893-1975), German (Rhine-Prussian) botanist and school teacher; teacher's examination 1919 and 1921; teacher at Waldbröl 1921-1958; active floristic botanist, batologist and bryologist. (*A. Schumach.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MSTR|MSTR]] (fide F. Runge 1967); some further material in [[Collection C|C]], [[Collection DBN|DBN]] (bryo.), [[Collection DPU|DPU]] (bry.), [[Collection GB|GB]], [[Collection M|M]] (lich.), [[Collection NMW|NMW]]. – Letters at [[Collection G|G]].
