### Author

Albert, Federico (fl. 1909), Chilean botanist, ornithologist and silviculturist, sometime in charge of the Seccion de Aguas; Bosques del Ministerio de Industria, Santiago de Chile. 
**Abbreviated name**: *F. Albert*
