### Author

Robinson, James Fraser (1857-1927), British (English) schoolmaster and naturalist at Hull, Yorkshire. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J.F. Robinson* \[standard form in IPNI: *J.F.Rob.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection HUL|HUL]] (destroyed 1943).

#### Note

Not to be confused with James Frodsham Robinson (1838-1884), writer on the flora of Cheshire and contributor to H.C. Watson, *Topogr. bot.* (see e.g. J. Britten, J. Bot. 42: 300-301. 1904.

#### Bibliography and biography

BL 2: 276, 709; BM 4: 1714; CSP 5: 238, 8: 764; Desmond p. 525; IH 2: (files); TL-2/see J.J. Marshall; Tucker 1: 601.
