### Author

Heinricher, Emil Johann Lambert (1856-1934), Austrian botanist; Dr. phil. Graz 1879; from 1889 director of the botanical garden and professor of botany at Innsbruck University. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Heinr.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection IB|IB]].
