### Author

Payrau, Vincent (*fl*. 1900), French botanist and pharmacist, Dr. pharm., Paris, 1900, resident pharmacist of the Paris hospitals. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Payrau* \[standard form in IPNI: *Payrau*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
