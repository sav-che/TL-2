### Author

O'Meara, Eugene (c. 1815-1880), Irish clergyman and botanist (diatomologist) at Dublin; M. A. Dublin 1858. 
**Abbreviated name**: *O'Meara*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BM|BM]] (Diatoms).
