### Author

Schöpfer, Franz Xaver (1777-1855), Austrian botanist; practicing physician at Innsbruck. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Schöpfer* \[standard form in IPNI: *Schöpfer*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection IBF|IBF]].
