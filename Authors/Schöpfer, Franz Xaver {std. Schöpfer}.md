### Author

Schöpfer, Franz Xaver (1777-1855), Austrian botanist; practicing physician at Innsbruck. (*Schöpfer*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection IBF|IBF]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 237 (d. 11 Oct 1855); BM 4: 1855; DTS 1: 269, xxiii; GR p. 459; IH 2: (files); PR 8317, ed. 1: 9239; Rehder 1: 439; Tucker 1: 634.
