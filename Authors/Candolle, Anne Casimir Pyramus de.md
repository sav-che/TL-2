### Author

Candolle, Anne Casimir Pyramus de (1836-1918), Swiss botanist, son of Alphonse de Candolle. 
**Abbreviated name**: *C. DC* \[standard form in IPNI: *C.DC.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection G|G]] ([[Collection G|G]]-DC).
