### Author

Mössier, Johann Christoph (*fl*. 1815), German teacher and botanist; in Hamburg 1804-1806. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Mössler* \[standard form in IPNI: *Mössler*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
