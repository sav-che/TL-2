### Author

Cerón y Martínez, Salvador (1828-1897), Spanish botanist, born 11 Jun 1828, died 8 Jan 1897 in Cádiz. (*Cerón*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 327; BM 1: 331; Kew 1: 503; Rehder 5(index): 168.
