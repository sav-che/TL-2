### Author

Claypole, Edward Waller (1835-1901), American geologist and palaeobotanist in Ohio (1883-1898) and in California (Pasadena) (1898-1901). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Claypole* \[standard form in IPNI: *Claypole*\]

#### Collections

Unknown.
