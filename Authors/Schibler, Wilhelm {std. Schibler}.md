### Author

Schibler, Wilhelm (1861-1931), Swiss botanist; Dr. med. et phil. Bern 1887; from 1890 physician at Davos. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Schibler*

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection Z|Z]].

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 12(3): 162; Barnhart 3: 224 (b. 9 Jun 1861, d. 18 Nov 1931); BFM no. 1050; BL 2: 575, 714; CSP 18: 511; Rehder 2: 748.

#### Biofile

- Suchlandt, O. & J. Ferdmann, Ber. Schweiz. bot. Ges. 41(1): 6-7. 1932 (obit.).
