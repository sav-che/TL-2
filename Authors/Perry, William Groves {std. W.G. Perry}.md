### Author

Perry, William Groves (1796-1863), British bookseller and botanist, curator of the Warwickshire Museum at Warwick. 
**Abbreviated name**: *W.G. Perry* \[standard form in IPNI: *W.G.Perry*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection WAR|WAR]]; further material at [[Collection OXF|OXF]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 71; BB p. 242; BM 4: 1551, Clokie p. 223; Desmond p. 490; IH (ed. 7): 339; Jackson p. 261; Kew 4: 279; PR 7053; Rehder 1: 397.
