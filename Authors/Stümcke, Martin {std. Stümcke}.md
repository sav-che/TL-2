### Author

Stümcke, Martin (1853-1927), German (Bremen) botanist; studied pharmacy 1869-1871 at Braunschweig and 1871-1877 at Halle; pharmacist at Lüneburg 1883-1884; chemist at a cement factory in Lüneburg 1884-1918. (*Stümcke*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BFM 36; GR p. 143 (b. 9 Mar 1853, d. 14 Jan 1927), cat. p. 55; LS 26084-26085.
