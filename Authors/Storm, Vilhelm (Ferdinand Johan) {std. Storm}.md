### Author

Storm, Vilhelm \[Ferdinand Johan\] (1835-1913), Norwegian botanist and zoologist; studied at the University of Oslo; curator of the Trondhjem Museum 1856-1913. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Storm* \[standard form in IPNI: *Storm*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection TRH|TRH]].
