### Author

Saunders, James (1839-1925), English botanist and geologist, and straw-hat and bonnet manufacturer at Luton. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. Saunders*

[[Collection LTN|LTN]]; other material in [[Collection AK|AK]], [[Collection BFD|BFD]], [[Collection BM|BM]], [[Collection K|K]] (Chara), [[Collection MANCH|MANCH]], [[Collection OXF|OXF]].
