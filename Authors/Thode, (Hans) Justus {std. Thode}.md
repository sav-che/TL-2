### Author

Thode, \[Hans\] Justus (1859-1932), German-born S. African plant collector; to S. Africa 1885 or 1886, collected from 1886-1931 maintaining himself as tutor at missionary schools and farms. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Thode*

#### Herbarium and types

Personal herbarium up till 1920 at [[Collection STE|STE]], id. after 1921 at [[Collection PRE|PRE]]; further material at [[Collection B|B]] (mainly destr.), [[Collection K|K]], [[Collection MO|MO]], [[Collection NH|NH]], [[Collection PH|PH]], [[Collection US|US]].
