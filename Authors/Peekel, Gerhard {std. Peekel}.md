### Author

Peekel, Gerhard (1876-1949), German missionary and plant collector; ord. 1902 Hiltrup; from 1904 in the Bismarck Archipelago; from 1908 at New Mecklenburg (New Ireland). (*Peekel*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection B|B]] (partly destroyed), [[Collection BO|BO]] (orig. after 1939); further material at [[Collection BPI|BPI]], [[Collection L|L]] and [[Collection Z|Z]].

#### Eponymy

*Peekelia* Harms (1920); *Peekeliodendron* Sleumer (1937); *Peekeliopanax* Harms (1926).
