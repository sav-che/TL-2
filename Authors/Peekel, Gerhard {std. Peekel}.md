### Author

Peekel, Gerhard (1876-1949), German missionary and plant collector; ord. 1902 Hiltrup; from 1904 in the Bismarck Archipelago; from 1908 at New Mecklenburg (New Ireland). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Peekel* \[standard form in IPNI: *Peekel*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection B|B]] (partly destroyed), [[Collection BO|BO]] (orig. after 1939); further material at [[Collection BPI|BPI]], [[Collection L|L]] and [[Collection Z|Z]].
