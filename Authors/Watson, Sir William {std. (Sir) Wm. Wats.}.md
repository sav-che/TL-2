### Author

Watson, Sir William (1715-1787), British physician, physicist and botanist; MD Halle & Wittenberg 1757; physician to the Foundling Hospltal, London 1762-1787.
**Abbreviated name**: (*Sir*) *Wm. Wats.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
