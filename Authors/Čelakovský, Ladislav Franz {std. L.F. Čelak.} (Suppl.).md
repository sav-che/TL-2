### Author

\* Čelakovský, Ladislav Franz (1864-1916), Czech botanist, son of L.J. Čelakovský, honor. docent at the Bohemian technical University 1897, subsequently full professor at the same university. 
**Abbreviated name**: *L.F. Čelak.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection PR|PR]], further material at [[Collection PL|PL]].

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 1: 230; Barnhart 1: 325 (d. Dec 1916, Praha); BM 1: 330, 6: 192; Futak-Domin p. 144; IH 2: 120; Kew 1: 501; LS 4909-4911; TL-2/1: 477.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Bot. Centralbl. 70: 399. 16 Jun 1897 (appt. honorary docent); Vesmír 32: 62. 1903; Allg. Bot. Z. 23: 32. 1917 (d.); Bot. Not. 1917: 141 (d. 1 Dec 1916); Hedwigia 59(1) (Beibl.): (63). 1917 (d., suicide through drowning, fide Voss. Zeit., Dec 1916); Leopoldina 53: 67. 1917 (d. Dec 1916); Nat. Nov. 39(1-2): 41. 1917 (d.); ÖBZ 66(7-9): 312. Jul-Sep 1916; Preslia 48(4): 298. 1976. (b. 3 Dec 1864, d. 31 Dec 1916).
- Baudyš, E., Živa 15 (53): 19-20. 1967.
- Degen, A. von, Magyar Bot. Lapok 15: 310. 1917.

#### Note

The exact date of Čelakovský’s death seems not to be known, most periodicals give December, Bot. Not. says 1 Dec 1916, Preslia mentions 31 Dec 1916; in ÖBZ his death is mentioned in the July-Sep issue (appeared later?).
