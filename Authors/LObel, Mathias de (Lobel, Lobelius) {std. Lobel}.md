### Author

L'Obel, Mathias de \[Lobel, Lobelius\] (1538-1616), Flemish botanist; worked with Rondelet at Montpellier 1565-1566; from 1566-1572 in England; from 1572-1584 in the Netherlands, from 1584 again in England; botanist to James I; curator of Lord Edward Zouche's garden at Hackney. (*Lobel*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection NO|NO]] herbarium material known to be extant. Legré states that after Rondelet's death in 1566, Pena and l'Obel went to London, taking their herbaria with them. At the time of l'Obel's stay with Rondelet at Montpellier herbaria were being made by Rondelet and his disciples.
