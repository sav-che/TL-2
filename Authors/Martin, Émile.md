### Author

Martin, Émile (1810-1895), French botanist and judge at Romorantin (Loir-et-Cher). 
**Abbreviated name**: *E. Martin* \[standard form in IPNI: *E.Martin*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection P|P]] (12.000).

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 6(2): 844 \[real first name stated to be Pierre\]; Barnhart 2: 454 (b. 18 Nov 1810, d. 15 Feb 1895); BL 2: 171, 689; BM 3: 1249; IH 2: 507; Jackson p. 290; Kew 3: 602; Morren ed. 10, p. 68; Rehder 5: 548; Tucker 1: 463.
- Franchet, A., Bull. Soc. bot. France 42: 725-726. 1895.
