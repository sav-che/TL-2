### Author

Ashton, Ruth Elisabeth (Mrs. Aven Nelson) (1896-x), Botanist, grad. Mt. Holyoke College 1922, MS Colorado Agricultural College 1930, worked in University of Wyoming herbarium as graduate assistant; naturalist in the Rocky Mountain National Park 1925-1931. (*R.E. Ashton*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BL 1: 170; Ewan ed. 1: 153, ed.2: 78.
