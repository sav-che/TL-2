### Author

Schramm, Alphons (1823-1875), French (Alsatian) government official and botanist in French Guiana and, from 1848-1873 in Guadeloupe. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Schramm*

#### Herbarium and types

The original herbarium made jointly with H.P. Mazé (q.v.) was destroyed by fire in Guadeloupe in 1871. – Some material at [[Collection B|B]] (destr.), [[Collection PC|PC]] and [[Collection US|US]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 240 (b. 1 Aug 1823, d. Mar 1875); BM 4: 1860; CSP 8: 885, 11: 341; De Toni 1: cxiii; Jackson p. 370; PR 8377; TL-2/5759, 7772, see H.P. Mazé.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Bot. Zeit. 7: 679-680. 1849 (obit. notice).
- Lindemann, E. von, Bull. Soc. imp. Natural. Moscou 61(1): 68. 1886.
- Urban, I., Symb. ant. 1: 105-107. 1898, 3: 123. 1902.

#### Composite works

With H.P. Mazé, *Essai alg. Guadeloupe*, see TL-2/5759. – *Note*: The date of the facsimile edition, Berlin (W.Junk) is given as 1904 by Bot. Centralbl. (14 Jun 1904) and Hewigia (15 Jul 1904).

#### Eponymy

*Schrammia* P.A. Dangeard (1889); *Note*: *Schrammia* N.L. Britton et J.N. Rose (1930) probably honors J.R. Schramm (1885-?), American biologist and early editor of *Biological abstracts*.
