### Author

Jelliffe, Smith Ely (1866-1945), American botanist at New York; Ph. D. Columbia University 1899, professor of pharmacognosy at the College of Pharmacy, City of New York (1894). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Jell.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
