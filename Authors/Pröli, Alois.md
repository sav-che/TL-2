### Author

Pröli, Alois (*fl*. 1839), Austrian physician and botanist; Dr. med. Wien 1839. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Pröll*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection W|W]].

Alumnus C.R. Convictus. In theses adnexas disputabitur in aedibus Universitatis die 9. martii anni mdcccxxxix. Viennae (Ex typographeo viduae Antonii Pichler) 1839. Oct.
