### Author

Lobb, Thomas (1820-1894), British plant collector and explorer for Veitch and Son (1843-1860) who collected in India and the Malesian area. (*T. Lobb*).

#### Herbarium and types

The original Veitch herbarium is at [[Collection K|K]]; duplicates are widely distributed; see IH and [[Collection SK|SK]] (itin.).
