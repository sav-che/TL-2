### Author

Piller, Mathias (1733-1788), Austrian botanist and clergyman (S. J.); professor of natural history at the Theresianum in Vienna; later idem in Buda. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Piller*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
