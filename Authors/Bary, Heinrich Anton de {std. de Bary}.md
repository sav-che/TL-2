### Author

Bary, Heinrich Anton de (1831-1888), German botanist, "The founder of modern mycology" (Ainsworth and Bisby). 
**Abbreviated name**: *de Bary*

#### Collections

BERN, BM (original, not located).
*Ref*.: IH 2: 58.
