### Author

Rayner, John Frederick (1854-1947), British florist and naturalist at Swaythling, Southampton. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Rayner*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BMH|BMH]]; further material in [[Collection OXF|OXF]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 133; BL 2: 242, 243, 244, 706; BM 4: 1657; Clokie p. 230; Desmond p. 514 (d. 19 Jan 1947); GR p. 644; Kelly p. 187; LS 38252-38255, 41630.

#### Biofile

- Kent, D.H., Brit. herb. 74. 1957.
- Lousley, J.E., Rep. Bot. Soc. Exch. Club Brit. Isl. 13(3): 233-234. 1948.

#### Composite works

*Fungi of the Isle of Wight*, in F. Morey, *A guide to the natural history of the Isle of Wight*, 1909, p. 42-64.
