### Author

Lauth, Thomas (1758-1826), Alsatian botanist and physician; Dr. med. Strasbourg 1781; professor of anatomy at Strasbourg. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lauth* \[standard form in IPNI: *Lauth*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
