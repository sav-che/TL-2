### Author

Watt, David Allan Poe (1830-1917), British-born Canadian naturalist at Montreal. (*D. Watt*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MO|MO]]; some further material at [[Collection BM|BM]], [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection K|K]]. *Letters* at [[Collection G|G]], [[Collection GH|GH]], [[Collection NY|NY]] (Barnhart corr.), [[Collection UPS|UPS]]-UB.
