### Author

Underwood, John (x-1834), British gardener; superintendent of the Botanic Garden, Glasnevin, Dublin 1798-1833. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. Underw.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection DBN|DBN]] (ex hort. Glasnevin).

#### Bibliography and biography
