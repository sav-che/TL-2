### Author

Bordère, Henri (1825-1889), French school teacher and botanist at Gèdre nr. Luz, Hautes Pyrenées, distributed series of exsiccatae of Pyrenean plants. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Bordère* \[standard form in IPNI: *Bordère*\]
