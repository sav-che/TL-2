### Author

Gessner, Johannes (1709-1790), Swiss naturalist, correspondent of Linnaeus, friend of Haller. (*Gessner*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection LINN|LINN]], [[Collection P|P]]-HA, [[Collection Z|Z]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 222.
