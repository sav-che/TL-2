### Author

Jolyclerc, Nicolas-Marie-Thérèse (1746-1817), French clergyman and botanist; for some time at Lyon; later teaching at the École centrale de l'Oise. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Jolycl.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
