### Author

Steiger, Emil (1861-1927), Swiss botanist and pharmacist at Basel; Dr. phil. h.c. Basel 1919. 
**Abbreviated name**: *E. Steiger* \[standard form in IPNI: *E.Steiger*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BAS|BAS]]; – *Letters* at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Biol.-Dokum. 17: 8891; BL 1: 568, 576, 719; PH 81, 567; Rehder 5: 822.

#### Biofile

- Becherer, A., Bauhinia 5(3): 167-168. 1975 (recollections; 6-8 Oct 1861, d. 31 Jan 1927).
- Senn, G., Ber. Schweiz. bot. Ges. 36: xiii-xvi. 1927; Verh. naturf. Ges. Basel 38: 527-531. 1927.

*Beiträge zur Morphologie der Polygala Senega* L., Inaugural-Dissertation... Berlin (Gebr. Borntraeger) 1920, Oct, 78 p. (repr. from Ber. deut. pharm. Ges. 1920). n.v.
