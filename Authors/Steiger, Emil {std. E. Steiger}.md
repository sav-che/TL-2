### Author

Steiger, Emil (1861-1927), Swiss botanist and pharmacist at Basel; Dr. phil. h.c. Basel 1919. 
**Abbreviated name**: *E. Steiger*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BAS|BAS]]; – *Letters* at [[Collection G|G]].

Biol.-Dokum. 17: 8891; BL 1: 568, 576, 719; PH 81, 567; Rehder 5: 822.

- Senn, G., Ber. Schweiz. bot. Ges. 36: xiii-xvi. 1927; Verh. naturf. Ges. Basel 38: 527-531. 1927.
