### Author

Merlet de la Boulaye, Gabriel-Eléonor (1736-1807), French botanist at Angers; director of the Angers botanical garden. (*Merlet*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 478 (b. 3 Apr 1736, d. 17 Feb 1807); BM 3: 1291; GR p. 340; PR 6106 (ed. 1: 6807); Rehder 1: 407; Tucker 1: 480.
- Candolle, A. P. de, Mém. souv. 172. 1862.
- Dawson, W. R., Smith papers 69. 1934.

#### Eponymy

The etymology of *Merleta* Refinesque (1840) is not given by Rafinesque.
