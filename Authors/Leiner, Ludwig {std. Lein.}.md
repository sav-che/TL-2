### Author

Leiner, Ludwig (1830-1901), German pharmacist and naturalist at Konstanz. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lein.*

#### Herbarium and types

Bryophytes at [[Collection G|G]].
*Exsiccatae*:
- (1) *Herb. plant. crit. select. hybr. flor. rhenan.* (1868), set at [[Collection L|L]].
- (2) *Kryptogamen Badens*, fasc. 1-21, 1860-1880, together with J. B. Jack and E. Stizenberger, sets at [[Collection BM|BM]], [[Collection FH|FH]], [[Collection G|G]], [[Collection HAL|HAL]], [[Collection K|K]], [[Collection NY|NY]], [[Collection S|S]], [[Collection STR|STR]].
*Ref*.: Baur, Mitt. Bad. bot. Ver. 87-89: 301-305. 1881 (list and ind. fasc. 1-10). (n.v., fide Nordstedt, C.F.O., Ind. Desmid. bibl. 35. 1896).
