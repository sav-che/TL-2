### Author

Lachmann, Heinrich Wilhelm Ludolph (1800-1861), German physician and botanist at Braunschweig. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lachm.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 3: 1042; CSP 3: 790; Jackson p. 302; Kew 3: 346; PR 4970 (ed. 1: 5485); Rehder 5: 481.

### Publications

##### n.4078. Flora brunsvicensis

**Title**
*Flora brunsvicensis*, oder Aufzählung und Beschreibung der in der Umgegend von Braunschweig wildwachsenden Pflanzen... Braunschweig (bei G. C. E. Meyer) 1827-1831, 2 vols. (in 3). Oct.
**Abbreviated title**: *Fl. brunsvic.*

**Notes**
*1*: Sep-Dec 1827 (p. x: 23 Mai 1827, p. xliv: Aug 1827), p. \[ii\]-xlvi, \[1-2, err.\], \[1\]-324, map, 4 charts.
*2*(*1*): Jun-Dec 1828 (p. xiv: Mai 1828; Acad. rd. 12 Jan 1829), p. \[ii\]-xiv, \[1\]-496.
*2*(*2*): 1831, p. \[1\]-352.
*Copies*: G, Regensburg. – *Alt. t.p.* vol. 1: "Flora der Umgegend von Braunschweig..." – Lachmann was a pioneer ecologist and geobotanist who pointed out correlations between certain plant associations and environmental conditions (Drude).
*Ref*.: Anon., Flora 11: 192, 561-569. 1828, 12: 746. 1829; Lit. Ber. Flora 1: 117-126. Mai-Jun 1831 (rev. 2(1, 2)).
- Drude, C. G. O., Hercyn. Florenbezirk 10, 14. 1902 (Veg. Erde vol. 6).
