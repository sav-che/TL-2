### Author

Pittoni, Josef Claudius, Ritter von Dannenfeldt (1797-1878), Austrian botanist and administrator ("Truchsess," sheriff). (*Pittoni*).

#### Eponymy

The genera *Pittonia* P. Miller (1754) and *Pittoniotis* Grisebach (1858), as well as the journal *Pittonia*; a series of papers relating to botany and botanists (Berkeley, California. Vol. 1-5. 1887-1905) honor Joseph Pitton Tournefort, *q.v.*
