### Author

Reverchon, Elisée (1835-1914), French plant collector, living at Bollème (Vaucluse) and later in Lyon; collected in S.E. France (1867-1877), Corsica (1878-1880), Sardinia (1881-1882), Crete (1883), Spain (1887-1906) and Algeria 1896-1898. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Reverchon* \[standard form in IPNI: *E.Rev.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Reverchon was a professional plant collector; his plants are found in many herbaria; important sets e.g. at [[Collection B|B]] (extant), [[Collection G|G]] and [[Collection P|P]]/[[Collection PC|PC]]. For a note on the reliability of the data on the labels see Burnat (1883).
