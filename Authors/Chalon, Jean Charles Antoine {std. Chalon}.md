### Author

Chalon, Jean Charles Antoine (1846-1921), Belgian botanist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Chalon* \[standard form in IPNI: *Chalon*\]

Unknown, material at [[Collection B|B]] and [[Collection DIN|DIN]].
