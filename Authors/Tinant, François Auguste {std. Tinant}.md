### Author

Tinant, François Auguste (1803-1853), Luxemburg botanist and administrator; customs officer 1828-1839, subsequently inspector-general of forests. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Tinant*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection LUX|LUX]]; further material (at least 1200) [[Collection BR|BR]].

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 2(2): 404; Backer p. 586; Barnhart 3: 385 (b. 3 Nov 1803, d. 26 Jan 1853); BL 1: 419, 724; BM 5: 2116; CSP 5: 998; Herder p. 172; PR 9362, ed. 1: 10325; Rehder 1: 392; SO add. 8449; TL-2/see A.L.S. Lejeune; Tucker 1: 692.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Biogr. luxemb. 2: 172-173. 1861.
- Crépin, F., Man. fl. belg. lxx. 1860.
- Klein, E.J., Monatsber. Ges. Luxemb. Naturf. ser. 2. 14: 125-126. 1920; Franç. Aug. Tinant. Ein Gedenkblatt zum 100. Jahrestage der Herausgabe seiner Flore luxemburgeoise, Luxemburg 1936, 27 p. (ex Arch. Grossh. Luxemb. Inst. nat.-mat. Sektion 14, 1936).
- Lefort, E.L., Soc. nat. Luxemb. Bull. 54: 31-160. 1950; Bull. Soc. Natural. Luxemb. ser. 2. 46: 188-203. 1952 (letters by T. to Lejeune).

#### Handwriting

Soc. nat. Luxemb. Bull. 54: 31-160. 1950 (ills. on plates in text.).

#### Eponymy

*Tinantia* Martens et Galeotti (1839) and probably also *Tinantia* Dumortier (1829, *nom. rej.*) and *Tinantia* Scheidweiler (1839, *nom. cons.*).
