### Author

Shannon, William Cummings (1851-1905), American physician and plant collector; MD Bellevue Medical Coll. 1874; assistant surgeon U.S. army 1875-1895; maj. surgeon 1895-1898; in retirement from 1898. (*Shannon*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection US|US]]; duplicates e.g. in [[Collection B|B]] (mainly destr.), [[Collection BR|BR]], [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection F|F]], [[Collection GH|GH]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 264 (b. 8 Mai 1851, d. 21 Apr 1905); BM 4: 1908; Urban-Berl. p. 397.

### Publications

##### n.11.856. An enumeration of the plants collected in Central America

**Title**
*An enumeration of the plants collected in Central America* by Dr. W.C. Shannon, assistant surgeon, U.S.A. John Donnell Smith 1891-1893. Washington 1898.

**Notes**
*Authorship*: unless stated otherwise the determinations are by John Donnell Smith (1829-1928); *Leguminosae* by M. Micheli; *Melastomaceae* and *Orchidaceae* by A. Cogniaux; *Begoniaceae* and *Piperaceae* by Cas. de Candolle; *Compositae* and *Solanaceae* by John M. Coulter; *Scitamineae, Iridaceae* and *Amaryllidaceae* by J.G. Baker, *Commelinaceae* by C.B. Clarke. – W.C. Shannon was the collector.
*Publ*.: 1898, p. \[1\]-24. *Copies*: BR, G, HH, US. – Intercontinental Railway Commission volume i, part ii, Report of surveys and explorations made by corps no. 1 in Guatemala, El Salvador, Honduras, Nicaragua and Costa Rica. Appendix iii.
