### Author

Richter, Paul Boguslav (1853-1911), German palaeobotanist and high school teacher at Quedlinburg (Saxony). 
**Abbreviated name**: *P.B. Richt.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection US|US]], Fossil collections at [[Collection S|S]]-PA, see Nathorst (1912).

#### Bibliography and biography

Andrews p. 321; Barnhart 3: 154 (b. 12 Dec 1853, d. 9 Oct 1911); BJI 2: 145; BM 4: 1700; CSP 18: 190; Kew 4: 464; Quenstedt p. 360.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Geol. Mag. ser. 2. dec. 5. 8: 528. 1911 (brief obit.).
- Nathorst, A.G., Palaeobot. Z. 1: 50-51. 1912. (palaeobot. coll. acquired by S-PA).
