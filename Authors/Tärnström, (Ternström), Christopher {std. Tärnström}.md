### Author

Tärnström, \[Ternström\], Christopher (1703-1746), Swedish teacher, clergyman and botanist; student at Uppsala 1724; teacher at Östhammar 1739; id. at Vaxholm 1741; Dr. phil. Uppsala 1746; chaplain in the Swedish East-Indian Company 1745; died at Pulo Condor (nr Cambodja) 1746. (*Tärnström*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection LINN|LINN]] (coll. Spain; other coll. lost). – For natural history collections see Jackson (1922) and Löwegren (1952). – For a manuscript travel journal see A.W. Grape (1918).
