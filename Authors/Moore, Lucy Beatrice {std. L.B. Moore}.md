### Author

Moore, Lucy Beatrice (1906-x), New Zealand botanist; M. Sc. Auckland 1929; at the Botany Division, Dept. Scientific and Industrial Research, Wellington. 
**Abbreviated name**: *L. B. Moore* \[standard form in IPNI: *L.B.Moore*\]
