### Author

Norman, Johannes Musaeus (1823-1903), Norwegian botanist; med. cand. Oslo 1847; lecturer (universitets-stipendiat) at Oslo 1849-1850, 1855-1859; from 1860-1876 forester in Troms and Finnmark. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Norm.*
