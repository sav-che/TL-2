### Author

Phillips, Henry (1779-1840), British botanist and horticultural writer; banker at Worthing, later living in London and Brighton. 
**Abbreviated name**: *H. Phillips* \[standard form in IPNI: *Phillips*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
