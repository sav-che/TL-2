### Author

Lortet, Mme. Clémence, née Richard (1772-1835), French botanist at Lyon; pupil and botanical companion of Gilibert, one of the founders of the Société linnéenne de Lyon. 
**Abbreviated name**: *C. Lortet* \[standard form in IPNI: *Lortet*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection LY|LY]], in the herbarium of the Société linnéenne.

#### Note on the lortet family

Clémence Richard (1772-1835; no known connection with the botanical Richard family) married Jean Pierre Lortet (x-1823) in 1791. Her son Pierre Lortet (1792-1868) physician and naturalist, also active as amateur botanist, married (1826) Nettchen Müller (1802-1837). Louis-Charles Lortet (1836-1909), physician and botanist was the third child born out of this marriage; see below for treatment. Clémence Lortet contributed to publications by Balbis and Gilibert; published-through Gilibert – a *Calendrier de Flore* (1809), and left a mss. *Promenades botaniques* (1804-1805).
