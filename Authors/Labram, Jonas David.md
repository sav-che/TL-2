### Author

Labram, Jonas David (1785-1852), Swiss botanical artist at Basel 
**Abbreviated name**: *Labram* \[standard form in IPNI: *Labram*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 331; BM 3: 1040; NI 1118-1119; PR (alph.) (ed. 1: 5483-5484); Rehder 5: 481.
- Anon., Verh. naturf. Ges. Basel 19(1): frontisp. 1907.
- Blunt, W., Art. bot. 111. 221, 264. 1950.
- Burckhardt, Verh. naturf. Ges. Basel 19: 1-36. 1907/08.
- Geiger, Verh. naturf. Ges. Basel 56: 372-389. 1945; Stultifera Navis 3: 28-33. 1946 (portr., full bibl.).
- Senn, Verh. naturf. Ges. Basel 54: 304-326. 1943 (on his publ. with Hegetschweiler, q.v.).
- Tecoz, Librarian 15(3): 166-179. 1972 (on Hegetschweiler and Labram).

#### Composite works

- (1) Drew the illustrations for J. Hegetschweiler, *Die Giftpflanzen der Schweiz* (see no. 2564).
- (2) For a complete list of Labram's works see Geiger, 1946, cited above.

#### Eponymy

*Labramia* Alph. de Candolle (1844).
