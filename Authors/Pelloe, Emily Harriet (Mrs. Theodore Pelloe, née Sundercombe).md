### Author

Pelloe, Emily Harriet (Mrs. Theodore Pelloe, née Sundercombe) (1878-1941), Australian botanist and artist, living mainly in Western Australia from 1901. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Pelloe* \[standard form in IPNI: *Pelloe*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
