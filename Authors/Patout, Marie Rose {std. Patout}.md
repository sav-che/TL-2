### Author

Patout, Marie Rose (*fl*. 1864), French herbalist and midwife at Toulon 
**Abbreviated name**: *Patout* \[standard form in IPNI: *Patout*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BL 2: 208, 700; Jackson p. 291; Rehder 5: 652; Tucker 1: 543.

### Publications

##### n.7496. Abrégé des plantes médicinales

**Title**
*Abrégé des plantes médicinales* croissant dans les environs de Toulon... Toulon (imprimerie et lithographie d'E. Aurel,...) 1864. Oct.
**Abbreviated title**: *Abr. pl. méd.*

**Notes**
*Publ*.: 1864 (p. 8: 4 Jul 1864), p. \[1\]-85. *Copy*: G. – We thought that one midwife/botanist would not be out of place in TL-2; the book is a simple enumeration of medicinal plants growing in the wild around Toulon.
