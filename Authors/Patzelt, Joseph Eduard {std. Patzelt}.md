### Author

Patzelt, Joseph Eduard (*fl*. 1842), Austrian botanist and physician in Wien, later in Bucarest. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Patzelt*

#### Herbarium and types

Some material in [[Collection DBN|DBN]], [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection K|K]] and [[Collection PR|PR]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 55; BM 4: 1530; Klášterský p. 140; PR 6984 (ed. 1: 7806); Rehder 1: 440.
