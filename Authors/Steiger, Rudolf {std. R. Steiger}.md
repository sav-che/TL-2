### Author

Steiger, Rudolf (1823-1908), Moravian botanist and administrator at Klobouk and Brünn (Brno). 
**Abbreviated name**: *R. Steiger* \[standard form in IPNI: *R.Steiger*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BRNU|BRNU]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Futak-Domin p. 563; Klásterský p. 176 (b. 26 Jun 1823, d. 15 Apr 1908).

- Oborny, A., Verh. naturf. Ver. Brünn 21(2): 25-26. 1882.
