### Author

Maximilian Alexander Philipp, Prinz zu Wied-Neuwied (1782-1867), German botanist and traveller in Brazil (1815-1817); in North America 1832-1834). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Maxim. Wied*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BR|BR]] (in herb. Martius), other sets at [[Collection B|B]], [[Collection LD|LD]], [[Collection LE|LE]] and [[Collection W|W]].

#### Note

Properly alphabetized under Wied-Neuwied.

#### Eponymy

*Neowedia* Schrader (1821); *Neuwiedia* Blume (1833). *Note: Maximiliana* C F. P. Martius (1824, *nom. cons.*) and *Maximilianea* C F. P. Martius in Schrank (1819, *nom. rej.*) are dedicated to Maximilian Joseph I, King of Bavaria, at whose command Martius undertook his famous journey to Brazil (1817-1820).
