### Author

Morgan, Robert (1863-1900), British artist and lithographer; contributed plates to e.g. J. Bot. 1882-1900; illustrated Fryer's *Potamogeton*. 
**Abbreviated name**: *R. Morgan* \[standard form in IPNI: *R.Morgan*\]

#### Herbarium and types

None; the originals of his drawings for Fryer and J. Bot. are at [[Collection BM|BM]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 513; BB p. 221; Desmond p. 450; TL-2/1: 1908 (q.v. for *The Potomagetons of the British Isles*).
- Anon., Proc. Linn. Soc. 113: 46-47. 1901.
- Britten, J., J. Bot. 38: 489-492. 1900 (portr.).
