### Author

Beinert, Carl Christian (1793-1868), German (Waitsdorf bei Bernstadt) pharmacist, palaeobotanist and mycologist at Charlottenbrunn (Schlesien), Dr. phil. Breslau 19 Aug 1849. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Beinert*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
