### Author

Debray, Marcel (fl. 1923-1971), French botanist, working in Le Havre mostly on the flora of the French coast. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Debray*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BL 2: 201, 204; Kew 2: 39.
