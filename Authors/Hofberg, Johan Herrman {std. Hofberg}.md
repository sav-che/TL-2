### Author

Hofberg, Johan Herrman (1823-1880), Swedish botanist; studied at Uppsala, Dr. phil. h.c. Jena 1877; curator of the Royal Armoury at Stockholm. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Hofberg* \[standard form in IPNI: *Hofberg*\]
