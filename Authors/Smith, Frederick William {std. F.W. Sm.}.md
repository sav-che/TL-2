### Author

Smith, Frederick William (1797-1835), British botanical artist. (*F.W. Sm.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 292; BM 4: 1945; Desmond p. 567 (b. 25 Aug 1797, d. 18 Jan 1835); DNB 18: 424; GR p. 76; NI 2: 269; PR 8730; TL-1/959, 1288; TL-2/7554.

#### Biofile

- Britten, J., J. Bot. 56: 238. 1918 (on F.W. Smith and the Florist's Magzine).

#### Composite works

Illustrator, J. Paxton, Mag. Bot., 1834-1835; R. Sweet, *Br. Flower Gard*. 1834-1835, artist.
