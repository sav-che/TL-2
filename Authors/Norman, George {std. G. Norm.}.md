### Author

Norman, George (1824-1882), British diatomologist and entomologist in Yorkshire. 
**Abbreviated name**: *G. Norm.* \[standard form in IPNI: *G.Norman*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection AWH|AWH]] and [[Collection BM|BM]] (155 slides); letters in Walker-Arnott corr. at [[Collection BM|BM]]. Newton (1952) reports that an algal herbarium at the Hull Museum was destroyed in World War II.

#### Eponymy

*Normania*Lowe (1872) is dedicated to Commander F. M. Norman R.N. (1833-?) who made many valuable botanical discoveries in Madeira.
