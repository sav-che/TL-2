### Author

Roques, Joseph (1772-1850), French botanist and physician; connected with the military hospitals and faculty of medicine, Montpellier; later at Paris. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Roques* \[standard form in IPNI: *Roques*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
