### Author

Kirmis, Max (x-1926), German high school teacher, archeologist, numismatist and botanist; from 1880 at the Neumünster Progymnasium. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Kirmis* \[standard form in IPNI: *Kirmis*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
