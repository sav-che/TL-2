### Author

Gelshorn, G. (fl. 1875), Alsatian school teacher and botanist, "Oberlehrer", Gymnasium in Zabern ca. 1874-1875. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Gelshorn*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Biofile
