### Author

Martinez, Maximino (1888-1964), Mexican botanist, founder of the Sociedad Botánica de México and the Boletin of that society. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Martinez* \[standard form in IPNI: *Martínez*\]

#### Herbarium and types
