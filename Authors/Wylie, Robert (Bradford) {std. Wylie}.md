### Author

Wylie, Robert \[Bradford\] (1870-1959), American botanist; B.S. Upper Iowa University 1897; Dr. phil. Chicago 1904; professor of biology Morningside College 1897-1899; assistant professor of botany University of Iowa, Iowa City 1906-1908, professor of botany ib. 1908-1940, emeritus professor 1940-1959; on U.S. kelp. exp., Alaska 1913; on Univ. Iowa Fiji-New Zealand exp. 1923; LL.D. Upper Iowa 1925. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Wylie*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection IA|IA]] (Fiji, New Zealand).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 526 (b. 28 Mar 1870); BL 1: 220, 324; Bossert p. 444; Hirsch p. 330; ME 3: 682; NW p. 62; Tucker 3: 342; Zander ed. 10, p. 734, ed. 11, p. 838 (d. 9 Jun 1959).

#### Biofile

- Anon., Amer. men sci. eds. 1-10. 1906-1961; Index Amer. bot. lit. 4: 587-588. 1969; Natl. Cycl. Amer. biogr. 50: 618. 1968 (portr.); New York Times 10 Jun 1959, P. 37; Who's who Amer. 14-26. 1926-1951; Who was who Amer. 3: 944. 1960.

#### Composite works

*Some experiences of a botanist in Fiji. Experiences of a botanist in New Zealand, in* C.C. Nutting, *Fiji-New Zealand expedition*, narrative and preliminary report of a scientific expedition from the University of Iowa to the South Seas... Iowa City, s.d., Botany on p. 142-153 and 283-298. Issued as Univ. Iowa Stud. nat. Hist. 10(5). 1924.
