### Author

Rohrer, Rudolph (1805-1839), West-Galician (Krakow-born) printer and botanist, active in Moravia. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Rohrer* \[standard form in IPNI: *Rohrer*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BRNM|BRNM]].
