### Author

Polscher, W. (1831-1861), German (Prussian) high school teacher at Duisburg. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Polscher* \[standard form in IPNI: *Polscher*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
