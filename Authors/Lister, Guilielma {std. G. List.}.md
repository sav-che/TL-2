### Author

Lister, Guilielma (1860-1949), British botanist; daughter of Arthur Lister; educated at home; constant companion of her father in his studies on Mycetozoa. 
**Abbreviated name**: *G. List.* \[standard form in IPNI: *G.Lister*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BCW|BCW]] (3000 British plants), [[Collection SRD|SRD]] (340, conif), myxomycetes at [[Collection BM|BM]] and [[Collection L|L]]. Letters and mss. at [[Collection K|K]].

#### Composite works

- (1) Miss Lister published eds. 2-3 of her father's *Monogr. Mycetozoa* as well as eds. 3-4 of his *Guide Bril. mycetozoa* (see above).
- (2) Made drawings for F. J. Hanbury, *Ill. monogr. Brit. Hierac.* (1889-98) and for A. Dallimore & A. B. Jackson, *A handbook of Coniferae* (1923).
