### Author

Tahourdin, Charles Baynard (1872-1942), British solicitor and amateur orchidologist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Tahourdin*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

Barnhart 3: 355 (b. 1872 fide LC catalogue; Desmond p. 597 (d. 5 Jul 1942).
