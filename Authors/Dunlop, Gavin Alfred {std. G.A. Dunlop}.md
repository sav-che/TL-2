### Author

Dunlop, Gavin Alfred (1868-1933), British botanist, born at Nottingham 1868, curator of the Warrington Museum, Lancs., died 3 Apr 1933. 
**Abbreviated name**: *G.A. Dunlop*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
