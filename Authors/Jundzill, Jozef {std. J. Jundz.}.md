### Author

Jundzill, Jozef (x-1877), Polish botanist at Wilna. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. Jundz.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 2: 950; Bossert p. 201; Kew 3: 202; LS 13447-13449; PR 4519; SO 618; TR 618.
