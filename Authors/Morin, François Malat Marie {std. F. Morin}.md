### Author

Morin, François Malat Marie (1856-1900(?)), French clergyman and botanist; Dr. Sci. Rennes 1893; high school teacher at Dinan; from 1895-1897 professor of botany at the free University of Lyon. 
**Abbreviated name**: *F. Morin* \[standard form in IPNI: *F.Morin*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 514; BM 3: 1351.
- Magnin, A., Bull. Soc. bot. Lyon 35: 49. 1910.

#### Eponymy

*Morinia* J. Cardot (1910). *Note*: *Morina* Linnaeus (1753) commemorates Louis Morin (1636-1715), French physician and botanist, collaborator of Tournefort in the Paris botanical garden.
