### Author

Knaut, Christian (1654-1716), German botanist at Halle, brother of Christoph Knauth. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Knaut*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Note

Knaut's brother Christoph Knauth \[sic\] (1638-1694), physician and botanist at Halle published an *Enumeratio plantarum circa Halam Saxonum* (1687, ed. 2 1689), see PR 4740, see below. We agree with Schmid that it is more likely that Knauth and Knaut were brothers, rather than father and son. They spelled their family name differently on the t.p.'s of their books.
