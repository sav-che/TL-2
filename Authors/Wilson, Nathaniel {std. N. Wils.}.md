### Author

Wilson, Nathaniel (1809-1874), British (Scottish) gardener, trained at Kew; botanist on Jamaica 1846-1867. 
**Abbreviated name**: *N. Wils.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection K|K]]; further material at [[Collection B|B]] (mainly destr.), [[Collection BM|BM]], [[Collection C|C]], [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection GOET|GOET]], [[Collection M|M]], [[Collection NA|NA]], [[Collection PH|PH]], [[Collection US|US]]. – *Letters* at [[Collection K|K]], [[Collection NY|NY]] (Torrey corr.).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 505; BB p. 330; BM 5: 2333; CSP 6: 388; Desmond p. 668 (b. 18 Apr 1809, d. 2 Mai 1874); Rehder 5: 925.
