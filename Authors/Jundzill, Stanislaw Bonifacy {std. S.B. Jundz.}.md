### Author

Jundzill, Stanislaw Bonifacy (1761-1847), Polish botanist at Wilna. 
**Abbreviated name**: *S. B. Jundz.* \[standard form in IPNI: *S.B.Jundz.*\]

[[Collection KRAM|KRAM]].
