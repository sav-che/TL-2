### Author

Demelius, Paula (née von Bach) (1862-1923), Austrian, later German botanist (mycologist), born 1862 at Vienna, married to Demelius and moved to Planegg near Munich, died 23 Mai 1923. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Demelius*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
