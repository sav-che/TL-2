### Author

Hornemann, Jens Wilken (1770-1841), Danish botanist, lecturer (1801), from 1808 professor of botany at Copenhagen, editor of *Flora danica* 1805-1841. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Hornem.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Hornem.*\]
