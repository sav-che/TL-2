### Author

Schawo, Michael (1850-1909), German (Bavarian) algologist; telegraph operator at München ca. 1879-1891; later "Telegraphen-Verwalter" and "Postverwalter" at Lindau. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Schawo* \[standard form in IPNI: *Schawo*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

CSP 18: 496; De Toni 4: 1.

#### Biofile

- Haesler, I., in litt. 17 Feb 1982 (member Bot. Ver. Landshut 1879-1882; at that time "Kön. Telegraphen-Amtsgehilfe" München; mentioned as "Kön. Telegraphen-Expeditor" in membership list Bay. bot. Ges. 1891; in idem 1899 "Kon. Telegraphen-Expeditor" in Lindau; id. 1904 "Kgl. Telegraphen-Verwalter" Lindau); id. 23 Apr 1982 (d. 20 Oct 1909, aged 59 yrs, 3 months).
- VanLandingham, S.L., Cat. Diat. 6: 3587. 1978, 7: 4224. 1978.
