### Author

Mayor, Eugène (1877-1976), Swiss physician and botanist at Neuchâtel, travelled in Colombia (1910-1911). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Mayor* \[standard form in IPNI: *Mayor*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection NEU|NEU]]. – Swiss material at [[Collection BERN|BERN]], [[Collection CUP|CUP]] and [[Collection NEU|NEU]], Colombian material at [[Collection B|B]], [[Collection BIRM|BIRM]], [[Collection CUP|CUP]], [[Collection H|H]], [[Collection L|L]], [[Collection MEDEL|MEDEL]], [[Collection NEU|NEU]], [[Collection NY|NY]], [[Collection P|P]], [[Collection PUR|PUR]], [[Collection S|S]], [[Collection Z|Z]].
