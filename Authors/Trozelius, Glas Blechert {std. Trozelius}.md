### Author

Trozelius, Glas Blechert (1719-1794), Swedish economist, clergyman and botanist; Dr. phil. Lund 1745; lecturer Lund 1746; clergyman 1754; Dr. theol. 1766, professor of economy Lund 1759-1788. (*Trozelius*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
