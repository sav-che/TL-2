### Author

Heiberg, Peder Andreas Christian (1837-1875), Danish algologist; Dr. phil. Kjøbenhavn 1863; one of the founders of the Dansk bot. Forening (1865). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Heib.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection C|C]].
