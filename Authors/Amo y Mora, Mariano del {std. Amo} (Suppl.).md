### Author

\* Amo y Mora, Mariano del (1820-1896), Spanish botanist, professor of botany and founder of the faculty of pharmacy at the University of Granada. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Amo* \[standard form in IPNI: *Amo*\]

#### Herbarium and types

See TL-2/1: 42.
