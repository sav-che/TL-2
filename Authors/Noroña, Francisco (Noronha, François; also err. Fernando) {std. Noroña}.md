### Author

Noroña, Francisco (Noronha, François; also err. Fernando) (ca. 1748-1788), Spanish physician and botanist who travelled to the Philippines and Java 1786-1787. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Noroña*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection G|G]], [[Collection P|P]]. – For Noroña's drawings, *Icones plantarum javanicarum*, at the Staatsbibliothek Preussischer Kulturbesitz, W. Berlin; see SK and Steenis-Kruseman (1977); for the set at [[Collection BM|BM]] see Britten (1903). Most detailed account of collections and literature: [[Collection SK|SK]] 1: 387-389.
