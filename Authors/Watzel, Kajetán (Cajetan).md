### Author

Watzel, Kajetán \[Cajetan\] (1812-1885), Bohemian physician and botanist at Böhmisch Leipa (Lípa). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Watzel* \[standard form in IPNI: *Watzel*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Some material in [[Collection PR|PR]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 466 (d. 29 Mai 1885); BM 5: 2274; Futak-Domin p. 649; Klásterský p. 200 (b. 4 Aug 1812); Maiwald p. 164, 165, 235, 236, 238; PR 10014-10015; Rehder 5: 906.
