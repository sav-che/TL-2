### Author

Ott, Johann (fl. 1851), Bohemian physician and botanist at Praha. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Ott* \[standard form in IPNI: *Ott*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – Possibly some material in the Muhlenberg herb. at [[Collection PH|PH]] but not located (Mears, 1978).

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 3: 1484; Futák-Domin p. 453; Herder p. 192; Jackson p. 264; PR 6870; Rehder 5: 638.
- Mears, J. A., Proc. Amer. philos. Soc. 122: 169. 1978 (material at PH not located).
