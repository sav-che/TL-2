### Author

Baillon, Henri Ernest (1827-1895), French botanist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Baill.*

#### Herbarium and types

The Baillon type-herbarium is one of the *herbiers historiques* at [[Collection P|P]], kept separately.
*Ref*.: IH 2: 49.
- Stafleu, The great Prodromus 19. 1966.
