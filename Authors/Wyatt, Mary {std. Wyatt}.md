### Author

Wyatt, Mary (x-c. 1850), British dealer in shells and algae exsiccatae at Torquay. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Wyatt* \[standard form in IPNI: *Wyatt*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – For her *Algae Danmonienses* see below. (Sets at [[Collection BM|BM]] (incl. [[Collection K|K]]), [[Collection CGE|CGE]], [[Collection DBN|DBN]], [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection FH|FH]], [[Collection KIEL|KIEL]], [[Collection L|L]], [[Collection LE|LE]], [[Collection MANCH|MANCH]], [[Collection NY|NY]], [[Collection OXF|OXF]], [[Collection PC|PC]]).

### Publications

##### n.18.389. Algae danmonienses

**Title**
*Algae danmonienses*, or dried specimens of marine plants, principally collected in Devonshire;... carefully named after Dr. Hooker's British flora... Torquae (Cocksem, printer and stationer) \[ca. 1833-1840\].
**Abbreviated title**: *Alg. danmon.*

**Notes**
*1*: ca. 1833 (Hooker J. Bot. 1: 157-159. 1834, vols. 1-2 publ.), title page and 50 specimens.
*2*: prob. 1834 (see bove), title-page and 50 specimens.
*3*: 1835-1836 (published by 1836, fide JT. Mackay, Fl. hibern. 253-254. 1836; Comp. Bot. Mag. received by 1 Jun 1836), title-page and 50 specimens.
*4*: 1837 (Comp. Bot. Mag. 2: 246. 1 Mar 1837), title-page and 50 specimens.
*5*: prob. 1840 (id.), title-page (Supplement to *Algae danmonienses*) and 37 specimens.
*Copy*: or set with t.p.'s: US. – Other copies, not seen by us listed above under Herbarium and Types.
*Ref*.: Sayre, G., Mem. N.Y. Bot. Gard. 19(1): 106. 1969.
