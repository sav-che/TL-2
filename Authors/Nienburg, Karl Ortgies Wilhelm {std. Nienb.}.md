### Author

Nienburg, Karl Ortgies Wilhelm (1882-1932), German botanist; Dr. phil. Berlin 1907; 1907-1920 in Berlin; curator at the Helgoland biological station 1921; from 1923 professor of botany at Kiel University. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Nienb.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Nienb.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Some *Fucus* at [[Collection B|B]].

#### Bibliography and biography

BJI 2: 124; BM 7: 926; Bossert p. 287; GR p. 117-118, *pl*. \[*20*\]; Hirsch p. 214; IH 2: (in press); Kew 4: 145; LS 37415, suppl. 20079-20085; Moebius p. 87, 111, 113, 335; TL-2/see Kuckuck, E. H. P.

#### Composite works

See P. Kuckuck; Nienburg edited K's *Fragmente einer Monographie der Phaeosporeen* (1929).
