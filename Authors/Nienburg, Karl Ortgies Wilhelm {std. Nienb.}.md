### Author

Nienburg, Karl Ortgies Wilhelm (1882-1932), German botanist; Dr. phil. Berlin 1907; 1907-1920 in Berlin; curator at the Helgoland biological station 1921; from 1923 professor of botany at Kiel University. (*Nienb.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Some *Fucus* at [[Collection B|B]].

#### Bibliography and biography

BJI 2: 124; BM 7: 926; Bossert p. 287; GR p. 117-118, *pl*. \[*20*\]; Hirsch p. 214; IH 2: (in press); Kew 4: 145; LS 37415, suppl. 20079-20085; Moebius p. 87, 111, 113, 335; TL-2/see Kuckuck, E. H. P.
- Anon., Ber. deut. bot. Ges. 50: I. 1932 (d. 1 Jan 1932); Österr. bot. Z. 72: 123. 1923 (app. Kiel), 81: 160. 1932 (d.).
- Christiansen, W. & W., Bot. Schrifttum Schl.-Holst. 318 \[index\]. 1936 (bibl.).

#### Composite works

See P. Kuckuck; Nienburg edited K's *Fragmente einer Monographie der Phaeosporeen* (1929).

#### Eponymy

*Nienburgia* Kylin (1935).
