### Author

Paulli \[Pauli\], Johan (1732-1804), Danish botanist and physician in Copenhagen. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Paulli* \[standard form in IPNI: *Paulli*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 56; BM 4: 1531; Dryander 3: 557; Frank 3(Anh.): 74; PR 6991 (ed. 1: 7817); Rehder 3: 66.

#### Biofile

- Christensen, C., Danske bot. hist. 1: 105, no. 1924, 2: 52-53. 1924 (bibl., b. 1732, d. 28 Feb 1804).
- Warming, E., Bot. Tidsskr. 12: 58-59. 1880.

#### Eponymy

*Paullia* Cothenius (1790, *orth. var.*) and *Paullinia* Linnaeus (1753) were named for Simon Paulli (1603-1680), botanical teacher and physician to the king of Denmark.
