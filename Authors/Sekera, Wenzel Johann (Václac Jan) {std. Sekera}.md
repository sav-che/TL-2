### Author

Sekera, Wenzel Johann \[Václac Jan\] (1815-1875), Bohemian pharmacist and botanist at Münchengrätz \[Mnichovo Hradište\]. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Sekera* \[standard form in IPNI: *Sekera*\]
