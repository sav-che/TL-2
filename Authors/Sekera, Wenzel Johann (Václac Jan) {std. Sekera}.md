### Author

Sekera, Wenzel Johann \[Václac Jan\] (1815-1875), Bohemian pharmacist and botanist at Münchengrätz \[Mnichovo Hradište\]. (*Sekera*).

[[Collection PR|PR]]; further material in [[Collection B|B]] (extant).
