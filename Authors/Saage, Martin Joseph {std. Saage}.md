### Author

Saage, Martin Joseph (1803-?), German (Bohemian-born) botanist and high school teacher at the gymnasium of Braunsberg (Eastern Prussia). (*Saage*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 1776; Herder p. 70; PR 7938, ed. 1: 8900; Rehder 1: 381.

### Publications
