### Author

Liou, Tchen-ngo (*fl*. 1930), Chinese botanist. (*Liou*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection PE|PE]]; further material at [[Collection A|A]], [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection NAS|NAS]], [[Collection NY|NY]], [[Collection S|S]], [[Collection W|W]].
