### Author

Dunn, Stephen Troyte (1868-1938), British botanist, assistant for India at Kew, stationed in Hongkong and travelled in China 1903-1910. (*Dunn*).

#### Herbarium and types

Mainly at [[Collection HK|HK]] and [[Collection K|K]], other material at [[Collection A|A]], [[Collection CGE|CGE]], [[Collection LE|LE]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 171.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 483; BM 1: 492, 6: 284-285; CSP 14: 730-731; IF suppl. 1: 81; Langman p. 255; MW p. 102-103, suppl. p. 57.
- Riddelsdell, J. Bot. 41: 141-142. 1903, 44: 138-142. 1906.
- I. H. Burkill, J. Bot. 76: 183-184. 1938.
- Fischer, Bull. misc. Inf. Kew 1938: 214-215.

#### Composite works

Author of first part of Gamble, *Flora of the presidency of Madras*, and of treatments of *Millettia* and *Wisteria* in *Plantae wilsonianae*.

#### Eponymy

*Dunnia* Tutcher (1905).
