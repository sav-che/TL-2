### Author

Komarov, Vladimir Leontjevic (1869-1945), Russian botanist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Kom.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection LE|LE]]; duplicates see IH.
*Exsiccatae*: *Fungi Rossiae exsiccati*, by Jaczewski, Komarov and Tranzschel, see Jaczewski.
