### Author

Musset, Charles Raymond (1826-1892), French botanist at Toulouse; from 1878-1883 lecturer, 1883-1892 professor of botany at the Faculté des Sciences, Grenoble. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Musset*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
