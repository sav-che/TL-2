### Author

Scholtz, \[Johann Eduard\] Heinrich (1812-1859), German (Silesian) botanist and zoologist; practicing physician at Breslau; Dr. med. Breslau 1836. (*H. Scholtz*).

[[Collection WRSL|WRSL]].
