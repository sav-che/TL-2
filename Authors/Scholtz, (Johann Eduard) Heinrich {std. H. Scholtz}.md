### Author

Scholtz, \[Johann Eduard\] Heinrich (1812-1859), German (Silesian) botanist and zoologist; practicing physician at Breslau; Dr. med. Breslau 1836. 
**Abbreviated name**: *H. Scholtz* \[standard form in IPNI: *H.Scholtz*\]
