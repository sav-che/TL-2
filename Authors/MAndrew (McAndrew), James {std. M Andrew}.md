### Author

M'Andrew \[McAndrew\], James (1836-1917), British school-teacher and botanist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *M'Andrew* \[standard form in IPNI: *McAndr.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection E|E]] (mosses, lichens); further material at [[Collection DFS|DFS]], [[Collection MANCH|MANCH]], [[Collection OXF|OXF]].
