### Author

Kurtz, Karl Marie Max (1846-1910), German botanist, high school teacher and botanist at Ellwangen 1876-1906. 
**Abbreviated name**: *K. Kurtz*

#### Herbarium and types

Kurtz made no herbarium.

#### Bibliography and biography

Lehmann, E., Schwäbische Apotheker 199. 1951.
- Schultheiss, Ellwanger Jahrb. 1950-53: 25-55; 26: 146. 1976.
