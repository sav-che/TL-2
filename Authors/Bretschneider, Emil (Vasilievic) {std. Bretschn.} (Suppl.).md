### Author

\* Bretschneider, Emil \[Vasilievic\] (1833-1901), Latvian sinologist and botanist, born in Riga, studied medicine, physician to the Russian Embassy at Teheran 1862-1865, id. at Peking 1865-1884. (*Bretschn.*).
