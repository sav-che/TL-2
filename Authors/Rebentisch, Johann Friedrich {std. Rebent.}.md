### Author

Rebentisch, Johann Friedrich (1772-1810), German (Prussian) botanist, active in the Brandenburger Neumark. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Rebent.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Rebent.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 134; BM 4: 1659; Futak-Domin p. 81; GR p. 39; GR, cat. 70; Hawksworth p. 185, 197; Herder p. 187, 244; Jackson p. 302; Kew 4: 424; LS 21859-21861; PR 7447-7448, ed. 1: 8405-8406; Rehder 1: 375, 5: xix; SBC p. 130; Stevenson p. 1255; TL-1/1042; Tucker 1: 585.

#### Biofile

- Ascherson, P., Fl. Provinz Brandenb. 1: 11. 1864 (d. 1 Mai 1830, err. \[for 1 Mai 1810\]); S.B. Ges. naturf. Fr. Berlin 1864 (for 16 Feb): 3 (on manuscript fragment diary of a *Prodromus florae neomarchicae*).

#### Eponymy

*Rebentischia* P.A. Karsten (1869) may honor this author, but the etymology could not be determined.
