### Author

Pickard, Joseph Fry (1876-1943), British draper, tea merchant and botanist in Yorkshire (Leeds). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Pickard*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BM|BM]] and [[Collection LES|LES]]; further material at [[Collection LIV|LIV]].
