### Author

Szepesfalvy, Janos (1882-1959), Hungarian bryologist at the Botanical Department of the Hungarian National Museum, Budapest. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Szepesfalvy* \[standard form in IPNI: *Szep.*\]

#### Herbarium and types
