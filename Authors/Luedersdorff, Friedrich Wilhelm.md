### Author

Luedersdorff, Friedrich Wilhelm (1801-?), German industrial and agricultural economist and botanist at Berlin. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Luedersd.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Luedersd.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
