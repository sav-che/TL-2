### Author

Bel, Jules (fl. 1880-1900), French botanist and highschool teacher at the Pensionat Stanislas Saint Sulpice, Tarn (S.W. France). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Bel* \[standard form in IPNI: *Bel*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection P|P]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 156; BL 2: 109, 205, 206; BM 1: 127; CSP 13: 416; IH 2: 65; Kew 1: 205; LS 2217-2220; Tucker 1: 71.

#### Biofile

- Garnier, G., Bull. Soc. mycol. France 100(2): cviii-cix. 1984 (list publ., "esprit curieux, bon mycologue et excellent botaniste").
