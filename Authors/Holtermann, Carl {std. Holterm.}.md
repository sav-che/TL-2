### Author

Holtermann, Carl (1866-1923), Norwegian-born German botanist; travelled in the old tropics 1895/96; later professor of botany at Berlin University. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Holterm.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Holterm.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection B|B]].
