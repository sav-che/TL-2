### Author

Bower, Frederick Orpen (1855-1948), British palaeontologist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Bower* \[standard form in IPNI: *Bower*\]

#### Collections

GL; dupl. MICH; NY.
*Ref*.: IH 2: 90.
- Kent, Brit. herbaria 44. 1947 (ref. to Nature 161: 753).
- Hedge and Lamond, Index coll. Edinburgh 63. 1970.
