### Author

Stenar, Helge \[Axel Helge Svensson\] (1896-1971), Swedish botanist; Dr. phil. Uppsala 1925; high school teacher at Östersund 1929-1946, at Södertäle 1946-1962. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Stenar*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
