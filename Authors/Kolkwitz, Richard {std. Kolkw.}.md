### Author

Kolkwitz, Richard (1873-1956), German botanist and ecologist at Berlin-Dahlem. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Kolkw.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Kolkw.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
