### Author

Pospíšil, Valentin (1913-x), Czechoslovak (Moravian) botanist (bryologist, geobotanist) at the Moravian museum at Brno. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Pospíšil*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BRNM|BRNM]] and [[Collection OLM|OLM]], see Klášterský.

#### Bibliography and biography

BFM no. 781; Bossert p. 315 (b. 26 Mar 1912); Futák-Domin p. 484; Klášterský p. 148 (b. 26 Mar 1912).
