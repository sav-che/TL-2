### Author

Lucand, Jean Louis (1821-1896), French soldier and botanist; army officer in the 29th infantry regiment 1842-1873; from 1874 retired at Autun. (*Lucand*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection AUT|AUT]] (including 1206 original drawings).
