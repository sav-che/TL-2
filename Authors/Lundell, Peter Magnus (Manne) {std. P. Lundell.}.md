### Author

Lundell, Peter Magnus \[Manne\] (1841-1930), Swedish botanist (algologist); Fil. kand. Uppsala 1872; teacher at Jakobs I. läroverket (1882) 1883-1908, Stockholm. 
**Abbreviated name**: *P. Lundell.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection S|S]] (Desmidiaceae).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 412; BM 3: 1194; Bossert p. 245; CSP 8: 279; De Toni 1: lxxix; IH 2: 469; Jackson p. 336; KR p. 472; Nordstedt p. 22; PR 10598.
- Almborn, O., In litt. 3 Dec 1979 (d. 1930).
