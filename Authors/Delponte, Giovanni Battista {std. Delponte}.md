### Author

Delponte, Giovanni Battista (1812-1884), Italian botanist at Torino. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Delponte* \[standard form in IPNI: *Delponte*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection TO|TO]]; other material at [[Collection FI|FI]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 158.
