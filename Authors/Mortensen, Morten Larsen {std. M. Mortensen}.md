### Author

Mortensen, Morten Larsen (1881-1911), Danish botanist and phytopathologist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *M. Mortensen*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection C|C]].

#### Eponymy

*Mortensenia*A. A. Weber-van Bosse (1926) commemorates Ole Theodor Jensen Mortensen (1868-1952), Danish zoologist.
