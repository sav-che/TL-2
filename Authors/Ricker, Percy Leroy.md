### Author

Ricker, Percy Leroy (1878-1973), American botanist; M.S. Univ. Maine 1901; assistant in biology University of Maine until 1901, from then on with USDA. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Richer* \[standard form in IPNI: *Ricker*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection WIS|WIS]] (10.000 parasitic fungi); collections and types in [[Collection US|US]]; other material in [[Collection DPU|DPU]] and [[Collection NY|NY]]. – Smithsonian Archives SIA 55, 221, S1.B25; letters to W.G. Farlow at [[Collection FH|FH]].
