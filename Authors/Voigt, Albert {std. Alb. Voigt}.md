### Author

Voigt, Albert (1858-?), German (Hannover) botanist; Dr. phil. Göttingen 1885; high school teacher at Hannover. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Alb. Voigt* \[standard form in IPNI: *Alb.Voigt*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 442; BJI 1: 60; CSP 19: 392.

#### Note

Published a *Lehrbuch der Pflanzenkunde* vols. 1-4, 1906. Hannover & Leipzig (Kahn'sche Buchhandlung). (n.v.).
