### Author

Lavy, Jean (1775-1851), Piedmont physician and botanist at Torino. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lavy* \[standard form in IPNI: *Lavy*\]

#### Herbarium and types

In part at [[Collection TO|TO]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 352; BM 3: 1069; Jackson p. 113, 320; Kew 3: 381; PR 5096-5099 (ed. 1: 5592-5595); Rehder 5: 494; Saccardo 2: 62.

- Cavillier, F. G., Boissiera 5: 59. 1941.
