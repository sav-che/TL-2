### Author

Hitchcock, Charles Leo (1902-x), American botanist in the Pacific Northwest. 
**Abbreviated name**: *C. Hitchc.*

#### Herbarium and types

Mainly at [[Collection WTU|WTU]]; duplicates in many herbaria.
