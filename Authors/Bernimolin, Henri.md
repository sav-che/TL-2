### Author

Bernimolin, Henri (fl. 1883), Belgian engineer and botanist, director of the technical school at Tournay. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Bernimolin*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 172; BL 2: 38; BM 1: 152; Kew 1: 230.
