### Author

Conway, Verona Margaret \[Miss\] (fl. 1934-1937), British botanist, Yarrow Research Student of Girton College, Cambridge, U.K., student of A.C. Seward. 
**Abbreviated name**: *V.M. Conway*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
