### Author

Loosjes, Adriaan (1761-1818), Dutch botanist, poet, novelist and bookseller; studied at Amsterdam, at Haarlem from 1782. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Loosjes* \[standard form in IPNI: *Loosjes*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
