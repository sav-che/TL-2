### Author

Milani, Giovanni Battista (1858-?), Italian botanist; high school teacher at the lyceum of Salerno, later id. at Avellino. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Milani*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
