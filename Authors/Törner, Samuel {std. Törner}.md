### Author

Törner, Samuel (1762-?), Swedish naturalist and librarian; studied in Uppsala with P. Thunberg; Dr. phil. ib., asst. to J. Dryander in Sir Joseph Banks's library 1792-1797. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Törner*

#### Herbarium and types

Plants from Sweden and Finland in Thunberg herb. [[Collection UPS|UPS]]. – Törner compiled a 20 vol. mss. *Nomenclator botanicus* while in London; this mss. is now in Uppsala University Library.
