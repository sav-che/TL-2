### Author

Écorchard, Jean-Marie (1809-1882), French botanist, physician, and horticulturist, born 7 Feb 1809 in Nantes, France, died 17 Dec 1882, was professor in Nantes. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Écorchard*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
