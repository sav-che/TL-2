### Author

Piscicelli, Maurizio (1871-?), Italian explorer of northeastern tropical Africa; with the Duchess of Aosta in Somalia 1911. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Piscicelli* \[standard form in IPNI: *Pisc.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection RO|RO]].

**Title**
*Nella regione dei Laghi Equatoriali* (sotto gli auspici della Reali Società geographica). Napoli (Libreria Luigi Pierro) \[1913\].
**Abbreviated title**: *Reg. Laghi equat.*
