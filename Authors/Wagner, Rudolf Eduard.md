### Author

Wagner, Rudolf Eduard (1842-1913), German (Oberlausitz/Sachsen) high school teacher and botanist at Löbau. 
**Abbreviated name**: *R.E. Wagner* \[standard form in IPNI: *R.E.Wagner*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Biofile

- Schade, A., Natura lusatica, Bautzen 5: 20, 21. 1961.
