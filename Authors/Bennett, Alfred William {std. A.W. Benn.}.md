### Author

Bennett, Alfred William (1833-1902), British botanist. (*A. W. Benn.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BM|BM]], [[Collection BIRM|BIRM]], [[Collection K|K]], [[Collection LIV|LIV]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 67.
