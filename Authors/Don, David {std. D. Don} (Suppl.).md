### Author

\* Don, David (1799-1841), British botanist, born 21 Dec 1799 at Doo Hillock, Forfarshire, Scotland, died London 8 Dec 1841, librarian to A.B. Lambert and to the Linnean Society, professor of botany, King's College, London 1826-1841. (*D. Don*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BM|BM]] and [[Collection LINN|LINN]], further material at [[Collection BR|BR]], [[Collection FI|FI]], [[Collection P|P]]-JU. – See TL-2/1: 668; A. de Candolle Phytographie 408. 1880 (D. Don had no herbarium, fide Bentham).
