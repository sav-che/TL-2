### Author

\* Archer, William (of Dublin) (1830-1897), Irish botanist and microscopist, librarian, Royal Dublin Society 1876, id. to National Library of Ireland 1877-1895. (*W. Archer, Dubl.*).

#### Note

See TL-2/1: 58.

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – The botanist William Archer who sent plants to J.D. Hooker was William Archer, F.R.S., 1820-1874, resident in Tasmania.
