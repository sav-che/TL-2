### Author

Rupprecht, Johann Baptist (1776-1846), Austrian botanist and gardener in Vienna. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Rupprecht*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown (some "Rupprecht" material from Germany in [[Collection BR|BR]]). Kukkonen (1971) mentions two *Pisum* specimens in [[Collection H|H]] (herb. Steven) "which are probably from this Ruprecht \[sic\]".

#### Note

For an obituary of the German micromycologist Heinrich Rupprecht (1890-1969) see H.J. Conert & D. Mollenhauer, Natur und Museum 99(5): 215-219. 1969 (coll. of fungi at FR; b. 21 Nov 1870, d. 1 Jan 1969).

Barnhart 3: 192; BM 4: 1767, 8: 1112; Herder p. 305; Kew 4: 549; MW p. 420; PR 7928 (d. 15 Oct 1846), ed. 1: 8880.
