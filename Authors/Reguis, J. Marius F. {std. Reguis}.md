### Author

Reguis, J. Marius F. (1850-?), French physician and mycologist at Allauch nr Marseille, later at Avignon. (*Reguis*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 138; BL 2: 192, 706; BM 4: 1666; CSP 18: 100; Kelly p. 108; Morren ed. 10, p. 65; Plesch p. 379; Rehder 1: 74, 712.
