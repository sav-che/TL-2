### Author

Struve, Gustav Adolph (1811-1889), German (Saxonian) chemist; Dr. phil. Berlin 1835; from 1840 pharmacist and owner of an enterprise for artificial mineral water at Dresden. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Struve* \[standard form in IPNI: *Struve*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 1812-1889 (b. 11 Jan 1812 but thesis says b. 1811); BM 5: 2038; PR 9018, ed. 1: 9973.
