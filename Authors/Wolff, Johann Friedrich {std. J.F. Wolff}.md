### Author

Wolff, Johann Friedrich (1778-1806), German (Bavarian) physician and botanist at Schweinfurt; Dr. med. Altorf 1801. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J.F. Wolff*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – *Letters* at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 2(2): 396; Backer p. 628; Barnhart 3: 514 (b. 13 Mar 1806); BM 5: 2349; Frank 3 (Anh.): 115; Hegi 2: 142; Herder p. 290; PR 10390, ed. 1: 11351.

#### Eponymy

*Pseudowolffia* C. den Hartog et F. van der Plas (1970); *Wolffia* Horkel ex Schleiden (1839, *nom. rej.*); *Wolffia* Horkel ex Schleiden (1844, *nom. cons.*); *Wolffiella *Hegelmaier (1895); *Wolffiopsis* C. den Hartog et F. van der Plas (1970).
