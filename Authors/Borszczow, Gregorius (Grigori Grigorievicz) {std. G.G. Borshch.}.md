### Author

Borszczow, Gregorius (Grigori Grigorievicz) (fl. 1857), Russian botanist, brother of Elias Borszczow. 
**Abbreviated name**: *G.G. Borshch.* \[standard form in IPNI: *G.G.Borshch.*\]
