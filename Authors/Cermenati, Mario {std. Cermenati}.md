### Author

Cermenati, Mario (1868-1924), Italian geologist and historian of natural sciences at the University of Roma, born 16 Oct 1860 in Lecco, died 8 Oct 1924 in Castelgandolfo. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Cermenati*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
