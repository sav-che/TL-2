### Author

Leydolt, Franz (1810-1859), Austrian mineralogist and botanist at Vienna: Dr. med. Wien 1837; assistant with J. F. von Jacquin 1838; from 1843 at the Vienna polytechnic, from 1847 as professor of mineralogy and geognosy. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Leyd.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 377; BM 3: 1107; CSP 3: 999, 12: 445; Kew 3: 434; PR 5264.

### Publications

##### n.4482. de Plantagineis

**Title**
Dissertatio inauguralis botanica *de Plantagineis* quam consensu et auctoritate illustrissimi ac magnifici domini praesidis et directoris, perillustris et spectabilis d.d. professorum pro doctoris medicinae laurea summisque in medicina honoribus et privilegiis rite et legitime obtinendis in antiquissima atque celeberrima universitate vindobonensi publicae disquisitioni submittit Franciscus Leydolt. Austriacus viennensis. Cum tabula lith. In theses adnexas disputabitur in Universitatis aedibus die I. Julii 1837. Vindobonae \[Wien\] (typis Joanni s B. Wallishausser). Oct.
