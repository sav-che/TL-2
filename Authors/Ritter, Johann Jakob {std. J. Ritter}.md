### Author

Ritter, Johann Jakob (1714-1784), Swiss botanist; sometime professor at Franeker, Netherlands; ultimately at Gnadenfrei in Silezia. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. Ritter*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 160 (b. 15 Jul 1714, d. 23 Nov 1784); Dryander 3: 447; PR 7648, ed. 1: 8603; Rehder 5: 727.

#### Biofile

- Spilger, L., Ber. Oberhess. Ges. Nat.-Heilk. Giessen, ser. 2. Naturw. 12: 40-77. 1929 (on his "Flora riedeselia").

#### Eponymy

*Rittera* Schreber (1789, also named for Albert Ritter (1681-1755)). *Note*: It is not clear for which Ritter *Rittera* Rafinesque (1840) is named.
