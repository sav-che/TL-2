### Author

Notcutt, William Lowndes (1819-1868), British pharmacist and botanist at Fareham, Kettering and Cheltenham. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Notcutt* \[standard form in IPNI: *Notcutt*\]

#### Herbarium and types

mainly at [[Collection MANCH|MANCH]]; another first set at [[Collection CLE|CLE]]; mosses at [[Collection BM|BM]]; further material at [[Collection CGE|CGE]], [[Collection LIV|LIV]], [[Collection NWH|NWH]], and [[Collection OXF|OXF]].
