### Author

Stebbins, George Ledyard (1906-x), American botanist and geneticist; Dr. phil. Harvard 1931, instructor in biology Colgate Univ. 1931-1935; junior geneticist Univ. Calif. Berkeley 1935-1939; asst. professor of genetics 1939-1946, full professor 1947-1950; chairman of the Dept. Genetics, Univ. Calif. Davis 1950-1963. (*Stebb.*).
