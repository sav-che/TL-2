### Author

\* Bonato, Giuseppe Antonio (1753-1836), Italian botanist, professor of botany at the University of Padua, director of the Botanical Garden of the University 1794-1835. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Bonato*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection PAD|PAD]].
