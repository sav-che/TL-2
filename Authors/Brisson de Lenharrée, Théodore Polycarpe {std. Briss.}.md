### Author

Brisson de Lenharrée, Théodore Polycarpe (1828-post 1891), French lichenologist at Chalon-sur-Marne. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Briss.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection PC|PC]](?), further material at [[Collection M|M]], [[Collection WRSL|WRSL]].
