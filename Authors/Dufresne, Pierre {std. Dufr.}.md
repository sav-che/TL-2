### Author

Dufresne, Pierre (1786-1836), French physician at Geneva. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Dufr.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Dufr.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown; if any, in [[Collection G|G]]-DG.
