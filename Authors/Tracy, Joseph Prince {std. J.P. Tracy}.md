### Author

Tracy, Joseph Prince (1879-1953), American abstractor of land titles at Eureka, Calif., amateur botanist and plant collector; studied at the University of California, Eureka, A.B. ib. 1903; accountant and landsurveyor with the Belcher Abstract and Title Company 1904-1953. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J.P. Tracy* \[standard form in IPNI: *J.P.Tracy*\]
