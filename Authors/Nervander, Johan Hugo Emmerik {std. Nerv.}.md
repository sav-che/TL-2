### Author

Nervander, Johan Hugo Emmerik (1827-1909), Swedish-born botanist; studied in Helsinki, fil. mag. 1853; high school teacher in Viborg 1857-1901. (*Nerv.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection H|H]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 545; BM 3: 1412; IH 2: (in press); KR p. 536 (b. 22 Apr 1827, d. 17 Apr 1909); Morren ed. 10, p. 100; Saelan p. 340.
