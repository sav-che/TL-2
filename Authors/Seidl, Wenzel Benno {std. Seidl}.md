### Author

Seidl, Wenzel Benno (1773-1842), Bohemian botanist and administrator ("Staatsbuchhaltungsbeamter") at Praha. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Seidl*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection PR|PR]]. – Co-editor exsiccatae "ökonomisch-technische Flora Böhmens" 1822, 7 fasc.; co-editors Vincenz Franz Kosteletzky (1801-1807), Wenzeslaus Blasius Mann (1799-1839) and "Moschner", distributed by P.M. Opiz.

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 2(2): 325; Barnhart 3: 257 (b. 14 Sep 1773, d. 7 Feb 1842); BM 4: 1894; Herder p. 191; Maiwald p. 87, 125, 146, 174, 177, 178, 209, 210; PR 632, ed. 1: 728; TL-2/434.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Flora 5: 47. 1822 (exsiccatae); Lotos 3: 188-191. 1853.
- Maiwald, P.V., Jahresber. Stifts-Obergymn. Braunau 7-8, 19. 1901.
- Opiz, P.M., Flora 7(2): 142-143. 1824 (founder of the Praha national herbarium; a Flora bohemica is expected).

#### Composite works

Co-author (for botany) of F. von Berchtold et al., *Oekon*.-*techn. fl. Böhm.* vol. 1(1) Jan 1836, see TL-2/434.

#### Eponymy

*Seidlia* Opiz (1826).
