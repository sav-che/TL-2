### Author

Tate, George Ralph (1835-1874), British physician and botanist; MD Edinburgh; assistant surgeon Royal Artillery; collected in China 1862-1864; son of George Tate (1805-1871). 
**Abbreviated name**: *G.R. Tate*

#### Herbarium and types

Original herbarium not traced; Chinese plants at [[Collection K|K]]; British material in [[Collection BM|BM]], [[Collection MANCH|MANCH]], [[Collection NMW|NMW]], [[Collection WAR|WAR]]; duplicates of Chinese lichens at [[Collection M|M]].
