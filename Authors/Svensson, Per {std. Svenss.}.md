### Author

Svensson, Per (1839-1917), Swedish botanist; fil. kand. Lund 1875; high school teacher at Härnösand 1881-1908. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Svenss.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Svenss.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 350; BL 2: 514, 517, 721; Bossert p. 389; KR p. 688 (b. 3 Jul 1839, d. 13 Mar 1917).
