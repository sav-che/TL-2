### Author

Ronniger, Karl (1871-1954), Austrian botanist and "Rechnungsdirector" at the ministry of finances Wien, specialist on *Thymus*. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Ronniger* \[standard form in IPNI: *Ronniger*\]
