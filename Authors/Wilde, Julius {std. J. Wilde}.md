### Author

Wilde, Julius (1864-1947), German (Rhenish Palatinate) self-taught botanist, dendrologist and conservationist; high school teacher at Neustadt a.d. H. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. Wilde* \[standard form in IPNI: *J.Wilde*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
