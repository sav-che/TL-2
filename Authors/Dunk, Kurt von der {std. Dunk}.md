### Author

Dunk, Kurt von der (1897-1985), German botanist (bryologist) from Elberfeld, Dr. ph. Frankfurt am Main 1921. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Dunk* \[standard form in IPNI: *Dunk*\]

#### Herbarium and types

PRIV (fide G. Sayre).

**Title**

**Abbreviated title**: *Monogr. Leuchtmooses*.
