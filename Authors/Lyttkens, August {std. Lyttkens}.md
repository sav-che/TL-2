### Author

Lyttkens, August (1845-1925), Swedish botanist and agriculturist; at Nydala near Halmstad 1876-1913. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lyttkens*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 417; BL 2: 512-513, 686; Bossert p. 246 (b. 14 Jan 1845, d. 20 Sep 1925); CSP 16: 933; KR p. 479-480.
- Osvald, H., *in* R. E. Fries, Short hist. bot. Sweden 138-139. 1950.
- Wittrock, V. B., Acta Horti Berg. 3(2): 61. 1903 (b. 14 Jan 1845), 3(3): 43. 1905.
