### Author

Mappus, Marcus, fil. (1666-1736), Alsatian botanist at Strasbourg; Dr. med. Strasbourg 1694; son of the botanist Marc (Marcus) Mappus (1632-1701). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Mappus*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 3: 314; Barnhart 2: 445 (err. d. 2 Mar 1738); BM 3: 1236 (p.p.); Dryander 3: 144; Herder p. 156, 174; Jackson p. 303, NI 1724; PR 5794 (err. listed under his father's name) (ed. 1: 6489); Rehder 1: 43, 5: 540 (p.p.); Tucker 1: 459 (p.p.).
- Heine, H. H., J. Soc. Bibl. Nat. Hist. 4(2): 147-150. 1963 (main study).
- Kirschleger, F., Fl. Alsace 2: xxxiii-xxxv. 1857 (err. d. 2 Mai 1738), for Marc Mappus father, see p. xxxii.
- Villars, D., Cat. méth. pl. École méd. Strasbourg xiii. 1807 (d. 1636).

#### Eponymy

*Mappia* N. J. Jacquin (1797), nom. cons. The eponymy of *Mappia* Heister ex Adanson (1763) and *Mappia* Schreber (1791) may commemorate either Marc Mappus (1632-1701), or his son (our author) (1666-1736) or both. *Mappa* A. H. L. Jussieu (1824) is based on the epithet of *Ricinus Mappa* L. – See Heine (1963) for further details.
