### Author

Krüger, Ernst (1860-1942), German bryologist at Eisenach; high school teacher at Sunnabei Vacha 1880-1897; at Eisenach 1897-1924. 
**Abbreviated name**: *E. Krüger* \[standard form in IPNI: *E.Krüger*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection JE|JE]] ("eines der schönsten und saubersten Moosherbarien, die ich kenne..." K. Mägdefrau in litt.). – We are grateful to Prof. Mägdefrau for drawing our attention to Krüger and his *Moosflora*.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 323; BFM 3238; LS suppl. 14923-14924.
