### Author

Tiselius, Gustaf \[August\] (1833-1904), Swedish botanist and high school teacher; Dr. phil. Uppsala 1869; with a high school in Stockholm 1868-1903; also teacher at Ultuna agricultural college 1868-1892; specialist on Potamogeton. (*Tiselius*).
