### Author

Hazslinszky von Hazslin, Friedrich August (Frigyes Ágost) (1818-1896), Hungarian cryptogamic botanist; high school teacher at Eperies. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Hazsl.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Hazsl.*\]
