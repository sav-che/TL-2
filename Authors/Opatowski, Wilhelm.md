### Author

Opatowski, Wilhelm (1810-1838), German physician and botanist at Saarfeld; Dr. med. Berlin 1836. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Opat.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Opat.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
