### Author

Sedgwick, Leonard John (1883-1925), British botanist; B.A. Cambridge 1905; in the Indian civil service from 1907. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Sedgwick* \[standard form in IPNI: *Sedgw.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BLAT|BLAT]](main Indian coll., 7000 sheets); further material at [[Collection BM|BM]] (Dixon, mosses), [[Collection CAL|CAL]], [[Collection DBN|DBN]], [[Collection H|H]] (mosses), [[Collection K|K]], [[Collection US|US]].

Barnhart 3: 255 (b. 27 Apr 1883, d. 27 Jun 1925); BB p. 272; BM 3: 1422, 4: 1891; Desmond p. 550; IH 1 (ed. 6): 364, (ed. 7): 342; Tucker 2: 276.

#### Eponymy

*Bryosedgwickia* J. Cardot et Dixon (1912) may be named for this author.
