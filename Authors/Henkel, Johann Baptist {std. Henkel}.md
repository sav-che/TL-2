### Author

Henkel, Johann Baptist (1815/1825-1871), German pharmacist at Würzburg 1851-1855; from 1860 lecturer and professor of pharmaceutical botany at Tübingen. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Henkel* \[standard form in IPNI: *Henkel*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
