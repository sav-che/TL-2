### Author

\* Béguinot, Augusto (1875-1940), Italian botanist, educated at the Lyceum-Gymnasium E.Q. Visconti, Rome, studied at the University of Rome 1894-1898, Dr. Sci. nat. 1898, field research on the Italian flora 1898-1900, assistant at the Istituto Botanico, Padova 1901, adjunct id. 1902, docent 1903-1915, director of the institute 1915-1920, also director Botanical Garden Ferrara 1918-1920, professor of botany at the University of Sassari 1921, id. Messina 1923, Modena 1924-1929, regular professor of botany at the University of Genoa and director of the Istituto botanico Hanbury and the Genoa botanical Garden. (*Bég.*).
