### Author

Rietsch, Maximilian (*fl*. 1882), French pharmacist and botanist; sometime professor at the school of Pharmacy and Medicine of Marseilles. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Rietsch* \[standard form in IPNI: *Rietsch*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
