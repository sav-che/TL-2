### Author

Andersen, Anton, (1856-1911), Danish botanist, teacher at a school in Havrehed 1876-1911. 
**Abbreviated name**: *A. Andersen*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 53; BL 2: 51, 622.
