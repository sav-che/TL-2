### Author

Lepechin, Ivan Ivanovich (1737-1802), Russian botanist; director of the St. Petersburg botanical garden. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lepech.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Lepech.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection LE|LE]]; other material at [[Collection H|H]].
