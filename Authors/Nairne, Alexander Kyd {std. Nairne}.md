### Author

Nairne, Alexander Kyd (*fl*. 1880s), British botanist; clergyman in the Bombay Civil Service (*Nairne*).

#### Herbarium and types

Bombay material at [[Collection K|K]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 536; CSP 17: 446; Desmond p. 459; IH 2: in press; Kew 4: 104; Rehder 1: 470, 475; Tucker 1:510.
