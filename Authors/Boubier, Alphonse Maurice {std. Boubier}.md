### Author

Boubier, Alphonse Maurice (1874-?), Swiss (Genève) botanist associated with Robert Chodat, Dr. phil. Univ. de Genève 1816. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Boubier*

#### Herbarium and types

If any at [[Collection G|G]].
