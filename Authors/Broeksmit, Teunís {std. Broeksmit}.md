### Author

Broeksmit, Teunís (1855-1946), Dutch physician at Rotterdam, Dr. med. 1891, bryologist and collector of Myxomycetes at Zeist (Netherlands). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Broeksmit*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection L|L]] (Bryol., Myxomyc.).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 256; IH 2: 99; JW 4: 379.

#### Biofile

- Steenis-Kruseman, M.V. van, Blumea 25(1): 48. 1979 (Myxomycetes at L).
- Touw, A., Blumea 25(1): 97. 1979 (bryological herb. at L).
