### Author

Selby, Prideaux John (1788-1867), British naturalist; M.A. Durham 1839; high sheriff, Northumberland 1823; mainly devoting himself to ornithology, forestry and entomology. 
**Abbreviated name**: *P. Selby*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown; letters in Winch corr. at Linnean Society.

#### Composite works

Co-founder and co-editor of *Magazine of zoology and botany* (2 vols.), *Annals of natural History* (5 vols.), *Annals and Magazine of natural History* (vols. 6-20, 1-20 and 1-19), 1837-1867. For the origin and the early years of this enterprise see S. Sheets-Pyenson (1981).

#### Eponymy
