### Author

Walther, Augustin Friedrich (1688-1746), German (Sachsen-Anhalt) physician and botanist; professor of pathology in Leipzig. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Au. Walther* \[standard form in IPNI: *A.Walther*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
