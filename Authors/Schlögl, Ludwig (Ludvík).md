### Author

Schlögl, Ludwig \[Ludvík\] (1846-1899), Moravian botanist and high school teacher at Ungarisch Hradisch (Uherské Hradište). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Schögl*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
