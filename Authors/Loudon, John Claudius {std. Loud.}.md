### Author

Loudon, John Claudius (1783-1843), British gardener, horticultural writer and botanist at Bayswater nr. London. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Loud.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Loudon*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
