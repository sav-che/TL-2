### Author

Poscharsky, Gustav Adolf (1832-1915), German gardener, botanist and plant collector; royal Saxonian garden inspector at the Dresden botanical garden 1866; ultimately living at Magyar Ovár (Ungarisch Altenberg). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Posch.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Posch.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown; plants collected by him at [[Collection B|B]] (extant), [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection KIEL|KIEL]], [[Collection L|L]], [[Collection MANCH|MANCH]], [[Collection PR|PR]] and [[Collection WB|WB]].

#### Note

Not to be confused with Oskar Poscharsky, b. 21 Jul 1856, d. 7 Feb 1914, gardener at Dresden, later director of the tree nurseries of Laubegast (see e.g. Flora, Dresden 28/29: 59-60. 1915).
