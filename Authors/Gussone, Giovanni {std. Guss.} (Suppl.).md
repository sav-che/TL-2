### Author

\* Gussone, Giovanni (1787-1866), Italian botanist, born 18 Feb 1787 in Villamina (Avellino), studied medicine, Univ. of Naples 1811, began collaborating with Michele Tenore in 1810 while still a student, dir., botanical gardens in Boccadifalco nr. Palermo and Caserta nr. Naples, succeeded Tenore as dir., botanical gardens in Naples 1861, died 14 Jan 1866 in Naples. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Guss.*

#### Herbarium and types

Mainly at [[Collection NAP|NAP]] (14,000 species), partly at [[Collection FI|FI]]; other material [[Collection B|B]], [[Collection BM|BM]], [[Collection BP|BP]], [[Collection C|C]], [[Collection CGE|CGE]], [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection G|G]], [[Collection G|G]]-DC, [[Collection GOET|GOET]], [[Collection K|K]], [[Collection KIEL|KIEL]], [[Collection LY|LY]], [[Collection M|M]], [[Collection MANCH|MANCH]], [[Collection P|P]], [[Collection TC|TC]], [[Collection W|W]]. The part of the K.P.J. Sprengel herb. that was acquired by B in 1890 contained material from Gussone.
