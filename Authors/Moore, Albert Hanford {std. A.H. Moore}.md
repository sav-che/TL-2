### Author

Moore, Albert Hanford (1883-?), American botanist; AM Harvard 1906; worked at the Arnold Arboretum, Smithsonian Institution, USDA and with the United Fruit Co. 
**Abbreviated name**: *A. H. Moore* \[standard form in IPNI: *A.H.Moore*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Mainly at [[Collection HH (sensu TL-2)|HH]]; further material at [[Collection F|F]], [[Collection ISC|ISC]], [[Collection MICH|MICH]], [[Collection NY|NY]], [[Collection PENN|PENN]], [[Collection POM|POM]].
