### Author

Postel, Emil A.W. (*fl*. 1856), German botanist, cantor and teacher at Parchwitz, Schlesien. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Postel*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 4: 1602; Jackson p. 62, 326 (mentions Russian edition, 1875, of his *Führer*); Kew 4: 347; PR 7278-7279; Rehder 5: 684.

#### Biofile

- Pax, F., Bibl. schles. Bot. 41, 66. 1929.
