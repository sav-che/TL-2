### Author

Schlatter, Theodor (1847-1918), Swiss botanist and merchant at St. Gallen; trained as a pharmacist at Würzburg, Basel and and Bern; head of the family firm of paints, dyes and colonial produce. (*Schlatter*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 6(1): 198; Barnhart 3: 228 (b. 27 Aug 1847, d. 12 Sep 1918); BFM 994, 1009; BL 2: 578, 579, 714; BM 4: 1837; Rehder 5: 769.
