### Author

Wilkinson, Caroline Catherine, Lady, née Lucas (1822-1881), British amateur botanist; married Sir Gardner Wilkinson 1856. (*Wilkinson*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 496; BM 5: 2323; Desmond p. 660 (b. 10 Mai 1822, d. 2 Oct 1881); Jackson p. 215; PR 10273; Rehder 1: 511.

#### Biofile

- Anon., J. Bot. 14: 94. 1876 (plans to publ. ills. of desert plants from Egypt collected by her husband; origs. at BM), 18: 224. 1880 (continues this work); J. Bot. 20: 159-160. 1882 (obit. not.).

#### Eponymy

*Wilkinsonia* F. v. Mueller (1879) was named for C.S. Wilkinson, Australian geologist of the 19th century.
