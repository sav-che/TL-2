### Author

Marissal, Félix Victor (1824-1881), Belgian botanist. (*Marissal*).

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection BR|BR]].

#### Name

Barnhart has "Victor Joseph Marissal", based on Bibl. nat. Belg. (b. 5 Jan 1884, 16 Sep 1881).

#### Bibliography and biography

BL 2: 38, 688; BM 3: 1241; De Toni 1: lxxxi, 2: lxxix; IH 2: 503; Jackson p. 273; Nordstedt p. 22; PR 5815; Rehder 5: 546; Tucker 1: 460.
- Crépin, F., Manuel fl. belg. lxx. 1860; Guide bot. Belgique 459. 1878.
- Evens, F. M. J. C., Gesch. algol. Belgie 180. 1944.
