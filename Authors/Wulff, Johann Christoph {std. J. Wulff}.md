### Author

Wulff, Johann Christoph (x-1767), German (Prussian) physician and botanist at Königsberg i.Pr.; Dr. med. ib. 1744. (*J. Wulff*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 5: 2367; Dryander 3: 171; GR p. 156; Herder p. 190; Jackson p. 309; LS 29393; PR 10421-10422, ed. 1: 11374-11375; Rehder 1: 373; SO 1222a; Tucker 1: 755.

#### Biofile

- Fries, Th.M., Bref Skrifv. Linné 1(6): 55. 1912.
- Lorek, C.G., Preuss. Bot. 3. 1849.
- Meusel, J.J., Lex. teut. Schriftst. 15: 326. 1816.

#### Eponymy

*Wulffia* Necker ex Cassini (1825) may be named for this author but no reference was given.
