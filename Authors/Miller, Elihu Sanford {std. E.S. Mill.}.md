### Author

Miller, Elihu Sanford (1848-1940), American botanist and horticulturist; at Wading River, N.Y. 1848-1888, 1902-1904, at Floral Park 1883-1902. (*E.S. Mill.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BK|BK]]; other material at [[Collection BKL|BKL]], [[Collection CGE|CGE]], [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection F|F]], [[Collection MASS|MASS]], [[Collection MO|MO]], [[Collection MSC|MSC]], [[Collection NY|NY]].
