### Author

Miller, Elihu Sanford (1848-1940), American botanist and horticulturist; at Wading River, N.Y. 1848-1888, 1902-1904, at Floral Park 1883-1902. 
**Abbreviated name**: *E.S. Mill.* \[standard form in IPNI: *E.S.Mill.*\]
