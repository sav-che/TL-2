### Author

Poggenburg, Justus Ferdinand (1840-1893), German-born (Hannover) American botanist; at his death business manager of the "New Yorker Staats Zeitung". 
**Abbreviated name**: *Poggenb.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection NY|NY]].
