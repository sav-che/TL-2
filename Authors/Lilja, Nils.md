### Author

Lilja, Nils (1808-1870), Swedish botanist, publicist and (from 1843) organist (klockare) at Billinge in Skåne. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lilja* \[standard form in IPNI: *Lilja*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
