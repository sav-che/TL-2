### Author

Cohn, Ferdinand Julius (1828-1898), German botanist and bacteriologist, "geheimer Regierungsrath" at Breslau. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Cohn*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown; collections of algae lost (fide Koster).
*Ref*.: Koster, Taxon 18: 550. 1969.
