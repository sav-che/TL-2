### Author

Ebbinghaus, Julius (fl. 1863-1878), German botanist (mycologist) in Leipzig. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Ebbinghaus*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 2: 503; Jackson p. 297; LS 7579; NI 577; PR 2596.
