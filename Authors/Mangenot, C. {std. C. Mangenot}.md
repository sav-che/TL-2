### Author

Mangenot, C. (*fl*. 1883), French physician and algologist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *C. Mangenot*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 3: 1233; De Toni 1: lxxxi.

#### Eponymy

*Mangenotia* Pichon (1954) is dedicated to Georges-Marie Mangenot (1899-x), who collected at the Ivory Coast around 1950.
