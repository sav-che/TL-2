### Author

Nilsson, Nils Hjalmar (1856-1925), Swedish botanist; Dr. phil. Lund 1886; lecturer (1886) and professor (1902) of botany at Lund; from 1889 connected with Svalöv experiment station. 
**Abbreviated name**: *N. Nilsson*
