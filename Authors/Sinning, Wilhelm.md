### Author

Sinning, Wilhelm (1792-1874), German (Prussian) horticulturist and botanist; in charge of the botanical garden of Bonn University 1819-1871; also lecturer at the Agricultural College Poppelsdorf from 1852. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Sinning* \[standard form in IPNI: *Sinning*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – *Letters* to D.F.L. von Schlechtendal at [[Collection HAL|HAL]]; other letters at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Backer p. 534; Barnhart 3: 282; BM 4: 1932; GF p. 76; Herder p. 362; Morren ed. 2, p. 8; NI (alph.); PR 6648-6663, ed. 1: 7424, 7443; Rehder 1: 46, 5: 801; TL-1/918; TL-2/6685; see T.F.L. Nees von Esenbeck; Tucker 1: 652.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Bot. Zeit. 32: 783. 27 Nov 1872 (d. 16 Nov 1874); Gartenflora 3: 308. 1854 (Roter Adlerorden iv. Cl.).

#### Composite works

With C.G. and Th.F.L. Nees von Esenbeck, *pl. hort. bonn. icon*. 1824, see TL-2/6685. Continued as *Sammlung schönblühender Gewächse*, see below.

#### Eponymy

*Sinningia* C.G.D. Nees (1825).
