### Author

Deventer, Wilhelmina Frederike von (fl. 1930), Dutch botanist at Utrecht, working under F.A.F.C. Went and Johanna Westerdijk. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Deventer* \[standard form in IPNI: *Deventer*\]
