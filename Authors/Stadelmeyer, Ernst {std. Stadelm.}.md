### Author

Stadelmeyer, Ernst (*fl*. 1840), German (Bavarian) physician and botanist; Dr. med. 1840. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Stadelm.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 314; BM 5: 1998; CSP 5: 789; PR 8892; Rehder 2: 726.
