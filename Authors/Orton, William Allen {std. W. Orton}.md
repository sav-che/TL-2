### Author

Orton, William Allen (1877-1930), American plant pathologist; M. Sc. Univ. Vermont 1898; from 1899-1930 with USDA, from 1907-1924 pathologist in charge of the office of cotton, truck and forage crop disease investigations; from 1924-1930 also scientific director of the Tropical plant research Foundation. (*W. Orton*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BPI|BPI]] and [[Collection CUP|CUP]].
