### Author

Sollmann, August (*fl*. 1862), German botanist. (*Sollm.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – Three specimens by "Sollmann" mentioned for [[Collection MW|MW]] by Lindemann (1886). – *Letters* to D.F.L. von Schlechtendal in [[Collection HAL|HAL]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 301; Biol.-Dokum. 17: 8767; Frank 3 (Anh.): 98; Jackson p. 297; LS 25335-25340; Stevenson p. 1258.

#### Biofile

- Lindemann, E. v., Bull. Soc. Natural. Moscou 61: 72. 1886 (3 specim. MW).
