### Author

Koppe, Carl \[Karl\] Friedrich August (1803-1874), German botanist and high school teacher at Soest. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Koppe* \[standard form in IPNI: *Koppe*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
