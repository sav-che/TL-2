### Author

Roster, Giorgio (x-1968), Italian botanist and horticulturist. (*Roster*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Note

A different Giorgio Roster, microbiologist and algologist, published *Il pulviscolo atmosferico ed i suoi microrganismi* in 1885; algae collected by him since 1874 are at FI. – See BM 4: 1736.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 4: 1736; MW p. 419.
