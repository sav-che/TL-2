### Author

Torssander, Axel \[Gustav Abraham\] (1843-1905), Swedish clergyman and botanist; studied at Uppsala; ultimately vicar at Vårdinge from 1885. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Torssander*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection GB|GB]]; other material at [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection L|L]], [[Collection MANCH|MANCH]] (Potamogeton). – *Letters* at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 343; BL 2: 547, 724; KR p. 722 (b. 17 Dec 1843, d. 14 Feb 1905).
