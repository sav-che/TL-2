### Author

Seiler, Jean (1878-1939), Swiss botanist; student of H. Schinz and C. Schröter; Dr. phil. Zürich 1909. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Seiler* \[standard form in IPNI: *Seiler*\]

#### Herbarium and types

The material collected by Christian G. Brügger (1833-1899) collected in Switzerland, upon which Seller's thesis is based, is at [[Collection CHUR|CHUR]] (duplicates [[Collection K|K]], [[Collection L|L]]). – *Letters* at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 257; BL 2: 576, 715; BM 4: 1895.
