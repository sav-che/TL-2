### Author

Lickleder, Max (1826-1893), German Benedictine clergyman; ordained Metten 1851; high school teacher and bryologist at the Studien-Anstalt Metten. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lickl.*

#### Herbarium and types

In 1911 still in the Metten monastery but later destroyed by fire (Mägdefrau 1978).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 379; CSP 16: 769.
- Anon., Ber. bot. Ver. Landshut 13: 113-114. 1894 (note preceding L's posthumous *Die Lebermoose der Umgegend von Metten*, ib. 115-124).
- Familier, I., Denkschr. bay. bot. Ges. Regensburg 11: 10, 12, 15. 1911. (herb., b. 23 Dec 1826, d. 15 Dec 1893).
- Mägdefrau, K., Hoppea 37: 146. 1978.
