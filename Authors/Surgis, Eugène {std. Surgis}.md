### Author

Surgis, Eugène (*fl*. 1921), French botanist and high school teacher at the Lycée Voltaire, Paris; Dr. sc. nat. Paris 1921. (*Surgis*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – *Letters* at [[Collection G|G]].
