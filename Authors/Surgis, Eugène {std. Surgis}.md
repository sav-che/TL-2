### Author

Surgis, Eugène (*fl*. 1921), French botanist and high school teacher at the Lycée Voltaire, Paris; Dr. sc. nat. Paris 1921. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Surgis* \[standard form in IPNI: *Surgis*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – *Letters* at [[Collection G|G]].
