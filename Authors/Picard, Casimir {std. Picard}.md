### Author

Picard, Casimir (1806-1841), French botanist and physician; studied medicine at Amiens; practicing physician at Abbeville 1828-1841; "administrateur" of the museum of Abbeville; founder of the Société Linnéenne du Nord de la France, 1838. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Picard* \[standard form in IPNI: *Picard*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 83; BM 4: 1571; CSP 4: 897-898; Jackson p. 144; Kew 4: 303; PR 7132-7133 (ed. 1: 8000-8001).

#### Biofile

- Anon., Actes Soc. Linn. Bordeaux 12(38): 32-33. 1841 (d. 31 Mar 1831, bibl.).
- Morgand, T., Mém. Soc. roy. Émul. Abbeville 1841-1843: 449-456 (obit., b. 16 Dec 1806, d. 13 Mar 1841).

#### Eponymy

*Picardaea* Urban (1903) honors Louis Picarda, French collector of plants in Haiti; *Picardenia* Steudel (1841) is an *orth. var.* of *Picradenia* W.J. Hooker (1833).
