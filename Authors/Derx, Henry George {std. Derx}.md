### Author

Derx, Henry George (1898-1953), Dutch microbiologist at Amsterdam, sometime head of the Treub Laboratory of Kebun Raya, Indonesia, Bogor, died 15 Aug 1953 at Amsterdam. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Derx*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
