### Author

Lemmermann, Ernst Johann (1867-1915), German algologist at Bremen. (*Lemmerm.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BREM|BREM]]; other material at [[Collection B|B]] and [[Collection S|S]].
*Ref*.: Koster, J. T., Taxon 18: 555. 1969.
