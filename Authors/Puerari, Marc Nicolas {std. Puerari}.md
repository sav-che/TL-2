### Author

Puerari, Marc Nicolas (1766-1845), Swiss botanist; pupil of Vahl in Copenhagen, teacher in Denmark. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Puerari* \[standard form in IPNI: *Puerari*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection G|G]] (presented by him to A.P. de Candolle in 1827). Puerari's library (incl. letters and portrait) is also at [[Collection G|G]].
