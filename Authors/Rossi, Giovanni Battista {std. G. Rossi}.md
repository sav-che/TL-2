### Author

Rossi, Giovanni Battista (*fl*. 1825), Italian gardener; curator of the botanical garden of Monza, nr. Milano 1825-1843. 
**Abbreviated name**: *G. Rossi*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Note

Not to be confused with the Modena botanist and gardener Gaetano (Cajetani) Rossi, 1717-1775.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 4: 1735; Jackson p. 435; PR 7776, ed. 1: 8736; Rehder 1: 62; Saccardo 1: 142.

- Anon., Flora 10 (Beil.): 91. 1827.
