### Author

Duursma, G.D. (fl. 1930), Dutch amateur botanist, succulent specialist living at Leeuwarden. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Duursma*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

TL-2/suppl. 1: no. 19.828.
