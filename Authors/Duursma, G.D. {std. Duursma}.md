### Author

Duursma, G.D. (fl. 1930), Dutch amateur botanist, succulent specialist living at Leeuwarden. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Duursma* \[standard form in IPNI: *Duursma*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
