### Author

Bowerbank, James Scott (1797-1877), British industrialist and naturalist at London. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Bowerbank* \[standard form in IPNI: *Bowerb.*\]

#### Collections

BM.
*Ref*.: Sherborn, Where is the – collection 21. 1940.

**Notes**
