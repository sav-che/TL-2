### Author

Maas Geesteranus, Rudolf Arnold (1911–x), Dutch mycologist and lichenologist; Ph. D. Leiden 1947; at the Leiden Rijksherbarium until 1976. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Maas G.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Maas Geest.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection L|L]].
