### Author

Mudie, Robert (1777-1842), British journalist and popular writer on natural history; 1802-1808 teacher in Scotland; to London 1808 as reporter with the Morning Chronicle and editor of the Sunday Times. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Mudie* \[standard form in IPNI: *Mudie*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 522; BB p. 223; CSP 4: 502; Desmond p. 454; DNB 39: 263; Herder p. 21; Jackson p. 471; PR 6501 (ed. 1: 7259); Rehder 5: 595; Tucker 1: 503.
