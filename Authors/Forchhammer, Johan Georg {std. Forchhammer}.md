### Author

Forchhammer, Johan Georg (1794-1865), Danish palaeobiologist and geologist at København. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Forchhammer*

#### Herbarium and types

Some herbarium material at [[Collection KIEL|KIEL]] (incl. Rubus from Weihe and Nees).
*Ref*.: IH 2: 202.
