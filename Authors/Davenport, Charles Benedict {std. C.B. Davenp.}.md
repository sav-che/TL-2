### Author

Davenport, Charles Benedict (1866-1944), American botanist and zoologist at the Station for experimental evolution (Carnegie Institution of Washington), at Cold Spring Harbor, Long Island, New York. 
**Abbreviated name**: *C.B. Davenp.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 422 (b. 1 Jun 1866; Stanford, Conn., d. 18 Feb 1944); Kew 2: 22.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Science II. 1899: 165, 195, 441, 442; Amer. men sci. ed. 7: 411-412. 1944.
- Rainger, R. et al. (eds.), The American development of biology 380 (index). 1988.
