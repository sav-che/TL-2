### Author

Silipranti, Giovanni (*fl*. 1887), Italian botanist; science teacher at the Technical college of Udine; later at Foggia. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Silipranti*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
