### Author

Allorge, Pierre (1891-1944), French botanist and ecologist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *P. Allorge*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection P|P]] and [[Collection PC|PC]]. – For exsiccatae see under Valentine Allorge.
