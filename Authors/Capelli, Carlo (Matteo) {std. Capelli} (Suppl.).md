### Author

\* Capelli, Carlo \[Matteo\] (1763-1831), Italian mathematician, pharmacist and botanist, born at Scarnafiggi (Cuneo), professor of botany and medical material at the University of Torino 1817-1829. (*Capelli*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
