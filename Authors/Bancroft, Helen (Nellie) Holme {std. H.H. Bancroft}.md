### Author

Bancroft, Helen \["Nellie"\] Holme (1887-?), British palaeobotanist, M.A. Oxon., B.S. London 1910, D. Sci. id. 1915, private research worker at Barneville (Manche), France. (*H.H. Bancroft*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
