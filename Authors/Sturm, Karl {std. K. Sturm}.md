### Author

Sturm, Karl (1879-?), German botanist; studied at Jena and Zürich; student of H. Schinz; Dr. phil. 1910. 
**Abbreviated name**: *K. Sturm*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown; thesis based on material at [[Collection Z|Z]].
