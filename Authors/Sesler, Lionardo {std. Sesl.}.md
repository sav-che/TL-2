### Author

Sesler, Lionardo (x-1785), Venetian physician and naturalist and director of a botanical garden at Santa Maria di Sala. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Sesl.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Sesl.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Eponymy
