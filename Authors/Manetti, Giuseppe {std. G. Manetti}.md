### Author

Manetti, Giuseppe (*fl*. 1831-1858), Italian botanist; head gardener of the royal garden at Monza nr. Milano (ca. 1844-1858). 
**Abbreviated name**: *G. Manetti*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
