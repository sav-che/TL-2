### Author

O'Connor, Patrick (1889-1969), Irish botanist; Ph. D. Dublin 1929; keeper, natural history division, National Museum of Ireland (1929). (*O'Connor*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection DBN|DBN]] (mycol.); some further material at [[Collection C|C]].
