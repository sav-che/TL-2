### Author

O'Connor, Patrick (1889-1969), Irish botanist; Ph. D. Dublin 1929; keeper, natural history division, National Museum of Ireland (1929). 
**Abbreviated name**: *O'Connor* \[standard form in IPNI: *O'Connor*\]
