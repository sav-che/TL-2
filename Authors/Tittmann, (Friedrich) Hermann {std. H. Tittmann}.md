### Author

Tittmann, \[Friedrich\] Hermann (1863-?), German (Saxonian) school-inspector and botanist at Leipzig; Dr. phil. Leipzig 1897, student of W.F.P. Pfeffer. 
**Abbreviated name**: *H. Tittmann* \[standard form in IPNI: *H.Tittmann*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 387 (b. 12 Oct 1863); CSP 19: 139; Rehder 5: 853.

#### Biofile

- Tittmann, H., *Vita* in Beobacht. Periderm. 1896 (p. \[40\]).
