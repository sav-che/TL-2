### Author

Lovén, Fredrik August (1847-1929), Swedish botanist; Dr. phil. Lund 1874; with the Skogsinstitut (wood and forestry) 1875-1802, at Uddeholmsverken 1884-1908. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lovén* \[standard form in IPNI: *Lovén*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
