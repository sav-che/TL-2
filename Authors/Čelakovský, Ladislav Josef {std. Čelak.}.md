### Author

Čelakovský, Ladislav Josef (1834-1902), Czeck botanist at Praha, leader of the Bohemian morphological school bridging typological and evolutionary morphology. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Čelak.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection PR|PR]].
*Ref*.: IH 1 (ed. 6): 357, 2: 120.
