### Author

Kane, Lady Katherine Sophia (née Bailey) (1811-1886), Irish botanist, married to Sir Robert Kane (1838). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Kane*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection CRK|CRK]] (170).
