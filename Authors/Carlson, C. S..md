### Author

Carlson, C. S. (fl. 1905), Swedish botanist, studied at the University of Zürich, id. Bern, id. Clermont Ferrand, Dr. phil. 1905 at Clermont Ferrand. 
**Abbreviated name**: *C.S. Carlson*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 311; KR p. 114.

#### Note

C.S. Carlson was, fide Krok, a Swede who studied in Zürich, Bern and Clermont Ferrand. No details beyond those from Krok came to us.
