### Author

Strömfeit, Harald Fredrik Georg (1861-1890), Swedish botanist; Dr. phil. Uppsala 1887; lecturer in botany at Uppsala Univ. and Regnellian amanuensis at the Stockholm Riksmuseum 1887-1890; travelled in Iceland 1883. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Strömf*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection S|S]]; duplicates at [[Collection C|C]], [[Collection H|H]], [[Collection KIEL|KIEL]], [[Collection NMW|NMW]]. – *Letters* at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 340; BJI 1: 56; BL 2: 323, 720; BM 5: 2037; Bossert p. 386; De Toni 1: cxx-cxxi; Herder p. 254; Kleppa p. 264; KR p. 673 (b. 9 Mar 1861, d. 29 Mar 1890); Rehder 1: 357.
