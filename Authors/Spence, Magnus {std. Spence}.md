### Author

Spence, Magnus (1853-1919), British schoolmaster and botanist at Orkney. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Spence* \[standard form in IPNI: *Spence*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection STS|STS]]; further material at [[Collection NMW|NMW]]. – See Druce (1927) and Kent and Allen (1984).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 308; BB p. 284; BL 2: 308, 719; BM 8: 1230; Desmond p. 576 (b. 1 Jan 1853, d. 20 Aug 1919); Tucker 2: xxxvi.

### Publications

##### n.12.598. Flora orcadensis

**Title**
*Flora orcadensis* containing the flowering plants arranged according to the natural orders by Magnus Spence,... and the mosses by Lieut.-Colonel James Grant,... Kirkwall (D. Spence,...) 1914. Oct.
**Abbreviated title**: *Fl. orcad.*

**Notes**
*Co-author* (for mosses): James Grant (1847-1924), mosses on p. \[107\]-125 (the dates of birth and death of Grant were supplied by S.W. Greene).
*Publ*.: 1914 (p. 109: 8 Sep 1913; Bot. Centralbl. 6 Oct 1914; J. Bot. Aug 1914), portr. Spence, p. \[i\]-xcv, \[1\]-148, 2 maps, portr. Grant. *Copy*: NY. – See BL 2: 308 for further botanical literature on Orkney.
*Ref*.: Anon., J. Bot. 52: 220-222. Aug 1914 (rev.).
