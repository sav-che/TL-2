### Author

Schiøtz, Ludvig Theodor (1821-1900), Danish pharmacist and amateur botanist; cand. pharm. 1844; high school teacher at Rödding 1851-1859; brewer at Odense 1859-1900. (*Schiötz*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection C|C]] (ca. 900 nos.).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 227 (b. 4 Sep 1821, d. 31 Aug 1900); Christensen p. 28, 42; Christiansen p. 319; CSP 5: 471; Kleppa p. 282; Rehder 4: 344.
