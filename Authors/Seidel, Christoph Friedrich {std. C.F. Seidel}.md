### Author

Seidel, Christoph Friedrich (*fl*. 1869), German (Saxonian) botanist at Dresden. 
**Abbreviated name**: *C.F. Seidel* \[standard form in IPNI: *C.F.Seidel*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 4: 1894; CSP 5: 630, 8: 929, 11: 384, 18: 676; Herder p. 297, 334, 434; LS 24589-24590; PR 8593; Rehder 5: 792; Tucker 1: 644.

#### Eponymy

*Seidelia* Baillon (1858) may be named for this author, but no etymology was given.
