### Author

Lambertye, Léonce, \[comte\] de (1810-1877), French botanist and horticulturist, from 1834 at Chaltrait, Épernay, Marne. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lambertye* \[standard form in IPNI: *Lambertye*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown; some material at [[Collection BR|BR]]. The type herbarium of the *Catalogue* was given to the Société d'Agriculture de Châlons-sur-Marne.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 338; BL 2: 179, 678; BM 3: 1050-1051; Hortus 3: 1198 ("Lambertye"); IH 2: 407; Jackson p. 418; Kew 3: 355; PR 5017-5018 (ed. 115513); Rehder 5: 484; Tucker 1: 409; Zander ed. 10, p. 682.
- André, Rev. hortic. 49: 386-388. 1877.
- Anon., Flora 48 (Repert. 1): 55. 1865.
- Guillaumin, A., Les fleurs des Jardins xliv. 1929.

#### Eponymy

*Bertya* Planchon (1845).
