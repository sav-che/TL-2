### Author

Peyl, Josef (*fl*. 1863), Bohemian garden director at Kacina near Kuttenberg (Kutná Hora); published mainly on fungi. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Peyl* \[standard form in IPNI: *Peyl*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection PR|PR]] (phan.), [[Collection PRM|PRM]] (fungi), [[Collection WRSL|WRSL]] (lich.); Maiwald states that Peyl's "Kryptogamensammlung" is at the Karolinenthaler \[Praze-Karlíne\] high school.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 77; CSP 4: 864, 12: 573; Futák-Domin p. 468; IH 2: (in press); Jackson p. 163; Klášterský p. 142; LS 20578-20581; Maiwald p. 120, 141-142, 204, 216, 231; Rehder 3: 194; Stevenson p. 1254.
