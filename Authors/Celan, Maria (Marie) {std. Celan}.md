### Author

Celan, Maria \[Marie\] (fl. 1935-1938), Roumanian algologist, Lic. Sci. nat., "chef de la travaux au Laboratoire de l’Université Mihǎileanǎ de Jaşi",worked on systematics, ecology and cytology of algae. (*Celan*).
