### Author

Champion, John George (1815-1854), British soldier and botanist, in Ceylon (1838-1847), in Hong Kong (1847-1850). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Champ.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Champ.*\]

#### Herbarium and types
