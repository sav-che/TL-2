### Author

Lejeune, Alexandre Louis Simon (1779-1858), Belgian physician and botanist; health officer in the French army 1801-1804, from then on physician at Verviers; Dr. med. Liège 1820. (*Lej.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BR|BR]]; other material at [[Collection G|G]], [[Collection GRO|GRO]], [[Collection H|H]], [[Collection L|L]], [[Collection LG|LG]], [[Collection MW|MW]].

#### Name

Also as Alexander Ludwig Simon Lejeune.
