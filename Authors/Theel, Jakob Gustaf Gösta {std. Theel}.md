### Author

Theel, Jakob Gustaf Gösta (1846-1885), Swedish botanist; Dr. phil. Uppsala 1872; ultimately at the Swedish state archives. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Theel*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 370; BM 5: 2089; Jackson p. 332; KR p. 702 (b. 18 Oct 1846, d. 2 Aug 1885).

#### Note

- (1) Theel's brother, Johan Hjalmar Theel (b. 1848), a zoologist at Stockholm (curator Evertebrata Stockholm Riksmus. 1892-1916), published some minor papers on botany; see KR p. 702.
- (2) For Johannes Theel (b. 16 Oct 1877, d. 6 Jul 1935), teacher of mathematics and physics at the Berlin "Graue Kloster" Gymnasium, an active floristic botanist, see E. Pritzel, Verh. bot. Ver. Brandenburg 76: 70-72. 1936 (portr., obit.).
