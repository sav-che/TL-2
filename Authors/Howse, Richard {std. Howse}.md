### Author

Howse, Richard (1821-1901), British palaeontologist; high school teacher at South Shields; curator of the Hancock Museum at Newcastle-upon-Tyne. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Howse* \[standard form in IPNI: *Howse*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
