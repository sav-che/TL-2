### Author

Pérez Arbeláez, Enrique (1896-1972), Colombian botanist; Dr. phil. Burgos 1918; Dr. theol. ib. 1926; Dr. Sci. München 1928. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Perez Arb.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Pérez Arbel.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection COL|COL]]; further material at [[Collection CAS|CAS]], [[Collection MA|MA]], [[Collection MEDEL|MEDEL]], [[Collection NY|NY]], [[Collection PH|PH]], [[Collection US|US]]. – Archival material in Smithsonian Archives.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 68; BL 1: 249, 250, 312; Bossert p. 306 (b. 17 Mar 1896); IH 1 (ed. 7): 339, 2: (in press); Kew 4: 273; MW 383; NI suppl. p. 7; TL-2/see J.C.B. Mutis y Bosio.
