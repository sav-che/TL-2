### Author

Brandt, J. Theodor H. (1877-1939), German lichenologist, studied at the University of Münster in Westphalen, Dr. phil. ib. 1906. (*T. Brandt*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

GR, cat. p. 61; LS 3743, SBC p. 116.
