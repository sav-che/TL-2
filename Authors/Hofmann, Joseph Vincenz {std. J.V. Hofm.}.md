### Author

Hofmann, Joseph Vincenz (1800-1863), Austrian clergyman ("Consistorialrath") and botanist at Brixen. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. V. Hofm.* \[standard form in IPNI: *J.V.Hofm.*\]
