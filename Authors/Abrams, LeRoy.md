### Author

Abrams, Le Roy (1874-1956), American botanist at Stanford University, California. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Abrams* \[standard form in IPNI: *Abrams*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection DS|DS]]. – For duplicates see IH.
*Ref*.: IH 1 (ed. 6): 355, 2: 26.
