### Author

Tita, Antonius (x-1729), Italian botanist at Padua, travelled and botanised in the southern Alps. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Tita* \[standard form in IPNI: *Tita*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection OXF|OXF]].

#### Note

Barnhart gives 1683 as year of birth; other sources "c. 1657".

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 5: 2117; Clokie p. 254; Dryander 3: 110, 148; DTS 1: 302, xxiii; GR p. 540; Herder p. 62, 155; Jackson p. 436; PR 9369, ed. 1: 10333; Rehder 1: 60; Saccardo 1: 164, 2: 107, cron. p. xxxv; Tucker 1: 692.

#### Biofile

- Burdet, H.M. et al., Ouvrages botaniques anciens no. 347. 1985.
- Saccardo, P.A., Stor. Lett. Fl. venet. 30, 33. 1869.

#### Handwriting

Clokie, H., Herb. Oxford 279. 1964.

#### Eponymy

*Titaea* P.A. Saccardo (1876) and the derived generic names *Titaeospora* Bubak (1916) and *Titaeosporina* A. van Luyk (1920). The derivation of *Tita* Scopoli (1777) could not be determined.
