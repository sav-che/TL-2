### Author

Grønlund, Carl Christian Howitz (1825-1901), Danish botanist, and microscopist at the NyKarlsberg brewery. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Grønlund* \[standard form in IPNI: *Grønlund*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection C|C]] and [[Collection LD|LD]].
