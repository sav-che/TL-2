### Author

Engelbrecht, Thies Hinrich (fl. 1899-1933), German author on historical subjects, and agriculture. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Engelbr.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Biofile

- Christiansen, W. and W., Bot. Schriftt. Schlesw. Holst. 75, 78, 113, 114. 1936.
