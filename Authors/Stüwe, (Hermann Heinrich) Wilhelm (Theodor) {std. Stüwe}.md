### Author

Stüwe, \[Hermann Heinrich\] Wilhelm \[Theodor\] (1875-?), German (Prussian) pharmacist and botanist; studied pharmacy in Breslau, Neusalz and Sorau; at the University of Greifswald 1903-1909. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Stüwe*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
