### Author

Holm, Jørgen Tyche (Georg Tycho) (1726-1759), Danish botanist, pupil of Linnaeus (1750-1751, 1754-1757), Dr. med. Uppsala 1757. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. Holm* \[standard form in IPNI: *J.T.Holm*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
