### Author

Sehlmeyer, Johann Friedrich (1788-1856), German (Hannover) botanist and pharmacist; from circa 1821 until 1852 in charge of the "Hofapothek" in Köln. (*Sehlm.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BHU|BHU]]. – Some further material at Bonn, [[Collection H|H]], [[Collection KIEL|KIEL]] and [[Collection NA|NA]] (bryo.). – *Letters* at [[Collection G|G]].
