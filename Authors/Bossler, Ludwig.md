### Author

Bossler, Ludwig (fl. 1877), German teacher and botanist, director of the "Realgymnasium" at Bischweiler. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Bossler*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BL 2: 119; BM 1: 206; Jackson p. 303; Rehder 1: 387.
