### Author

Mertens, Franz Karl (Carl) (1764-1831), German botanist, director of a business school in Bremen. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Mert.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection LE|LE]] (Herder 1893, p. 262); further material in several herbaria, e.g. [[Collection K|K]](2000), [[Collection PC|PC]] (types of algae), [[Collection OXF|OXF]].
