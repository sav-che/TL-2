### Author

Schmitz, Johann Joseph (1813-1845), German (Rheinland) botanist and teacher; studied natural sciences at Bonn 1834-1838; at the "Ritteracademie" in Bedburg a. Rhein. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J.J. Schmitz* \[standard form in IPNI: *J.J.Schmitz*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 234 (d. 14 Aug 1845); BM 4: 1850; Herder p. 188; PR 8292, ed. 1: 9221; Rehder 1: 380; Tucker 1: 632.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Bot. Zeit. 4: 703. 1846 (d. 14 Aug 1845, teacher at the "Ritteracademie", Bedburg).
- Weiling, F., Decheniana 126: 23. 1974.

#### Eponymy

*Schmitzomia* E.M. Fries (1849).
