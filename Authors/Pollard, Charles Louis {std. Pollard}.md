### Author

Pollard, Charles Louis (1872-1945), American botanist; AM Colombia Univ. 1894; curator of plants U.S. natl. Mus. 1895-1903. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Pollard* \[standard form in IPNI: *Pollard*\]
