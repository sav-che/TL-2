### Author

Peirson, Frank Warrington (1865-1951), American botanist and plant collector, from 1902 realtor at Altadena, Calif.; AB Haverford 1889, AM ib. 1890; plant collector in the Pacific States. (*Peirson*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection RSA|RSA]] (12-14.000); other material at [[Collection A|A]], [[Collection BH|BH]], [[Collection CAS|CAS]], [[Collection DS|DS]], [[Collection GH|GH]], [[Collection JEPS|JEPS]], [[Collection NEBC|NEBC]], [[Collection NO|NO]], [[Collection UT|UT]], [[Collection WELC|WELC]].
