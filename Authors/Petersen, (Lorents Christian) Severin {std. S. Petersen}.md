### Author

Petersen, \[Lorents Christian\] Severin (1840-1929), Danish mycologist, schoolmaster at Slotsbjergby near Slagelse, later at Sorø. 
**Abbreviated name**: *S. Petersen*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection C|C]].
