### Author

Massart, Jean (1865-1925), Belgian botanist; curator at the Jardin botanique de l'État, Bruxelles (1902-1905); director of the botanical institute Léo Errera (1906-1925). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Massart*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BR|BR]].
