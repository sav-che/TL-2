### Author

Sickmann, Johann Rudolph (1779-1849), German (Osnabrück)/Hannover) botanist; ultimately assistant at the Botanical Garden, Hamburg. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Sickmann* \[standard form in IPNI: *Sickmann*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection HBG|HBG]], [[Collection KIEL|KIEL]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 275 (b. 20 Mai 1779, d. 18 Jan 1849); BM 4: 1921; Christiansen A 176; Herder p. 189; PR 8663, ed. 1: 9624; Rehder 1: 379; Tucker 1: 649.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Bot. Zeit. 7: 207, 328. 1849 (d.; succeeded by J. Steetz); Flora 32: no. 1849 (d.).
- Fischer-Benzon, R.J.D. *in* P. Prahl, Krit. Fl. Schlesw. Holst. 2: 54-55. 1890.
- Voigt, A., Bot. Inst. Hamburg 32, 33, 34. 1897.

#### Eponymy

*Sickmannia* C.G.D. Nees (1834) probably honors Sickmann but no etymology was given.

### Publications

##### n.11.939. Enumeratio stirpium phanerogamicarum circa Hamburgum

**Title**
*Enumeratio stirpium phanerogamicarum circa Hamburgum* sponte crescentium auctore J.R. Sickmann. Hamburgi (Typis Joannis Augusti Meissneri) 1836. Oct.
**Abbreviated title**: *Enum. stirp. phan. Hamb.*

**Notes**
*Publ*.: 1836 (Flora rd before 6 Mar 1837 (20(1): 369), p. iv: 9 Mai 1836), p. \[i-iv\], \[1\]-80. *Copies*: B-S, G, HH, MO, NY. – A slip in the copy at G states "Ausgegeben im Monat März 1839. Diese Schrift ist früher nicht im Buchhandel gekommen". Even though the book may not have been for sale through the book-trade, copies were evidently distributed by the author in 1836/7 judging by e.g. the Notice in Flora 1837. Hinrichs lists it as received 28 Apr-4 Mai 1839.
*Ref*.: Anon., Flora 20 (Lit.) 6: 92-93. 28 Jul 1837 (rev.), Linnaea 11 (Lit.): 147-148. 1836-1838 (rev.), 13 (Lit.): 165. 1839-1840 (note on availability in book trade).
