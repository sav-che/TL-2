### Author

\* Descourtilz, Michel Étienne (1775-1836), French botanist and physician at the "Faculté de Paris", born 25 Nov 1775, died 1836. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Descourt.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Descourt.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography
