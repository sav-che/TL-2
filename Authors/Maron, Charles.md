### Author

Maron, Charles (1851 -1926), French horticulturist; from ± 1900 conducting a nursery at Brunoy, Seine-et-Oise. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Maron* \[standard form in IPNI: *Maron*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 449 (d. 25 Feb 1926); Rehder 3: 514, 707; TL-2/3024. A.E.P.G., J. Kew Guild 4: 526-527. 1927 (portr., obit.).
- Anon., Gard. Chron. ser. 3. 79: 163. 1926 (d.).
- Guillaumin, A., Les fleurs des jardins lxix. 1929.

#### Eponymy

*Maronella* M.Steiner (1959) and *Maroneomyces* Ciferri & Tomaselli (1953) are related to *Maronea* A.Massalongo (1856), which is dedicated to Nicoló Marogna (latin: Maronea) (fl. 1608), Italian botanist.
