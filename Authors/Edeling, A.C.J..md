### Author

Edeling, A.C.J. (x-1895), Lieutnant in the Dutch Navy, in 1857 appointed Administrator of the Depot of hydrographic maps, books, etc. at Batavia \[Djakarta\], 1860-1870 and from 1876 Head Hydrographic Dept., 1870-1872 on European leave, returned to Minahassa (N. Celebes) to investigate the culture of coffee there, pensioned in 1894. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Edeling*
