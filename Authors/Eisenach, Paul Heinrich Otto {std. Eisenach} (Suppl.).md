### Author

\* Eisenach, Paul Heinrich Otto (1847-1917), German physician and botanist, born 27 Jun 1847 at Rotenburg a. d. Fulda, magistrate in that town, died 14 Jan 1917 at Hanau. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Eisenach*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
