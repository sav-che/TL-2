### Author

Wolpert, Josef \[Johann Baptist\] (1881-?), German (Franconian) botanist; Dr. phil. München 1909; assistant at the botanical division of the Kön. forstliche Versuchsanstalt, München 1911. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Wolpert*

#### Herbarium and types
