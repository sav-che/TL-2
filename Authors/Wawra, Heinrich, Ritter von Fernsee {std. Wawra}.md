### Author

Wawra, Heinrich, Ritter von Fernsee (1831-1887), Moravian-Austrian physician and botanist; studied medicine at Vienna University 1849-1856; physician with the Austrian navy 1856-1878; collecting on various journeys, e.g. to Brazil, Luanda (Benguela) and S. Africa 1857-1858, to Brazil 1859-1860; on the "Novara" to Mexico 1864-1865; on the "Donau" around the world via eastern Asia and the Pacific 1868-1871; in North America 1872-1873, returning via Hawaii, Australia, Indonesia, to Brazil 1879; ennobled 1873. (*Wawra*).
