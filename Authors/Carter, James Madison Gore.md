### Author

Carter, James Madison Gore (1843-1919), American physician and medical botanist, born in Johnson County, Illinois, A.B. St. John's 1877, M.D. Chicago medical college 1880, Ph.D. 1887, professor of pathology, college physicians and surgeons, Illinois 1891-1893, clinical medic. 1893-1899, emeritus med. professor from 1899. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J.M. Carter*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
