### Author

Bower, Frederick Orpen (1855-1948), British palaeontologist. (*Bower*).

#### Collections

GL; dupl. MICH; NY.
*Ref*.: IH 2: 90.
- Kent, Brit. herbaria 44. 1947 (ref. to Nature 161: 753).
