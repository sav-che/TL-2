### Author

Mohr, Nicolai (i Pedersen) (1742-1790), Faeroes-born Danish botanist; visited Iceland 1780, Groenland 1787; employed by the Royal Danish China factory. 
**Abbreviated name**: *N. Mohr* \[standard form in IPNI: *Mohr*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection C|C]].
