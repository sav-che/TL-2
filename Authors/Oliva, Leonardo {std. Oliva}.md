### Author

Oliva, Leonardo (1805-1873), Mexican botanist and pharmacologist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Oliva* \[standard form in IPNI: *Oliva*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown; circa 100 specimens from Mexico in [[Collection P|P]].
