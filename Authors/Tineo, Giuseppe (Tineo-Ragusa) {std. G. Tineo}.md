### Author

Tineo, Giuseppe ("Tineo-Ragusa") (1757-1812), Italian botanist; director of the Palermo botanic garden at Porta Carini 1780-1789, at id. Villa Giulia 1789-1812. 
**Abbreviated name**: *G. Tineo*
