### Author

Sonet \[Sonnet\], Ernest (*fl*. 1872), Belgian botanist; technician at the Jardin botanique de l'État, Brussels, 1871-1890; from 1890 in retirement because of invalidity. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Sonet* \[standard form in IPNI: *Sonet*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BR|BR]]. – Sonet issued a "collection de 100 mousses de la flore belge, fasc. 1, Bruxelles 1883", set at [[Collection BR|BR]]. – See also Sayre (1971). Other sets of exsiccatae issued by Sonet are listed by him in his *Fl. méd. belg.*
