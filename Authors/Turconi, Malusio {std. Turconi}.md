### Author

Turconi, Malusio (1879-1929), Italian mycologist at the Laboratorio crittogamico di Pavia from 1899. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Turconi* \[standard form in IPNI: *Turconi*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection PAV|PAV]].

#### Bibliography and biography
