### Author

Timm, Joachim Christian (1734-1805), German (Pommern) pharmacist and botanist; from 1760 pharmacist (later also burgomaster) at Malchin (Mecklenburg); associated with J. Hedwig. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Timm*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown; some lichens at [[Collection M|M]].

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 4: 95; Barnhart 3: 385 (b. 7 Dec 1734, d. 3 Feb 1805); BM 5: 2116; DAB 2: 680; Dryander 3: 160; Frank 3 (Anh.): 104; GR p. 144; Herder p. 189; Hortus 3: 1205; LS 26844; PR 9361, ed. 1: 10.323; Rehder 1: 374; SO add. 724b; TL-1/1312; Zander ed. 10, p. 724, ed. 11, p. 826.
