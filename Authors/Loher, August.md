### Author

Loher, August (x-1930), German botanist; wholesale dealer in pharmaceuticals; from 1889 resident in the Philippines. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Loher* \[standard form in IPNI: *Loher*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection M|M]] (5000) and [[Collection PNH|PNH]]; other large sets at [[Collection B|B]], [[Collection K|K]], [[Collection L|L]], [[Collection P|P]], [[Collection NY|NY]], [[Collection US|US]]. – See IH.
