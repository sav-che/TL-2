### Author

Dalton, James, senior (1764-1843), British clergyman and botanist at Croft, Yorkshire. 
**Abbreviated name**: *James Dalton* \[standard form in IPNI: *Js.Dalton*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection YRK|YRK]], bryophytes at [[Collection CGE|CGE]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 150.
