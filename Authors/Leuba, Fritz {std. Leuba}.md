### Author

Leuba, Fritz (1848-1910) Swiss pharmacist and mycologist at Corcelles near Neuchâtel. (*Leuba*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 3: 1098; LS 15279-15282; NAF 9(6): 441; NI 1183.

**Title**
