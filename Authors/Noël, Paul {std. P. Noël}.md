### Author

Noël, Paul (*fl*. 1910), French mycologist and phytopathologist; director of the regional laboratory of agricultural entomology of the dept. Seine-Inférieure. (*P. Noël*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

LS 37447-37448, suppl. 20178-20185; Rehder 5: 622.
- Grün, W. et al., Neu. Jahrb. Geol. 147: 300. 1974 (epon.).

#### Eponymy

*Noellithina* Grün et Zweili (1974) and probably also *Noëlites* Caratini (1963) honor D. Noël, a present-day French (?) micropaleontologist.
