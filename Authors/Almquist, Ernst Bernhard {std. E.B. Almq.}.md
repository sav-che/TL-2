### Author

Almquist, Ernst Bernhard (1852-1946), Swedish physician and botanist, at Karol. inst. Stockholm 1891-1917, brother of S. O. I. Almquist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *E.B. Almquist*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection S|S]] and [[Collection UPS|UPS]] (Vega expedition, 1878-1880), also [[Collection H|H]], [[Collection LD|LD]] and [[Collection Z|Z]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 33.
