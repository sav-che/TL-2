### Author

Ormezzano, Quentin Jean Baptiste (1854-1912), French botanist and building contractor at Autun. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Ormezzano* \[standard form in IPNI: *Ormezz.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

A teratological collection (and other material?) at [[Collection AUT|AUT]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 32: BL 2: 196, 698; Tucker 1: 531.
- Anon., Rev. sci. Bourbon. 25: 101-102. 1912 (obit.).
- Chateau, E., Soc. Hist. nat. Autun, Bull. 26: 15-21. 1913 (obit., b. 15 Sep 1854, d. 15 Aug 1912).
