### Author

Kelaart, Edward Frederick (1818?-1860), Ceylon-born British botanist; sometime army physician at Gibraltar. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Kelaart*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection CGE|CGE]], [[Collection G|G]], [[Collection LINN|LINN]]. – Letters at Kew.
