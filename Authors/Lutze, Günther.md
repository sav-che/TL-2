### Author

Lutze, Günther (1840-1930) German botanist and high school teacher at Sondershausen 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lutze* \[standard form in IPNI: *Lutze*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 6(1): 79; Barnhart 2: 414; BFM 409; BJI 1: 38; Hegi 4(2): 10-13; Rehder 5: 528; Tucker 1: 446.
- Müller, H., Mitt. thür. bot. Ver. ser. 2. 40: iii-iv. 1931 (bibl.).

#### Eponymy

*Lutzia* Gandoger (1923) is dedicated to Louis Charles Lutz (1871-1952), French botanist.
