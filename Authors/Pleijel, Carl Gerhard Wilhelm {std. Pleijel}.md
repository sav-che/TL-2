### Author

Pleijel, Carl Gerhard Wilhelm (1866-1937), Swedish pharmacist and botanist at Alvesta, Småland (1911-1920), pharmacist at Stockholm 1920-1936. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Pleijel* \[standard form in IPNI: *Pleijel*\]
