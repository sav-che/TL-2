### Author

Murphy, Edmund (1828-1866), Irish horticulturist and botanist; professor of horticulture, Queen's College, York 1849. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Murphy* \[standard form in IPNI: *Murphy*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
