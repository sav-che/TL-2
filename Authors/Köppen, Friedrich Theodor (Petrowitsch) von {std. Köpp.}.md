### Author

Köppen, Friedrich Theodor (Petrowitsch) von (1833-1908), Russian (Baltic-German?) botanist at St. Petersburg. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Köpp.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Köppen*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
