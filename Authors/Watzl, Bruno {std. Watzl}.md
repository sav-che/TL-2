### Author

Watzl, Bruno (1886-x), Austrian botanist; Dr. phil. Wien 1909 (student of Richard Wettstein); professor at the Bundesgymnasium Wien XIX. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Watzl*

[[Collection WU|WU]]; *Veronica* material at [[Collection W|W]].
