### Author

Tai, Fung-Ian \[Tai, L.Y.\] (1893-x), Chinese mycologist and phytopathologist; B.S. Cornell Univ. 1918; professor of plant pathology at Nanking (Southeastern Univ.). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Tai* \[standard form in IPNI: *F.L.Tai*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – *Letter* to B.O. Dodge at [[Collection NY|NY]].

#### Note

Lin Yu Tai collected in China about 1926, main coll. at WH; other material at ILL and MICH.

#### Bibliography and biography

Hirsch p. 294; LS 399 (corr. with Dodge); MW p. 481, suppl. p. 344-345; Stevenson p. 1259.
