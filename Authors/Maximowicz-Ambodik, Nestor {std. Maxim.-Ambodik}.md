### Author

Maximowicz-Ambodik, Nestor (1740-1812), Russian physician, botanist and lexicographer. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Maxim.-Ambodik* \[standard form in IPNI: *Maxim.-Amb.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Note

Frequently alphabetized under Ambodik, M.N. – We are grateful to S. Lipschitz for information on this author.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 49; BM 1: 36; PR ed. 1: 11727; TR 176.
