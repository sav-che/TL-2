### Author

Schüep, Otto (1888-x), Swiss botanist, student of C. Schröter; Dr. phil. Zürich 1911, subsequently lecturer in botany at the University of Basel. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Shüepp* \[standard form in IPNI: *Schüep*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
