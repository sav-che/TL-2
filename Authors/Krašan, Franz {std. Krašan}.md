### Author

Krašan, Franz (1840-1907), Austrian botanist, high school teacher at Graz. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Krašan* \[standard form in IPNI: *Krašan*\]

#### Herbarium and types

main material at [[Collection GZU|GZU]]; further specimens at [[Collection P|P]] and [[Collection LE|LE]].
