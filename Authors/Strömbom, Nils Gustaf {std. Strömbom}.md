### Author

Strömbom, Nils Gustaf (1847-1897), Swedish clergyman and mycologist; ordained 1870. (*Strömbom*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 340; Kelly p. 220; KR p. 672 (b. 28 Mai 1847, d. 3 Apr 1897).
