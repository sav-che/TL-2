### Author

Munby, Giles (1813-1876), British botanist; studied at Edinburgh and in Paris (under Adrien de Jussieu); in Algeria 1839-1861. (*Munby*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection K|K]], [[Collection YRK|YRK]] (Brit.). For duplicates see IH.
