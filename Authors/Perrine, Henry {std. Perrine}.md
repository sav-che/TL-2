### Author

Perrine, Henry (1797-1840), American botanist; introduced many tropical plants into the southern U.S.; consul at Campeachy, Yucatan, 1827-1831, 1832-1837, at Key West (Indian Key) from 1937. (*Perrine*).

#### Herbarium and types

Most collections and many manuscripts destroyed when he was murdered by a band of Spanish Indians during a raid on Indian Key (Millspaugh, mss.); some material at [[Collection NY|NY]].

#### Bibliography and biography
