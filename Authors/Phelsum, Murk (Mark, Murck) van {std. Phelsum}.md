### Author

Phelsum, Murk \[Mark, Murck\] van (*fl*. 1769, d. ca. 1780), Dutch naturalist; Dr. med. Franeker 1755; practicing physician at Sneek, Friesland, from 1764. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Phelsum*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection GRO|GRO]] (1200) (see Moll, 1918, and JW 5: 248), all without date and locality.

#### Bibliography and biography

Dryander 3: 67, 345; IH 2: (in press); Jackson p. 32; JW 4: 401, 5: 248; NNBW 10: 721; PR 7116 (ed. 1: 7982); Rehder 5: 669.
