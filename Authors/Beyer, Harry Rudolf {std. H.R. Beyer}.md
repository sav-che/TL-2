### Author

Beyer, Harry Rudolf (1874-?), German botanist, born at Danzig, educated at the Danzig Gymnasium, studied classic languages at Königsberg i. Pr. 1892, switched to pharmacy 1894-1895 at Danzig, assistant pharmacist at Leipzig, Frankfurt a. M., Hamburg and Berlin, studied pharmacy at Berlin 1898-1899, worked with Engler on African An(n)onaceae. 
**Abbreviated name**: *H.R. Beyer*
