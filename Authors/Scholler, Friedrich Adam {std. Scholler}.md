### Author

Scholler, Friedrich Adam (1718-1795), German (Bayreuth) botanist; teacher at the community of Moravian Brethern at Barby (Prussian Saxony). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Scholler*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 2(2): 411; Backer p. 660; Barnhart 3: 237 (d. 3 Apr 1785); BM 4: 1855; Dryander 3: 162; Frank 3 (Anh.): 92; GR p. 45-46, cat. 71; Herder p. 188; Hortus 3: 1203; LS 24152; PR 8319, ed. 1: 9241; Rehder 1: 374; Tucker 1: 634; Zander ed. 10, p. 713, ed. 11, p. 813.

#### Biofile

- Ascherson, P., Fl. Prov. Brandenb. 1:13. 1864; Nachtr. Fl. Magdeburg 69-70. 1894.
- Fries, T.M., Bref Skrifv. Linné 1(5): 224. 1911.

#### Eponymy

*Schollera* A.W. Roth (1788); *Schollera* Rohr (1792). *Note*: *Schollera* Schreber (1791) and the derived genus *Scholleropsis* H. Perrier de la Bathie (1936) probably also honor F.A. Scholler, but no etymology was given.
