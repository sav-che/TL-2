### Author

Tiesenhausen, Manfred von (1875-?), Austrian botanist; studied at Genève 1907-1908; Dr. phil. Bern 1912; later with the Institute of general botany at Cluj, Roumania. (*Tiesenhausen*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Hirsch p. 300.

#### Biofile
