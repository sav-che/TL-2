### Author

Milne-Redhead, Edgar Wolston Bertram Handsley (1906-x), British botanist; B. A. Cantab. 1928; at Kew (1929-1971). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Milne-Redh.*

[[Collection K|K]]; further material at [[Collection DBN|DBN]].
