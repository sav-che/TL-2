### Author

Foerster, Arnold (1810-1884), German highschool teacher and botanist at Aachen (Aix-la-Chapelle). 
**Abbreviated name**: *A. Foerster*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 553; BM 2: 588; DTS 1: 71-72.
- Wackerzapp, Verh. Naturh. Ver. preuss. Rheinl. 43 (corr.): 33-41. 1886. (bibl.)
