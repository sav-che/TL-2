### Author

Flot, Léon Alfred Victor (1857-x), French botanist, born 4 Dec 1857 in Montmorency (Seine-et-Oise), instructor ("Professeur"), Lycée Charlemagne, Dr. ès-sciences 1890 Paris. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Flot*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
