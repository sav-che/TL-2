### Author

Bergeret, Jean Pierre (1751-1813), French botanist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. P. Bergeret*

#### Herbarium and types

Bergeret's collections seem to be incorporated in the Fée herbarium at [[Collection STR|STR]].
