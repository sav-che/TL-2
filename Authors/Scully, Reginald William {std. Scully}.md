### Author

Scully, Reginald William (1858-1935), Irish botanist; studied medicine but never practiced, dedicating himself to botany. (*Scully*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection DBN|DBN]]; some further material at [[Collection CGE|CGE]], [[Collection K|K]], [[Collection MANCH|MANCH]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 253; BL 2: 280, 281, 287; BM 4: 1888; Clokie p. 241; Desmond p. 549 (d. Mai 1935); IH 2: (files); Lenley p. 364; TL-2/6270.
