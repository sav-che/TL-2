### Author

Smirnova, Zoya \[Zoë\] Nikolayevna (1898-1979), Russian bryologist; at the Botanical Institute V.L. Komarov of the Academy of Sciences, Leningrad. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Smirn.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Smirnova*\]
