### Author

Nicholson, William Edward (1866-1945), British lawyer and bryologist at Lewes, Sussex. 
**Abbreviated name**: *W. E. Nichols.* \[standard form in IPNI: *W.E.Nicholson*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection CGE|CGE]] (with diary). – Other material at [[Collection DBN|DBN]], [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection FH|FH]] (Hepat.), K. (Bryoph.) and [[Collection OXF|OXF]] (Bryoph.). Letters at [[Collection CGE|CGE]].
