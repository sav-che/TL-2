### Author

Cassel, Franz Peter (1784-1821), German botanist, later in life professor of botany in Gent. (*Cassel*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 320; BM 1: 323; CSP 1: 813; Jackson p. 68; Kew 1: 294; PR 1579-1581.

#### Eponymy

*Casselia* Dumortier (1822, *nom. rej.*) [^1]; *Casselia* C. G. D. Nees & C. F. P. Martius (1823, *nom. cons.*)

### Publications

*Lehrbuch der natürlichen Pflanzenordnung*. Frankfurt am Main (Andrea) 1817. Oct.
**Abbreviated title**: *Lehrb. nat. Pflanzenordn.*
