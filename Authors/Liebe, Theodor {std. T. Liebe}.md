### Author

Liebe, Theodor (*fl*. 1862), German botanist, high school teacher at the Friedrichs-Werdersche Gewerbeschule in Berlin. (*T. Liebe*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Note

Theodor Liebe is *not* identical with the above K.L.T. Liebe. The latter was teacher at the Gera Rutheneum from 1861-1894; the present Theodor Liebe (the author of purely botanical publications) was teacher at the Friedrichs-Werdersche Gewerbeschule in Berlin from (at least) 1862 onward. We have not been able to find further details on Theodor Liebe, who is also the author of *Grundriss der speciellen Botanik*, Berlin 1866, ed. 3. 1891, and *Die Elemenle der Morphologie* ed. 1, Berlin 1868, ed. 2. 1877, ed. 3, ib. 1881, ed. 4. 1888 (see BM 3: 1111).
