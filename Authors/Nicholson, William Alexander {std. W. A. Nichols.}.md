### Author

Nicholson, William Alexander (1858-1935), British bank clerk, botanist, secretary and editor of the Norfolk and Norwich Naturalists Society 1891-1912. 
**Abbreviated name**: *W. A. Nichols.*

[[Collection NWH|NWH]].
