### Author

Eeden, Frederik Willem van (1829-1901), Dutch agronomist and botanist, first director of the Colonial Museum at Haarlem. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Eeden*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection L|L]] (fungi), [[Collection NBV|NBV]].
