### Author

Luyken, Johann (Joannes) Albert (1785-1867), German-Dutch physician and lichenologist, born at Wessel, ultimately at Landfort nr. Gendringen (Arnhem region) (*Luyk.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 3: 1197; GR p. 108-109; Kew 3: 522; Krempelh. 2: 77-79; LS 16126; PR 5697(ed. 1: 6375); SBC p. 126 ("Luyk."); TL-2/1: 11.

#### Eponymy

*Luykenia* Trevisan (1860).
