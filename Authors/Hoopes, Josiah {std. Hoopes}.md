### Author

Hoopes, Josiah (1832-1904), American nurseryman; popular author on gardening; ornithologist; at West Chester, Pa. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Hoopes* \[standard form in IPNI: *Hoopes*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection DWC|DWC]].
