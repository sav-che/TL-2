### Author

Schelle, Ernst (1864-1929), German gardener and botanist; head gardener at the University botanical Garden of Tübingen. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Schelle* \[standard form in IPNI: *Schelle*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Biofile
