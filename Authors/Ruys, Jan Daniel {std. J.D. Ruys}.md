### Author

Ruys, Jan Daniel (1897-1954), Dutch botanist; Dr. phil. Utrecht 1925; director of the Moerheim nursery 1926-1954. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J.D. Ruys*

#### Herbarium and types

Some material from Algeria at [[Collection U|U]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 195 (b. 27 Apr 1897); BFM no. 2988; BJI 2: 150; BL 2: 421, 711; Hortus 3: 1203; JW 2: 200.
