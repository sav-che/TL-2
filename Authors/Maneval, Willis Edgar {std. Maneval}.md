### Author

Maneval, Willis Edgar (1877-1956), American mycologist; Ph. D. Johns Hopkins 1912; at the University of Missouri, Columbia, 1915-1947. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Maneval* \[standard form in IPNI: *Maneval*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection UMO|UMO]].
