### Author

Martius, Theodor Wilhelm Christian (1796-1863), German pharmacist and botanist at Erlangen; brother of G. F. P. von Martius. 
**Abbreviated name**: *T. Mart.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BR|BR]] (in the C. F. P. von Martius herbarium).
