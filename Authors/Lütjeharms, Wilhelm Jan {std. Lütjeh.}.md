### Author

Lütjeharms, Wilhelm Jan (1907-x); Dutch mycologist and historian of botany in South Africa. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lütjeh.*

#### Herbarium and types

Main collections at [[Collection BLFU|BLFU]] and [[Collection L|L]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Ainsworth p. 17, 24, 25, 308, 329; Barnhart 2: 410 (b. 24Jan 1907); BL 2: 425; IH 2: 470; Kew 3: 518; MW suppl. p. 211; SK 1: 332-333; TL-1/608; TL-2/3874.
- Kalkman, C. & P. Smit, eds., Blumea 25(1): 18, 84. 1979.

#### Composite works

Edited vol. 28 of Kops, *Fl. bat*., 1934, together with Cornelius August de Wever (1874-1947), see TL-2/3874.
