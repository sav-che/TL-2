### Author

Walz., Jacob Jacoblevic (1840-1904), Russian botanist; studied in Kiev and Berlin (with P.F.A. Ascherson); professor of botany in Odessa. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. Walz*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MW|MW]] (200). – *Letters* at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

De Toni 1: cxxviii-cxxix; Frank 3 (Anh.): 110; Herder p. 255; LS 28283-28284; Morren ed. 2, p. 30; PR 9989; TR 1546-1557.
