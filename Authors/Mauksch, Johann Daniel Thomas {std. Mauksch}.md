### Author

Mauksch, Johann Daniel Thomas (1748-1831), Hungarian clergyman and botanist, living in Schlagendorf und Kesmark (Kežmarok), botanical explorer of the Tatra; collaborator of Kitaibel, Dr. med. Tyrnau 1776. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Mauksch*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BP|BP]] (major part).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 464; IH 2: 517; Kanitz 98; PR (alph.) ed. 1: 6671; Rehder 1: 97.
- Pax, F., Grundz. Pfl.-Verbr. Karpathen 1: 9-10. 1898.
- Rumy, G.C., Isis, Zürich 1834 (6/7): 656-664 (fungi in Zipser Comitat, Hungary, listed by Mauksch).
