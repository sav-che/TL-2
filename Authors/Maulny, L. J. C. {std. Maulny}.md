### Author

Maulny, L. J. C. (ca. 1760-ca. 1830), French botanist and naturalist; author of a flora of Mans. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Maulny* \[standard form in IPNI: *Maulny*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
