### Author

Sauer, Friedrich \[Fritz\] Ludwig Ferdinand (1852-?), German (Prussian) botanist ; studied at the Potsdam Gymnasium and the University of Berlin; Dr. phil. Halle 1880. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Sauer*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BL 1: 87, 317; BM 4: 1810; Jackson p. 508; Kew 4: 601; Rehder 1: 498; Tucker 1: 624.

#### Eponymy

*Saueria* Klotzsch (1854) honors F.W.H. Sauer, German horticulturist.

### Publications

##### n.10.336. Catalogus plantarum in canariensibus insulis

**Title**
*Catalogus plantarum in canariensibus insulis* sponte et subsponte crescentium. Dissertatio inauguralis quam ad summos in philosophia honores ab amplissimo philosophorum ordine in Academia fridericiana halensi cum vitebergensi consociata rite impetrandos scripsit Fritz Sauer, potsdamensis. Halis Saxonum \[Halle a.S.\] (Typis Ploetzianis (R. Nietschmann) 1880. Oct.
