### Author

Mattioli, Pietro \[Pier\] Andrea Gregorio \[Matthiolus, Petrus Andreas\] (1500-? 1577), Italian physician and botanist. (*Mattioli*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Note

Mattioli, being pre-linnaean, is mentioned here summarily with only a few references to special treatments.
