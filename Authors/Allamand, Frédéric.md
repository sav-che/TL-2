### Author

Allamand, Frédéric (1736-x), Swiss botanist and physician, correspondent of Linnaeus. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Allam.* \[standard form in IPNI: *F.Allam.*\]

#### Collections and manuscripts

LINN.
*Ref*.: IH 2: 31.

#### Bibliography and biography

#### Eponymy
