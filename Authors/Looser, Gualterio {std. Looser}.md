### Author

Looser, Gualterio (1898-x), Chilean botanist at Santiago de Chile. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Looser* \[standard form in IPNI: *Looser*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection PAE|PAE]]; duplicates in many herbaria.
