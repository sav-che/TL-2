### Author

Mason, Francis (1799-1874), British missionary and botanist; in the United States 1818-1830; in Burma from 1830. 
**Abbreviated name**: *F. Mason* \[standard form in IPNI: *Mason*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection NY|NY]] (via Mitten).
