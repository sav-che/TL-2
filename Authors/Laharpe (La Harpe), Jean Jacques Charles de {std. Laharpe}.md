### Author

Laharpe (La Harpe), Jean Jacques Charles de (1802-1877), Swiss botanist; physician at the provincial hospital of Lausanne. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Laharpe* \[standard form in IPNI: *Laharpe*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection LAU|LAU]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 335; BM 3: 1046; CSP 3: 804-805, 8: 148-149, 10: 496; IH 2: 256; Langman p. 425; LS 14797; MW p. 260; PR 4995; Rehder 5: 483. M.R., *in* Dict. hist. biogr. Suisse 4: 430. 1928.

#### Eponymy

*Laharpia* Heer (1859, also dedicated to Philippe George de Laharpe (1830-1882), Swiss paleontologist).
