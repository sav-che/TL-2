### Author

Downing, Andrew Jackson (1815-1852), American horticulturist, nurseryman and landscape architect, born 30 Oct 1815, Newburgh, N.Y., d. 28 Jul 1852 near Yonkers, N.Y. 
**Abbreviated name**: *A.J. Downing*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
