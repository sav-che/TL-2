### Author

Charpentier, Jean G. F. de (1786-1855), German born Swiss geologist, conchologist and botanist, director of the mines of the canton Waadt (Vaud). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Charpentier* \[standard form in IPNI: *Charpent.*\]
