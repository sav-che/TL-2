### Author

Thielau, Friedrich Joachim Siegismund von (1796-?), German (Silesian) landed proprietor, agriculturist and forester, managing the estate of Lampersdorf (nr Breslau) from 1822. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Thielau* \[standard form in IPNI: *Thielau*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 372 (b. 22 Jul 1796); Kelly p. 242; Ratzeburg p. 481-483; TL-2/11.208-11.209; Tucker 1: 684.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Brit. Mus. gen. cat. print. books 237: 281. 1964.

#### Eponymy

*Thielavia* W. Zopf (1876) and the derived genera *Thielaviella* J.A. von Arx et T. Mahmood (1968) and *Thielaviopsis* F. Went (1898).
