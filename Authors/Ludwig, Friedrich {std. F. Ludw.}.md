### Author

Ludwig, Friedrich (1851-1918), German botanist; from 1875 teacher at the gymnasium of Greiz 
**Abbreviated name**: *F. Ludw.* \[standard form in IPNI: *F.Ludw.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
