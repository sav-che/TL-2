### Author

Leandro do Sacramento (1778-1829), Brazilian botanist; calced Carmelite (White Friar); director of the Rio de Janeiro botanical garden; Dr. phil. Coimbra 1806. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Leandro*

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection P|P]] and [[Collection M|M]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Backer p. 318-319; Barnhart 2: 354; CSP 3: 903; IH 2: 419; Kew 3: 389; Lasègue p. 315, 482, 505; PR 5105.
- Colmeiro, M., Bot. penins. hisp. lus. 202. 1858.

(genera): *Leandra* Raddi (1825); *Leandria* \[sic\] E. Rangel (1915); (journal): *Leandra*. Rio de Janeiro. Vol. 1-x. 1971-x.
