### Author

Kurz, Herman (1886-x), American bryologist; Ph. D. Chicago 1922; from 1922 at Tallahassee, Fla. 
**Abbreviated name**: *H. Kurz* \[standard form in IPNI: *H.Kurz*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MICH|MICH]] (189 mosses), [[Collection NY|NY]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 329; CSP 16: 520; IH 2: 397; Kew 3: 340.
- Ewan, J. et al., Short hist. bot. U.S. 93. 1969.
