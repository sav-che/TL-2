### Author

Pottier, Jacques Georges (1892-x), French botanist; studied at the École pratique des Hautes Études; Dr. sci. Paris 1920; later at the Faculté des Sciences de Besançon. (*Pottier*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – Letters at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 103; Hirsch p. 237; Kew 4: 350.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Rev. bryol. lichénol. 7: 47, 62, 63. 1920, 53: 30. 1926.
- Pottier, J.G., Titres et travaux scientifiques de M. Jacques Pottier, Besançon, ca. 1934, 26 p. (bibl.).
