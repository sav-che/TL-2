### Author

Jansen, Pieter (1882-1955), Dutch agrostologist and high school teacher at Rotterdam (1900-1912), at the teachers college of Amsterdam 1912-1952. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Jans.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Jansen*\]
