### Author

Giesebrecht, Werner (1880-x), German apothecary and mycologist, born 29 Jan 1880 in Stettin (now Szczecin, Poland), doctorate 1915 Würzburg. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Giesbrecht*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
