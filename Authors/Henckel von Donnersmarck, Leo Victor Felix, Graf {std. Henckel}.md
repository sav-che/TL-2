### Author

Henckel von Donnersmarck, Leo Victor Felix, Graf (1785-1861), German administrator, amateur botanist, and "treuer Mitarbeiter am Thesaurus literaturae botanicae." (Pritzel). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Henckel*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection JE|JE]].
