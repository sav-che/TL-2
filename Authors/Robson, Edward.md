### Author

Robson, Edward (1763-1813), British (English) botanist at Darlington; correspondent of W. Withering and J.E. Smith; one of the original fellows of the Linnean Society of London. 
**Abbreviated name**: *E. Robson* \[standard form in IPNI: *E.Robson*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Sunderland Museum (inf. P.S. Davis and G.G. Graham). Other material at [[Collection LIV|LIV]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 166; BB p. 259; CSP 5: 243; Desmond p. 526 (b. 17 Oct 1763, d. 21 Mai 1813); DNB 49: 62; Dryander 3: 650; Henrey 2: 135, 136; Hortus 3: 1202; Rehder 2: 246; Zander ed. 10, p. 707, ed. 11, p. 807.
