### Author

Herter, Wilhelm Gustav Franz (Guillermo Herter) (1884-1958), German botanist; Dr. phil. Berlin 1908, resided in Uruguay 1907, 1909-1910 and 1923-1939; in Germany 1939-1941; at Cracow in Poland 1941-1944; later Uruguayan consul at Bern and Basel; ultimately in Hamburg. (*Herter*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection G|G]]; in herb. Stiftung Paul Aellen (20.000 specimens) ([[Collection G|G]]-PAE). Herter's *Plantae uruguayenses exsiccatae* (cent. 1427, nos. 1-2700) are in many herbaria.
*Ref*.: Stevenson, J., Beih. Nova Hedwigia 36: 185-192. 1971.
- Sayre, G., Mem. New York Bot. Gard. 19(3): 337. 1975.
