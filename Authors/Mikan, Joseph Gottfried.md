### Author

Mikan, Joseph Gottfried (1743-1814), Bohemian (Czech) botanist at Praha; founder and director of the botanical garden in Smichow (Praha) 1775-1811; father of J. C. Mikan. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. G. Mikan* \[standard form in IPNI: *J.G.Mikan*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Eponymy

*Kanimia* G. Gardner (1847, anagram); *Mikania* Willdenow (1803, *nom. cons.*); *Mikaniopsis* Milne-Redhead (1956). – We could not establish with certainty the derivation of *Mikania* F. W. Schmidt (1795, *nom. rej.*).
