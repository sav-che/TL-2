### Author

Mercier de Coppet, Elysée (1802-1863), Swiss botanist. (*Mercier*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection G|G]] (donated by his son in 1913).

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 2(1): 192; Barnhart 2: 477; BL 2: 571, 691; BM 3: 1289; Hortus 3: 1199 ("Merc."); Rehder 2: 304; Tucker 1: 180.
- Briquet, J., Annu. Cons. Jard. bot. Genève 20: 482-483. 1919 (on herb. at G).
- Miège, J. & Wuest, Saussurea 6: 132. 1975.

### Publications

##### n.5838. Rubi genevenses

**Title**
*Rubi genevenses*... Genève (Librairie allemande de J. Kessmann) \[1861\]. Oct.
**Abbreviated title**: *Rubi genev.*

**Notes**
*Publ*.: 1861, p. \[1\]-39. *Copies*: FI, G (with corrections by author). Reprinted from Georges François Reuter (1805-1872), *Catalogue* \[détaillé\] *des plantes vasculaires qui croissent naturellement aux environs de Genève*, ed. 2, Genève 1861, p. 259-295.
