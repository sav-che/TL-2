### Author

Mercier de Coppet, Elysée (1802-1863), Swiss botanist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Mercier* \[standard form in IPNI: *Mercier*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection G|G]] (donated by his son in 1913).
