### Author

Lorey, Félix-Nicolas (x-1841), French botanist at Dijon, later at Marseille. 
**Abbreviated name**: *F. Lorey*

#### Herbarium and types

Musée d'Histoire naturelle, Dijon. Further material at [[Collection FI|FI]] and [[Collection H|H]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 403; BL 2: 141, 684; BM 3: 1179; DTS 6(4): 91; IH 2: 461; Kew 3: 501; MD p. 177; PR 5616-5617 (ed. 1: 6270-6271); Rehder 5: 523; Saccardo 1: 98, 2: 125; TL-1/782-783; Tucker 1: 441.

- Candolle, Alph. de, Phytographie 429. 1880 (herb. Dijon).

- Steinberg, C. H., Webbia 32(1): 31. 1977.

#### Eponymy

*Loreya* A. P. de Candolle (1828).
