### Author

Olivier, \[Joseph\] Ernest (1844-1914), French administrator, zoologist and botanist; grandson of G. A. Olivier; travelled frequently in Algeria; newspaper publisher at Moulins; later "maire" of Chemilly. 
**Abbreviated name**: *E. Olivier* \[standard form in IPNI: *E.Olivier*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
