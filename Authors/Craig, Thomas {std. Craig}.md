### Author

Craig, Thomas (1839-1916), Canadian botanist, born 14 Jun 1839, Montreal, died 1916, member of the Torrey Botanical Club, 1916, business man. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Craig* \[standard form in IPNI: *Craig*\]
