### Author

Geer van Jutphaas, Jan Lodewijk Willem, \[Baron\] de (1784-1857), Dutch magistrate and naturalist living at Jutphaas near Utrecht. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Geer* \[standard form in IPNI: *Geer*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
