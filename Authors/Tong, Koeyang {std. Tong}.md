### Author

Tong, Koeyang (1896-x), Chinese botanist in Chichow, Anhuei. (*Tong*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 390 (b. 4 Oct 1896); MW p. 500.

#### Composite works

Translated *Die Flora von Central-China*, L. Diels, Bot. Jahrb. 29: 169-659. 1900-1901, into Chinese: J. Coll. Sci. Sun Yatsen Univ. 3: 684-723. 1932, 4: 85-132, 221-281. 1932, 4: 427-479, 619-671. 1933, 5: 267-316. 1933, 5: 455-506. 1934, 6: 25-60, 277-316. 1934.
