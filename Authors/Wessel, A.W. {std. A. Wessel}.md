### Author

Wessel, A.W. (*fl*. 1858), German (East-Frisian) botanist and high school teacher at Aurich. 
**Abbreviated name**: *A.Wessel* \[standard form in IPNI: *A.Wessel*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 5: 2296; Jackson p. 308; PR 10194; Rehder 1: 384.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Brit. Mus. gen. cat. print. books 255: 656. 1965 (wrote also *Die Nordsee-Insel Spiekeroog*, Aurich 1863).
