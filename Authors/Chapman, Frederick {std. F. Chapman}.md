### Author

Chapman, Frederick (1864-1943), British palaeobiologist who worked in Australia. 
**Abbreviated name**: *F. Chapman* \[standard form in IPNI: *F.Chapm.*\]

#### Collections

BM, also National Museum, Melbourne.
