### Author

Pasquale, Fortunato (1856-1917), Italian botanist and agronomist at Napoli; son of Giuseppe Antonio Pasquale; connected with the R. Istituto Tecnico di Napoli. 
**Abbreviated name**: *F. Pasq.* \[standard form in IPNI: *F.Pasq.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection NAP|NAP]]; some mycological material at [[Collection FI|FI]] and [[Collection PAD|PAD]]. – Some letters at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 53; BL 2: 336-343, 699; CSP 10: 996, 17: 722-723; Jackson p. 204; Kew 4: 243; LS 19845, suppl. 21009; Morren ed. 10, p. 85; Rehder 5: 651; Saccardo 1: 123; Tucker 3: 542.
