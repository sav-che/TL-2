### Author

Barentin, Friedrich Wilhelm (1810-1886), German (Berlin) botanist and teacher at a "Real Gymnasium" in Berlin. (*Barentin*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 121 (b. 14 Oct 1810, Berlin); BM 1: 98 (d. 1886); PR 412, ed. 1: 476.

#### Biofile
