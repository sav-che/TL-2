### Author

Trautwein, Johannes \[Carl\] (1858-?), German (Saxonian) botanist; studied at the Universities of Leipzig, Halle and Tübingen; Dr. phil. Halle 1885. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Trautwein* \[standard form in IPNI: *Trautwein*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 398 (b. 4 Dec 1858, ex diss.); BM 5: 2134.
