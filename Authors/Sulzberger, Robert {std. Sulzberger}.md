### Author

Sulzberger, Robert (*fl*. 1888), Belgian botanist and school teacher. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Sulzberger* \[standard form in IPNI: *Sulzb.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
