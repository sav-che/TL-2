### Author

Taylor, Adam (*fl*. 1769), British gardener to J. Sutton, New Park, near Devises, Wilts. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Ad. Tayl* \[standard form in IPNI: *A.Taylor*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
