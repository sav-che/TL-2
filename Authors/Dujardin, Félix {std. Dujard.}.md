### Author

Dujardin, Félix (1801-1860), French invertebrate zoologist and botanist at Rennes. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Dujard.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Dujard.*\]

#### Collections

Botanical collections unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
