### Author

Lieblein, Franz Kaspar (1744-1810), German botanist and high school teacher at Fulda," Hochfürstlich-Fuldischen Hofkammerrath und Hofapotheker". (*Liebl.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 379 (*b*. 15 Sep 1744, *d*. 28 Apr 1810); BM 3: 1112; Dryander 3: 157, 5: 326; Herder p. 185; Hortus 3: 1198 ("Liebl."); PR 5300 (ed. 1: 5877); Rehder 5: 510.

- Poggendorff, J. C., Biogr.-lit. Handw.-Buch 1: 1461. 1863 (b. 15 Sep 1744, d. 28 Apr 1810).
