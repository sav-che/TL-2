### Author

Koelpin, Alexander Bernhard (1739-1801), German physician, botanist and teacher at the academic gymnasium at Stettin. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Koelp.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Koelp.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection B|B]].

#### Bibliography and biography
