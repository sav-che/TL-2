### Author

McCoy, (Sir) Frederick (1817-1899), Irish-born palaeontologist; from 1854 at Melbourne as professor of natural sciences; D. Sc. Cantabr. 1886. 
**Abbreviated name**: *McCoy*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
