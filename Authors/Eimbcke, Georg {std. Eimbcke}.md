### Author

Eimbcke, Georg (1771-1843), German pharmacist and botanist, born 17 Dec 1771 at Hamburg, died 20 Apr 1843 at Eppendorf near Hamburg. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Eimbcke*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
