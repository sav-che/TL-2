### Author

Olsson, Peter \[Petter\] (1838-1923), Swedish botanist and zoologist born in Skåne; Dr. phil. Lund 1865; lecturer at Lund 1867-1869; high school teacher at Östersund 1869-1905. (*P. Olsson, Skåne*).

#### Herbarium and types

Material at [[Collection BR|BR]], [[Collection C|C]], [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection GB|GB]], [[Collection IBF|IBF]], [[Collection PC|PC]] (some of this material may also be of P. Olsson, Värml.).
