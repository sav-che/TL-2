### Author

Michalet, \[Louis\] Eugène (1826-1862), French botanist and magistrate at Besançon. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Michalet* \[standard form in IPNI: *Michalet*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection AUT|AUT]], [[Collection BR|BR]], [[Collection CGE|CGE]], [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection G|G]], [[Collection P|P]]. – Issued *Herbier des plantes critiques du Jura*, nos. 1-150.

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 1: 382; Barnhart 2: 485; BL 2: 169, 691; BM 3: 1304; CSP 4: 375, 12: 506; IH 2: 535; PR 6191; Rehder 1: 411; Tucker 1: 483.
- Aderhold, R., Gen.-Reg. Bot. Zeit. 275. 1895.
- Anon., Bull. Soc. bot. France 9: 145. 1862, 10: vii. 1863, 13(bibl.): 192. 1866, 15: xiv. 1868.
- Blanche, H., Bull. Soc. bot. France 13: cxlviii-cxlix. 1866 (acquired Michalet's herbarium; Michalet died 12 Feb 1862).
- Candolle, Alph. de, Phytographie 433. 1880 (specimens in herb. Burnat \[now: G\], and Cosson \[now: P\]).
- Hedge, I. C. & J. M. Lamond, Index coll. Edinb. herb. 110. 1970 (pl. E).
