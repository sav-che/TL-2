### Author

Reguis, J. Marius F. (1850-?), French physician and mycologist at Allauch nr Marseille, later at Avignon. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Reguis* \[standard form in IPNI: *Réguis*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
