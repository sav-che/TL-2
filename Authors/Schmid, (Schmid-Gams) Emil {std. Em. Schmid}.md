### Author

Schmid, \[Schmid-Gams\] Emil (1891-x), German (Württemberg) botanist; Dr. phil. Zürich 1923; curator at the Botanical Museum, Univ. Zürich; professor of botany at the University of Zürich 1936-1961. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Em. Schmid* \[standard form in IPNI: *Em.Schmid*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection Z|Z]]. – *Letters* at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 12(3): 201; Barnhart 3: 230 (b. 18 Feb 1891); BFM 2076-2078, 2128; Biol.-Dokum. 16: 8105; BJI 2: 156; BL 2: 387, 572, 585, 591, 714; Hirsch p. 264; IH 1 (ed. 6): 364, (ed. 7): 342; MW p. 437; Tucker 3: 272.

#### Biofile

- Anon., World Biography L-Z, p. 4238. 1948.
- Haesler, I., Letter 23 Apr 1982 (Schmid-Gams living at Zollikon, Switzerland).

#### Composite works

- (1) Collaborator, G. Hegi, *Ill. Fl. Mitt. Eur.* 4(1) and 4(2), see TL-2/2571.
- (2) Edited and revised C. Schröter, *Flora des Südens*, ed. 2, 1956; see C. Schröter.

#### Festschrift
