### Author

Sprague, Isaac (1811-1895), American botanical and zoological artist in Massachusetts, who worked for e.g. Audubon and Asa Gray. 
**Abbreviated name**: *I. Sprague* \[standard form in IPNI: *I.Sprague*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
