### Author

Schulz, Richard (*fl*. 1904), German (Silesian) botanist; Dr. phil. Zürich (student of H. Schinz and F.A. Pax) 1904. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Ri. Schulz*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BJI 2: 160; BM 4: 1871; DTS 6(4): 196; Hortus 3: 1203.
