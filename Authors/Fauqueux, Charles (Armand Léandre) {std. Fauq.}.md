### Author

Fauqueux, Charles \[Armand Léandre\] (1879-1968), French botanist and educator, born 27 Mar 1879 in Saint-André-Farivillers (Oise), dir. of a school at Fayel (Oise). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Fauq.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
