### Author

Tominz, Raimondo (*fl*. 1879), Austro-Hungarian (Trieste/Italian) botanist; director of the Civico Orto botanico, Trieste from 1879. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Tominz* \[standard form in IPNI: *Tominz*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Morren ed. 10, p. 35; Rehder 1: 66; Saccardo 1: 164.
