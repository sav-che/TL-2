### Author

Vadas, Jenö \[Eugen; Eugène\] (1857-?), Hungarian forestry botanist; professor of forestry at the Selmecbányar Forestry College from 1891. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Vadas*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

LS 27592, 40196-40197; Rehder 5: 872; Tucker 1: 712, 3: 320.

- Krücken, O. von & I. Parlagi, Das geistige Ungarn 2: 668-669. 1818.
- Szinnyei, J., Magyar írók 14: 720-721. 1914.

#### Composite works

Editor, with E. Henry, *L'expérimentation forestière en Hongrie*, Nancy 1910, 18 p. (Ann. Sc. agron. franç. étrang. Jun 1910, n.v.).
