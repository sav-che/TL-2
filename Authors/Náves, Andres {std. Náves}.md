### Author

Náves, Andres (1839-1910), Spanish Augustinian friar; collected in the Philippine Islands 
**Abbreviated name**: *Náves* \[standard form in IPNI: *Náves*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Original collections destroyed; some material at [[Collection MA|MA]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Backer p. 390; Barnhart 2: 540; BM 3: 1404; Hortus 3: 1200 (Náves): IH 2: (in press); Kew 4: 117; Render 5: xxvi; SK 1: 381; TL-1/362; TL-2/553, 1766, sec also M.J. Berkeley; Tucker 1: 512; Zander ed. 10, p. 696, ed. 11, p. 794.
- Merrill, E. D., Species blancoanac 14-21. 1918, Enum. Philipp. pl.4: 212. 1926 (on Novissima Appendix).
- Moldenkc, H. N., Plant Life 2: 73. 1947 (1948).

#### Composite works

Editor (and co-author of Noviss. App.) of Blanco, *Fl. Filip., ed. 9, q.v.* (TL-2/553). For a review of the *Noviss App*. see for instance Peter, Bot. Centralbl. 18: 175-176. 1884.
