### Author

Petrowsky \[Petrowski, Petrovsky\], Andrei \[Andreas\] Stanislavovic (1832-1882), Russian botanist; high school teacher at Jaroslaw. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Petrowsky* \[standard form in IPNI: *Petrovsky*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MW|MW]]; duplicates [[Collection B|B]], [[Collection CGE|CGE]], [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection LE|LE]], [[Collection U|U]]. – According to a note by De Bary (Bot. Zeit. 26: 447. 1868), Petrowsky published a "Herbarium der Flora von Jaroslaw", cent. 1-2).
