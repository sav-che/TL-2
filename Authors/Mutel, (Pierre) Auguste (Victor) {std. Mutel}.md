### Author

Mutel, (Pierre) Auguste (Victor) (1795-1847), French botanist and soldier, sometime military administrator at Le Hâvre. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Mutel*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection GRM|GRM]].
