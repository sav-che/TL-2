### Author

De Toni, Giuseppe (fl. 1900), Italian algologist, son of G. B. De Toni. 
**Abbreviated name**: *De Toni. fil.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Eponymy

*Detonia* Frenguelli (1949) is dedicated to his father Giovanni Batista De Toni (1864-1924), q.v.
