### Author

Zermann, Chrysostomus Alexander (*fl*. 1893-1895), Austrian Benedictine clergyman; sometime teacher at the Stiftsgymnasium at Melk. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Zermann* \[standard form in IPNI: *Zermann*\]

Unknown.
