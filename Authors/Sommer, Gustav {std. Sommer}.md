### Author

Sommer, Gustav (*fl*. 1886), German gardener at Karlsruhe, Baden. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Sommer* \[standard form in IPNI: *Sommer*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 5: 1969; Morren ed. 10, p. 24; Rehder 1: 48; Tucker 1: 659.

#### Eponymy

*Sommera* Schlechtendal (1835) commemorates C.N. Sommer, an entomologist.
