### Author

Lespinasse, Jean Martial Gustave (1807-1876), French botanist at Bordeaux. (*Lesp.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BORD|BORD]], other material at [[Collection BR|BR]] and [[Collection E|E]].

#### Bibliography and biography

### Publications

##### n.4409. Florula sebastopolitana

**Title**
*Florula sebastopolitana* seu enumeratio plantarum anno 1855 circa Sebastopolim et Balaclavam à clariss. doct. Jul. Jeannel exercit. Gall. pharm. maj. collectarum simul cum animadversionibus adnotationibusque criticis auctore Gust. Lespinasse... Burdigalae \[Bordeaux\] (ex typis G. Gounouilhou) 1881. Duod.
**Abbreviated title**: *Fl. sebastop.*

**Notes**
*Editor*: Victor Félix Raulin (1815-1905).
*Publ*.: Jan-Mai 1881 (Bot. Zeit. 29 Jul 1881; Nat. Nov. Jun(2) 1881), p. \[i-ii\], 317-394.
*Copies*: BR, NY. – Reprinted, with special t.p. and cover, from Actes Acad. Sci. Belles-Lettres Arts Bordeaux 42(2): 317-394. 1880. – We have no further data on Jules Jeannel except that he was chief pharmacist with the French eastern army during the siege of Sébastopol.
