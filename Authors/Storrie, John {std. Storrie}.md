### Author

Storrie, John (1843-1901), British botanist; employed on the Cardiff Western mail; curator Cardiff Museum 1877-1892. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Storrie*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection NMW|NMW]]; further material [[Collection DOR|DOR]].
