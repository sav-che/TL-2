### Author

Schummel, \[Theodor\] Emil (1785-1848), German (Silesian) botanist, entomologist and high school teacher at the medical college, Breslau. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Schummel*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection WRSL|WRSL]].

#### Bibliography and biography
