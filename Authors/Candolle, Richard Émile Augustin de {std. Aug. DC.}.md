### Author

Candolle, Richard Émile Augustin de (1868-1920), Swiss botanist, son of Casimir de Candolle. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Aug. DC* \[standard form in IPNI: *Aug.DC.*\]

#### Herbarium and types
