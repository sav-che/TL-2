### Author

Knowles, Matilda Cullen (1864-1933), Irish lichenologist at the \[National\] Science and Art Museum in Dublin from 1902. 
**Abbreviated name**: *M. Knowles*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection DBN|DBN]].
