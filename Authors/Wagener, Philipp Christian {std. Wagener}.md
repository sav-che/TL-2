### Author

Wagener, Philipp Christian (*fl*. 1798), German botanist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Wagener*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 447; PR 9898, ed. 1: 10871; Rehder 1: 375.

#### Eponymy

*Wageneria* Klotzsch (1854) honors H. Wagener, a German collector of plants in Venezuela (see below).

#### Note

Hermann Wagener(1823-1877), Orchid collector in Venezuela and Colombia (see e.g. K.S. Levonsen, Orchid Rev. 82: 348-352. 1974) who did not publish himself on his collections. See D.F.L. von Schlechtendal, *Plantae Wagenerianae Columbiae* in Linnaea 25: 743-750. Dec 1853, 26: 127-144. Apr 1854, and 26: 631-674. Sep 1855 and H.G. Reichenbach, Bonplandia 2: 9-26. 15 Jan 1854, 3: 65-72. 15 Mar 1855. See also H. Pittier, *Manual de las plantas usuales de Venezuela* 1926, p. 5.
