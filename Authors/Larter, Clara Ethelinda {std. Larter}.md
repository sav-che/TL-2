### Author

Larter, Clara Ethelinda (1847-1936), British botanist at Torquay. (*Larter*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection TOR|TOR]] (herb.) and [[Collection OXF|OXF]]; algae at [[Collection K|K]].
*Ref*.: Kent, D. H., Brit. herb. 64. 1957.
