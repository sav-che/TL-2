### Author

Stoltz, Jean Louis (1804-1896), Alsatian physician at Andlau, strongly interested in agronomy and enology. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Stoltz* \[standard form in IPNI: *Stoltz*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

NI 1899; PR 8991; Rehder 3: 104, 546; Tucker 1: 670.
