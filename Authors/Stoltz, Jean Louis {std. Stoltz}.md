### Author

Stoltz, Jean Louis (1804-1896), Alsatian physician at Andlau, strongly interested in agronomy and enology. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Stoltz*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

NI 1899; PR 8991; Rehder 3: 104, 546; Tucker 1: 670.

- Anon., Cat. gén. livr. impr. Bibl. natl. 178: 948-949. 1950.
- Sitzman, E., Dict. biogr. hommes célèbr. Alsace 2: 837. s.d. (d. 1896 "à 93 ans").
