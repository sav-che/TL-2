### Author

Watson, William (of Kew) (1858-1925), British gardener and botanist; Kew gardens 1879, asst. curator 1886; curator of the Royal Botanic Gardens Kew 1901-1922. (*Wm. Wats.* (*Kew*)).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection K|K]].
