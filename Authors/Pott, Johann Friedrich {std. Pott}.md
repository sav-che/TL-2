### Author

Pott, Johann Friedrich (1738-1805), German botanist; physician to the Duke of Braunschweig (Brunswick). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Pott* \[standard form in IPNI: *Pott*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection LE|LE]] (in herbarium Gorenkianum) ; some further specimens are at [[Collection LINN|LINN]]; other material is at [[Collection H|H]] and [[Collection SBT|SBT]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 103 (d. 13 Apr 1805); Hortus 3: 1202 (J.F. Pott); NI 1: 198; PR 2560, 7281 (ed. 1: 8174); TL-2/1599; Tucker 1: 566; Zander ed. 10, p. 703.

#### Biofile

- Herder, F. v., Bot. Centralbl. 55: 260. 1893 (herb. at LE in herb. Gorenkianum).
- Jackson, B.D., Notes cat. Linn. herb. 19. 1922 (some plants in LINN). Kukkonen, I., Herb. Christ. Steven 81. 1971.

Published ed. 2 of J.P. Du Roi, *Harbk. Baumz.* (1775-1800), TL-2/1599.
