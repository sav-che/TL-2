### Author

Mazza, Angelo (1844-1929), Italian algologist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Mazza* \[standard form in IPNI: *Mazza*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection PI|PI]]; some further material at [[Collection BR|BR]] and [[Collection L|L]].
