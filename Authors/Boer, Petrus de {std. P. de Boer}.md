### Author

Boer, Petrus de (1841-1890), Dutch (Frisian) botanist, studied at the University of Utrecht, pupil of F.A.W. Miquel, Dr. phil. Utrecht 1866, succeeded 1871 H.C. van Hall as professor of botany and director of the Hortus Botanicus at the University of Groningen. 
**Abbreviated name**: *P. de Boer*

#### Herbarium and types

If any at [[Collection GRO|GRON]]; material used for his thesis (see below) at [[Collection L|L]] and [[Collection U|U]].

#### Note

Theodoric Valeton (1855-1929), TL-2/6: 649-651, was a student of P. de Boer, his dissertation, *Crit. overz. Olacin*, 1886, was defended under de Boer as "promotor".
