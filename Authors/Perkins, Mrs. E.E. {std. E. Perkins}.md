### Author

Perkins, Mrs. E.E. (*fl*. 1830-1840), British botanist at Chelsea; "professor of botanical flower painting" (t.p. Elem. bot.). 
**Abbreviated name**: *E. Perkins* \[standard form in IPNI: *E.Perkins*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
