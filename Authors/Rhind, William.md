### Author

Rhind, William (*fl*. 1830-1860), British (Scottish) botanist; lecturer in botany, Marischal College, Aberdeen. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Rhind* \[standard form in IPNI: *Rhind*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
