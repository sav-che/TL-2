### Author

Filarszky, Nandor (1858-1941), Hungarian algologist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Filarszky* \[standard form in IPNI: *Fil.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BP|BP]], dupl. [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection GB|GB]], [[Collection L|L]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 196.
- Koster, Taxon 18: 552. 1969.
