### Author

Clifford, Montagu Howard (fl. 1933-1940), British palaeobotanist, studied at Cambridge (Downing), B.A. 1933, M.A. 1937. (*M.H. Clifford*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 358; Rehder 2: 175.
