### Author

Schneider, George (1848-1917), French-born gardener; went to England 1870; from 1872 employee of Veitch & Sons, Chelsea. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Geo. Schneid.* \[standard form in IPNI: *G.Schneid.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 235; Desmond p. 545.

**Title**
*The book of choice ferns* for the garden, conservatory, and stove. Describing and giving explicit cultural directions for the best and most striking ferns and selaginellas in cultivation. Illustrated with coloured plates and numerous wood engravings, specially prepared for this work... London (L. Upcott Gill,...) 1892-1894, 3 vols. Qu.
