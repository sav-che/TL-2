### Author

Happe, Andreas Friederich (1733-1802), German naturalist and artist at the Berlin Academy of Sciences. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Happe* \[standard form in IPNI: *Happe*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Name
