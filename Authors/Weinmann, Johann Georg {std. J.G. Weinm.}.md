### Author

Weinmann, Johann Georg (1764-1769), German (Schwaben) physician and botanist; town physician of Reutlingen. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J.G. Weinm.* \[standard form in IPNI: *J.G.Weinm.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 5: 2285; Dryander 3: 201; Herder p. 418; PR 3393, 10138, ed. 1: 11123; Rehder 3: 72.
