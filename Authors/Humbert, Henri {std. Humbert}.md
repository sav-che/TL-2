### Author

Humbert, Henri (1887-1967), French botanist and botanical traveller at Paris, explorer of Madagascar. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Humbert* \[standard form in IPNI: *Humbert*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection P|P]], [[Collection PC|PC]].
