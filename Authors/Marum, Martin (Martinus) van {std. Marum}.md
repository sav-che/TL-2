### Author

Marum, Martin \[Martinus\] van (1750-1837), Dutch physician and naturalist; Ph. D. Groningen 1773; main founder of the collections and library of Teyler's Stichting at Haarlem. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Marum*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
