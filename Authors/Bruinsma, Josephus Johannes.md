### Author

Bruinsma, Josephus Johannes (1805-1888), Dutch-Frisian apothecary and botanist at Leeuwarden (Friesland). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Bruinsma* \[standard form in IPNI: *Bruinsma*\]

#### Herbarium and types
