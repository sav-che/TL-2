### Author

Baskerville, Thomas (1812-1840), British physician, member of the Royal College of Surgeons, London, M.R.C.S. 1835, practised in Canterbury. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Baskerville* \[standard form in IPNI: *T.Baskerv.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
