### Author

Heyne, Benjamin (1770-1819), German Moravian missionary; at Tranquebar 1792; superintendent Bangalore Gardens 1802-1888; in England 1813. 
**Abbreviated name**: *B. Heyne* \[standard form in IPNI: *B.Heyne*\]
