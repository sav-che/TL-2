### Author

Krabbe, Gustav \[Heinrich\] (1855-1895), German botanist; Dr. phil. Berlin 1882; from 1890 at Berlin University with Schwendener. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Krabbe* \[standard form in IPNI: *Krabbe*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
