### Author

Morris, George Edward (1853-1916), British-born American mycologist; drawing-master in the public schools of Waltham, Mass. 
**Abbreviated name**: *G. Morris* \[standard form in IPNI: *G.Morris*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection CUP|CUP]], [[Collection NY|NY]].

#### \[Bibliography and biography\]

- Lloyd, C. G., Mycol. notes 43: 589-590. 1916 (portr.; d. 5 Jul 1916).
- Morse, A. P., List of the water-color drawings of fungi by G. E. Morris 3-6. 1928 (portr., brief biogr., b. 25 Feb 1853, d. 5 Jul 1916).
