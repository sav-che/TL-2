### Author

Gogela, Franz (fl. 1892-1900), Czech cleric ("Pfarrer"), amateur botanist and school teacher (?) (i.e. "Cooperator und Bürgerschulkatechet in Mistek"). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Gogela*
