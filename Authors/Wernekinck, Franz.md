### Author

Wernekinck, Franz (1764-1839), German (Westphalian) botanist and physician; studied in Göttingen; practicing physician and professor of botany in Münster (Wstf.). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Wernekinck* \[standard form in IPNI: *Wernek.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

ADB 42: 21-22; Barnhart 3: 478 (b. 19 Feb 1764, d. 6 Feb 1839); Jackson p. 307; NI 2137; Rehder 1: 375.
