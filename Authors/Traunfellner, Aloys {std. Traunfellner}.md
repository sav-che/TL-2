### Author

Traunfellner, Aloys (1782-1840), Austrian pharmacist and botanist; practicing pharmacist in Klagenfurt from 1801; collected in Dalmatia (1806) and Istria. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Traunfellner*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection KL|KL]]; further material in [[Collection GOET|GOET]], [[Collection MW|MW]]. – Owned also a large mineralogical and numismatic collection. – List of duplicates in Flora 1: 616-621. 20 Dec 1818. – *Letters* at [[Collection G|G]], [[Collection UPS|UPS]]-UB.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 398 (b. 27 Sep 1782, d. 13 Oct 1840); IH 1 (ed. 6): 365, (ed. 7): 344.
