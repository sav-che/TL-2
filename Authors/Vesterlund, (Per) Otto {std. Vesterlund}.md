### Author

Vesterlund, \[Per\] Otto (1857-1953), Swedish botanist and forester; ultimately in Alvdalen, prov. Dalarna (Dalecalia). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Vesterlund* \[standard form in IPNI: *Vesterl.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection GB|GB]], [[Collection LD|LD]], [[Collection UPS|UPS]]. – Lichens at [[Collection M|M]], some Musci in [[Collection DPU|DPU]]; further material at [[Collection E|E]]. – *Letters* to K. Keck at [[Collection UPS|UPS]]-UB.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 433; BJI 2: 519, 529, 727; CSP 19: 335; KR p. 735 (bibl., b. 25 Mar 1857); Rehder 1: 73.
