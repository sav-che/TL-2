### Author

Leszczyc-Suminsky, Michael Hieronim (1820-1898), Polish botanist and painter. (*Leszc.-Sum.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 3: 1097; CSP 3: 978; Jackson p. 151; Kew 3: 420; Moebius p. 132, 133, 345; PR 5246.
