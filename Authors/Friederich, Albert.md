### Author

Friederich, Albert (1879-1961), German botanist (lichenologist), born 30 Jul 1879 in Cannstatt a. Neckar (Württemberg), attended Hochschule in Stuttgart and then the Univ. of Würzburg. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Friederich*

#### Herbarium and types
