### Author

Quer y Martinez, José (1695-1764), Spanish botanist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Quer* \[standard form in IPNI: *Quer*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MA|MA]]; other material in [[Collection G|G]] (see Briquet 1919) and [[Collection S|S]] (herb. Montin).
