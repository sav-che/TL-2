### Author

Beckhaus, Konrad Friedrich Ludwig (1821-1890), German clergyman, highschool teacher and botanist at Höxter, Westfalen. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Beckhaus* \[standard form in IPNI: *Beckh.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MSTR|MSTR]].
