### Author

Millais, John Guille (1865-1931), English naturalist, gardener, sportsman and popular writer on birds, hunting and cultivated plants; Victoria medal of honor, R. H. S. 1928. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Millais*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
