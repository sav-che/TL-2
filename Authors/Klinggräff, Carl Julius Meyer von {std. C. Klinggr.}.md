### Author

Klinggräff, Carl Julius Meyer von (1809-1879), German physician and botanist; Dr. med. Königsberg 1832; at first physician at Marienwerder; from 1836 landowner at Paleschken bei Stuhm in Prussia. 
**Abbreviated name**: *C. Klinggr.*

#### Herbarium and types

At the former "Westpreussisches Provinzial-Museum zu Danzig." According to our information this museum was destroyed during World War II, but recent confirmation from Poland is lacking. Other material: [[Collection B|B]], [[Collection L|L]], [[Collection PC|PC]].
