### Author

Railonsala, Artturi Nikodemus (orig. A.N. Helenius) (1902-1982), Finnish botanist, teacher and school inspector at Tornio; taraxacologist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Railonsala* \[standard form in IPNI: *Rail.*\]
