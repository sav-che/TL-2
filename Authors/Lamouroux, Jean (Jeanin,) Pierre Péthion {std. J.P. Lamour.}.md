### Author

Lamouroux, Jean \[Jeanin,\] Pierre Péthion (1797-1866), French physician; Dr. med. Paris 1818; popular writer on botany; brother of J. V. F. Lamouroux. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. P. Lamour.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 339; BM 1: 87; 3: 1052; MD p. 172. 174; PR 5027-5028 (ed. 1: 5521-5524); Rehder 5: 484.
- Debeaux, Rev. de Bot., Toulouse 13\["12"\]: 10. 1895.
