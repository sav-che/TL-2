### Author

Cornaz, Charles Auguste Edouard (1825-1911), French-Swiss physician and botanist, born Marseille 29 Sep 1825, died 1911, Neuchâtel, Suisse, Dr. méd., physician at Neuchâtel, Suisse. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Cornaz*

#### Herbarium and types
