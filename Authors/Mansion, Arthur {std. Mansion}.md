### Author

Mansion, Arthur (1863-1905), Belgian bryologist; Dr. sei. Liège; high school teacher at Huy and, from 1902, at Namur. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Mansion*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BR|BR]].

#### Bibliography and biography
