### Author

Poech, Josef \[Alois\] (1816-1846), Bohemian botanist, born at Snedovice (Wegstädl) nr Praha, studied medicine, assistant to the botanical cabinet of Praha University; associated with Opiz. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Poech*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection PR|PR]] (main set), [[Collection REG|REG]] (see Flora 1846), and [[Collection W|W]] (musci; Austrian plants).
*Exsiccatae*: *Musei bohemici*, nos. 1-100, Prag 1846 (see Sayre 1971).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 94 (b. 22 Mai 1816, d. 20 Jan 1846); BM 4: 1590; CSP 4: 949; Hortus 3: 1201 (Poech); Jackson p. 314; Kew 4: 327; Klášterský p. 145; Maiwald p. 69, 119, 144, 145, 189, 256; PR 7221 (ed. 1: 8093); Rehder 5: 679; SBC p. 129; Tucker 1: 561; Zander ed. 10, p. 702, ed. 11, p. 801.

#### Eponymy

*Poechia* P.M. Opiz (1852). Although the author does not give the etymology for *Poechia* Endlicher (1848), the name likely honors Poech.
