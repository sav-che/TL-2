### Author

Stansfield, Abraham (1802-1880), British nurseryman at Ecclesholme and Vale Gardens, Todmorden. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Stansf.*

#### Herbarium and types

Formerly at [[Collection TDN|TDN]] and [[Collection TOD|TOD]]; not traced (Kent 1957; Kent & Allen 1984).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 316 (b. 12 Jan 1802, d. 15 Aug 1880); BB p. 286; BL 2: 252, 279, 719; Desmond p. 580; Jackson p. 152.
