### Author

Moffatt, Will (William) Sayer (1847-1941), American mycologist; MD Chicago 1868; practiced medicine at Wheaton, Ill., and Sycamore, Ill.; to Los Angeles 1926. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Moffatt*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection ILL|ILL]] (11.000); a large collection is also at [[Collection ISM|ISM]]; further material at [[Collection F|F]], [[Collection MIN|MIN]], [[Collection MSC|MSC]], [[Collection WTU|WTU]].
