### Author

Paget, Sir James (1814-1899), British surgeon and botanist, baronet 1871 LL.D. Cambridge 1874. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Paget* \[standard form in IPNI: *Paget*\]

[[Collection NWH|NWH]] (1000) and [[Collection K|K]].
