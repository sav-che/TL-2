### Author

Paiche, Philippe (1842-1911), Swiss botanist at Genève; studied Potentilla, Hieracium and Rosa. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Paiche* \[standard form in IPNI: *Paiche*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection G|G]] (via herb. Boissier); some further material at [[Collection BR|BR]] and [[Collection E|E]]. – Letters at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography
