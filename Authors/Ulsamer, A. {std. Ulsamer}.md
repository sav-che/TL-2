### Author

Ulsamer, A. (*fl*. 1895), German high school teacher at Dillingen. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Ulsamer* \[standard form in IPNI: *Ulsamer*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Note

Not to be confused with Johann Alfred Ulsamer, teacher at Kempten, author of e.g. *Unsere essbaren Pilze* (Schwämme), Kempten (Verlag der Jos. Kösel'schen Buchhandlung) 1896. Oct. (*copy*: FH).

#### Bibliography and biography
