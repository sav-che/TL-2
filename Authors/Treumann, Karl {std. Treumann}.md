### Author

Treumann, Karl (*fl*. 1880), Baltic-German botanist; Mag. pharm. Dorpat 1880, later at St. Petersburg. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Treumann* \[standard form in IPNI: *Treumann*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
