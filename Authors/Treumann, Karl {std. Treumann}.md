### Author

Treumann, Karl (*fl*. 1880), Baltic-German botanist; Mag. pharm. Dorpat 1880, later at St. Petersburg. (*Treumann*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Jackson p. 494; Rehder 3: 333; Tucker 1: 699.
