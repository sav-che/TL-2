### Author

Zigra, Johannes Hermann (1775-1857), German (Lübeck) botanist and horticulturist at Riga. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Zigra*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown; some material at [[Collection MW|MW]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 541 (b. 19 Jul 1775, d. 5 Jan 1857); BM 5: 2391; Herder p. 382, 395; PR 10497, ed. 1: 11445-11453; Rehder 5: 940; TR 1626-1628; Tucker 1: 758.
