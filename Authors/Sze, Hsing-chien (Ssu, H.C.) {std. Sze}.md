### Author

Sze, Hsing-chien \[Ssu, H.C.\] (1902-x), Chinese paleobotanist at the Institute of Paleontology, Nanking. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Sze* \[standard form in IPNI: *H.C.Sze*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Inst. Paleont. Nanking.

#### Bibliography and biography

Andrews p. 338; IH 1 (ed. 3): 108, (ed. 4): 119, (ed. 5): 127; Roon p. 111.
