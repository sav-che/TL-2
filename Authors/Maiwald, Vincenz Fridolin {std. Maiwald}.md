### Author

Maiwald, Vincenz Fridolin (1862-?), Czech historian of botany; high school teacher at Braunau, Bohemia 
**Abbreviated name**: *Maiwald*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2:438; BJI 2: 110; BM 3: 1224; DTS 1: 1900, 6(4): 168; Futák-Domin p. 376; LS 16676-16677.

### Publications

##### n.5276. Die opizische Periode in der floristischen Erforschung

**Title**
Jahres-Bericht des öffentlichen Stift-Obergymnasiums der Benedictiner zu Braunau in Böhmen... 1901... I. *Die opizische Periode in der floristischen Erforschung* Böhmens... Braunau (Selbstverlag des Benedictinerstiftes...) 1901. Qu.

**Notes**
*Publ*.: 1901 ("am Schlusse des Schuljahres 1901"), p. \[1\]-120, followed by *Schulnachrichten*, p. \[i\]-xxxiv. *Copy*: WU. – To be cited from Jahres-Bericht... 1901. ÖbZ 63: 221 (1913) mentions a continuation, 1902, 83 p., not seen by us. Important historical publication, dealing with Philipp Maximilian Opiz (1787-1859) and his collaborators.
