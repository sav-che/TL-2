### Author

Camper, Peter (Petrus) (1722-1789), Dutch naturalist and palaeobiologist at Franeker and Groningen. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Camper* \[standard form in IPNI: *Camper*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection GRO|GRO]].
*Ref*.: AKL 1820(2): 115, 1839(1): 45 (library).
