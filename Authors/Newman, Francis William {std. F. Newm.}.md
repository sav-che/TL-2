### Author

Newman, Francis William (c. 1796-1859), Australian botanist; superintendent botanical garden, Hobart from 1845. 
**Abbreviated name**: *F. Newm.* \[standard form in IPNI: *F.Newman*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
