### Author

Secondat, Jean Baptiste baron de (1716-1796), French botanist. (*Secondat*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 4: 1889; De Toni 1: cxv; Dryander 3: 91, 207, 285, 322, 345, 350; Herder p. 22; LS 24570; PR 8568 (d. 17 Jun 1796), ed. 1: 9524-9525; Rehder 5: 790; Tucker 1: 643.
