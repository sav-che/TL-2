### Author

Lacroix, Louis-Sosthène Veyron de (1818-1864), French clergyman and botanist at Montmorillon, Saint-Romain-sur-Vienne, ultimately "Aumônier des Petites Soeurs des Pauvres" at Poitiers. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lacr.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Lacroix*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Lacroix left a herbarium of the Poitou region upon his death; the present location is unknown but Sayre (mss) mentions that his bryophytes are at [[Collection BM|BM]] (Schimper herb.). Some further material is at [[Collection MW|MW]].
