### Author

Didrichsen, Axel (fl. 1894-1905), Danish botanist, born at Copenhagen, mag. sc. bot. 1895, assistant at the "Statsanstalten Dansk Frøkontrol" from Nov 1904. 
**Abbreviated name**: *A. Didr.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
