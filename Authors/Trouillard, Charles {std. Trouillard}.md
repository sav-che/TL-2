### Author

Trouillard, Charles (1821-1888), French banker and amateur botanist at Saumur (Maine-et-Loire). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Trouillard* \[standard form in IPNI: *Trouill.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection ANGUC|ANGUC]]. – *Letters* to K. Keck at [[Collection UPS|UPS]]-UB.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 403 (b. 19 Feb 1821, d. 30 Apr 1889).
