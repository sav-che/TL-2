### Author

Corrêa da Serra, José Francisco (1751-1823), Portuguese botanist and clergyman. (*Corrêa*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown; manuscripts at the Royal Society, London; algae at [[Collection K|K]].
*Ref*.: Dickinson, Phycol. Bull. 1: 11. 1952.
