### Author

Newton, Isaac (1840-1906), British-Portuguese businessman and bryologist at Porto, Portugal. (*I. Newton*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection PO|PO]]; further material at [[Collection B|B]] (through Geheeb), [[Collection COI|COI]], [[Collection H|H]] and [[Collection L|L]].

#### Eponymy

Newtonia Baillon (1888) honors Sir Isaac Newton (1642-1727), English mathematician, philosopher and scientist; *Newtonia* O. Hoffmann (1892 & 1894) was named in honor of Francis Newton (1864-1909), British-born Portuguese plant collector in West Africa.
