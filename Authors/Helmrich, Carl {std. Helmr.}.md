### Author

Helmrich, Carl (1833-1868), German botanist from Silesia, practicing physician at Berlin. (*Helmr.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 2: 820; Kew 2: 640; PR 3949; Rehder 1: 384.
