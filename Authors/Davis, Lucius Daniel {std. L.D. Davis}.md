### Author

Davis, Lucius Daniel (1826-1900), Methodist Episcopal clergyman, born in Jerusalem, N.Y., later at New York, died 31 Oct 1900, Newport, RI. 
**Abbreviated name**: *L.D. Davis* \[standard form in IPNI: *L.D.Davis*\]

Unknown.
