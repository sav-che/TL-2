### Author

Madden, Edward (1805-1856) Irish-born British botanist; officer in the Bengal Army 1830-1849 
**Abbreviated name**: *Madden*

[[Collection K|K]]; other material at [[Collection CAL|CAL]], [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection OXF|OXF]].
