### Author

Alefeld, Friedrich Georg Christoph (1820-1872), German physician and botanist at Ober-Ramstadt. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Alef.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Alef.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection B|B]] (destroyed).
*Ref*.: IH 2: 30.
