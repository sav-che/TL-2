### Author

Czeczott, Hanna (1888-1982), Polish botanist, born at St. Petersburg, worked at Warszawa in taxonomy, plant-geography and palaeobotany. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Czeczott*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection KRAM|KRAM]](1000).
