### Author

Mori, Antonio (1847-1902), Italian botanist; acting director botanical garden of Pisa 1880-1881; director of the botanical garden of Alodena (1883-1902), professor of botany from 1888. 
**Abbreviated name**: *A. Mori*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MOD|MOD]] and [[Collection PAD|PAD]]; other material at [[Collection AK|AK]], [[Collection DBN|DBN]], [[Collection DOMO|DOMO]], [[Collection FI|FI]], [[Collection GB|GB]] and [[Collection MANCH|MANCH]].
