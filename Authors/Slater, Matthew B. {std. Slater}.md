### Author

Slater, Matthew B. (c. 1829-1918), British bryologist at Malton; friend of Richard Spruce. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Slater*

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection KGY|KGY]]. – *Letters* to F.F.G. Renauld at [[Collection PC|PC]]; other letters at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 286; BB p. 278; Desmond p. 563; Lenley p. 370; LS 24936.
