### Author

Vallentin, Elinor Frances (formerly Nichol; née Bertrand) (1873-1924), Falkland Islands-born British botanical artist; married Rupert Vallentin 23 Mar 1904; to Plymouth, England 1904. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Vallentin* \[standard form in IPNI: *Vallentin*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection K|K]]. – Some lichens at [[Collection M|M]]. – Drawings at [[Collection K|K]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 421 (b. 14 Jan 1873, d. 12 Mar 1924); BB p. 309; BL 1: 89, 322; Desmond p. 627 (b. 14 Jan 1873, d. 12 Mar 1924); NI 2038.

#### Biofile

- Bridson, G.D.R., Nat. hist. mss. res. Brit. Isl. 269.367. 1980.
- Frodin, D.G., Guide standard floras world no. 389. 1984.

#### Note

- (1) The plants collected by Mrs Vallentin in the Falklands were described by C.H. Wright, *Flora of the Falklands Islands*, J. Linn. Soc. 39: 313-339. 4 Jul 1911, and by A.L. Cotton, *Cryptogams from the Falkland Islands*... ib. 43: 137-143. 30 Nov 1915; the Fresh-water algae in Bull. Misc. Inf. Kew 1924: 283-287.
- (2) For *Rupert Vallentin* (1859-1934), husband of E.F. Vallentin see J.T. Cunningham, Proc. Linn. Soc. 147: 191-194. 13 Dec 1935.
