### Author

Stoltenberg, Nicolaus Theodor Marcus (1844-?), German high school teacher at Tondern \[now Tønder, Denmark\]. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Stoltenberg* \[standard form in IPNI: *Stoltenb.*\]
