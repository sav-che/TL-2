### Author

Jack, Joseph Bernhard (1818-1901), German pharmacist at Salem in Baden (1848-1874); later retired at Konstanz; Dr. h.c. Freiburg 1898. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. B. Jack* \[standard form in IPNI: *J.B.Jack*\]
