### Author

Gaertner, Philipp Gottfried (1754-1825), German botanist, apothecary at Hanau. 
**Abbreviated name**: *P. Gaertn.* \[standard form in IPNI: *G.Gaertn.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection W|W]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 218.
