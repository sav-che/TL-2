### Author

Degen, Arpád von (1866-1934), Hungarian botanist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Degen* \[standard form in IPNI: *Degen*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BP|BP]] – duplicates see IH. – Exsiccata: *Gramina hungarica* (fasc. 1-7, 1900-1911, 350 nos.), e.g. at [[Collection B|B]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 156.
