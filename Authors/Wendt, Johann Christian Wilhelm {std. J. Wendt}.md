### Author

Wendt, Johann Christian Wilhelm (1778-1838), Danish physician and botanist; med. exam. 1804; teacher at the Copenhagen Veterinary College 1802-1806; subsequently military physician; chief physician in the Copenhagen general hospital 1814-1838. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. Wendt*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 476; BM 5: 2292; Bossert p. 431; KR p. 753-754 (b. 16 Sep 1778); TL-2/11.393.

#### Eponymy

*Wendtia* Meyen (1834, *nom. cons.*) honors Captain W. Wendt who was the commander of the ship during Meyen's (*q.v.*) trip around the world. The derivation of *Wendia* G.F. Hoffmann (1814, *nom. rej.*) could not be determined.
