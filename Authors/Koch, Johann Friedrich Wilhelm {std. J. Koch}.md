### Author

Koch, Johann Friedrich Wilhelm (1759-1831), German botanist and "Consistorialrath" in Magdeburg. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. Koch* \[standard form in IPNI: *Koch*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
