### Author

Burgeff, Hans Edmund Nicola (1883-x), German botanist, professor of botany in Halle, München, Göttingen and Würzburg. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Burgeff* \[standard form in IPNI: *Burgeff*\]

#### Herbarium and types
