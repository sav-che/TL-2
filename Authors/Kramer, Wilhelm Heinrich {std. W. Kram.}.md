### Author

Kramer, Wilhelm Heinrich (x-1765), German-born Austrian botanist and physician at Bruck a. Leitha. 
**Abbreviated name**: *W. Kram.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Kramer*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
