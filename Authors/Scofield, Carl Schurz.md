### Author

Scofield, Carl Schurz (1875-1965), American agricultural botanist; B.S. Univ. Minnesota 1900; with the USDA in various functions 1900-1945. 
**Abbreviated name**: *C. Scofield* \[standard form in IPNI: *Scofield*\]
