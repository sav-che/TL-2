### Author

Rivas Mateos, Marcelo (1875-1931), Spanish botanist; professor of botany at the Fac. de Farmacia, Barcelona and Madrid. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Riv. Mat.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Riv.Mateos*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MAF|MAF]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 160 (b. 16 Jan 1875); BL 2: 493, 494, 502, 709; BM 4: 1709; CSP 18: 228; GR p. 763-764 (b. 16 Jan 1875, d. 25 Jan 1931); Hirsch p. 249; IH 2(files); Rehder 1: 423.
