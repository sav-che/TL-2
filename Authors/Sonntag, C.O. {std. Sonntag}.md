### Author

Sonntag, C.O. (*fl*. 1894), Scottish high school teacher and botanist at Edinburgh. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Sonntag*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BL 2: 307, 719; Rehder 1: 535.

**Abbreviated title**: *Pocket fl. Edinburgh*.
