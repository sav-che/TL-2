### Author

Gilkinet, \[Charles\] Alfred (1845-1926), Belgian mycologist and paleobotanist, born 21 Mai 1845 in Ensival, Dr. en sciences naturelles, prof. of pharmacy, Liége Univ. 1884, died 30 Sep 1926. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Gilkinet*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BR|BR]].
