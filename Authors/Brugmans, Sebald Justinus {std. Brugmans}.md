### Author

Brugmans, Sebald Justinus (1763-1819), Dutch botanist, professor of botany at Franeker and Leiden. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Brugmans* \[standard form in IPNI: *Brugmans*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – Zool. coll. in Mus. Gesch. Natuurw., Leiden.
