### Author

Sloboda, Daniel (1809-1888), Slovak botanist and clergyman, priest at Rottalovitz (Moravia), floristically active in Moravia. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Sloboda* \[standard form in IPNI: *Sloboda*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BRNM|BRNM]].

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 4: 1940; Futak-Domin p. 546; Klásterský p. 173-174 (detailed refs., b. 20 Dec 1809 in Skalica, Slovakia; d. 10 Nov 1888); Maiwald p. 149; PR 8724; Rehder 1: 269.
