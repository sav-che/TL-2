### Author

Burtt, Arthur H. (fl. 1899), English botanist from Darlington, studied at the Eberhard-Karls-Universität in Tübingen, Dr. Phil. ib. 1899. 
**Abbreviated name**: *A.H. Burtt*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
