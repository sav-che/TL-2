### Author

Turton, William (1762-1835), British physician and naturalist (especially conchologist); practicing physician in Swansea, Dublin, Torquay and Bideford. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Turton* \[standard form in IPNI: *Turton*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
