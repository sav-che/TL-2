### Author

Rooke, Hayman (c. 1722-1806), British (English) botanical artist and soldier, of Whitehaven, Cumberland. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Rooke* \[standard form in IPNI: *Rooke*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
