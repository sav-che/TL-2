### Author

Bruch, Carl \[Karl\] Ludwig (fl. 1758), German physician from Bergzabern, Pfalz, studied at the University of Strasbourg, Dr. med. ib. 22 Mai 1758. 
**Abbreviated name**: *C.L. Bruch*
