### Author

Gentil, Ambroise \[François\] (1842-1927), French botanist and zoologist, born 8 Apr 1842 in Saint-Macaire-du-Bois (Maine-et-Loire), prof. of natural sciences at Le Mans Lycée 1886-1906, died 13 Jul 1927 in Le Mans. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Gentil* \[standard form in IPNI: *Gentil*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BR|BR]] (Rosaceae). In addition, Le Musée Vert-Véron de Forbonnais, Le Mans, has a 30 vol. herb. assembled by Gentil.

#### Bibliography and biography

APN p. 223 (d. "1929"); Barnhart 2: 38 (d. "1929"); BL 2: 180, 197, 658; BM 2: 655; CSP 9: 986, 15: 259; DBF 15: 1078; IH 2: 221 (d. "1929"); Kew 2: 395; MW p. 136; Plesch p. 235; Portr. 3: 472; Rehder 5(index) 315; Tucker 1: 274, 3: 117; Zander ed. 17: 949 (d. "1929").
