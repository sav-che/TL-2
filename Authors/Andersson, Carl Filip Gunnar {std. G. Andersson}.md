### Author

Andersson, Carl Filip Gunnar (1865-1928), Swedish bryologist, palaeobotanist and plant-geographer at Stockholm. 
**Abbreviated name**: *C. Andersson* \[standard form in IPNI: *G.Andersson*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection GB|GB]] (all material?).
*Ref*.: IH 2: 36.
