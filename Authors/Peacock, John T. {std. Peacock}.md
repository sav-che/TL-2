### Author

Peacock, John T. (*fl*. 1878), British country gentleman of Sudbury House, Hammersmith; collector of succulent plants. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Peacock* \[standard form in IPNI: *Peacock*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Desmond p. 484; Tucker 1: 544.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Bull. misc. Inf. Kew 1897: 232 (living plants from Peacock's collections were purchased by Kew in 1889).
