### Author

Seemann, Wilhelm Eduard Gottfried (x-1868), German (Hannover) botanist, geologist and editor; brother of B.C. Seemann. 
**Abbreviated name**: *W. Seem.* \[standard form in IPNI: *W.Seem.*\]
