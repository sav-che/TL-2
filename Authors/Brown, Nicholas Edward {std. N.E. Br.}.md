### Author

Brown, Nicholas Edward (1849-1934), British botanist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *N. E. Brown* \[standard form in IPNI: *N.E.Br.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

Many contributions to Oliver, *Fl. trop. Afr.*, listed under Oliver.
