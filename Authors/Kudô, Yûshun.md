### Author

Kudô, Yûshun (1887-1932), Japanese botanist; at Hokkaido University with Miyabe; later at Taihoku Imperial University. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Kudô* \[standard form in IPNI: *Kudô*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Material at [[Collection A|A]], [[Collection MO|MO]] and [[Collection SAP|SAP]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 324; BJI 2: 98; Hirsch p. 163; Hortus 3: 1197 (Kudo"); IH 2: 393; Kew 3: 323; MW p. 253-254, suppl. p. 184-185; Zander ed. 10, p. 681.
- Hibino, S., Trans. Nat. Hist. Soc. Formosa 22 (no. 123): 399-405. 1932 (portr., bibl.).
- Masamune, G., Bot. Mag., Tokyo 46: 439-441. 1932 (portr., bibl.).
- Miyabe, K., Trans. Sapporo nat. Hist. Soc. 12(2-3), 1932 (Japanese; n.v.).

#### Composite works

with K. Miyabe (q.v.), *Flora of Hokkaido and Saghalien*, vol. 1, 1930-1934, preceded by *Materials for a flora of Hokkaido*, 11 parts, 1913-1924.

#### Eponymy

(genera): *Kudoa* Masamune (1930); *Kudoacanthus* Hosokawa (1933); (journal): *Kudoa*. Taihoku (= Taipei, Taiwan). Vol. 1-5. 1933-1937.
