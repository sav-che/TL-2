### Author

Palanza, Alfonso (1851-1899), Italian botanist and plant collector; sometime private teacher at Napoli; from 1894 high school teacher at the Liceo di Bitonto. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Palanza* \[standard form in IPNI: *Palanza*\]

#### Herbarium and types

A general and a local (Bari) herbarium were acquired by the province of Bari for its technical institute. We do not know whether this collection was later transferred to [[Collection BI|BI]]. – Some material at [[Collection FI|FI]] (inf. C. Steinberg). – Some letters at [[Collection G|G]].
