### Author

Chandler, Marjorie Elizabeth Jane (1897-x), British botanist and palaeobotanist, born 18 Mai 1897, at British Museum (Natural History) London. 
**Abbreviated name**: *M. Chandler*

#### Herbarium and types

Material at [[Collection BM|BM]].
