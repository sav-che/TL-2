### Author

Schulze-Menz, Georg Karl Wilhelm (1908-1978), German (Prussian) botanist; studied at the University of Berlin 1928-1935; Dr. phil. 1936; temporary scientific assistant at the Botanical Museum and Garden, Berlin-Dahlem, 1934-1935; with the Bot. Staatsanstalten M端nchen 1935-1936; again in Berlin-Dahlem 1936; regular assistant 1940, in active service and prisoner-of-war 1941-1948; scientific officer at the office of plant conservation, Berlin 1948-1952; from 1952 again with Berlin-Dahlem as scientific officer 1952, curator 1953, divisional superintendent 1962, head curator 1968, director 1972-1973, also as professor of botany; specialist on Rosales. (Schulze-Menz as of 10 Sep 1938);
