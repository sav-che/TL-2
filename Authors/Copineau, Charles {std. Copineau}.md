### Author

Copineau, Charles (fl. 1866-1898), French judge at the civil court, member of the Société botanique de France, living at Doullens (Somme). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Copineau*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MPU|MPU]], [[Collection US|US]].
