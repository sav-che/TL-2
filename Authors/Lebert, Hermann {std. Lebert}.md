### Author

Lebert, Hermann (1813-1878), German botanist and naturalist; Dr. med. Zürich 1834; active in Lavey, Paris, Zürich (1855), Breslau (1859), Vevey (1875). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lebert*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
