### Author

Olin, Johan Henric (1769-1824), Swedish botanist; Dr. phil. Uppsala 1794, Dr. med. ib. 1797; amanuensis at the Uppsala bot. museum, later regional physician in Växjö distr., professor's title 1812. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Olin* \[standard form in IPNI: *Olin*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 27; Hortus 3: 1201 ("Olin"); KR p. 564; PR 6826 (ed. 1: 7629-7630); Rehder 5: 633.
- Löwegren, Y., Naturaliekabinett i Sverige 223, 359. 1952 (had a herbarium).
- Scheutz, N. J. W., Bot. Not. 1863: 70.

#### Eponymy

*Olinia*Thunberg (1800, *nom. cons.*).
