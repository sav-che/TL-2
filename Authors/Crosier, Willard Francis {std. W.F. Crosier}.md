### Author

Crosier, Willard Francis (1904-x), American plant pathologist, born at Junista, Nebraska, studied at the University of Kansas A.B. 1927, Ph.D. 1932 at Cornell, laboratory assistant in botany at Univ. of Kansas 1925-1927, science assistant in botany 1927-1932, instructor in botany, assistant at New York Experiment Station at Geneva 1932, from 1934 assistant professor at Cornell University. 
**Abbreviated name**: *W.F. Crosier*

Unknown.
