### Author

Muehlenbeck, Heinrich Gustav (1798-1845), Alsatian botanist and physician; Dr. med. Paris; practicing physician in Gebweiler 1822-1833; from then on at Mulhouse (Mühlhausen); friend of W. P. Schimper. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Muehlenbeck*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection STR|STR]]; some further material at [[Collection B|B]], [[Collection K|K]], and [[Collection MW|MW]]. Many specimens at [[Collection STR|STR]] labelled *Lichens des Pyrenées. –* 159 letters to and from J. B. Mougeot at [[Collection P|P]] (Bibl. centr.).
