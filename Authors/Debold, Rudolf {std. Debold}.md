### Author

Debold, Rudolf (fl. 1892), German pharmacist, born at Offenburg (Baden), studied at the University of Munich with Radlkofer and Solereder. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Debold*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
