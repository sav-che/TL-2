### Author

Ruppius, (Rupp), Heinrich Bernard (1688-1719), German (Thuringian) botanist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Ruppius* \[standard form in IPNI: *Ruppius*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
