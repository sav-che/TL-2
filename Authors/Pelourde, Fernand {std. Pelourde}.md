### Author

Pelourde, Fernand (1884-1916), French palaeobotanist; Dr. phil. Paris 1907; "préparateur" at the Muséum d'Histoire naturelle, Paris (*Pelourde*).

#### Herbarium and types

Palaeobotanical types at Mus. Hist. nat., Paris.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 65 (b. 7 Jul 1884, d. 16 Feb 1916).

#### Biofile

- Mangin, L., Bull. Mus. Hist. nat. Paris 1916(2): 66-68 (obit., b. 7 Jul 1884, d. 16 Feb 1916, bibl.).

#### Eponymy

*Pelourdea* A.C. Seward (1917).
