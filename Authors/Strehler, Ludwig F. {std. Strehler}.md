### Author

Strehler, Ludwig F. (*fl*. 1841), German (Bavarian) botanist and high school teacher at the "Landwirthschafts- und Gewerbs-Schule" at Ingolstadt. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Strehler*

#### Herbarium and types

Original herbarium at Ingolstadt; Agricultural and Technical College; present location not known to us.

#### Bibliography and biography

PR 9001, ed. 1: 9955-9956; Rehder 1: 380; Tucker 1: 671.

#### Biofile

- Ilg, W., Hoppea 42: 344. 1984.
