### Author

Cajander, (Kalela), Aarno (Aino Aarno Antero), Finnish botanist after 1935 Aarno Kalela (1908-1977). 
**Abbreviated name**: *A. Cajander*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
