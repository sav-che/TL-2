### Author

Desrousseaux, Louis Auguste Joseph (1753-1838), French cloth manufacturer and botanist at Sedan, born there 27 Jul 17553, died 20 Jan 1838 at Vendières, Marne, member of the Linnean Society at Paris from 1790. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Desr.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Desr.*\]

Unknown, if any at [[Collection P|P]]-LA.

#### Composite works

Desrousseaux was collaborator with Lamarck and Poiret for the *Encyclopédie méthodique*. *Botanique*. Vol. 3 from P- is by Lamarck and Desrousseaux and Vol. 4 is by Desrousseaux, Poiret and Savigny. – See also TL-2/2: no. 4136.
