### Author

Westhoff, Friedrich \[Fritz\] (1867-1896), German (Westphalian) entomologist and botanist; lecturer ("Privatdocent") at the University of Münster; Dr. phil. ib. 1882. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Westh.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MSTR|MSTR]].
