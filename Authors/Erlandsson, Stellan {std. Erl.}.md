### Author

Erlandsson, Stellan (1902-x), Swedish botanist, cytologist and diatomist, born 1902, Ph.D. at Uppsala in 1936. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Erl.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Erl.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
