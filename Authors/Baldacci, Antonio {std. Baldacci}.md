### Author

Baldacci, Antonio (1867-1950), Italian botanist, collected on the Balcan and in Greece. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Baldacci*

#### Herbarium and types

Material in many herbaria, especially [[Collection FI|FI]], see IH.
*Ref*.: IH 2: 51.
