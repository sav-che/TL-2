### Author

Monckton, Horace Woollaston (1857-1931), British barrister, botanist and geologist in Berkshire; treasurer of the Linnean Society 1905-1930. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Monckton* \[standard form in IPNI: *Monckton*\]
