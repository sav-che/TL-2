### Author

Roth, Wilhelm (1819\[?\]-1875), German (Silesian) botanist and "Webermeister" \[weaver\] at Langenbielau in Silezia. 
**Abbreviated name**: *W. Roth* \[standard form in IPNI: *W.Roth*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection WRSL|WRSL]]; European material collected by a "W. Roth" is at [[Collection BR|BR]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Herder p. 188; IH 2: (files); Jackson p. 310; LS 22955. Pax, F., Bibl. schles. Bot. 109. 1929.
