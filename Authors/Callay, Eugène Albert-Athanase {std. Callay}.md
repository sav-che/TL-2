### Author

Callay, Eugène Albert-Athanase (1822-1896), French pharmacist and botanist at Le Chesne. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Callay* \[standard form in IPNI: *Callay*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection L|L]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 112.
