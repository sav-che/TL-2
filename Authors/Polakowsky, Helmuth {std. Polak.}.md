### Author

Polakowsky, Helmuth (1847-1917), German botanist; until 1880 in Berlin. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Polak.*

#### Herbarium and types

material from Costa Rica at [[Collection B|B]] (mainly destroyed), [[Collection BM|BM]], [[Collection C|C]], [[Collection GOET|GOET]], [[Collection L|L]], [[Collection QK|QK]], [[Collection W|W]] (301), [[Collection WRSL|WRSL]] (lich.). Bryophyte types were in herb. Müller ([[Collection B|B]], destroyed).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 96; BL 1: 147, 313; CSP 11: 41, 12: 582, 17: 947-948; GR p. 121; Herder p. 225; Hortus 3: 1201 (Polak.); Kew 4: 322; LS 21054-21054a; Morren ed. 10, p. 7; Rehder 5: 681; SBC p. 129; Tucker 1: 563; Urban-Berl. p. 268, 280, 289, 310, 384.

*Polakowskia* Pittier (1910).
