### Author

Leuduger-Fortmorel, Georges (1830-1902), French physician and diatomologist; ultimately at Saint-Brieuc. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Leud.-Fortm.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Leud.-Fortm.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 373; BM 3: 1099; CSP 12: 443, 16: 843; De Toni 1: lxxviii, 2: lxxv-lxxvi, xcv; Jackson p. 184, 282, 396.
- Cupp, E. E., Mar. plankton diatoms W. Coast N. Amer. 217. 1943.
- Deby, J., Bibl. microsc. microgr. stud. 3(Diat.): 37. 1882.
- Petit, P., Notice nécrologique sur Leuduger-Fortmorel (Diatomophile). Padova 1903, 3 p. (Nuova Notarisia 14: 62-64, 94- 1903).

#### Eponymy

*Leudugeria* Tempère ex Van Heurck (1896).
