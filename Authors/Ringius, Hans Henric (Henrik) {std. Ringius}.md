### Author

Ringius, Hans Henric \[Henrik\] (1808-1874), Swedish clergyman and botanist in Skåne; Dr. phil. Lund 1838; ultimately rector at Silvåkra and Revinge. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Ringius* \[standard form in IPNI: *Ringius*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection C|C]], [[Collection GB|GB]], and [[Collection GH|GH]]. – Issued sets of exsiccatae: *Herbarium normale plantarum rariorum et criticarum Sueciae*, fasc. 1-2, Lund 1835-1836, continued by E.M. Fries; for sets see under Fries.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 158; BM 4: 1705; KR p. 590 (b. 30 Mar 1808, d. 28 Jun 1874); PR 7633, ed. 1: 8590; TL-2/see E.M. Fries.
