### Author

Cazin, C. E. (fl. 1859), French pharmacist, general secretary of the society for pharmaceutical sciences, member of the chemical society and several other societies. 
**Abbreviated name**: *C.-E. Cazin*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
