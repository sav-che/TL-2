### Author

Zohary, (né Schein) Michael (1898-1983), Polish (Galicia)-born Israeli botanist; migrated to Palestine 1920; Dr.phil. Praha 1936; with the Hebrew University 1926-1967; in retirement continuing his botanical work. (*Zoh.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection HUJ|HUJ]]. – Further material in [[Collection BM|BM]], [[Collection C|C]], [[Collection CAIM|CAIM]], [[Collection GB|GB]], [[Collection GH|GH]]. – *Exsiccatae*: (with N. Feinbrun) *Flora exsiccata Palestinae. – Letters* at [[Collection G|G]].
