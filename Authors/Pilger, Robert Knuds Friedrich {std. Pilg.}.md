### Author

Pilger, Robert Knuds Friedrich (1876-1953), German botanist; Dr. phil. Berlin (with Engler) 1898; collected in Brazil 1898-1900; assistant at Berlin-Dahlem 1900; Custos 1908; habil. Berlin 1908; titular professor 1913, vice-director 1921, director 1945-1950. (*Pilg.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection B|B]] (partly destroyed; Matto Grosso material extant); further material at [[Collection BM|BM]], [[Collection NY|NY]], [[Collection P|P]], [[Collection POM|POM]], [[Collection RB|RB]], [[Collection VT|VT]]. – Letters and portrait at [[Collection G|G]].
