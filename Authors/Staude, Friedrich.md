### Author

Staude, Friedrich (x-1861), German (Coburg) physician and botanist; town-physician and asst. director of the natural history cabinet at Coburg. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Staude* \[standard form in IPNI: *Staude*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 318 (d. 26 Aug 1861); BM 5: 2004; LS 25705; PR 8907.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Bonplandia 9: 248. 1861 (d.); Österr. bot. Z. 11: 409. 1861 (d.).

### Publications

##### n.12.834. Die Schwämme Mitteldeutschlands

**Title**
*Die Schwämme Mitteldeutschlands*, insbesondere des Herzogthums Coburg... Coburg (Druck der Dietz'schen Hofbuchdruckerei) 1857. Fol.
**Abbreviated title**: *Schwämme Mitteldeutschl.*

**Notes**
*Publ*.: 1857, p. \[i\*\], \[i\]-xxviii, col. 1-150, *pl. 1-10* (col. liths.). *Copies*: FH, MICH, Stevenson. – Festgabe für die Mitglieder der xix. Versammlung deutscher Land- und Forstwirthe in Coburg 1857. – From the review by Schlechtendal it is clear that the book was reissued in 1858 as *Erste Lieferung* of a major work, which, however, was not continued. See Donk (1954) for the standing (validly published) of the generic names for Agarics.
*Ref*.: Donk, M.A., Reinwardtia 2(3): 495-498. 1954 (generic names for agarics first introduced by Staude are validly published).
