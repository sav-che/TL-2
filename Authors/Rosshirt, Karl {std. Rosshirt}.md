### Author

Rosshirt, Karl (*fl*. 1888), German botanist and high school teacher at Colmar (Elsass; Alsace). (*Rosshirt*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BL 2: 158, 710.
