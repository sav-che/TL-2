### Author

Adams, George Jr. (1750-1795), London optician and microscopist. (*G. Adams, Jr.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 13 (d. 14 Aug 1795); BB p. 2; BM 1: 10; Jackson p. 219; PR 18.
