### Author

Lidbeck, Eric Gustaf (1724-1803), Swedish botanist at Lund; Dr. phil. Uppsala 1749; adjunct nat. hist. Lund 1750, curator ("prefekt") of the botanical garden 1752; professor of natural history at Lund University 1756-1795. (*Lidb.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection SBT|SBT]].

#### Note

L's son Anders Lidbeck (b. 26 Jul 1772, Lund; d. 11 Mai 1829, Stockholm) published e.g. a *Dissertatio de plantis, in suecorum memoriam nominatis*. Diss.... 22 Dec 1792, Lund. For further details see KR p. 388-389.
