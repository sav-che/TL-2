### Author

Kol, Erszébet \[Elizabet\] (1897-x), Hungarian botanist at the Budapest Museum of Narural History; interested in cryobiology; Dr. phil. Szeged 1925. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Kol*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BP|BP]]; other material at [[Collection E|E]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 311; Bossert p. 256; IH 2: 377; Kew 3: 293; NAF ser. 2. 6: 67; Roon p. 62.
- Hultén, E., Bot. Not. 1940: 342.
- Kol, E., Personal communication to senior author 1957 (inch bibl.); a complete mss. bibliography compiled by P. Komáromy Zsuzsa was received in February 1978 and is now in the TL-2 archives. When closing the manuscript for this volume Dr. Kol was working on Hungarian Desmidiaceae.

#### Eponymy

*Koliella* F. Hindák (1963).
