### Author

Anderson, Thomas (1832-1870), Scottish botanist, superintendent and later director of the Calcutta Botanic Garden. 
**Abbreviated name**: *T. Anderson*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection K|K]] and [[Collection CAL|CAL]]; duplicates e.g. [[Collection B|B]], [[Collection BM|BM]], [[Collection L|L]].
*Ref*.: IH 1 (ed. 6): 355, 2: 36.
- Candolle, Phytographie 392. 1880.
