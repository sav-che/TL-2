### Author

Stahlecker, Eugen (1867-?), German (Württemberg) botanist and high school teacher at Korntal; student of L.M. Fünfstück. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Stahlecker* \[standard form in IPNI: *Stahlecker*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 315 (b. 22 Mai 1867); GR p. 140; LS 25662, 39472.
