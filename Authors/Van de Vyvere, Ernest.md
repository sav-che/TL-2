### Author

Van de Vyvere, Ernest (1811-1853), Belgian pharmacist and botanist at Brugge (Bruges). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Van de Vyvere* \[standard form in IPNI: *Van de Vyvere*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BR|BR]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 423 (b. 10 Mai 1811, d. 24 Jun 1853); BL 2: 37, 726.

- Crépin, F., Manuel p. lxix. 1860, Guide bot. Belgique 251, 481. 1878.
