### Author

Woynar, Heinrich Karl (1865-1917), Austrian botanist; studied pharmacy at the University of Innsbruck 1887-1889; from 1900 living at Graz as private scientist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Woyn.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection GZU|GZU]]. – Further material in [[Collection GB|GB]], [[Collection GJO|GJO]], [[Collection STU|STU]]. — *Letters* at [[Collection UPS|UPS]]-UB.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 522; Hortus 3: 1207; IF suppl. 2: 39; Zander ed. 10, p. 734, ed. 11, p. 838.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Österr. bot. Z. 67: 1184. 1918 (d. 7 Aug 1917).
- Widder, F.J. & H. Teppner, Phyton 20(3-4): 207-213. 1980 (biogr., bibl., coll., b. 3 Jun 1865, d. 8 \[not 7\] Aug 1917).

#### Note

For H.K. Woynar's father, Johann Woynar (1829-1900), practicing pharmacist at Innsbruck, who also published on the flora of Tirol, see Widder & Teppner (1980). — Collections in GZU (and B, herb. Ullepitsch, E, GOET); letters e.g. at UPS-UB.

#### Handwriting

Phyton 20: 212. 1980.
