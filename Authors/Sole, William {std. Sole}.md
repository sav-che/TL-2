### Author

##### n.12.353. Menthae britannicae

**Title**
*Menthae britannicae*: being a new botanical arrangement of all the British mints hitherto discovered. Illustrated with twenty-four copper-plates, of the natural size, done from the life by able artists; exhibiting a figure of every distinct mint herein treated of, including all those enumerated by Ray and Hudson; together with several new species hitherto unnoticed. By W. Sole. Bath (printed by R. Cruttwell;...) 1798. Qu. (in twos).
