### Author

Merrill, George Knox (1864-1927), American lichenologist, journalist and photographer; active at Rockland, Maine were he kept an art studio. 
**Abbreviated name**: *G. Merr.* \[standard form in IPNI: *G.Merr.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection FH|FH]] (50.000).
*Exsiccatae*: *Lichenes exsiccati*, fasc. 1-11, nos. 1-275 (1909-1913), ser. 2, fasc. 1-6, nos. 1-150, (1925-1927); for sets see IH, for dates etc. see Sayre and Stevenson.
