### Author

Thayer, Emma, née Homan (1842-1908), American artist, educated at Rutgers University and the National Academy of Design; in Colorado from 1882; married George A. Graves 1860 (d. 1864), Elmer A. Thayer 1877. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Thayer* \[standard form in IPNI: *Thayer*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Ewan ed. 1: p. 320, ed. 2: 218; NI 1947n, na; Rehder 1: 325; Tucker 1: 683.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Who's who Amer. 2-3, 1901-1905; Who was who Amer. 1: 1225. 1968.

#### Eponymy

*Thayeria* Copeland (1906) honors Alfred Thayer, a professor at the University of Texas, U.S.A.
