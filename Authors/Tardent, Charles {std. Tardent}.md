### Author

Tardent, Charles (*fl*. 1841), Swiss naturalist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Tardent* \[standard form in IPNI: *Tardent*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – *Letters* at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 5: 2072; Hortus 3: 1205; LS 26301; PR 9097, ed. 1: 10046; Rehder 1: 361; TR 1399.

#### Note

Henri Alexis Tardent (1854-1929), Swiss (canton de Vaud) scientist and journalist; spent some time in a "Swiss agricultural colony in Southern Russia" before emigrating to Queensland, Australia where he established a winery at Roma, and subsequently became director of an experimental state farm. Tardent published mainly on agricultural botany and forestry as well as on many topics outside botany. (See HR and Kelly p. 225). The possible relationship to Charles Tardent is not known to us.
