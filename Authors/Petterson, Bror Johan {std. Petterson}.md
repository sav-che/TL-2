### Author

Petterson, Bror Johan (1895-x), Finnish botanist, curator at the Botanical Museum, University of Helsinki 1959-1965; travelled in the Canary Islands 1947, 1949, 1951 and Sudan & Egypt 1962. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Petterson*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection H|H]]; early collections destroyed during World War II.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 76; BL 2: 65, 85, 456-702; Collander p. 413-414.

#### Biofile

- Jalas, J., Luonnon Tutkija 69: 189-190. 1965 (portr., biogr. sketch).
