### Author

Christ, Konrad Hermann Heinrich (1833-1933), Swiss pteridologist and plant geographer, professor of botany at Basel. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Christ* \[standard form in IPNI: *Christ*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BAS|BAS]].
*Ref*.: IH 1 (ed. 6): 357, 2: 126.
