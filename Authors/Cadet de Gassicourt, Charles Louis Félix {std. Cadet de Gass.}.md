### Author

Cadet de Gassicourt, Charles Louis Félix (1789-1861), French botanist, living in Paris. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Cadet de Gass.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 295 (b. 11 Oct 1789, Paris, d. Dec 1861 ib.); BL 1: 252; CSP 1: 753; PR p. 50, ed. 1: 1608-1609; Rehder 5(index): 143; TL-2/4: no. 9741a; Tucker 1: 130.
