### Author

Linden, Lucien (1851-1940), Belgian horticulturist, son of J. J. Linden, 
**Abbreviated name**: *L. Linden*

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection K|K]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Backer p. 656 (b. 1865); BM 3: 1118; IH 2: 446.

- (2). Editor, *L'Illustration horticole* 28-43, 1881-1896.
