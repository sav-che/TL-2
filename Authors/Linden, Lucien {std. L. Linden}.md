### Author

Linden, Lucien (1851-1940), Belgian horticulturist, son of J. J. Linden, (*L. Linden*).

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection K|K]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Backer p. 656 (b. 1865); BM 3: 1118; IH 2: 446.
- Jackson, B. D., Bull. misc. Inf. Kew 1901: 40.

#### Composite works

(1), Editor of *Lindenia*, Iconographie des Orchidées (with E. Rodigas), vols. 1-17, 1855-1903 (NI 2348).
(2). Editor, *L'Illustration horticole* 28-43, 1881-1896.
