### Author

Kirillow, Peter (*fl*. 1849), Russian botanist and physician at Charkow. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Kirillow*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MW|MW]].
*Ref*.: Lindemann, Bull. Soc. Natural. Moscou 59(1): 311. 1884.
