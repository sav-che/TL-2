### Author

Lesson, Adolphe Pierre (1805-1888), French explorer; with Dumont d'Urville on the voyage in *L'Astrolabe* (1826-1829); brother of R. P. Lesson. 
**Abbreviated name**: *A. Lesson* \[standard form in IPNI: *A.Lesson*\]
