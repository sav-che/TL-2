### Author

Stampfer, Cölestin (1823-1895), Austrian botanist; Benedictine clergyman and high school teacher at the Gymnasium of Meran (Merano). (*Stampfer*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 315 (b. 3 Sep 1823, d. 19 Jan 1895); DTS 1: 286-287; WU 37: 125-126.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Brit. Mus. gen. cat. print. books 228: 411. 1964; Cat. gén. livr. impr. Bibl. natl. 177: 146. 1950.
