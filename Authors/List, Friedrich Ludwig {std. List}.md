### Author

List, Friedrich Ludwig (fl. 1837), German botanist and high school teacher at Tilsit, Western Prussia. 
**Abbreviated name**: *List* \[standard form in IPNI: *List*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection KBG|KBG]] (destroyed).

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 3: 1154; PR 5542-5543 (ed. 1: 6189-6190); Rehder 5: 517.
- Candolle, Alph. de, Phytographie 429. 1880.

#### Eponymy

*Listia* E. H. F. Meyer (1836).
