### Author

Ravaud, Louis Célestine Mure, abbé (1822-1898), French clergyman and botanist; educated, later teacher, at the Dauphiné seminary of Rondeau; ultimately "curéarchiprêtre" at Villars-de-Lans (Isère). (*Ravaud*).
