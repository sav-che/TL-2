### Author

Mudd, William (A.) (1830-1879), British gardener and lichenologist; at first at Great Ayton, Cumb.; later (1865) curator of the Cambridge botanic garden. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Mudd* \[standard form in IPNI: *Mudd*\]
