### Author

Buckland, William (1784-1856), British geologist, dean of Westminster. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Buckland* \[standard form in IPNI: *Buckland*\]

#### Collections

BM and OXF (*Reliq. diliv.* types).
*Ref*.: Sherborn, Where is the... collection 27. 1941.
