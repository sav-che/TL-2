### Author

Stenfort, F. (*fl*. 1874), French botanist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Stenfort* \[standard form in IPNI: *Stenfort*\]

#### Herbarium and types

*Exsiccatae* see below *Les* \[*plus belles*\] *plantes de la mer*.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 5: 2014.

**Title**
