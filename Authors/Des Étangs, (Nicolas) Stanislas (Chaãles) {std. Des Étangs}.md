### Author

Des Étangs, \[Nicolas\] Stanislas \[Chaãles\] (1801-1876), French botanist and justice of the peace at Bar-sur-Aube, born 23 trim. an x (14 Dec 1801) at Andelot, died 10 Dec 1876, Paris. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Des Étangs*
