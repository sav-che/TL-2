### Author

Uexküll-Gyllenbrand, Margarete von (*fl*. 1901), Baltic-German (Latvian) botanist; Dr. phil. Zürich 1901; student of C. Schröter. (*Uexküll*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 413; BM 5: 2160.
