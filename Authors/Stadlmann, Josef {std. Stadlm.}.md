### Author

Stadlmann, Josef (1881-x), Austrian botanist and high school teacher; Dr. phil. Wien 1907, Student of Richard Wettstein; later director of the Bundes-Gymnasium, Wien xii. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Stadlm.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection WU|WU]]; further material at [[Collection C|C]]. – *Letters* at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 314 (b. 18 Nov 1881); Biol.-Dokum. 17: 8840; BJI 2: 169; DTS 6(4): 199; Tucker 2: xxxvi.

#### Biofile

- Janchen, E., Richard Wettstein 190. 1933.

#### Composite works

Collaborator, A. Ginzberger, *Pflanzengeographisches Hilfsbuch*, 1939.
