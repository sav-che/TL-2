### Author

Lyngbye, Hansen \[Hans\] Christian (1782-1837), Danish phycologist, clergyman at Gjesing and Nørager (nr. Aarhus) 1819-1827; id. at Søberg and Gilleleje 1827-1837. (*Lyngb.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection C|C]]; some further material at [[Collection HBG|HBG]], [[Collection KIEL|KIEL]] and [[Collection L|L]]. – Original drawings at [[Collection FH|FH]].
