### Author

Prahl, Johann Friedrich (*fl*. 1837), German botanist; teacher at the gymnasium of Güstrow (Mecklenburg-Schwerin), later clergyman at Hehen-Horn (Lauenburg). 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. Prahl* \[standard form in IPNI: *J.Prahl*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
