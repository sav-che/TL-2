### Author

Robin, Jean (1550-1629), French botanist and royal gardener; from 1590 at the Jardin des Plantes, Paris. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. Robin* \[standard form in IPNI: *J.Robin*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 6(2): 713; Backer p. 496; Barnhart 3: 164 (d. 25 Apr 1629); BM 4: 1713; Bossert p. 334; Hegi 4(3): 1390; Jackson p. 422; NI alph.; PR 7670-7673, 9671, ed. 1: 6171-6172, 8637-8640, 10643; Rehder 5: 730; Tucker 1: 601.

#### Eponymy

*Robina* Cothenius (1790, *orth. var.*); *Robinia* Linnaeus (1753) and the derived names *Robincola* Velenovsky (1947) and *Robinioxylon* W.R. Müller-Stoll et E. Mädel (1967).
