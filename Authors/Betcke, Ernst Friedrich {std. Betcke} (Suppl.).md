### Author

\* Betcke, Ernst Friedrich (1815-1865), German (Mecklenburg) physician in Pentzlin. (*Betcke*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown; had a herbarium of ca. 5900 species, offered for sale 1866 (see E.F.A. Boll, 1866).

#### Bibliography and biography
