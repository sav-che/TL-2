### Author

Pereboom, Nicolaas Ewoud (*fl*. 1787), Dutch botanist, son of Cornells Pereboom. 
**Abbreviated name**: *N. Pereb.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Pereb.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
