### Author

\* Achepohl, Ludwig (x-1902), German palaeontologist, "Obereinfahrer" at Essen; worker on the carboniferous flora. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Achepohl* \[standard form in IPNI: *Achepohl*\]

#### Collections

Unknown.
