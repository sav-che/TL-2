### Author

Schmidt, Alfred (1886-?), German (Silesian) mycologist; travelled in East Africa 1910; student of F. Pax; D. phil. Breslau 1912. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Alfr. Schmidt* \[standard form in IPNI: *Alf.Schmidt*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
