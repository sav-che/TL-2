### Author

Killias, Eduard (1829-1891), Swiss botanist, entomologist and physician in Chur, Le Prese (nr. Puschlaf), and Tarasp-Schuls (Nairs). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Killias* \[standard form in IPNI: *Killias*\]
