### Author

Seyot, Pierre (1876-1942), French mycologist; professor of botany at the Faculté de Pharmacie, Nancy, from 1920 onward. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Seyot*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 263; Tucker 1: 646.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Qui êtes-vous? 699. 1924.
- Dillemann, G., Bull. Soc. mycol. France 100(2): ci-cii. 1984.

**Title**
