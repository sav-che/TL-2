### Author

Pilling, Friedrich Oscar (1824-1897), German botanist and pomologist; high school teacher at Altenburg. (*Pilling*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 87; TL-2/6527.
