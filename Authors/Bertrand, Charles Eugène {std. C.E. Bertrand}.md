### Author

Bertrand, Charles Eugène (1851-1917), French palaeobotanist at Lille. (*C. E. Bertrand*).

#### Collections

LILLE. Herbarium material from France at L and MANCH (not *orig.*)
*Ref*.: IH 2: 72.
