### Author

Bertrand, Charles Eugène (1851-1917), French palaeobotanist at Lille. 
**Abbreviated name**: *C. E. Bertrand* \[standard form in IPNI: *C.E.Bertrand*\]

#### Collections

LILLE. Herbarium material from France at L and MANCH (not *orig.*)
*Ref*.: IH 2: 72.
