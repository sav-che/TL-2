### Author

Cunningham, Richard (1793-1835), British gardener, Kew collector in Australia, colonial botanist in New South Wales 1833-1835, brother of Allan Cunningham. 
**Abbreviated name**: *R. Cunn.* \[standard form in IPNI: *R.Cunn.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection K|K]], incl. mss. flora of New Zealand, other material at [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection GL|GL]], [[Collection MEL|MEL]], [[Collection MO|MO]], [[Collection NSW|NSW]], [[Collection OXF|OXF]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 147.
- Clokie, Account herbaria Oxford 152. 1964.
- Hedge and Lamond, Index coll. Edinburgh 72. 1970.
- D'Arcy, Missouri Bot. Gard. Bull. 59(1): 24. 1971.
