### Author

Caldas, Francisco José de, (1768-1816), Spanish astronomer at Santa Fé de Bogotá, pupil of Mutis. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Caldas* \[standard form in IPNI: *Caldas*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MA|MA]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 112.
