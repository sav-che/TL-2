### Author

Olsson, Peter (1833-1906), Swedish botanist of Värmland, Dr. phil. Uppsala 1866; high school teacher at Norrköping 1864-1902. 
**Abbreviated name**: *P. Olsson, Värml.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection UPS|UPS]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 29; KR p. 564-565; Morren ed. 10, p. 106; PR 6836.
- Anon., Bot. Not. 1906: 304 (b. 26 Sep 1833, d. 25 Nov 1906).
- Olsen, S., Bot. Not. 1949: 269-276 (comments on A. Braun. *Fragm. monogr. Charac.*, TL-2/717).
- Wittrock, V. B., Acta Horti Bergiani 3(2): 65. 1903.
