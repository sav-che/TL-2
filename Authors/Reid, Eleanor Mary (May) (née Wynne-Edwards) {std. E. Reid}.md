### Author

Reid, Eleanor Mary (May) (née Wynne-Edwards) (1860-1953), British mathematician and palaobotanist; married Clement Reid (1897). 
**Abbreviated name**: *E. Reid* \[standard form in IPNI: *E.Reid*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection K|K]]; other material at [[Collection TCD|TCD]].
