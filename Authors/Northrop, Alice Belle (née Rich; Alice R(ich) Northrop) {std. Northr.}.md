### Author

Northrop, Alice Belle (née Rich; Alice R(ich) Northrop) (1864-1922), American botanist; wife (from 1889) of John Isaiah Northrop (1861-1891); tutor in botany at Normal College, New York 1885-1889. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Northr.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Northr.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection F|F]]; other material in [[Collection A|A]], [[Collection B|B]], [[Collection G|G]], [[Collection GH|GH]], [[Collection K|K]], [[Collection NY|NY]], [[Collection US|US]].
*Exsiccatae*: *Bahama plants* (1890), sets at [[Collection B|B]], [[Collection G|G]].
