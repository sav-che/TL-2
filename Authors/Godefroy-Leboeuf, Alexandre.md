### Author

Godefroy-Leboeuf, Alexandre (1852-1903), French orchidologist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Godefroy-Leb.* \[standard form in IPNI: *God.-Leb.*\]

[[Collection P|P]]; other material [[Collection C|C]], [[Collection K|K]], [[Collection W|W]].
