### Author

Sauzé, Jean Charles (1815-1889), French botanist at Mothe St. Héray, Deux-Sèvres. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Sauzé*

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection CGE|CGE]], [[Collection FI|FI]]. – Herbaria left to the Société botanique des Deux-Sèvres.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 216 (b. 12 Feb 1815, d. 10 Mar 1899); BL 2: 145, 714; BM 4: 1814; Jackson p. 281, 283; PR 5055; Rehder 5: 761; TL-2/see P.N. Maillard; Tucker 1: 625.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Bull. Soc. bot. Deux-Sèvres *17*: *pl. 4.* 1906 (portr.); Cat. gén. livr. impr.
- Bibl. natl. 163: 1041. 1943.
- Souché, B., Flore du Haut Poitou xviii. 1901 (herb. at Soc. bot. Deux-Sèvres).
