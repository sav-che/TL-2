### Author

Banning, Florens (1835-1882), German (Westfalen, Münster) botanist and highschool teacher at Minden (Westfalen). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Banning*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 6(1): 511 (b. 18 Jan 1835, Lengerich i. W., d. 19 Feb 1882, Minden); Barnhart 1: 118; Jackson p. 307; Morren ed. 10, p. 15; Rehder 2: 301.
