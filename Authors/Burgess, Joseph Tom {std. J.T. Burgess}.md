### Author

Burgess, Joseph Tom (1828-1886), British wood-engraver and botanist, born 17 Feb 1828 at Cheshunt, Herts, died 4 Oct 1886 at Leamington, Warwickshire, editor of Clare Journal and local English newspapers, also as antiquary. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J.T. Burgess*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 284 (b. 17 Feb 1828, Cheshunt, Herts, d. 4 Oct 1886, Leamington); BB third suppl. ed. 2; Desmond p. 107; DNB suppl. 1: 335; Jackson p. 237; Kew 1: 426; Rehder 1: 402.
