### Author

Rosenthal, David August (1821-1875), German (Silesian) physician, botanist and theological author; practicing physician as well as municipal physician at Breslau from 1855. (*Rosenthal*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BL 1: 5, 316, 2: 7, 710; BM 4: 1733; GR p. 174 (b. 16 Apr 1821, d. 29 Mar 1875); Herder p. 120, 411; Jackson p. 202; LS 22535; MW p. 418; PR 7772; Rehder 3: 58, 462; Tucker 1: 607.
