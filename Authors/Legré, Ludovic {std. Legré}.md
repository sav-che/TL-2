### Author

Legré, Ludovic (1838-1904), French jurist and botanical historian, "bâtonnier de l'ordre des avocats de Marseille." 
**Abbreviated name**: *Legré* \[standard form in IPNI: *Legré*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
