### Author

Koelle, Johann Ludwig Christian (1763-1797), German physician ("Medicinalrath") and botanist at Bayreuth. (*Koelle*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 5(2): 610, 758; Barnhart 2: 308; BM 2: 1005; Dryander 3: 292; DTS 1: 159; Hortus 3: 1197 ("Koelle"); PR 4783-4784 (ed. 1: 5322-5323); Rehder 1: 375.
- Familier, I., Denkschr. bay. bot. Ges. Regensburg 11: 12, 18. 1911.
- Römer, J. J., Arch. bot. 2(1): 111. 1799.

#### Eponymy

*Koellea* Biria (1811); *Koellia* Moench (1794).
