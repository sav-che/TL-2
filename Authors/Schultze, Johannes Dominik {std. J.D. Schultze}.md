### Author

Schultze, Johannes Dominik (1752-1790), German (Hamburg) physician; studied at Göttingen 1772-1775; Dr. med. 1775; practicing physician in Hamburg 1776. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J.D. Schultze*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown (Fischer-Benzon mentions a large herbarium of which the location was unknown to him).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 245; BM 4: 1870; Dryander 3: 267; PR 8488.

#### Biofile

- Fischer-Benzon, R.J.D. *in* P. Prahl, Krit. Fl. Schlesw.-Holstein 2: 54. 1890 (b. 16 Jun 1751, d. 22 Mai 1790).
- Voigt, A., Bot. Inst. Hamburg 4, 5, 6, 9, 59. 1897.
