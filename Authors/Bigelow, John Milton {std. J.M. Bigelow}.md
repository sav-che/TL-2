### Author

Bigelow, John Milton (1804-1878), American surgeon and botanist on the Whipple Expedition. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. M. Bigelow* \[standard form in IPNI: *J.M.Bigelow*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Mainly at [[Collection GH|GH]] and [[Collection MO|MO]].
