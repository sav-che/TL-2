### Author

Moruzi, Constance (1899-x), Roumanian mycologist and lichenologist; Dr. Sci. Paris 1932; active at the University, the Agronomical Institute and the Pedagogical Institute in Bucarest. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Moruzi*
