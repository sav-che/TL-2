### Author

Blenk, Paul (fl. 1884), German botanist who studied at the University of Erlangen, Dr. phil. id. 1884, died at San Remo on 30 Dec 1886. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Blenk*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 200; Rehder 1: 110.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Ber. deut. bot. Ges. 4: cccxiv. 1886 (d. 30 Dec, San Remo).
