### Author

Corbière, Louis (1850-1941), French botanist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Corb.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection CHE|CHE]]; a type herbarium of the Normandy flora is also at [[Collection CN|CN]]; further details IH.
*Ref*.: IH 2: 138.
- Koster, Taxon 18: 550. 1969.
