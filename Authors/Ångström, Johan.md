### Author

Ångström, Johan (1813-1879), Swedish bryologist and physician, educated at Uppsala, practising at Lycksele (1853) and Örnsköldsvik.
**Abbreviated name**: *Ångstr.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Ångstr.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection S|S]]; further material at [[Collection H|H]].
*Ref*.: IH 1 (ed. 6): 355, 2: 38; KR p. 787.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 65; BM 1: 47; CSP 1: 73-74, 7: 38, 9: 52; Frank p. 5; KR p. 787-789; PR 186.
- Scheutz, Bot. Not. 1863: 73.
- Anon., J. Bot. 17: 128. 1879.
- Wittrock, Acta Horti Berg. 3(3): *pl. 101.* 1905 (portr.)

#### Eponymy

*Angstroemia* Bruch, Schimper & Gümbel (1846); *Aongstroemiopsis* M. Fleischer (1904).
