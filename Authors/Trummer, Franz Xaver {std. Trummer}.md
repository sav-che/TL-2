### Author

Trummer, Franz Xaver (x-1858), Austrian gardener at the agricultural experiment garden at Graz, specialized in fruit-growing and wine-making. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Trummer*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
