### Author

Moloney, Sir Cornelius Alfred (1848-1913), British colonial administrator in W. Africa from 1867; governor of Lagos 1887-1891; in the West Indies 1891-1904. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Moloney*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection K|K]].
