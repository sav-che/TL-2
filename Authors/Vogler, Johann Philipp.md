### Author

Vogler, Johann Philipp (1746-1816), German (Hessen) physician and botanist; practicing physician at Weilburg; Dr. med. Giessen 1773. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J.P. Vogler* \[standard form in IPNI: *Vogler*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 6(2): 242; Barnhart 3: 442 (d. 14 Apr 1816); Dryander 3: 213, 563, 584-586; Frank 3 (Anh.): 109; Herder p. 271; PR 9831-9832, ed. 1: 10810-10811; Rehder 4: 502; TL-2/11.175, 11.178.

#### Biofile

- Gaertner, P.G. et al., Oek.-Techn. Fl. Wetterau 2: 501-502. 1800.

#### Eponymy

*Voglera* P.G. Gaertner, B. Meyer et Scherbius (1800, also honors J.A. Vogler, *q.v.*).
