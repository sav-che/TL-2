### Author

Zuccagni, Attilio (1754-1807), Italian physician and botanist; director of the Giardino dei semplici, Firenze, and director of the Museo di físico e storia naturale, Firenze; made a trip to Spain and Portugal. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Zuccagni* \[standard form in IPNI: *Zuccagni*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Originally at [[Collection FI|FI]]. When Parlatore arrived at Florence in 1842 he found the Zuccagni herbarium destroyed by insects and without indications of localities; he threw the remnants away. – Some plants collected by Z. in [[Collection UPS|UPS]]-Thunb. – *Letters* at [[Collection G|G]].
