### Author

Schlenker, Georg (1847-1932), German (Schwaben) botanist, high school teacher at Cannstadt, Württemberg from 1877; Dr. h.c. Stuttgart 1925. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Schlenker* \[standard form in IPNI: *Schlenker*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Note

Not to be confused with Gerhard Schlenker, author *of Experimentelle Untersuchungen in der Sektion Beccapunga... Veronica*, Flora 130: 305-350. 1935-1936.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 229; Biol.-Dokum. 16: 8071; DTS 1: 266-267; MW p. 437; Tucker 3: 272.

#### Biofile

- Lehmann, E., Schwäbische Apotheker 204. 1951.
- Schlenker, L., Lebensbilder aus Schwaben und Franken 9: 388-399. 1963 (extensive biogr., bibl., portr.).
