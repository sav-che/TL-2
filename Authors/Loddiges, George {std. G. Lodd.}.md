### Author

Loddiges, George (1784-1846), British horticulturist, nurseryman at Hackney; son of Conrad Loddiges. 
**Abbreviated name**: *G. Lodd.* \[standard form in IPNI: *G.Lodd.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Material of cultivated plants at [[Collection BR|BR]], [[Collection CGE|CGE]], [[Collection FI|FI]] and [[Collection K|K]]. Letters at [[Collection K|K]].

#### Composite works

George Loddiges was the main author of the text of *The Botanical Cabinet*; he conducted it from 1817-1834.
