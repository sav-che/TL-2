### Author

Schildknecht, J. (*fl*. 1863), German botanist; high school teacher at Freiburg i. Br. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Schildknecht* \[standard form in IPNI: *Schildkn.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection KR|KR]].

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 4: 1832; PR 8182; Rehder 1: 385, 5: xix.
