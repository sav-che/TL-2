### Author

Kamiénski, Frans (Franciszek) (1851-1912), Polish botanist, sometime director of the Odessa botanical garden. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Kamiénski* \[standard form in IPNI: *Kamieński*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
