### Author

Wegelin, Heinrich (1853-1940), Swiss botanist; professor of botany at the Kantonschule of Thurgau in Frauenfeld and director of its botanical garden 1890-1920. 
**Abbreviated name**: *H. Wegelin* \[standard form in IPNI: *H.Wegelin*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. Some fungi from Switzerland in [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection WRSL|WRSL]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 470; BFM 995; BL 2: 582, 730; LS 28490-28491.
