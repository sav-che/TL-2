### Author

Cornet, Arthur Théodore Erasme (1863-1939), Belgian stationmaster and bryologist, born at Viemme, "Chef de Station" at Juslenville 1901-1928, moved to Moha. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Cornet*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BR|BR]].

#### Biofile
