### Author

Scheuchzer, Johannes (1684-1738), Swiss botanist at Zürich; brother of J.J. Scheuchzer. (*J. Scheuchzer*).

#### Herbarium and types

See J.J. Scheuchzer; many specimens also in [[Collection P|P]]-HA.
