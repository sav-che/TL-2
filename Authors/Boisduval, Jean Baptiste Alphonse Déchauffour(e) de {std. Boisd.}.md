### Author

Boisduval, Jean Baptiste Alphonse Déchauffour(e) de (1801 \[1799?\]-1879), French naturalist and physician at Paris. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Boisduval* \[standard form in IPNI: *Boisd.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
