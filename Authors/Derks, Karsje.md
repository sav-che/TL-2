### Author

Derks, Karsje (1868-1929), Dutch female botanist, born 25 Mai 1868 at Havelte, Drente, Netherlands, died 25 Sep 1925, at Klosters, Switzerland, where she had a pharmacy. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Derks*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
