### Author

Dubois, François (1802-1868), Belgian physician, born 4 Jul 1802 at Leuze, physician at the faculty of medicine at Liège, died 28 Nov 1868 at Tournai. 
**Abbreviated name**: *F. Dubois*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
