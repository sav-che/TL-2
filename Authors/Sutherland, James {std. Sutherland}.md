### Author

Sutherland, James (c. 1639-1719), British botanist; professor of botany, Edinburgh 1695-1706; King's botanist for Scotland 1699; director of the Royal Botanical Garden, Edinburgh 1699-1714. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Sutherland*

#### Herbarium and types

Not traced. Material sent to J. Petiver in [[Collection BM|BM]]-Sloane and to W. Sherard in [[Collection OXF|OXF]].

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 6(2): 724; Barnhart 3: 349; BB p. 293; BM 5: 2051; Desmond p. 592 (d. 24 Jun 1719); Dryander 3: 101; Henrey 13, 197-198, 199 note; Herder p. 41, 150; Jackson p. 411; PR 9056, ed. 1: 10.000; Rehder 1: 52; TL-2/see A. Buddle; Tucker 1: 674.
