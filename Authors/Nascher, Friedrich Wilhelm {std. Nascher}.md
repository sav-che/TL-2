### Author

Nascher, Friedrich Wilhelm (1702-1764), fictitious German botanist, stated to have resided in Havana and to have travelled in South America. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Nascher*

#### Note

One of the fictitious botanists listed by Barnhart (J. New York Bot. Gard. 20: 176-177. 1919) from Appleton's Cyclopaedia of American biography (4: 478-479. 1888). Some of the equally fictitious book-titles are: "Neueste Geschichte und Beschreibung des Brodbaums" 1758 (n.b. the bread-fruit tree was first introduced into tropical America in 1793), "Flora cubana" 2 vols., Leipzig 1758.
