### Author

Zenkert, Charles Anthony (1886-1972), American botanist; in 1935 research associate in botany at the Buffalo Museum of Science, Buffalo, New York. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Zenkert*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BUF|BUF]]; further in [[Collection NYS|NYS]], [[Collection WELC|WELC]]. – *Letters* at [[Collection NY|NY]] (Barnhart corr.).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 539 (b. 6 Jul 1886); BL 1: 204, 325; IH 1 (ed. 2): 33, (ed. 3): 57, (ed. 4): 39.
