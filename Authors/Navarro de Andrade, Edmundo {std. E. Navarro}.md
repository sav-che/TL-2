### Author

Navarro de Andrade, Edmundo (1881-1941), Brazilian entomologist, botanist and forester at São Paulo. 
**Abbreviated name**: *E. Navarro* \[standard form in IPNI: *Navarro*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection R|R]].
