### Author

Schlümbach, Friedrich Alexander von (*fl*. 1809-1811), German (Bavarian) forestry botanist at Nürnberg. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Schlümbach*

#### Herbarium and types

A "xylotheka" consisting of 158 boxes with wood and bark samples as well as dried specimens of branches, leaves and fruits, is at the Museum 't Coopmanshûs, Franeker, the Netherlands; this wood collection was assembled by von Schlümbach and bought by Louis Napoleon \[with Dutch funds\] for the then Frisian University at Franeker.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 4: 1841; Herder p. 294; NI 1778; PR 8246; Rehder 3: 289; Tucker 1: 631.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Het botanisch kabinet, prospectus exhibit 7 Jul-4 Oct 1980, Museum 't Coopmanshûs, Franeker, 1981 (brief description with sample illustration of xylotheca).
