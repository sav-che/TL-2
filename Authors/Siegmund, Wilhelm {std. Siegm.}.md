### Author

Siegmund, Wilhelm (1821-1897), Bohemian botanist, factory-owner and "Stadtrat" in Reichenberg (Bohemia). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Siegm.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection PR|PR]] (12.000). – Some lichens at [[Collection M|M]] and [[Collection WRSL|WRSL]]; further material at [[Collection B|B]] (mainly destr.), [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection GOET|GOET]], [[Collection PRM|PRM]]. – Contributed to series of exsiccatae issued by Opiz and Rabenhorst.– *Letters* at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 276 (b. 7 Mai 1821, d. 18 Nov 1897); Futak-Domin p. 537; Klásterský p. 171 (d. 18 Nov 1879, err.); LS 24885; Maiwald p. 140, 145, 163, 231, 256; Urban-Berl. 282, 291.
