### Author

Strähler, Adolf (1829-1897), German (Silesian) botanist and forester; forester in Görbersdorf 1858-1880, idem at Theerkeute nr. Wronke (Posen) ultimately at Jauer. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Straehler*
