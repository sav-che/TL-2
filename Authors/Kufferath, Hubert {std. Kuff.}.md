### Author

Kufferath, Hubert (1882-1957), Belgian algologist and bacteriologist; Dr. sci. Bruxelles 1910; from 1904-1920 at the Institut Pasteur du Brabant; from 1920-1947 director of the Institut Meurice-Chimie in Bruxelles. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Kuff.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Kuff.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Diatoms and possibly other collections at [[Collection BRLU|BRLU]].
*Ref*.: Wornardt, W. W., Int. direct, diatom. 34. 1968.
- Koster, J. T., Taxon 18: 554. 1969.
