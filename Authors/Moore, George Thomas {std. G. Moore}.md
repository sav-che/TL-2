### Author

Moore, George Thomas (1871-1956), American algologist; Ph. D. Harvard 1900; plant physiologist at Washington Univ. 1909-1912; director of the Missouri Botanical Garden 1912-1953 
**Abbreviated name**: *G. Moore*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MO|MO]].
