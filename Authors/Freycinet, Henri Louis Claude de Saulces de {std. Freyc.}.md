### Author

Freycinet, Henri Louis Claude de Saulces de (1779-1840), French naval explorer. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Freyc.*

#### Herbarium and types

For the botanical collections made on Freycinet's voyages see Gaudichaud-Beaupré.
