### Author

Bubani, Pietro (1806-1888), Italian physician and botanist who studied the flora of the Pyrenées while in exile. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Bubani* \[standard form in IPNI: *Bubani*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection GE|GE]].
*Ref*.: IH 1 (ed. 6): 356, 2: 103; Saccardo 2: 25.
