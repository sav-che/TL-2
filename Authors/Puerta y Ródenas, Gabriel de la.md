### Author

Puerta y Ródenas, Gabriel de la (*fl*. 1891), Spanish botanist, professor of botany at the Universidad Central, Madrid. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Puerta* \[standard form in IPNI: *Puerta*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
