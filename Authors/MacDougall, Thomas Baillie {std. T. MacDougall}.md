### Author

MacDougall, Thomas Baillie (1895-1973), Scottish-born American plant collector, explorer and anthropologist (*T. MacDougall*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection E|E]], [[Collection NY|NY]], [[Collection US|US]]. Manuscripts at American Museum of Natural History.
