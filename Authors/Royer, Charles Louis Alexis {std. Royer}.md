### Author

Royer, Charles Louis Alexis (1831-1883), French botanist; landowner at Quincy, Côte-d'-Or. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Royer*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection AUT|AUT]].

#### Note

Not to be confused with the Namur (Belgian) pomologist Auguste Philippe Antoine Royer (1796-1867) (see e.g. Ed. Morren, Bull. Féd. Soc. Hort. Belgique 1868: 207-219).
