### Author

Wilke, Fritz \[Wilhelm\] (1888-x), German (Prussian/Westphalian) botanist; studied natural sciences at Jena, Berlin and Halle; Dr. phil. Halle 1913. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Wilke*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
