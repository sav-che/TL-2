### Author

Lodder, Jacomina (1905-x), Dutch mycologist at Delft; specialist on yeasts. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lodder* \[standard form in IPNI: *Lodder*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Original material in [[Collection CBS|CBS]] collections.

#### Bibliography and biography

Ainsworth p. 213, 332.

#### Composite works

With Nellie J. W. Kreger-van Rij (1920-x) *The yeasts*, a taxonomic study, Amsterdam 1952, 713 p.; ed. 2. 1970. (Taxon 2: 34 Mar 1953).

#### Eponymy

*Lodderomyces* J. P. van der Walt (1966).
