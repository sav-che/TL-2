### Author

White, Violetta Susan Elizabeth (1875-1949), American botanist; married John Ross Delafield 1904; associated with the New York Bot. Gard. 1901-1902. 
**Abbreviated name**: *V.S. White*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection NY|NY]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 487 (b. 10 Mai 1875, d. Mai 1949); Kelly p. 241; LS 28797-28799; NI 3: 264; Stevenson p. 1262.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Index Amer. bot. lit. 1886-1966. 4: 521. 1969.
- Barnhart, J.H., J. New York Bot. Gard. 12: 144. 1911.
