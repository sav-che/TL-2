### Author

Schrader, Wilhelm (1818-1895), German (Prussian-Saxony) mathematician, botanist and high school teacher; Dr. phil. Halle, teacher at Halle and Erfurt. 
**Abbreviated name**: *W. Schrad.* \[standard form in IPNI: *W.Schrad.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
