### Author

Garden, Alexander (1730-1791), American physician and botanist, correspondent of Collinson, Ellis and Linnaeus. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Garden* \[standard form in IPNI: *Garden*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection LINN|LINN]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 217.
