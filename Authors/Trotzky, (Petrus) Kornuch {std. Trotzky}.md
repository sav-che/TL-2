### Author

Trotzky, \[Petrus\] Kornuch (1803-1877), Russian botanist; Dr. phil. Dorpat 1832, student of C.F. v. Ledebour. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Trotzky* \[standard form in IPNI: *Korn.-Trotzky*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
