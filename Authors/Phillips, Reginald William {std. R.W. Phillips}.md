### Author

Phillips, Reginald William (1854-1926), British botanist; B.A. Cambr. 1884; Dr. sci. 1898; head Dept. Biol. Bangor 1884; professor of botany ib. until 1922. 
**Abbreviated name**: *R.W. Phillips*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection UCNW|UCNW]].
