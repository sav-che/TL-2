### Author

Morgenroth, Eduard (1861-?), German palaeobotanist; Dr. phil. Leipzig 1883; ultimately "Oberstudienrat" in Berlin 
**Abbreviated name**: *Morgenroth* \[standard form in IPNI: *Morgenr.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Andrews p. 304; BM 3: 1350; Quenstedt p. 486 (b. 10 Nov 1861).
