### Author

Thurston, Charles Orion (1857-1933), American botanist; AB Dartmouth 1884. 
**Abbreviated name**: *C. Thurst.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection DS|DS]] (for Dudley and Thurston, see below); other material at [[Collection US|US]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 382 (b. 23 Feb 1857); BL 1: 212, 321; PH 937; Tucker 1: 214, 690.
