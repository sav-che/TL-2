### Author

Eckstein, Karl (1859-1939), German botanist and zoologist at the Forst-Hochschule Eberswalde, born 28 Dec 1859 in Grünberg (Oberhessen), died 22 Apr 1939 on a study tour in Ragusa. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Eckstein*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
