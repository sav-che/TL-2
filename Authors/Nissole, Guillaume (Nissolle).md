### Author

Nissole, Guillaume \[Nissolle\] (1647-1735), French botanist and physician at Montpellier. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Nissole* \[standard form in IPNI: *Nissole*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BM|BM]]-SL; perhaps also at [[Collection OXF|OXF]] (not found by Clokie).

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 6(2): 982; Barnhart 3: 7; Bossert p. 287; Clokie p. 218; Dryander 3: 34, 241, 301, 325; Frank 3(Anh.): 72; IH 2: (in press); Kew 4: 151; PR (alph.); Rehder 5: 621; Tucker 1: 521.
- Dandy, J. E., The Sloane herbarium 172. 1958.
- Flahault, C., Univ. Montpellier, Inst. Bot. 35. 1890.
- Jacquin, N. J., Enum. pl. Carib. 7, 27. 1760 (epon.).
- Stafleu, F. A., Intr. Jussieu Gen. Pl. xlii, 1964; Linnaeus and the Linnaeans 267. 1971.

#### Handwriting

Dandy, J. E., The Sloane herbarium 1958, facsimile no. 86.

#### Eponymy

*Nissolia*N.J. Jacquin (1760, *nom. cons.*), *Nissolia* P. Miller (1754, *nom. rej.*) and *Nissolius* Medikus (1787, *nom. rej.*) were probably named for Nissole.
