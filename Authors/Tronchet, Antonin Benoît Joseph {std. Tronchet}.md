### Author

Tronchet, Antonin Benoît Joseph (1902-x), French botanist; Dr. sci. nat. Univ. Lyon 1930; professor of botany at the Faculté des Sciences, Besançon. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Tronchet* \[standard form in IPNI: *Tronchet*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – *Letters* at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 402; BL 2: 149, 725; GR p. 353-354; Hirsch p. 303 \["Antonin T."\].

#### Festschrift

*Recueil de travaux dédiés au professeur A. Tronchet*, Ann. Sci. Univ. Besançon ser. 3 (Bot.): 12, 1972 (portr., dedic., bibl.).
