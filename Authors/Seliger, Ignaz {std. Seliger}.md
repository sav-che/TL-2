### Author

Seliger, Ignaz (1752-1812), German (Silesian) clergyman and botanist at Wölfeisdorf; early student of Silesian cryptogams. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Seliger* \[standard form in IPNI: *Seliger*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection WRSL|WRSL]] (lich., also other crypt.?; through C. Chr. Günther), possibly some material in [[Collection B|B]]-Bridel (extant; Sayre); further material in [[Collection PR|PR]]. – *Letters* at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 258 (b. 14 Dec 1752, d. 30 Feb 1812); Frank 3 (Anh.): 96; Klásterský p. 163; SBC p. 132; Urban-Berl. p. 313.
