### Author

Deecke, Johannes Ernst Wilhelm (1862-1934), German geologist and palaeobiologist at Freiburg i. Br. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Deecke* \[standard form in IPNI: *Deecke*\]

#### Collections

Freiburger geologisches Institut.
