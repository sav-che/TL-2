### Author

Brown, Margaret Sibella (1866-1961), Australian (born Sydney Mines, N.S.) bryologist, educated at private schools at Halifax, N.S., Stuttgart, Württemberg, and London. 
**Abbreviated name**: *M.S. Br.*
