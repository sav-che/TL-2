### Author

Ordoyno, Thomas (*fl*. 1790s-1810s), British nurseryman and seedsman of Newark-on-Trent. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Ordoyno* \[standard form in IPNI: *Ordoyno*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
