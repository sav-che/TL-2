### Author

Calkoen Az., Hendrik Joän (1855-1937), Dutch mycologist, born on St. Maarten, died in Dieren Gelderland, in the Netherlands. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Calkoen*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 300 (b. 5 Apr 1855, St. Maarten); LS 4597; Rehder 1: 391; TL-2, see 13.434.

#### Biofile

- Boerlage, J.G. and P.P.C. Hoek, Bibl. Fl. Fauna Nederland 1753-1886, *in* Alg. Aardrijksk. Bibl. Ned. 2, Leiden 1888, facsimile Amsterdam 1975, p. 26 (Galinsoga parviflora), 44 (thesis).
- Calkoen, H.J., Ned. kruidk. Arch. ser. 2. 4(1): 61. 1883 (Galinsoga parviflora found outside of Amsterdam).
- Verdoorn, F., Chron. bot. 4(1): 71. 1938 (died at Dieren, Netherlands, 1937).

#### Note

Calkoen was the editor of editions 10, 11, and 12 of the "*Zakflora*" of Willem Frederik Reinier Suringar, see TL-2/5: 100, no. 13.434.
