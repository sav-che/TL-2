### Author

Pehersdorfer, Anna (1849-1925), Austrian botanist, teacher at a girl's college in Steyr. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Pehersd.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Pehersd.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown; some material at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 63; CSP 17: 762; GR p. 456-457 (b. 22 Jul 1849, d. 3 Apr 1925); LS 20375, 37784; Rehder 1: 71, 5: 657.

#### Biofile

- Janchen, E., Cat. fl. austr. 1: 23. 1956 (bibl.).
