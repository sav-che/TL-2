### Author

Vogler, Johann Andreas (*fl*. 1781), German (Hessen) physician; Dr. med. Giessen 1781, later physician at Ems; brother of J.P. Vogler. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J.A. Vogler* \[standard form in IPNI: *J.A.Vogler*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 5: 2232; Dryander 3: 340; Hortus 3: 1206; IF p. 736; PR 9830, ed. 1: 10809.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Cat. gén. livr. impr. Bibl. natl. 213: 495. 1972.

#### Eponymy

*Vaglera* P.G. Gaertner, B. Meyer et Scherbius (1800, also honors J.P. Vogler, *q.v.*).

### Publications

##### n.16.303. Dissertatio

**Title**
*Dissertatio* inauguralis botanica sistens Polypodii speciem nuperis auctoribus ignotam *Polypodium montanum* vocatum. Quam gratiosi medicorum ordinis consensu pro gradu doctoris die \[\] Decembr. anni \[1781\] publice defendet Johannes Andreas Vogler Darmstadinus. Gissae \[Giessen\] (apud Johannem Jacobum Braun,..) \[1781\]. Qu.
**Abbreviated title**: *Diss. Polypod. mont.*

**Notes**
*Publ*.: Dec 1781, p. \[1\]-16. *Copy*: B-S. – Vogler notes that the new *Polypodium* had actually been discovered by his brother J.P. Vogler (see below).
