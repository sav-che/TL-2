### Author

Lillo, Miguel (1861-1931), Argentine self-taught botanist; Dr. h.c. Univ. Plata 1914; from 1892 active at the Tucuman "Oficina Químia." (*Lillo*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection LIL|LIL]]; further material at [[Collection SI|SI]].
