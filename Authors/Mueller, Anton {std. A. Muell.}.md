### Author

Mueller, Anton (1798-1864), Moravian naturalist; educated at the Gymnasium of Brünn (Brno); administrator in the "Kreisamt" Brünn until 1858. 
**Abbreviated name**: *A. Muell.* \[standard form in IPNI: *A.Muell.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
