### Author

Popovici, Alexandru P. (1866-?), Rumanian botanist, professor of botany at the University of Jassi; pupil of W. Pfeffer; Dr. phil. Bonn 1893. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Popovici* \[standard form in IPNI: *Popovici*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection I|I]].
