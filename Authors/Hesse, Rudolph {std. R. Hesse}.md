### Author

Hesse, Rudolph (1844-1912), German botanist and agronomist teaching at the agricultural colleges of Arendsee and Marburg. 
**Abbreviated name**: *R. Hesse* \[standard form in IPNI: *R.Hesse*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
