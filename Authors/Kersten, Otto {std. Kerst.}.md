### Author

Kersten, Otto (1839-1900), German botanist, participated with Hermann Linck in the Decken expedition (1862-1865); collected later in the Mediterranean area. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Kerst.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Kerst.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection B|B]].
