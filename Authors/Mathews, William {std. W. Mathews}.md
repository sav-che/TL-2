### Author

Mathews, William (1828-1901), British land surveyor and botanist at Birmingham; MA Cantab. 1856. 
**Abbreviated name**: *W. Mathews* \[standard form in IPNI: *Mathews*\]

#### Herbarium and types

parts at [[Collection BIRM|BIRM]], [[Collection GL|GL]], [[Collection K|K]] and [[Collection WOS|WOS]] (see Bull. misc. Inf. Kcw 1906: 172-173).
