### Author

Gay, Claude (1800-1873), French botanist and traveller. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Gay*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection P|P]], [[Collection PC|PC]]. – Gay made two voyages to South America, in 1828-1832 and in 1834-1842.
