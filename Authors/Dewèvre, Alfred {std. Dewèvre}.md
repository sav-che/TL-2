### Author

Dewèvre, Alfred (1866-1897), Belgian botanist who worked on the Station Luebo nr. Kassai, born 20 Mar 1866, Brussels, died 27 Feb 1897, Leopoldville, Belgian Congo. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Dewèvre*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BR|BR]], further material at [[Collection B|B]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 450 (b. 20 Mar 1866, Bruxelles, Belg., d. 27 Feb 1897, Léopoldville, Congo Belge); BL 1: 23, 293; BM 1: 450; CSP 14: 595; IH 2: 161; LS 7016-7022; Rehder 5(index): 225; Tucker 1: 201; Urban-Berl. p. 340.
