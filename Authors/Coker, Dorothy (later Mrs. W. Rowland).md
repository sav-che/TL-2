### Author

Coker, Dorothy (later Mrs. W. Rowland) (1894-x), American bryologist, studied at Columbia University, New York, Dr. phil. ib. 1918. 
**Abbreviated name**: *D. Coker* \[standard form in IPNI: *D.Coker*\]
