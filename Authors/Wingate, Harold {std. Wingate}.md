### Author

Wingate, Harold (1852-1926), American businessman with the International Navigation Company and amateur botanist; student of Myxomycetes in Philadelphia. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Wingate*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection PH|PH]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 506 (b. 25 Aug 1852, d. 25 Mai 1926); Kelly p. 242; Lenley p. 439; LS 29068-29074 (bibl.); Stevenson p. 1263.

#### Biofile

- Harshberger, J.W., Bot. Philadelphia 363. 1899 (bibl.).
- Mears, J.A., Proc. Acad. nat. Sci. Philadelphia 133: 165. 1981 (coll. PH).

#### Eponymy

*Wingina* O. Kuntze (1891).

#### Note

*A new genus of Myxomycetes* \[*Orthotricha*\], J. Mycol. 2(11): 125-126. Nov 1926, see also Proc. Acad. nat. Sci. 1889: 189.
