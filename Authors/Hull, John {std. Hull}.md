### Author

Hull, John (1761-1843), British botanist; MD Leiden (1792); later physician at Manchester. (*Hull*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BM|BM]] (fide Desmond p. 328), further material (mosses) at [[Collection LINN|LINN]] via Withering (fide Sayre).
