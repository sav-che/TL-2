### Author

Quartin-Dillon, Richard (x-1841), French botanist, explorer of Abyssinia (1839-1840). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Quart.-Dill.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Quart.-Dill.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection P|P]] and [[Collection PC|PC]], duplicates e.g. in [[Collection B|B]], [[Collection BR|BR]], [[Collection CN|CN]], [[Collection G|G]], [[Collection K|K]], [[Collection LE|LE]], [[Collection MO|MO]], [[Collection W|W]].

#### Bibliography and biography

#### Eponymy
