### Author

Ludwig, Christian Friedrich (1757-1823), physician and botanist; professor of natural history and pathology at the University of Leipzig 1782-1823. 
**Abbreviated name**: *C. F. Ludw.*

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection BM|BM]].
