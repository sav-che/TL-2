### Author

Mosén, Carl Wilhelm Hjalmar (1841-1887), Swedish botanist; Dr. phil. Uppsala 1875; collector on Regnell's expedition (1873-1876), curator of the Brazilian collections of the Stockholm Riksmuseum 1872-1873, 1876-1883. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Mosén* \[standard form in IPNI: *Mosén*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection S|S]]; duplicates see IH.

#### Eponymy

*Mosenia*Lindman (1891); *Moseniella* Brotherus (1918); *Mosenodendron* R. E. Fries (1900). *Note*: *Mosenthinia* O. Kuntze (1891) is dedicated to "meine lieben Schwester Marie und ihrem Gemahl Franz Mosenthin" (O. Kuntze, Rev. Gen. 1: 16. 1891).
