### Author

Dauphin, Jean (fl. 1904-1908), French botanist (cryptogamist) and teacher, préparateur at the faculty of science in Paris and teacher at an Alsatian school. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Dauphin*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
