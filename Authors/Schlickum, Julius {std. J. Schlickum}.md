### Author

Schlickum, Julius (1804-1884), German (Prussian) pharmacist and botanist at Winningen a.d. Mösel. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. Schlickum*

#### Herbarium and types

Material at [[Collection AMD|AMD]], [[Collection BR|BR]], [[Collection MW|MW]] and [[Collection OXF|OXF]].

#### Note

August Schlickum (b. 7 Aug 1867), son of Julius Schlickum and also pharmacist from Winningen, obtained a degree Dr. phil. Marburg 1895, on a dissertation *Morphologischer und anatomischer Vergleich der Kotyledonen und ersten Laubblätter der Keimpflanzen der Monokotylen*, p. \[i-ii\], \[1\]-80, \[1\]. Marburg 1895, subsequently published as Bibl. bot. 35, 1896. Herbarium material attributed to A. Schlickum was at B. (AG 12(1): 198).
