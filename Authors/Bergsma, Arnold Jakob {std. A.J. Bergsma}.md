### Author

Bergsma, Arnold Jakob (fl. 1857), Dutch botanist, Dr. phil. Utrecht 1857. (*A.J. Bergsma*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Note

It is not clear to us whether A.J. Bergsma was a son of C.A. Bergsma (see below).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 170; JW 4: 374; PR 675; Rehder 1: 170, 202.

### Publications

##### n.20.952. de Parthenogenesi plantarum

**Title**
Dissertatio inauguralis *de Parthenogenesi plantarum*, quam, annuente summo numine, ... pro gradu doctoratus summisque in mathesi et philosophia naturali honoribus ac privilegiis in Academia Rheno-Trajectina rite et legitime consequendis, eruditorum examini submittit Arnoldus Jacobus Bergsma, Rheno-Trajectinus. A.D. xvii m. Martii anni mdcclvii, hora ii. Trajecti ad Rhenum (apud Kemink et filium) 1857. Oct.
**Abbreviated title**: *Parthenogen. pl.*

**Notes**
*Publ*.: 17 Mar 1857 (date of defense thesis), p. \[i-viii\], \[1\]-103. *Copies*: G, U. − On p. \[v\] a dutch title page: *Over de parthenogenesis in het Plantenrijk*... Utrecht (Kemink en Zoon) 1857. Oct. − The printer, Kemink et filium was the same firm which printed the early volumes of Taxon and Regnum vegetabile in the 1950-ies.
