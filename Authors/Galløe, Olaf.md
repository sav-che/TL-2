### Author

Galløe, Olaf (1881-1965), Danish lichenologist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Galløe* \[standard form in IPNI: *Galløe*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection C|C]], some material also at [[Collection L|L]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 215.
