### Author

Arrondeau, E.-Théodore (x-1882), French botanist and highschool teacher, sometime at the Lycée de Toulouse, later residing in Vannes ("Inspecteur d' Académie"). (*Arrondeau*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection P|P]].
