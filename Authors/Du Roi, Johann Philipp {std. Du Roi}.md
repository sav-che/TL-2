### Author

Du Roi, Johann Philipp (1741-1785), German physician, botanist and dendrologist at Braunschweig. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Du Roi*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 486; BM 1: 496; PR 2559-2560.

#### Eponymy
