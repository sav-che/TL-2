### Author

Du Roi, Johann Philipp (1741-1785), German physician, botanist and dendrologist at Braunschweig. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Du Roi* \[standard form in IPNI: *Du Roi*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
