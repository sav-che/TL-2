### Author

Sandi, Alessandro Francesco (1794-1849), Italian botanist and practicing physician at Beluno. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Sandi* \[standard form in IPNI: *Sandi*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Saccardo (2: 96) states that the herbarium was at the Liceo Tiziano at Belluno by 1867; it is described in some critical detail by Minio (1912).

#### Bibliography and biography

BL 2: 416, 713; PR 8026, ed. 1: 8986; Rehder 1: 426; Saccardo, P.A., Storia lett. fl. ven. somm. 111-112. 1869.

#### Biofile
