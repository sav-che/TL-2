### Author

Bisceglie, Vito (fl. 1809), Italian clergyman, canon cantor at the church of Terlizzi, prov. Bari, Apulia and botanist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Bisceglie*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
