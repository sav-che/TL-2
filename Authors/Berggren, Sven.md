### Author

Berggren, Sven (1837-1917), Swedish botanist at Lund, travelled to and collected in Spitsbergen (1868), Greenland (1870), N. Zealand, California 1874-1875. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Berggr.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Berggr.*\]
