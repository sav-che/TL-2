### Author

Errera, Leo \[-Abram\] (1858-1905), Belgian botanist of Italian/German descent, physiologist, evolutionary biologist and philosopher. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Errera* \[standard form in IPNI: *Errera*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BR|BR]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 185.
