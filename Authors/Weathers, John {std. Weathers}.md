### Author

Weathers, John (1867-1928), British horticulturist and gardener; sometime Kew gardener; assistant secretary Royal Horticultural Society, later market gardener at Isleworth. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Weathers* \[standard form in IPNI: *Weathers*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BM|BM]] (545 orchids and sketches of orchids).

#### Bibliography and biography

Backer p. 663; Barnhart 3: 467 (b. 17 Jun 1867, d. 10 Mar 1928); BM 5: 2275; Desmond p. 647; Hortus 3: 1206; MW p. 526; NI 2113a-2115; Tucker 1: 735, 3: 328; Zander ed. 10, p. 730, ed. 11, p. 833.
