### Author

Perrin, Ida Southwell (née Robins; Mrs. Henry Perrin), (1860-?), British popular writer on botany and botanical artist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Perrin* \[standard form in IPNI: *Perrin*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BL 2: 215, 701; BM 8: 1000; Kew 4: 277; NI 1509; Tucker 1: 778-779.
