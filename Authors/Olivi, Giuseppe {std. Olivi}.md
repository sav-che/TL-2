### Author

Olivi, Giuseppe (1769-1795), Italian zoologist at Padua 
**Abbreviated name**: *Olivi* \[standard form in IPNI: *Olivi*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MCVE|Museo Civico Storia naturale]], Venetia (algae).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 28; BM 3: 1469; Bossert p. 293; Cesati p. 57, 298-304; CSP 4: 675; Dryander 3: 346, 415; Moebius p. 334; PR 6831 (ed. 1: 7632); RS p. 121; Saccardo 1: 119, 2: 79.
- Camerano, L., Boll. Mus. Zool. Anat. comp. r. Univ. Torino 20: 1-6. 1905.
- Nissen, C, Zool. Buchill. 302. 1969 (b. 19 Mar 1769, d. 25 Aug 1795).
- Poggendorff, J. C, Biogr.-lit. Handw.-Buch 2: 322. 1858.
- Wittrock, V. B., Acta Horti Berg. 3(2): 164. 1903, 3(3): 179. 1905.

#### Eponymy

*Olivia* Bertoloni (1810). *Note*: *Olivia* Montagne (1846) and *Olivia* S. F. Gray (1821) may also commemorate him.
