### Author

Häyren, Ernst Fredrik (1878-1957), Finnish botanist, associate professor of botany at Helsinki University. (*Häyr.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection H|H]]; other material at [[Collection TURA|TURA]], [[Collection UPS|UPS]], [[Collection W|W]].
