### Author

Rossi, Pietro (1738-1804), Italian botanist and entomologist at Pisa. 
**Abbreviated name**: *P. Rossi*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Note

for Pietro Rossi (1871-1950) see Atti Soc. ital. Sci. nat. Milano 89(3/4): 242-245. 1950.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 4: 1735; Dryander 3: 395, 541; Herder p. 411; PR 7777-7778, ed. 1: 8738-8739; Rehder 1: 199, 3: 527; Saccardo 1: 142.
