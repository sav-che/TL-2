### Author

Schneider, Ferdinand (1834-1882), Swiss botanist and pharmacist in Basel. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Fe. Schneid.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Ferd.Schneid.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection G|G]]. – Letters at [[Collection G|G]].
