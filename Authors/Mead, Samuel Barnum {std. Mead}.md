### Author

Mead, Samuel Barnum (1798-1880), American botanist and physician; B. A. Yale 1820; from 1824-1835 practicing at Greenwich, Conn.; from 1833 at Augusta, 111. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Mead* \[standard form in IPNI: *Mead*\]
