### Author

\* Detharding, Georg Gustav (1765-1838), German botanist born at Rostock 22 Jun 1765, died ib. 3 Feb 1838. (*Dethard.*).

[[Collection ROST|ROST]], further material at [[Collection B|B]], [[Collection L|L]].
