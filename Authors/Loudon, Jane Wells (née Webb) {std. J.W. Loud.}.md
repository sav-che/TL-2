### Author

Loudon, Jane Wells (née Webb) (1807-1858), British botanist and horticulturist; married (1830) John Claudius Loudon. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. W. Loud.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Note

Published anonymously *Young Lady's book of botany* (1838). See NI and PR for futher "Ladies' flower books." Jane Loudon also edited a later edition of her husband's *Hortus britannicus* (1850).
