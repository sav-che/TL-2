### Author

Lagerberg, Karl Erik Torsten (1882-x), Swedish botanist; Dr. phil. Uppsala 1909; from 1910 at Skogshögskolan 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lagerberg*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection LD|LD]]; further material at [[Collection C|C]] and [[Collection E|E]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 334; BFM 1439; BL 2: 477, 516, 531, 677; Bossert p. 225; Hirsch p. 166; IH 2: 403; Kew 3: 347; KR p. 365-367; LS suppl. 15339-15358; MW p. 260; Stevenson p. 1248.
- Anon., Bot. Not. 1909: 303, 1910: 198, 1912: 227.
- Wittrock, V. B., Acta Horti Berg. 3(3): 20. 1905.

#### Composite works

For L's *Vilda växter i Norden* (ed. 1, 1937-1939; ed. 2, 1947-1949; ed. 3, 1952-1958) see BL 2: 477. He was editor of *Sv. bot. Tidskr.* vols. 12-32, 1918-1938.
