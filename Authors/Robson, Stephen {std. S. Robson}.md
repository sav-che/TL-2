### Author

Robson, Stephen \[1741-1779), British (English) botanist at Darlington (Durham), uncle of Edward Robson; linen manufacturer and grocer. 
**Abbreviated name**: *S. Robson*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection YRK|YRK]].
