### Author

Sigriansky, Alexandre (1882-?), Russian botanist; studied natural sciences at Geneva 1911-1913; worked at the Botanical Institute; Dr. sci. nat. 1913. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Sigriansky* \[standard form in IPNI: *Sigr.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
