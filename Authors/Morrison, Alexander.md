### Author

Morrison, Alexander (1849-1913), Scottish physician and botanist; to Australia 1877; practiced in Melbourne, travelled in the New Hebrides 1892; West Australian Government botanist in the Bureau of Agriculture 1897-1906. 
**Abbreviated name**: *A. Morrison* \[standard form in IPNI: *Morrison*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection E|E]]; further important sets at [[Collection B|B]], [[Collection CANB|CANB]], [[Collection K|K]] and [[Collection PERTH|PERTH]]. The medical and botanical library went to Tasmanian University.
