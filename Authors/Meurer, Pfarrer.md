### Author

Meurer, Pfarrer (*fl*. 1848), German clergyman and botanist; high school teacher at Rinteln. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Meurer* \[standard form in IPNI: *Meurer*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Note

Nothing is known to us about Pfarrer (clergyman) Meurer beyond the details given here. He should not be confused with the Thüringian F. F. Meurer (1809-1882), treated above.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 3: 1295; PR 6130; Rehder 1: 382.
