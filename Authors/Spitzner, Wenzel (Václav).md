### Author

Spitzner, Wenzel (Václav) (1852-1907), Bohemian botanist (lichenologist). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Spitzn.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Spitzn.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BRNU|BRNU]].

#### Bibliography and biography
