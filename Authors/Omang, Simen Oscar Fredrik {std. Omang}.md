### Author

Omang, Simen Oscar Fredrik (1867-1953), Norwegian botanist; specialist on *Hieracium*. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Omang*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection O|O]]; further material at [[Collection BM|BM]], [[Collection C|C]], [[Collection GB|GB]], [[Collection H|H]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 29; BJI 2: 127; BL 2: 50, 457, 697; BM 7: 955; CSP 17: 616; Hirsch p. 219; Kew 4: 188; Kleppa p. 8, 329 \[index\].
- Anon., Bot. Not. 1902: 158 (stipend), 1904: 138 (id.).
- Holmboe, J., Norsk biogr. Leks. 10: 484-485. 1949.
- Størmer, P., Blyttia 12: 153-157. 1954 (obit., portr., bibl., b. 23 Mai 1867; *Hieracium* libr. and herb. to O in 1926).
- Wittrock, V. B., Acta Horti Berg. 3(3): 184. 1905.
