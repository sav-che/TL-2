### Author

Omang, Simen Oscar Fredrik (1867-1953), Norwegian botanist; specialist on *Hieracium*. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Omang*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection O|O]]; further material at [[Collection BM|BM]], [[Collection C|C]], [[Collection GB|GB]], [[Collection H|H]].
