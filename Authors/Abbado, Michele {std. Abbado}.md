### Author

Abbado, Michele (fl. 1900-1915), Italian phytopathologist, Dr. sci. nat., assistant at the R. Scuola superiore d'Agricoltura, and professor of natural history at the Lyceum Beccacia, Milano. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Abbado*

[[Collection PAD|PAD]].
