### Author

Mancini, Vincenzo (1853-?), Italian mycologist and ethnologist at Conegliano and Arellino. (*Mancini*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection PAD|PAD]].

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 3: 1232; Bossert p. 251; CSP 16: 1036; IH 2: 498; Kew 3: 577; LS 16738-16745; Saccardo 1: 101.

#### Composite works

Contributed to *Sylloge hymenomycetum* by J. Cuboni for P. A. Saccardo, *Sylloge fungorum*, vols 5-6, 1887-1888 q.v.
