### Author

Llano, George Albert (1911-x), Cuban-born American lichenologist; student of C. W. Dodge. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Llano* \[standard form in IPNI: *Llano*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection FH|FH]]; further material at [[Collection MIN|MIN]] and [[Collection US|US]].
