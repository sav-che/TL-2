### Author

Obermeyer \[Obermeijer\], Anna Amelia, later Mrs. Antonie Mauve (1907-x), South African botanist at the National Herbarium, Pretoria. (*Oberm.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection NH|NH]] and [[Collection PRE|PRE]]; further material in [[Collection BM|BM]], [[Collection F|F]], [[Collection K|K]], [[Collection SRGH|SRGH]].
