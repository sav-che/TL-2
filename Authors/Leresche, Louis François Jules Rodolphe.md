### Author

Leresche, Louis François Jules Rodolphe (1808-1885), Swiss botanist and plant collector; clergyman at Château d'Oex(Vaud) 1845-1866. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Leresche* \[standard form in IPNI: *Leresche*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Main material (personal herb.) at [[Collection LAU|LAU]]; duplicates in many herbaria, see IH.
