### Author

\* Bluff, Mathias Joseph (1805-1837), German (Köln-born) physician and botanist, studied medicine at Köln, Dr. med. ib. 1829, practicing physician at Aachen. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Bluff*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
