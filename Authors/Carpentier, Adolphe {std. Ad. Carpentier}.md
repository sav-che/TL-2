### Author

Carpentier, Adolphe (fl. 1869), French botanist, studied at the École Superieure de Pharmacie,Paris. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Ad. Carpentier*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
