### Author

Carpentier, Adolphe (fl. 1869), French botanist, studied at the École Superieure de Pharmacie,Paris. (*Ad. Carpentier*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Kew 1: 482; Rehder 2: 85, 3: 338; Tucker 1: 141.

#### Biofile
