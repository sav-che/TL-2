### Author

Jüngst, Ludwig Volrad (1804-1880), German botanist at Bielefeld. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Jüngst* \[standard form in IPNI: *Jüngst*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
