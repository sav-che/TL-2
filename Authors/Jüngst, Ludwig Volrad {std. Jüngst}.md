### Author

Jüngst, Ludwig Volrad (1804-1880), German botanist at Bielefeld. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Jüngst*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 2: 949; PR 4512-4514 (ed. 1: 4974-4976); Rehder 5: 440.
