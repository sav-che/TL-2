### Author

Ernst, Otto (1906-x), German botanist and ecologist, born 26 Nov 1906 at Bad Salzungen (Thüringen), moved to Saalfeld a. d. Saale, visited schools in Sonneberg (Thüringen), Meiningen and Wiesbaden, from 1926 studying natural sciences at Tübingen, Marburg, and Frankfurt am Main. 
**Abbreviated name**: *O. Ernst*

Unknown.
