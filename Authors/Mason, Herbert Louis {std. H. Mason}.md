### Author

Mason, Herbert Louis (1896-x), American botanist; B.A. Stanford 1922; Ph.D. Berkeley 1932; Mills. coll. 1923-1925; Univ. of Calif. Berkeley, in various functions from 1925. 
**Abbreviated name**: *H. Mason* \[standard form in IPNI: *H.Mason*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection UC|UC]]; early collections at [[Collection DS|DS]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 457 (b. 3 Jan 1896); Bossert p. 256; Hortus 3: 1199 ("H. L. Mason"); IH 2: 511; Langman p. 483-484; Roon p. 74; Zander ed. 10, p. 689, ed. 11, p. 786.
- Reifschneider, O., Biogr. Nevada bot. 115-116. 1964 (portr.).

#### Note

Mason published *A flora of the marshes of California*, Berkeley and Los Angeles (University of California Press) 1957, viii, 878 p.
