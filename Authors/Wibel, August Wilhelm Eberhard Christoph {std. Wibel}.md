### Author

Wibel, August Wilhelm Eberhard Christoph (1775-1813), German (Württemberg) physician and botanist; studied in Jena from 1797; Dr. med. 1797; practicing at Wertheim am Main (Baden). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Wibel*

#### Herbarium and types

Material at [[Collection GOET|GOET]], [[Collection H|H]] (some bryoph.), [[Collection JE|JE]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Backer p. 625; Barnhart 3: 490; BM 5: 2314; Frank 3 (Anh.): 113; GR p. 54 (b. 1 Aug 1775, d. 25 Dec 1813), cat. p. 73; Hegi 1: 245, 844; Herder p. 190; Hortus 3: 1207; PR 10217-10219, ed. 1: 11199-11201; Rehder 1: 375; TL-2/see E. Berger; Zander ed. 11, p. 836.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Brit. Mus. gen. cat. print. books 74. 1965.
- Sayre, G., Bryologist 80(3): 520. 1977.

#### Eponymy

*Wibelia* P.G. Gaertner, B. Meyer et Scherbius (1801); *Wibelia* Bernhardi (1801); *Wibelia* Persoon (1805).
