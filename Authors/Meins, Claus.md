### Author

Meins, Claus (1806-1873), German theologist and botanist; high school teacher at the Glückstadt gymnasium 1848-1865; from 1865 clergyman at St. Margarethen. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Meins* \[standard form in IPNI: *Meins*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
