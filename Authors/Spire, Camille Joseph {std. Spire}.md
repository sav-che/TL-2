### Author

Spire, Camille Joseph (*fl*. 1903), French military colonial physician and botanist; in Indochina ca. 1903. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Spire* \[standard form in IPNI: *Spire*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection P|P]]. – Some further material was at [[Collection B|B]] (destroyed).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 310; IH 2: (files); MW p. 464; SK 1: 497 ("O. Spire"); Tucker 1: 664; Urban-Berl. p. 400.

*Spirella* Constantin (1912).
