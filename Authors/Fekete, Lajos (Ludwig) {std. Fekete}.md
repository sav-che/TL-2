### Author

Fekete, Lajos \[Ludwig\] (1837-1916), Hungarian forester, born 18 Jun 1837 in Torda (Siebenbürgen), prof. at the forestry academy in Selmecbánya (Schemnitz), died 29 Jun 1916 in Selmecbánya. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Fekete*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
