### Author

Mann, Robert James (1817-1886), British physician (MD St. Andrews 1854) and botanist at Norwich and Buxton; in Natal 1857-1864. 
**Abbreviated name**: *R. Mann* \[standard form in IPNI: *R.Mann*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection K|K]].

#### Bibliography and biography

BB p. 205; BL 2: 259, 687; BM 3: 1234; CSP 4: 216, 8: 319, 10: 707, 708, 16: 1043; Desmond p. 684; DNB 36: 43-44; GR cat. p. 67; Jackson p. 44; Kew 3: 580; MW p. 298.
- Anon., Leopoldina 22: 170. 1886 (d. 4 Aug 1886); Nature 34: 346. 1886 (obit.).
- Kent, D. H., Brit. herb. 67. 1957.

#### Composite works

edited Henry Brooks, *Natal*; a... description of the colony, London 1876, viii, 336 p., *6 pl., 8 photos*, 2 maps, see BM 1: 257.
