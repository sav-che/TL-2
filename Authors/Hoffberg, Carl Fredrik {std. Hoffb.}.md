### Author

Hoffberg, Carl Fredrik (1729-1790), Swedish physician and botanist, ultimately court-physician (1762-1788) at Stockholm. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Hoffb.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 188; BM 2: 855; Dryander 2: 95, 516, 3: 21; GR p. 508; KR p. 298-299; Krempelh. 1: 515; LS 12393; PR 4121 (ed. 1: 4551-4552): Rehder 5: 397; SO 650b, 650c, 650d, 1299, 1823.
