### Author

Hoffberg, Carl Fredrik (1729-1790), Swedish physician and botanist, ultimately court-physician (1762-1788) at Stockholm. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Hoffb.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Hoffb.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
