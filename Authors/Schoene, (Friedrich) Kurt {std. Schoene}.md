### Author

Schoene, \[Friedrich\] Kurt (1880-?), German (Saxonian) botanist; studied at Leipzig, Dresden and, from 1902, at Jena; Dr. phil. Jena 1905. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Schoene*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Note

For the Saxonian floristic botanist Richard Schöne (b. 4 Feb 1882, d. 18 Aug 1955) see Ber. Arbeitsgem. sächs. Bot. ser. 2. 8: 178-183. 1968.
