### Author

Petrov, Vsevolod Alexeevic (1896-x), Russian botanist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Petrov* \[standard form in IPNI: *Petrov*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection LE|LE]].

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 8: 1004; IF suppl. 3: 216; Kew 4: 288; MW p. 385.

#### Eponymy

*Petrovanella* Kylin (1956) is named for J. Petrová, a worker in Rhodophyceae.
