### Author

Haartman, Johan Gustav (1777-1799), Finnish botanist; born in Sauvo. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Haartm.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Haartm.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Dryander 4: 148; Saelan p. 146; TR 509.
