### Author

Brun, Jacques-Joseph (1826-1908), Swiss diatomist at Genève. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Brun* \[standard form in IPNI: *Brun*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection G|G]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 102.
- Briquet, Annuaire Cons. Jard. bot. Genève 4: 3-4. 1900, 5: 3-4. 1901, 6: 176. 1902, 7/8: 337-338. 1904, 10: 4. 1906.
- Wornardt, Int. direct. diatomists 33. 1968.
- Koster, Taxon 18: 550. 1969.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 268; BM 1: 265, 6: 150; CSP 13: 867; Jackson p. 278, 343; Kew 1: 403; KR p. 107; MW p. 56.
- Briquet, Journal de Genève 24 Nov 1908 (fide Briquet 1940).
- Julliard, Cact. Ouvr. Univ. Genève 181. 1909.
- Sarasin, Mém. Soc. Phys. Hist. nat. Genève 36: 21-22. 1909.
- Beauverd, Bull. Herbier Boissier ser. 2. 8: 982.
- Briquet, Bull. Soc. bot. Suisse 50a: 94-98. 1940 (bibl.)

#### Eponymy

*Brunia* Tempère ex G. B. De Toni (1894); *Bruniopsis* G. Karsten (1928); *Neobrunia* O. Kuntze (1894). -*Note*: *Brunia* Linnaeus (1753) commemorates the English naval physician Alexander Brown (fl. 1692-1698), fide Backer, Verkl. woordenb. 87. 1936.
