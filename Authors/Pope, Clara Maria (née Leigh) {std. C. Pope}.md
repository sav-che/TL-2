### Author

Pope, Clara Maria (née Leigh) (*fl*. 1760s-1838), British flower painter, wife of the actor Alexander Pope (Francis Wheatley). 
**Abbreviated name**: *C. Pope* \[standard form in IPNI: *C.Pope*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. Drawings of Paeonia at [[Collection BM|BM]].

#### Bibliography and biography

BB p. 247; Blunt p. 4, 208, 211-212, *pl. 37*; BM 4: 1596 (drawings); Desmond p. 500; DNB 46; DU 85; NI 122, see also 1: 436-438; TL-2/1283-1284.
