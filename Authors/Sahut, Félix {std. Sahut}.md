### Author

Sahut, Félix (1835-1904), French horticulturist, viticulturist and botanist at the Établissement d'Horticulture Claude Sahut, Montpellier. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Sahut* \[standard form in IPNI: *Sahut*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
