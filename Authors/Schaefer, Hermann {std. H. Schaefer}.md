### Author

Schaefer, Hermann (*fl*. 1872), German (Lausitz) botanist. (*H. Schaefer*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 4: 1821; CSP 8: 844; Rehder 1: 386.

#### Note

Little is known to us about B. Schaefer, author of a *Flora von Brotterode*, Cassel (Druck von Weber & Weidemeyer) 1906, cover-t.p., p. 1-44. *Copy*: B. – Born 29 Mar 1864, Dr. phil. Marburg 1889.
