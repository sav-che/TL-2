### Author

Mayrhoffer, Johann Nepomuk \[Mayrhofer, I.N.\] (1764-1832), Austrian botanical artist, later at München. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Mayrh.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Mayrh.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
