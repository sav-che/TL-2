### Author

Monguillon, Eugène Louis Honoré (1865-1940), French teacher and cryptogamist; at various schools in the département Sarthe; after 1923 retired at Mans. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Mong.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Mong.*\]
