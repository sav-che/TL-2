### Author

Maille, Alphonse (1813-1865), French botanist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Maille* \[standard form in IPNI: *Maille*\]

#### Herbarium and types

The Maille herbarium, which contained material from various collectors, was split up and sold as *Reliquiae Mailleanae*, with important sets at [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection FI|FI]], [[Collection G|G]], [[Collection K|K]], [[Collection LE|LE]], [[Collection P|P]], [[Collection PI|PI]], and [[Collection W|W]]. See TL-2/3907 for a catalogue. Maille himself had planned to issue the material as exsiccatae together with T. Puel. After his death distribution took place by L. Kralik. Many identifications are by E. St.-Ch. Cosson.
