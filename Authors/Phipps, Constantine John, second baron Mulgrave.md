### Author

Phipps, Constantine John, second baron Mulgrave (1744-1792), British captain in the Royal Navy; arctic explorer. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Phipps* \[standard form in IPNI: *Phipps*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Plants collected on the voyage towards the North Pole in the Racecourse and Carcass, 1773, with Israel Lyons, described by D.C. Solander (in *Voy. North Pole* p. 200-204) at [[Collection BM|BM]].
