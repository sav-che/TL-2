### Author

Lützow, G. (*fl*. 1895), German bryologist. (*G. Lützow*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 3: 1191; CSP 16: 905; Kew 3: 518.

#### Note

Not to be confused with the Prussian botanist Carl Friedrich Wilhelm Lützow (1846-1899), see R. Caspary, Lebensbeschr. ost.-westpreuss. Bot. 246-247. 1912, and J. Dörfler, Botaniker-Adressbuch 24, 236. 1896.
