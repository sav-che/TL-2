### Author

Dietzow, W. Ludwig \[von\] (fl. 1909-1938), German school teacher and bryologist living at Grünhagen (Kreis Pr. Holland). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Dietzow*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
