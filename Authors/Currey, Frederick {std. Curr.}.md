### Author

Currey, Frederick (1819-1881), British mycologist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Curr.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Curr.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection K|K]]; mss on fungi at [[Collection LINN|LINN]].
*Ref*.: BB p. 80; IH 2: 147.
