### Author

Michot, Norbert Louis (1803-1887), Belgian botanist; educated at Thuin and Tournay; abbé at Mons from 1840; almoner of the Carmelites 
**Abbreviated name**: *Michot*

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection BR|BR]].
