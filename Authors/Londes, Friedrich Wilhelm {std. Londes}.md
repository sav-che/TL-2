### Author

Londes, Friedrich Wilhelm (1780-1807), German botanist at Goettingen. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Londes*

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection H|H]].

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 7: 24, 5(1): 165; Barnhart 2: 399; BM 3: 1167; CSP 4: 78 Herder p. 94, 185; IH 2: 457: Kew 3: 489; PR 5594-5597 (ed. 1: 6255-6258); Rehder 1: 84, 5: 521; SO add. 784a; TR 829.
- Lipschitz, S., Fl. URSS fontes no. 641. 1975.

#### Eponymy

*Londesia* F. E. L. Fischer & C. A. Meyer (1836).
