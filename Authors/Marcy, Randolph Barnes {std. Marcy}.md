### Author

Marcy, Randolph Barnes (1812-1887), American explorer, soldier, naturalist, and author. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Marcy* \[standard form in IPNI: *Marcy*\]

**Title**
32d Congress, 2nd Session. Senate. Executive, no. 54. *Exploration of the Red River of Louisiana*, in the year 1852: by Randolph B. Marey,... assisted by George B. McClellan,... With reports on the natural history of the country, and numerous illustrations. Washington (Robert Armstrong,...) 1853. Oct.
