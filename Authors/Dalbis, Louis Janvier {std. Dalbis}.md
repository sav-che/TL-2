### Author

Dalbis, Louis Janvier (fl. 1912-1919), French botanist, "Licencié ès Sciences" at the Faculty of Sciences at Paris, student of G. Bonnier, Dr. Fac. Sci. Paris 1921. (*Dalbis*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
