### Author

Doumenjou, Jean-Bazile (?-1856), French botanist, birth date unknown to us, died 9 Mar 1856 at Villemagne (Aude), studied the flora of the Montagne-Noire. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Doumenjou*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection FI|FI]]-W.
