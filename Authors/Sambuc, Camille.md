### Author

Sambuc, Camille (1859-?), French naval pharmacist from Toulon, later professor of pharmacy at the Faculté de Médecine et de Pharmacie de Lyon. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Sambuc* \[standard form in IPNI: *Sambuc*\]

#### Herbarium and types
