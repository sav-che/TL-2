### Author

Wirsing, Adam Ludwig (1734-1797), German (Saxonian) botanical artist at Nürnberg. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Wirsing* \[standard form in IPNI: *Wirsing*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – A collection of 120 col. copper engravings, without text "Sammlung von verschiedenen nach der Natur illuminierten Pflanzen", Nürnberg, by A.L. Wirsing, is at the Natl. Library, Vienna.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 2: 747, 5: 2340; Dryander 3: 78; NI 2: 274 (index); PR 9496, 9500, 10343, ed. 1: 11316; Rehder 1: 262; TL-1/1327, 1331; TL-2/15.130, 15.135

#### Biofile

- Anon., Cat. gén. livr. impr, Bibl. natl. 225: 291-293. 1976.
- Nissen, C, Philobiblon 6: 347. 1933.

#### Composite works

- (1) Engraver for M.F. Ledermüller, *Phisicalisch mikroskopische Vorstellung*... Nürnberg 1765 (see NI 1157; see also no. 1160).
- (2) Engraver, J.C. Schaeffer, *Fung. Bavar. Palat. nasc.* 1762-1771, TL-2/10.476.
- (3) Engraver and publisher, C.J. Trew, *Hort. nitid.* 1750-1786 (TL-2/15.130) and pl. *rar*. 1763 (TL-2/15.135).
