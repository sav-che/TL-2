### Author

Cicioni, Giulio (fl. 1910), Italian clergyman, botanist and taxonomist, professor and abbot, at seminary of Perugia. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Cicioni*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
