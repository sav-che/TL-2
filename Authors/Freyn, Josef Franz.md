### Author

Freyn, Josef Franz (1845-1903), Bohemian engineer, botanist and "Fürstlich colloredo'scher Baurath" (AG) in Prague. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Freyn* \[standard form in IPNI: *Freyn*\]
