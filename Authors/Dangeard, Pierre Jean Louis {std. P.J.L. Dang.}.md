### Author

Dangeard, Pierre Jean Louis (1895-1970), French algologist at Bordeaux, son of P. C. A. Dangeard. 
**Abbreviated name**: *P.J.L. Dang.* \[standard form in IPNI: *P.J.L.Dang.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BORD|BORD]].
*Ref*.: Koster, Taxon 18: 551. 1969.
