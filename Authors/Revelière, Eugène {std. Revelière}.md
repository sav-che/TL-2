### Author

Revelière, Eugène (1822-1892), French botanist and entomologist at Saumur; collected in Corsica 1854 and 1856-1858, later also in Algeria; ultimately living in Corsica. (*Revelière*).

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection GFW|GFW]], [[Collection HEID|HEID]], [[Collection L|L]], [[Collection LE|LE]], [[Collection LY|LY]], [[Collection MPU|MPU]], [[Collection W|W]]. – According to Souché (1901) the original herbarium was with R's brother J. Revelière, Blaise (Loire-Inf.).
