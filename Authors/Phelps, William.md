### Author

Phelps, William (1776-1856), British clergyman, topographer and botanist; B.A. Oxford 1797, rector at Oxcombe 1851. 
**Abbreviated name**: *W. Phelps* \[standard form in IPNI: *Phelps*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 80; BB p. 243 (d. 17 Aug 1856); BM 4: 1564; Desmond p. 492 (d. 17 Aug 1856); DNB 45: 150; Herder p. 235; Jackson p. 233; Kew 4: 292; PR 7115 (ed. 1: 7981); Rehder 5: 669.

**Title**

Arranged according to their time of flowering under each month of the year... London (printed by Harding and Wright,... for Lackington, Allen and Co.,...) 1810. Oct. (in fours).
