### Author

Steinvorth, Heinrich (1817-1905), German (Hannoverian) botanist and high school teacher at the Johanneum, Lüneburg; later in retirement at Hannover. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Steinvorth* \[standard form in IPNI: *Steinv.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection HAN|HAN]].

#### Note

Johannes Steinvorth wrote, with R. von Fischer-Benzon as senior author, *Ueber die Flora der Umgegend von Hadersleben*... Hadersleben 1873, see P. Ascherson, Bot. Zeit. 31: 443-444. 1873. See also R.J.D. Fischer-Benzon in P. Prahl, Krit. Fl. Schl.-Holst. 2(1). 56-57. 1890.
