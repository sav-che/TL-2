### Author

Salmon, John Drew (ca. 1802-1859), British botanist and ornithologist; manager of the Wenham Lake Ice Company; at Thetford 1835-1843; later at Godalming. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J.D. Salmon*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection NWH|NWH]] (2000); further material in [[Collection GOD|GOD]], [[Collection OXF|OXF]] and [[Collection RTE|RTE]]. Diaries at [[Collection NWH|NWH]].

#### Bibliography and biography

#### Composite works

- (1) Contributed Appendix A, *An outline of the flora of the neighbourhood of Godalming* to E. Newman et al. (anonymous), *The letters of Rusticus* 1849 (Edward Newman, 1801-1872, TL-2/6776-6777). Salmon plants cited in this book are at GOD (Kent 1957).
- (2) James Alexander Brewer (1818-1886), *Fl. Surrey* 1863, TL-2/750, was based on J.D. Salmon manuscripts.
