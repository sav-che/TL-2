### Author

Tovey, James Richard (1873-1922), Australian botanist; assistant at the National Herbarium, South Yarra, Melbourne 1889-1922. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Tovey* \[standard form in IPNI: *Tovey*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MEL|MEL]]; further material at [[Collection A|A]], [[Collection E|E]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 394; BL 1: 73, 75, 321; BM 5: 2129; Bossert p. 404; HR (b. 16 Apr 1873); TL-2/6350.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Hedwigia 66: (119). 1926 (d. 30 Dec 1922); Vict. Natural. 39: 115. (obit. not.), 127 (d.) 1923.
- Tovey, J.R., Vict. Natural. 24: 146-147. 1908 (remarks on MEL).
