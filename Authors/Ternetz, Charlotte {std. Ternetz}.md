### Author

Ternetz, Charlotte (*fl*. 1900), Swiss high school teacher and botanist; Dr. phil. Zürich 1900; teacher at Basel. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Ternetz* \[standard form in IPNI: *Ternetz*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
