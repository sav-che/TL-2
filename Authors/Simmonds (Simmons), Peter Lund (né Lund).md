### Author

Simmonds (Simmons), Peter Lund (né Lund) (1814-1897), Danish-born British author, journalist, publisher, editor and exhibition commissioner in London. 
**Abbreviated name**: *P. Simmonds* \[standard form in IPNI: *P.Simmonds*\]
