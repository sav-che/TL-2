### Author

Browne, Daniel Jay (1804-1867), American author. (*D. J. Browne*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 265; BM 1: 262; CSP 1: 666; Jackson p. 360; Kew 1: 397; Langman p. 165; PR 1252.
