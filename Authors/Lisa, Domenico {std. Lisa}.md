### Author

Lisa, Domenico (1801-1867) Italian botanist in Piemonte, head gardner at the Torino botanical garden. (*Lisa*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection TO|TO]] (2.000); further material (e.g. musci) at [[Collection PI|PI]].
