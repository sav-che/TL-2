### Author

Macchiati, Luigi (1852-1921), Italian cryptogamist; until 1905 at Savona; later scientific director and president of the R. Instituto Tecnico, Venezia. (*Macch.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection PAD|PAD]].
