### Author

Morel, L. F. (*fl*. 1865), French clergyman, high school teacher and mycologist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *L. Morel*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Note

Morel was also the author of various religious treatises, such as Vie et doctrine de J. C., L'art de bien nourir, Catéchisme romain, and Montée de l'âme vers dieu.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 3: 1349; PR 6423.
