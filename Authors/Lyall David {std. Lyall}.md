### Author

Lyall David (1817-1895), British surgeon, naturalist and explorer; on Ross's Antarctic Voyage (1839-1842); in New Zealand 1847; in the Arctic with Belcher 1852; on Oregon Boundary Commission 1858-1861. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lyall*

#### Herbarium and types

In general at [[Collection K|K]]; the plants of the Oregon Boundary Commission (in part collected by John Buttle) both at [[Collection GH|GH]] and [[Collection K|K]] with duplicates in various other herbaria.
