### Author

Wohlleben, Johann Friedrich (x-1796), German (Westphalen) botanist; physician in Halle; Dr. med. Halle 1796. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Wohll.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Wohll.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection REG|REG]] (Lich.).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 512; BM 5: 2347; Herder p. 190; PR 10375, ed. 1: 11336; Rehder 1: 373, 375; TL-2/4483; Tucker 1: 752.
