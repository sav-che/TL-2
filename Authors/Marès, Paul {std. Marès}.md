### Author

Marès, Paul (1826-1900), French botanist, explorer of Algeria and the Balearic Islands. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Marès* \[standard form in IPNI: *Marès*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MPU|MPU]]; other material at [[Collection AL|AL]], [[Collection FI|FI]], [[Collection LY|LY]], [[Collection P|P]], [[Collection W|W]]. – 50 letters to Malinvaud at [[Collection P|P]].

#### Eponymy

*Maresia* Pomel (1874) is in all likelihood dedicated to P. Marès.

### Publications

##### n.5403. Catalogue raisonné des plantes vasculaires des îles Baléares

**Title**
*Catalogue raisonné des plantes vasculaires des îles Baléares*... Paris (G. Masson, éditeur ...). 1880. Oct.
**Abbreviated title**: *Cal. pl. vasc. Baléares*.

**Notes**
*Co-author*: Guillaume Vigineix (x-1877).
*Publ*.: Oct 1880 (Nat. Nov. Nov(1) 1880; Bot. Centralbl. 15-19 Nov 1880; Bot. Zeit. 3 Dec 1880; J. Bot. Nov 1880), p. \[i\*-iii\*\], \[i\]-xlvii, \[1, note\], \[1\]-370, \[1, cont.\], *pl. 1-9* (uncol.). *Copies*: G, MO, NY, USDA. – The presentation of a set of proofs of p. 1-160 to the Société botanique de France on 8 Nov 1878 does not constitute effective publication (BSbF 26: 197. 1897). The same holds for the deposit of a larger set of proofs on 23 Mai 1879 (ib.). On 9 Jul 1880 (ib. 27: 242. 1880) Marès presented "le volume complet" to the Société; however it is not clear whether this was a full set of proofs or the effectively published book. It is reviewed by the Bull. Soc. bot. France 27(bibl.): 148-150. Nov-Dec. 1880.
*Ref*.: Willkom, M., Bot. Centralbl. 6: 373-375. 1881.
