### Author

Janka \[von Bulcs\], Victor (1837-1900), Austrian soldier and botanist, from 1871 curator of BP. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Janka* \[standard form in IPNI: *Janka*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection CL|CL]]; much material also at [[Collection BP|BP]]. Duplicates of his Balcan collections in many herbaria.
