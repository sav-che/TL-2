### Author

Sperk, Gustav (1845/1846-1870), Russian (Charkow) botanist. (*Sperk*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 4: 1917 (Ghustav Fedorovich Shperk); De Toni 1:cxxviii; Jackson p. 133, 327; PR 8821, 10640, 10641; Rehder 1: 118; TR 1346-1348.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Bot. Zeit. 29: 269. 1871 (d. Aug 1870 in München at the age of 24); Flora 54: 205. 1871 (d.).
- Pitr, A., Vospominaniya o Gh.F. Shperk, *in* Obshch. ispuith. prir. Kharkov, Trud. 3(2), 1871 (n.v., fide BM 4: 1917).
