### Author

Hombron, Jacques Bernard (1800-1852), French explorer on the voyage of the Astrolabe and Zelée (1837-1840); surgeon-botanist; later stationed at Senegal. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Hombr.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Hombr.*\]
