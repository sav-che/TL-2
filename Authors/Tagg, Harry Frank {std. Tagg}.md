### Author

Tagg, Harry Frank (1874 (1873?)-1933), British botanist at the Royal Botanic Garden, Edinburgh from 1894 in charge of its museum; specialist on Rhododendron. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Tagg* \[standard form in IPNI: *Tagg*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection E|E]] (museum material).

#### Bibliography and biography

Backer p. 572; Barnhart 3: 355 (d. 9 Aug 1933); Desmond p. 597 (b. c. 1873); Hortus 3: 1205; LS 39836; MW p. 480-481 (bibl. Rhododendr. publ.); Zander ed. 10, p. 723, ed. 11, p. 825.
