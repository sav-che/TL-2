### Author

Paulsen, Ove Vilhelm (1874-1947), Danish botanist; M.Sc. Copenhagen 1897; Dr. phil. ib. 1911; in the Danish West Indies 1895-1896, on the Pamir expedition 1898-1899; from 1900-1918 curator at the Botanical Museum, Copenhagen; professor of botany at the Danish pharmaceutical college 1920-1944. (*Paulsen*).
