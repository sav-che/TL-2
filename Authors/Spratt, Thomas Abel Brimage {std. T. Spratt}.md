### Author

Spratt, Thomas Abel Brimage (1811-1888), British vice-admiral; member of the mediterranean hydrographical survey. (*T. Spratt*).

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection K|K]] (plants collected by Edward Forbes).
