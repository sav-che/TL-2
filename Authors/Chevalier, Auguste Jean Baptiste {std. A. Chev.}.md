### Author

Chevalier, Auguste Jean Baptiste (1873-1956), French botanist, explorer and bio-historian. 
**Abbreviated name**: *A. Chev.* \[standard form in IPNI: *A.Chev.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection P|P]], [[Collection PC|PC]].
*Ref*.: IH 1 (ed. 6): 357, 2: 125.
