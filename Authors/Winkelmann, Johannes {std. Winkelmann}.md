### Author

Winkelmann, Johannes (1842-1921), German (Berlin) high school teacher, botanist and conservationist; ultimately professor at the Schiller-Realgymnasium in Stettin. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Winkelmann*

#### Herbarium and types

Some material in [[Collection B|B]] (mainly destr.), [[Collection C|C]] and [[Collection POZ|POZ]].

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 2(2): 166; Barnhart 3: 506 (b. 13 Sep 1842, d. 7 Nov 1921); Biol.-Dokum. 19: 10082; BM 5: 2337; CSP 19: 661; SBC p. 135; Tucker 1: 750; Urban-Berl. p. 317, 410.
