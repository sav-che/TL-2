### Author

Pucci, Angiolo (1851-1935), Italian botanist and horticulturist; professor at the Pomological and Horticultural College at Firenze. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Pucci*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection FI|FI]].

#### Bibliography and biography
