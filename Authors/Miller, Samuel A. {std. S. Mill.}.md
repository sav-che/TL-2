### Author

Miller, Samuel A. (1837-1897), American politician and lawyer at Cincinnati; amateur geolist and palaeontologist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *S. Mill.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Andrews p. 303; Quenstedt p. 296. W. R. B., The Ottawa Naturalist 11: 208. 1898 (obit., d. 18 Dec 1897). F. A. B., Geol. Mag. 406: 192. 1898 (obit., b. 28 Aug 1837).
