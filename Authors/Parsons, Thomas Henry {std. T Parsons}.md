### Author

Parsons, Thomas Henry (*fl*. 1926), British gardener, trained at Kew; from 1913 curator of the Peradeniya botanical Garden. 
**Abbreviated name**: *T Parsons*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 52; Kew 4: 242.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Bull misc. Inf. Kew 1913: 417 (app. Peradeniya).
- Gager, C.S., Brooklyn Bot. Gard. Rec. 27: 183. 1938 (dir. Peradeniya from 1914).
