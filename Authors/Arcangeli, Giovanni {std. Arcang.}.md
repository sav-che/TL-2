### Author

Arcangeli, Giovanni (1840-1921), Italian botanist, professor of botany and director of the botanic garden of Pisa. (*Arcang.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown (at [[Collection PI|PI]]?); collections in many herbaria, e.g. bryophytes at [[Collection H|H]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 40.
