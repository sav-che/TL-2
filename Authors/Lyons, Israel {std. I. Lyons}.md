### Author

Lyons, Israel (1739-1775), British astronomer and botanist at Oxford; later with the Board of Longitude, London. (*I. Lyons*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Backer p. 342; Barnhart 2: 416; BB p. 196; Dawson p. 669, 944; Desmond p. 401; DNB 34: 357; Dryander 3: 136, 5: 340; Henrey 2: 54, 55, 256, 3: 79; Jackson p. 249; Kew 3: 525; PR 5703 (ed. 1: 6379); Rehder 5: 529; Tucker 1: 446.

- Baines, J. E., Vict. Natural. 90: 229. 1973 (epon.).

#### Eponymy

*Lyonsia* R. Brown (1810); *Lyonsiaephyllum* H. Deane (1907).
