### Author

Lueder, Franz Hermann Heinrich (x-1791), German horticulturist, "superintendent" at Dannenberg (Lüneburg). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lueder*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 3: 1190; PR 5682 (ed. 1: 6349-6352); Rehder 5: 526.

#### Eponymy

*Luederitzia* K. M. Schumann (1888) is dedicated to August Lüderitz, a German merchant who collected in South Africa, whereas *Bisluederitzia* Kuntze (1903) and *Neoluederitzia* Schinz (1894) commemorate his brother Adolf, who was active in German colonial politics and who perished in Namibia.
