### Author

Rainville, Frédéric (x-1779), French-born botanist, private teacher and translator at Rotterdam; student of grasses. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Rainville*

#### Herbarium and types

Small herbarium Teyler (Haarlem) (see e.g. van Ooststroom 1958).

#### Bibliography and biography

Dawson p. 693; Dryander 3: 428; IH 2: (in press); JW 4: 402; LS 21684.
