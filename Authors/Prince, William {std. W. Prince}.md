### Author

Prince, William (1766-1842), American botanist and horticulturist, proprietor of the "Linnaean botanic Garden" at Flushing, N.Y. 
**Abbreviated name**: *W. Prince*

#### Herbarium and types

Material at [[Collection PH|PH]] (Schweinitz coll).
