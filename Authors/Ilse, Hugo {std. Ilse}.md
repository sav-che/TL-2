### Author

Ilse, Hugo (1835-1900), German botanist and forester at Oderberg (1867), Trier (1868), Hohenheide/Stettin (1870), Pfalzburg (1870). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Ilse*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection B|B]].
