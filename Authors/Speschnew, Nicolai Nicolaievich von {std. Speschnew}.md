### Author

Speschnew, Nicolai Nicolaievich von (1844-1907), Russian mycologist; state mycologist of the Russian department of agriculture in the Caucasus and director of the department's mycological laboratory at Tiflis. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Speschnew*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 309 (d. 26 Feb/11 Mar 1907); Bossert p. 378; LS 25572-25587, 39424-39431.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Allg. Bot. Z. 13: 108. 1907 (d.); Bot. Centralbl. 104: 502. 1907 (d.); Hedwigia 46: (144). 1907 (d.); Nat. Nov. 29: 237. 1907 (d.).
- Kuznetsov, N., Acta Horti Univ. Jurj. 9: 107-110. 1909.
