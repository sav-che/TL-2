### Author

Teschner, Hans (1894-x), German (Schleswig-Holstein) botanist; educated in Breslau and Berlin; on active service 1915-1916; studied natural sciences at the Friedrich-Wilhelms-Universität, Berlin; Dr. phil. ib. 1931. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Teschner* \[standard form in IPNI: *Teschner*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection B|B]] (mainly destr.).

#### Bibliography and biography

BJI 2: 176.

#### Note

Clara Teschner geb. Richartz, b. 22 Mai 1899, Charlottenburg, wife of H. Teschner, took her degree of Dr. phil. Univ. Berlin on 17 Jul 1931 on a thesis *Beiträge zur Entwicklungsgeschichte und Anatomie der Blüte von Erica und ihre systematische Bedeutung*,...
