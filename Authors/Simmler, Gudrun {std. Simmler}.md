### Author

Simmler, Gudrun (1884-?), Austrian botanist; Dr. phil. 1909; worked at the Botanical Institute of the University of Graz. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Simmler* \[standard form in IPNI: *Simmler*\]

#### Herbarium and types
