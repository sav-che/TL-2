### Author

Schoch, Gottlieb (1853-1905), German (Anhalt-Dessau) gardener; ultimately municipal garden director at Magdeburg. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Schoch* \[standard form in IPNI: *Schoch*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
