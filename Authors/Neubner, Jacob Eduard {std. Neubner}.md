### Author

Neubner, Jacob Eduard (1855-?), German lichenologist; Dr. phil. Leipzig 1883; high school teacher at Plaven/Vogtland from 1882. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Neubner* \[standard form in IPNI: *Neubner*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
