### Author

Putterlick, Alois \[Aloys\] (1810-1845), Austrian botanist and physician; custos (adjunct) at the Natural History Museum, Vienna, 1840-1845. (*Putterl.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection W|W]]; mosses also at [[Collection MJ|MJ]] (Jihlava). – Letters at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

#### Eponymy

*Putterlickia* Endlicher (1840) very likely commemorates him; *Putterlichia* W.H. Harvey (1868, *orth. var.*).
