### Author

De Toni, Antonio (1889-1915), Italian botanist (phycologist), born 6 Jun 1889 at Venezia, died 1915. 
**Abbreviated name**: *A. De Toni* \[standard form in IPNI: *A.De Toni*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
