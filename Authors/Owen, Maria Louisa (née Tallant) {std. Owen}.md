### Author

Owen, Maria Louisa (née Tallant) (1825-1913), American botanist at Springfield, Mass. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Owen*

#### Herbarium and types

Springfield Botanical Club, Springfield, Mass. (fide Day 1901).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 39; BL 1: 187, 311; CSP 17: 663; IH 2: (in press); Tucker 1: 534.
- Day, M., Rhodora 3: 259. 1901 (herb.).
- Deane, Walter, Rhodora 36: 153-160. 1914 (obit., portr., b. 13 Feb 1825, d. 8 Jun 1913).

#### Eponymy

*Owenia* F. v. Mueller (1857) is dedicated to Sir Richard Owen (1804-1892), English naturalist and paleontologist.
