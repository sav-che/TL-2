### Author

Glück, Christian Maximilian Hugo (1868-1940), German biologist at Heidelberg. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Glück* \[standard form in IPNI: *Glück*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Material at [[Collection B|B]], [[Collection DUKE|DUKE]] and [[Collection NY|NY]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 228.
