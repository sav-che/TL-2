### Author

Fougeroux de Bondaroy, Auguste Denis (1732-1789), French agronomist and botanist, nephew of Duhamel du Monceau. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Foug.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Foug.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
