### Author

Schultz, Arthur (1838-1915), German (Silesian) floristic botanist and physician practicing at Storkow, Finsterwalde; ultimately in Wiesbaden. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Arth. Schultz* \[standard form in IPNI: *A.Schultz*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection B|B]] (rd 1905; mainly destr.), *Flora istriaca exsiccata*, ed. 1868, 168 nos.; sets at [[Collection A|A]], [[Collection CGE|CGE]], [[Collection GOET|GOET]], [[Collection L|L]], [[Collection MANCH|MANCH]]. – Lichens from Brandenburg in [[Collection M|M]]; other material in [[Collection BM|BM]], [[Collection DAO|DAO]], [[Collection G|G]], [[Collection NMW|NMW]].

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 2(2): 70; Barnhart 3: 244 (b. 29 Mai 1839, d. 31 Jan 1915); Morren ed. 10, p. 11; Saccardo 1: 150; Urban-Berl. 282, 286, 290, 394.
