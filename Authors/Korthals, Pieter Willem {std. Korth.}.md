### Author

Korthals, Pieter Willem (1807-1892), Dutch botanist and traveller in the East Indies, member of the "Natuurkundige Commissie voor Nederlandsch-Indie" Mai 1831-Feb 1836. (*Korth.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection L|L]]. –The diary of his trip to the East Indies (Jul 1831-Aug 1837) is also at [[Collection L|L]]. For duplicates see IH and [[Collection SK|SK]].
