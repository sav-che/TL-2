### Author

Arendt, Johann Josef Franz (*fl*. 1828-1840), German botanist at Osnabrück. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Arendt*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 72; BM 1: 58-59; PR 224-225; SO add 840a; for details see MBL.
