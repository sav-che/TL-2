### Author

Vogl, Balthasar (*fl*. 1888), Austrian botanist and high school teacher at Salzburg. 
**Abbreviated name**: *B. Vogl* \[standard form in IPNI: *B.Vogl*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Biofile

- Janchen, E., Cat. fl. Austr. 1: 41. 1956.
