### Author

Vogl, Balthasar (*fl*. 1888), Austrian botanist and high school teacher at Salzburg. (*B. Vogl*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Biofile

- Janchen, E., Cat. fl. Austr. 1: 41. 1956.

### Publications

##### n.16.300. Flora der Umgebung Salzburgs

**Title**
