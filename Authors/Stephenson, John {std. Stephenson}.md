### Author

Stephenson, John (*fl*. 1820-1830), British physician and botanist; MD Edinburgh. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Stephenson*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 325; BB p. 287; BM 1: 350, 5: 2016; Desmond p. 583; GR p. 428; Jackson p. 201; Langman p. 721; NI 1891; Plesch p. 416; PR 8946, ed. 1: 9906; Rehder 3: 57; Tucker 1: 668.

#### Eponymy

The etymology of *Stephensoniella* Kashyap (1914) was not given.
