### Author

Barrande, Joachim (1799-1883), French palaeontologist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Barrande*

#### Collections

Some material in the Sedgwickian, Cambridge.
*Ref*.: Sherborn, where is the...collection 13. 1940.
