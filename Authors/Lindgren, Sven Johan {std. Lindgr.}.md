### Author

Lindgren, Sven Johan (1810-1849), Swedish botanist; teacher at the Degeberg agricultural college in Västergötland. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lindgr.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Lindgr.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection C|C]]. – Published (with Knut Frederik Thedenius, 1814-1894, and Olof Leopold Sillén 1813-1894): (1). *Musci sveciae exsiccati*, fasc. 1-2, nos. 1-50, Upsala 1835;
- (2). *Musci sveciae exsiccati*, fasc. 3-4, nos. 51-100, Gevaliae 1844;
- (3). *Musci sveciae exsiccati*, fasc. 5-6, nos. 101-150, Gevaliae 1838 (ed. by K. F. Thedenius);
- (4). Musci sveciae exsiccati, fasc. 7-8, nos. 151-200, Gevaliae 1838 (ed. by O. L. Sillén). *Sets*: [[Collection BM|BM]], [[Collection C|C]], [[Collection FH|FH]], [[Collection H|H]], [[Collection PC|PC]], [[Collection QK|QK]], [[Collection S|S]].
*Ref*.: Sayre, G., Mem. New York Bot. Gard. 19(2): 226-227. 1971; Bryologist 80: 512. 1978.
