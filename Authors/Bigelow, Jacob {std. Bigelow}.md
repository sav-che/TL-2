### Author

Bigelow, Jacob (1787-1879), American botanist at Harvard. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Bigel.*

#### Herbarium and types

Mostly [[Collection GH|GH]], but specimens in many collections, see IH (erroneously sub J. M. Bigelow).
*Ref*.: IH 2: 74.
