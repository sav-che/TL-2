### Author

Hall, Thomas Batt (1814-1886), British naturalist at Coggeshall, Essex (1814-1835, 1839-1852), Liverpool (1835-1839), in Australia (1852-1886). 
**Abbreviated name**: *T. Hall* \[standard form in IPNI: *T.B.Hall*\]
