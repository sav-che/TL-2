### Author

Cazalas, Robert (fl. 1930), French biologist, student of Pierre Dangeard at the Faculté des Sciences de l'Université de Paris, Dr. phil. ib. 1930. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Cazalas*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
