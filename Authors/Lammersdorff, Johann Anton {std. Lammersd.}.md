### Author

Lammersdorff, Johann Anton (*fl*. 1781), German botanist, born at Hannover; Dr. med. Gotting. 1781. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lammersd.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 3: 1051; Dryander 3: 441, 5: 315; PR 5020 (ed. 1: 5515).
