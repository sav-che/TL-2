### Author

Fortuné, Henri (fl. 1884-1887), French pharmacist "Préparateur", École supérieure de pharmacie, Montpellier, pharmacist first class who won several prizes in 1884. 
**Abbreviated name**: *H. Fortuné*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Kew 2: 312.

#### Eponymy

*Fortunaea* J. Lindley (1846); *Fortunea* Naudin (1846, *orth. var.*); *Fortunearia* Rehder & E.H. Wilson (1913); and *Fortunella* Swingle (1915) commemorate Robert Fortune (1812-1880).
