### Author

Stenström, Karl Otto Edvard (1858-1901), Swedish botanist; Dr. phil. Uppsala 1890; from 1897 teacher at a high school in Norrköping. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Stenström* \[standard form in IPNI: *Stenstr.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Material at [[Collection C|C]], [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection GB|GB]], [[Collection LD|LD]]. – Hieracium collections at [[Collection S|S]] (left to H. Dahlstedt) (Bot. Not. 1901: 20).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 324; Biol.-Dokum. 17: 8941; BJI 1: 56; BM 5: 2014; CSP 18: 942; IH 2: (files); KR p. 667-668 (b. 10 Dec 1858, d. 16 Jan 1901, bibl.); Rehder 1: 219.
