### Author

Monti, Gaetano Lorenzo (1712-1797), Italian botanist; professor of botany and director (1760-1792) of the botanical garden at Bologna. (*Monti*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BOLO|BOLO]].

#### Note

Not to be confused with his father, Giuseppe Monti (1682-1760), also professor of botany and director of the Bologna botanical garden, whose publications are all pre-linnaean. Lozenzo Monti (*fl*. 1817) pharmacist and botanist at Verona, author of a *Dictionario botanico Veronese* (1817) (*Copy*: FI), is not known to be a relation of the Bologna Monti's.

#### Eponymy

*Montia* Linnaeus (1753), *Montia* P. Miller (1754), *Montiastrum* (A. Gray) Rydberg (1918) and *Montiopsis* O. Kuntze (1898) commemorate Guiseppe Monti (1682-1760), Italian botanist, director of the botanic garden in Bologna. *Montira* Aublet (1775) commemorates a Monsieur de Monti, French planter and colonial officer in Guyana, who supported Aublet's botanical exploration of the colony.

### Publications

##### n.6261. Indices botanici et materiae medicae

**Title**
*Indices botanici et materiae medicae* quibus plantarum genera hactenus instituta: simplicium quoque tam vulgarium, quam exoticarum nomina, & facultates summatim recensitur ... Bononiae \[Bologna\] (Ex typographia Laelii a Vulpe) 1753. Qu.
**Abbreviated title**: *Ind. bot. mat. med.*

**Notes**
*Publ*.: 1753, before 1 Mai (p. 160: 29 Jan 1753), p. \[i\*-viii\*\], i-xx, \[xxi-xxviii\], 1-160, 2 maps. *Copies*: FI, MO, USDA; IDC 6080. – Reviewed by GGA on 15 Nov 1753, which, in the absence of other data, does not constitute sufficient proof of publication after 1 Mai. Imprimatur dated 29 Jan 1753. The indices are a new feature added \[on p. 129-160\] to the second edition (edited by G. L. Monti) of Giuseppe Monti, *Exoticorum simplicium medicamentorum varii indices* (ed. 1, Bologna 1724, Qu.).
