### Author

Comelli, Franceso (1793-1852), Italian botanist, pharmacist, plant collector at Udine, Italy, born 1 Sep 1793 and died 23 Nov 1852 at Udine. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Comelli* \[standard form in IPNI: *Comelli*\]

#### Herbarium and types
