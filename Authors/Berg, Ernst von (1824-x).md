### Author

Berg, Ernst von (1824-?), Livland born librarian at the Botanical Garden of St. Petersburg until 1865, later director of the school system in Lodz, Poland. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Berg, St. Petersb.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
