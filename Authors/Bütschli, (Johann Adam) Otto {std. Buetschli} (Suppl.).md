### Author

\* Bütschli, \[Johann Adam\] Otto (1848-1920), German zoologist, born in Frankfurt a. M., professor of zoology in Heidelberg, mainly working on protozoa. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Buetschli* \[standard form in IPNI: *Buetschli*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
