### Author

Trachsel, Kaspar \[Caspar\] (1788-1832), Swiss botanist and physician at Rüggisberg (Riggisberg) nr Bern; Dr. med. Univ. Bern. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Trachsel*

#### Herbarium and types

Owned a herbarium of the Canton of Bern, location unknown. – Further material at [[Collection BR|BR]], [[Collection M|M]], [[Collection OXF|OXF]]. – Manuscript *Populäre Flora der Schweiz* in municipal library Bern.

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 12(2): 729; Biol.-Dokum. 18: 9340; Clokie p. 256; DTS 1: 304-305; Hegi 6(2): 1293; LS 26974; TR 696.
