### Author

Ludwig, Rudolph August Birminhold Sebastian (1812-1880) German palaeontologist, technologist and administrator at Schwarzenfels and Darmstadt. 
**Abbreviated name**: *R. Ludw.* \[standard form in IPNI: *R.Ludw.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
