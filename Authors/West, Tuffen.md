### Author

West, Tuffen (1823-1891), British botanical artist; studied at York; settled in London as illustrator of "microscope books". 
**Abbreviated name**: *T. West* \[standard form in IPNI: *T.West*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 479; BB p. 321; BM 5: 2296; CSP 6: 328, 8: 1222; Desmond p. 651 (d. 19 Mar 1891); De Toni 1: cxxx, 2: cxxi; NI 943, 1868, 2163; Nordstedt p. 32; Rehder 2: 768; TL-1/2921; TL-2/1504, 12.305.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Bot. Not. 1892: 47 (d.); Brit. Mus. gen. cat. print. books 255: 796. 1955; J. Bot. 29: 224. 1891 (obit. not.).
- Bridson, G.D.R. et al., Nat. hist. mss. res. Brit. Isl. 207.16, 328.6. 1980.

#### Composite works

- (1) Illustrated William Smith (1808-1857), *A synopsis of the British Diatomaceae* 1853-1856, TL-2/12.305.
- (2) Illustrated A.S. Donkin, *The natural history of the British Diatomaceae* 1870-1873, TL-2/1504.
- (3) Illustrated (4 pl.) J.E. Eliot, *The Quinology of the East Indian plantations* 1869-1876, see NI 943.
- (4) Illustrated William Crawford Williamson, *On the recent Foraminifera of Great Britain *1858, see NI 2163.
- (5) Illustrated *Common objects of the microscope*, London 1864, iv, 188 p., *12 pl*. (col.).
- (6) Illustrated, J. Hogg, *The microscope*: its history... London 1867.

#### Note

New taxa in *Remarks on some Diatomaceae* new or imperfectly described and a new Desmid, Trans. R. Micr. Soc. ser. 2. 8: 147-153. *pl*. 7. 1860.
