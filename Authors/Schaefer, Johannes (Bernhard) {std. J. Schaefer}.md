### Author

Schaefer, Johannes \[Bernhard\] (1864-?), German (Prussian-Saxony) botanist; Dr. phil. Marburg 1889; high school teacher at Altkirch. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. Schaefer*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BFM 2821; BL 2: 153, 714.
