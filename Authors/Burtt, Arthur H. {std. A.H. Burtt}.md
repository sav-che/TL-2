### Author

Burtt, Arthur H. (fl. 1899), English botanist from Darlington, studied at the Eberhard-Karls-Universität in Tübingen, Dr. Phil. ib. 1899. (*A.H. Burtt*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Kew 1:436; Rehder 2: 18; Tucker 1: 128.
