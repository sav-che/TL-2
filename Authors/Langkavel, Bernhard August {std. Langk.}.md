### Author

Langkavel, Bernhard August (1825-1902), German teacher in classical languages; zoologist and botanist at the Friedrichwerderschen Gymnasium in Berlin. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Langk.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
