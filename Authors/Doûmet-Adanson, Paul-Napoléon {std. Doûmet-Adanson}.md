### Author

Doûmet-Adanson, Paul-Napoléon (1834-1897), French botanist, born Guéret (Creuse) 1834, died Baleine (Manche) 31 Mai 1897, worked in the province of Constantine 1874, in 1884 in South Tunisia. (*Doûmet-Adanson*).
