### Author

Leichhardt, Friedrich Wilhelm Ludwig (1813-1848), German botanist and explorer; from 1842 in Australia; lost on a mission in 1848. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Leichh.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Leichh.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MEL|MEL]] and [[Collection NSW|NSW]]; further material at [[Collection B|B]], [[Collection BM|BM]], [[Collection P|P]], [[Collection PC|PC]]. – Letters at Kew; manuscripts at Mitchell library, Sydney.

#### Eponymy

*Leichardtia* \[sic\] R. Brown (1849), *Leichhardtia* F. von Mueller (1876). *Leichhardtia* Shepherd (1851) probably also commemorates him. – Leichhardt River, Queensland (Northern Australia), discovered by A. C. Gregory 1855.
