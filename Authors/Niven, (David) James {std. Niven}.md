### Author

Niven, \[David\] James (1774-1826), Scottish gardener at Edinburgh and Syon House, Middlesex; collected in South Africa 1798-1812. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Niven*

#### Herbarium and types

Main collection at [[Collection BM|BM]]; duplicates in [[Collection DBN|DBN]], [[Collection FI|FI]], [[Collection MANCH|MANCH]], [[Collection MO|MO]], [[Collection PH|PH]], [[Collection SAM|SAM]]. – Mss. on *Erica*at [[Collection K|K]].
