### Author

Treffer, Georg (1847-1902), Austrian botanist, plant collector and mountain guide at Luttach, Tirol. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Treffer* \[standard form in IPNI: *Treffer*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection B|B]] (large coll. in herb. Ullepitsch); further material in [[Collection A|A]], [[Collection AK|AK]], [[Collection AUT|AUT]], [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection GB|GB]], [[Collection GOET|GOET]], [[Collection MANCH|MANCH]], [[Collection TUR|TUR]]. – Sent out annual Catalogues of dried plants (mainly from Tirol) from 1880-1902 (nos. 1-22) which could be bought per century or per specimen. Apart from the plants from Tirol, Treffer offered in these catalogues material from other parts of Europe and from the Orient sometimes collected by others. – *Letters* to K. Keck in [[Collection UPS|UPS]]-UB.
