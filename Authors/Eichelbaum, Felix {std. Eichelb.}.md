### Author

Eichelbaum, Felix (1848-1922), German physician and botanist (mycologist), living at Hamburg, working on a "Pilzflora" for Hamburg. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Eichelb.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Eichelb.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
