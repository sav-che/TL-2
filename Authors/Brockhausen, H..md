### Author

Brockhausen, H. (fl. 1907-1917), German botanist and teacher ("Oberlehrer") at the Katholiches Gymnasium Dionysianum zu Rheine. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Brockhausen*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
