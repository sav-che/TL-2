### Author

Beijerinck, Martinus Willem (1851-1931), Dutch biologist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *M. Beijerinck* \[standard form in IPNI: *Beij.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection NBV|NBV]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 73 (sub Beyerinck).
