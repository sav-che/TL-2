### Author

Letourneux, Aristide-Horace (1820-1890), French magistrate, botanist and malacologist in Algeria (1851-1876, 1881-1890) and at Alexandria (1876-1881); collaborator of Cosson. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Letourn.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Letourn.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection P|P]] and [[Collection PC|PC]] (through Cosson); duplicates in many herbaria; see IH.

#### Eponymy

*Tourneuxia* Cosson (1859, also dedicated to Henri René Letourneux de la Perraudière (1831-1861), who is also commemorated by *Perralderia* Cosson (1859)).
