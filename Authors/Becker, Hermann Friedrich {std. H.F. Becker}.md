### Author

Becker, Hermann Friedrich (fl. 1791), German (Mecklenburg) forester and botanist at Rostock, later at Rövershagen. 
**Abbreviated name**: *H.F. Becker* \[standard form in IPNI: *H.F.Becker*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
