### Author

\* Bondam, Rutger (1817-1896), Dutch floristic botanist, living subsequently in Kampen, Harderwijk, Amersfoort and 's Gravenhage. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Bondam*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection L|L]] and [[Collection NBV|NBV]].
