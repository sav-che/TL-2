### Author

Laestadius, Lars Levi (1800-1861), Swedish clergyman and botanist; born in Arjepluog (Lapland); clergyman at Karesuando (1825-1848) and Pajala (Norrbotten) (1848-1861); pupil of Wahlenberg. (*Laest.*).
