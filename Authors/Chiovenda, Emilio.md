### Author

Chiovenda, Emilio (1871-1940), Italian botanist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Chiov.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Chiov.*\]

[[Collection FI|FI]] and [[Collection FT|FT]].
*Ref*.: IH 1 (ed. 6): 125, 2: 125.
