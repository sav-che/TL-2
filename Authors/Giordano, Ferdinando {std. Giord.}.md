### Author

Giordano, Ferdinando (fl. 1833-1842), Italian medical doctor in Naples (Kingdom of the Two Sicilies), associated with M. Tenore. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Giord.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Giord.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
