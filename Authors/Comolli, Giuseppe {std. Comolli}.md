### Author

Comolli, Giuseppe (1780-1849), Italian botanist and agronomist at Pavia. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Comolli*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection PAV|PAV]], also at [[Collection FI|FI]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 135, Saccardo 2: 35.
- Candolle, Phytographie 404. 1880.
