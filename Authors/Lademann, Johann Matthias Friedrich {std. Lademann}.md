### Author

Lademann, Johann Matthias Friedrich (1760-1810), German botanist; born at Hasselberg, Altmark; Dr. phil. Helmstadt 1785. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lademann*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 333; PR 4975; Rehder 5: 482.
