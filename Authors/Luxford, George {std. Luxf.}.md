### Author

Luxford, George (1807-1854), British botanist, printer, editor and journalist in London; lecturer on botany at St. Thomas Hospital, 1846-1851. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Luxf.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Luxf.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
