### Author

Echegaray, Saile (fl. 1876), Argentinian botanist, born at San Juan, Argentina, collected in Argentina 1876 (herb. univ. Córdoba). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Echegaray*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection B|B]].
