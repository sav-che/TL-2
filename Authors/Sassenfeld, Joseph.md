### Author

Sassenfeld, Joseph (*fl*. 1884), German (Prussian Rhineland) botanist at Trier. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Sassenfeld* \[standard form in IPNI: *Sassenf.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BFM 328; BM 4: 1809; Kew 4: 600.
