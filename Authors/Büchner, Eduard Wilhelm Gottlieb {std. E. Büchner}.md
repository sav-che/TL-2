### Author

Büchner, Eduard Wilhelm Gottlieb (1805-1867), German professor and teacher of mathematics and natural science at the Herzogl. Gymnasium at Hildburghausen. 
**Abbreviated name**: *E. Büchner*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – Issued artificial fungi, 32 edible, 32 poisonous, see ÖBZ 9: 377. 1859 (whether any set is still extant is not known to us). – See also TL-2/4931 (A.G. von Lösecke).

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 1: 279; TL-2/4931.
