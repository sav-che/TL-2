### Author

Lagerstedt, Nils Gerhard Wilhelm (1847-1925), Swedish high school teacher, librarian and botanist at Stockholm (Östermalm); Dr. phil. Uppsala 1869. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lagerst.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Lagerst.*\]
