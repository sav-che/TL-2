### Author

Thomann, Anton (*fl*. 1859), Austrian high school teacher and botanist at the Gymnasium of Krems. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Thomann* \[standard form in IPNI: *Thomann*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
