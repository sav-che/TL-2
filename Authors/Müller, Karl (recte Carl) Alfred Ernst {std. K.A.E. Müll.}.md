### Author

Müller, Karl (recte: "Carl") Alfred Ernst (1855-1907), German botanist, mainly plant physiologist; Dr. phil. Berl. 1883; from 1886-1895 at Pringsheim's plant-physiological institute; from 1895 at the Technische Hochschule Berlin (Charlottenburg); from 1896 as professor; from 1886 also part time and from 1903 fully paid by the Gärtnerlehranstalt at Dahlem while remaining associated with the Technische Hochschule. (*K. A. E. Müll.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
