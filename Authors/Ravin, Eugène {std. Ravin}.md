### Author

Ravin, Eugène (*fl*. 1861), French botanist and pharmacist; director of the botanical garden at Auxerre. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Ravin* \[standard form in IPNI: *Ravin*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection P|P]], [[Collection PC|PC]].
