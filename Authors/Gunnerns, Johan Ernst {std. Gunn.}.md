### Author

Gunnerns, Johan Ernst (1718-1773), Norwegian botanist and clergyman. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Gunn.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection LINN|LINN]], [[Collection TRH|TRH]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 245.
