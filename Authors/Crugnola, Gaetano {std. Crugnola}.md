### Author

Crugnola, Gaetano (1850-1910), Italian botanist, born in Induno Olona, engineer in the Provincial Office at Teramo, died in Induno Olona 6 Sep 1910. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Crugnola*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
