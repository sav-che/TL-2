### Author

Bartlett, Emily Mary (1891-x), American botanist, Ph.D. Stanford 1925, instructor in botany at Wellesley College 1925-1926, later with the University of California. 
**Abbreviated name**: *E.M. Bartlett*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
