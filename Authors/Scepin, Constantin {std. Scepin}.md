### Author

Scepin, Constantin (1727- ?), Russian botanist and physician; Dr. med. Leiden 1758. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Scepin* \[standard form in IPNI: *Scepin*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 219 (b. 1727, Viatka, Russia); CSP 3: 80; Hortus 3: 1203; PR 8096, ed. 1: 9052; Rehder 1: 180; RS p. 140; SA 2: 601; TL-1/1151.

#### Biofile

- Hulth, J.M. & A.H. Uggla, Bref Skrifv. Linné 2(2): 122. 1943.

#### Eponymy

*Scepinia* Necker ex Cassini (1827) probably honors Scepin but no etymology was given.

### Publications

##### n.10.447. de acido vegetabili

**Title**
Schediasma chemico-medicum inaugurale *de acido vegetabili*, quod cum annotationibus botanicis, annuente deo ter opt. max, ex auctoritate magnifici rectoris, D. Adriani van Royen,... nec non amplissimi senatus academici consensu, & nobilissimae facultatis medicae decreto, pro gradu doctoratus, summisque in medicina honoribus & privilegiis rite ac legitime consequendis, publico & amico examini sistit Constantinus Scepin, e Wiatka Russus. Ad diem 19. Maji 1758. hora locoque solitis... Lugduni Batavorum \[Leiden\] (apud Gerardum Potvliet) 1758. Qu.
**Abbreviated title**: *Acid. veg.*

**Notes**
*Publ*.: 19 Mai 1758, p. \[i-viii\], 1-44. Dissertatio on p. 1-16, theses p. \[17-20\], annotationes botanicae on p. 21-44. *Copies*: L (Univ. libr.), NSUB; IDC 5711. – *Note*: the entry for *Crassina* in *Index nominum genericorum* should be corrected to "19 Mai 1758".
