### Author

Seenus, Joseph Freiherr von (*fl*. 1805), Austro-Hungarian botanist; travelled in Istria and Dalmatia ca. 1798-1802. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Seenus*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown; a few specimens at [[Collection MW|MW]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 255; Kanitz no. 96; PR 8584, ed. 1: 9529; Rehder 1: 447; Saccardo 1: 152.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Bot. Zeit., Regensburg 2: 255. 1803 (travel rep.).
- Beck, G., Veg.-Verh. Illyr. Länd. 3, 43. 1901 (Veg. Erde 4).
- Lindemann, E. v., Bull. Soc. Natural. Moscou 61(1): 70. 1886 (some material in MW).
- Sabidussi, H., *in* Beitr. naturw. Heimatk. Kärntens, Sonderheft 1, Carinthia 2: 55. 1930.
