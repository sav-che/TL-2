### Author

Dive, Paul (fl. 1872), French pharmacist who studied at the École supèrieure de Pharmacie de Paris, born at Mont de Marsan. 
**Abbreviated name**: *P. Dive*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
