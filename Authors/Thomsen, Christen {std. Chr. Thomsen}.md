### Author

Thomsen, Christen (1822-1874), Danish botanist and high school teacher at Vordingborg, Randers and Roskilde. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Chr. Thomsen*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection C|C]] (1400).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 378; BL 2: 57, 723; Jackson p. 334; LS 41780; Rehder 1: 357, 5: xviii; Tucker 1: 687.

#### Biofile

- Warming, E., Bot. Tidsskr. 12: 232. 1881 (curr. vit., bibl., b. 7 Aug 1822, d. 1 Dec 1874).
