### Author

Wilson, Albert (1862-1949), British pharmaceutical chemist and botanist (bryologist, lichenologist) at Bradford, Yorkshire. 
**Abbreviated name**: *A. Wils.* \[standard form in IPNI: *A.Wilson*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection YRK|YRK]] and [[Collection LIV|LIV]]. – Further material in [[Collection CGE|CGE]], [[Collection DBN|DBN]], [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection EGHB|EGHB]], [[Collection MANCH|MANCH]], [[Collection NMW|NMW]], [[Collection OXF|OXF]]. – See also Kent & Allen (1984) and A. Wilson (1953).

### Publications

##### n.17.879. A census catalogue of British hepatics

**Title**
*A census catalogue of British hepatics* with list of the botanical vice-counties and their boundaries and lists of sources of records (3rd edition) compiled for the British Bryological Society (formerly the Moss Exchange Club) by Albert Wilson, F.L.S. Showing the comparative rarity or frequency of each species and variety by means of a census, indicating its distribution through the 112 Watsonian vice-county divisions of Great Britain, and R. Lloyd Praeger's 40 county divisions of Ireland, also the Channel Islands... Berwick-on-Tweed (Martin's printing works Ltd.,...) 1930. Oct.
**Abbreviated title**: *Census cat. Brit. hepat.*

**Notes**
*Publ*.: 1930 (p. 4: Dec 1929), p. \[1\]-42, \[43, index\]. *Copies*: B, H.

##### n.17.880. The flora of Westmorland

**Title**
*The flora of Westmorland* an account of the flowering plants, ferns and their allies, mosses, hepatics and lichens so far known to occur in the county. With particulars as to their localities, usual habitats, grade of citizenship, distribution in the six river basins, altitudinal range, time of flowering, first records &c. Also description of the physical geography, topography, geology, climate and botanical features, a bibliography, and a short account of some early pioneers of Westmorland field botany... Arbroath (printed by T. Buncle & Co., Ltd.) 1938. Oct.
**Abbreviated title**: *Fl. Westmorland*.

**Notes**
*Publ*.: 24 Mar 1938 (letter author to A. Dallman in copy LIV), p. \[i-ii\], frontisp., \[iii-vii\], 1-413, *36* further *pl*. (photos), map. *Copies*: BR, G, HH, LIV, MO, USDA.
*Ref*.: A.J.W., J. Bot. 76: 242-243. Aug 1938 (rev.).
