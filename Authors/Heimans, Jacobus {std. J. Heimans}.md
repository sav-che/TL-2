### Author

Heimans, Jacobus (1889-1978), Dutch botanist; son of Eli Heimans; specialized on Desmidiaceae, the genus *Mentha* and the phytogeography of the Netherlands. (*J. Heimans*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection AMD|AMD]], [[Collection NDB|NDB]].
