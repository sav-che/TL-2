### Author

Moore, John William (1901-x), American botanist; Ph. D. Univ. Minnesota 1933, on Raiatea Island 1926-1927. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. W. Moore*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MIN|MIN]]; further material at [[Collection A|A]], [[Collection BISH|BISH]], [[Collection DAO|DAO]], [[Collection GH|GH]], [[Collection IA|IA]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 509; Hortus 3: 1200 ("J. W. Moore"); IH 1 (ed. 6): 362; Pennell p. 614; Zander ed. 10, p. 694, ed. 11, p. 791.
- Merrill, E. D., B. P. Bishop Mus. Bull. 144: 138. 1837 (Polynes. bibl.); Contr. U. S. natl. Herb. 30(1): 216. 1947.

#### Eponymy

*Moorella* P. R. Rao & D. Rao (1964) is dedicated to Royall T. Moore, contemporary mycologist. *Mooreisporites* R. Neves (1958) commemorates Leslie Ravsell Moore (1912-x), English paleobotanist.
