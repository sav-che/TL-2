### Author

Spruner, Wilhelm von (1805-1874), German (Bavarian) pharmacist and plant collector; studied in a München pharmacy and at the University of Erlangen; assistant military pharmacist (later in charge of the military pharmacy in Athens) in the Greek army 1834-1843; military pharmacist in the Bavarian army 1844-1858, ultimately as "Oberapotheker ii. Klasse", in retirement in München. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Sprun.*

#### Herbarium and types

Spruner's personal herbarium was acquired in 1879 by H. Dingier at Aschaffenburg; it came with Dingler's herbarium to [[Collection B|B]] (almost entirely destroyed 1943). Duplicates from Greece (1840-1843) at [[Collection BR|BR]], [[Collection C|C]], [[Collection CAS|CAS]], [[Collection DBN|DBN]], [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection G|G]], [[Collection GOET|GOET]], [[Collection KIEL|KIEL]], [[Collection L|L]], [[Collection LE|LE]], [[Collection LZ|LZ]] (destr.), [[Collection M|M]], [[Collection W|W]]. – *Letters* at [[Collection G|G]].
