### Author

Sondén, Carl Marten (1846-1913), Swedish physician and botanist; Dr. med. Uppsala 1874; professor at a school for midwives in Stockholm 1896-1911. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Sondén* \[standard form in IPNI: *Sondén*\]
