### Author

Gibelli, Giuseppe (1831-1898), Italian botanist at Modena (1874-1879), Bologna (1879-1883) and Torino (1883-1898). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Gibelli* \[standard form in IPNI: *Gibelli*\]

#### Herbarium and types
