### Author

Filet, G. J. (1825-1891), Dutch army physician in the Netherlands East Indies. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Filet* \[standard form in IPNI: *Filet*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Backer, Verkl. woordenb. 214. 1936.

#### Eponymy
