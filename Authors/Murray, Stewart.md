### Author

Murray, Stewart (1789?-1858), Scottish botanist, superintendent of the Glasgow botanic Garden. 
**Abbreviated name**: *S. Murray* \[standard form in IPNI: *S.Murray*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
