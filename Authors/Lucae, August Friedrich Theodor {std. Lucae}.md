### Author

Lucae, August Friedrich Theodor (1800-1848), German pharmacist and botanist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lucae* \[standard form in IPNI: *Lucae*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection KIEL|KIEL]] (40.000; destroyed), dupl. at [[Collection BR|BR]], [[Collection MW|MW]], [[Collection P|P]], [[Collection W|W]].
