### Author

Braun, Ernst (fl. 1933), German (Schömberg, Württemberg) pharmacist, studied at the University of Basel, Dr. phil. ib. 1933 
**Abbreviated name**: *E. Braun*

#### Note

No further information beyond his thesis.
