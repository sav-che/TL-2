### Author

Jörlin, Engelbert (1733-1810), Swedish botanist; pupil of Linnaeus; docent in natural history at Lund (1769); adjunct ib. (1781); ultimately rector of a school in Göteborg (1784). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Jörl*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
