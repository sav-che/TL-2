### Author

Radius, Justus Wilhelm Martin (1797-1884), German (Saxonian) physician and botanist; practicing physician and professor of medicine at the University of Leipzig, director of the "medicinische Gesellschaft" in Leipzig. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Radius* \[standard form in IPNI: *Radius*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection CGE|CGE]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 123 (b. 14 Nov 1797, d. 7 Mar 1884); BM 4: 1637; Herder p. 318; Hortus 3: 1202; Kew 4: 388; PR 7393, ed. 1: 8300-8301; Rehder 2: 657; Tucker 1: 575.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Bonplandia 3: 33 1855, 6: 50. 1858 (cogn. Leopoldina Ludwigius).

#### Eponymy

*Radiusa* H.G.L. Reichenbach (1828) was probably named for this author.
