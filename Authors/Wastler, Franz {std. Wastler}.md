### Author

Wastler, Franz (1837-1936), Austrian high school teacher and botanist at the Staats-Ober-Realschule, Linz. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Wastler* \[standard form in IPNI: *Wastler*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
