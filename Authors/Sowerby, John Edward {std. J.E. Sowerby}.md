### Author

Sowerby, John Edward (1825-1870), British botanical artist and publisher; grandson of James Sowerby. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J.E. Sowerby* \[standard form in IPNI: *J.E.Sowerby*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
