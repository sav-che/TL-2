### Author

Westerlund, Carl Gustaf (1864-1914), Swedish botanist and high school teacher; fil. lic. Uppsala 1894; teacher at Norrköplngs h. läroverk from 1906; son of C.A. Westerlund. (*C.G. Westerl.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection S|S]]; further material at [[Collection A|A]], [[Collection AK|AK]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 480; Biol.-Dokum. 19: 9962; BL 2: 518, 524, 552, 553. 731; KR P. 756 (b. 22 Jun 1864, d. 25 Mai 1914).

#### Biofile

- Anon., Hedwigia 56: (79). 1915 (d.).
- Birger, S. & T. Vestergren, Sv. bot. Tidskr. 9: 383-384. 1915 (obit., bibl., portr.).
