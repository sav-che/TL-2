### Author

Koeler, Georg Ludwig (1765-1807), German botanist at Mainz. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Koeler* \[standard form in IPNI: *Koeler*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection W|W]].
*Ref*.: Burdet, H., Candollea 31: 141. 1976.
