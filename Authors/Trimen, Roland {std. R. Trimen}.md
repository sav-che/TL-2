### Author

Trimen, Roland (1840-1916), British entomologist; brother of Henry Trimen; studied insect fertilization of orchids; in the Auditor-General's office Cape of Good Hope 1860, subsequently working in various other government departments, part-time curator South African Museum 1873-1876, full-time id. 1876-1895. (*R. Trimen*).
