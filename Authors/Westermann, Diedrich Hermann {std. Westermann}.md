### Author

Westermann, Diedrich Hermann (1875-1956), German (Hannover) ethnologist, linguist and explorer of Africa at Berlin University Institut für Landforschung from 1921. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Westermann*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
