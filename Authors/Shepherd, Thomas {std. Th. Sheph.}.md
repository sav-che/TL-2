### Author

Shepherd, Thomas (1779-1835), Scottish nurseryman; emigrated to Sydney, Australia, where he established Darling Nursery in 1827; lectured on horticulture and landscape gardening. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Th. Sheph.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

#### Note

Thomas William Shepherd (1824-1884), son of Thomas Shepherd, became proprietor of the Darling Nursery at Sydney after his father's death. He collected in N.S.W. and published popular botanical articles.
