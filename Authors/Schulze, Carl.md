### Author

Schulze, Carl (1865-?), German (Holstein) botanist and pharmacist; pharmaceutical state exam. 1891; Dr. phil. Erlangen 1895. 
**Abbreviated name**: *C. Schulze* \[standard form in IPNI: *C.Schulze*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
