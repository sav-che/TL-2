### Author

Kalchbrenner, Károly (Karl) (1807-1886), Austro-Hungarian clergyman and mycologist; studied at Halle; later clergyman at Szepes-Olaszi (Zips, Wallendorf). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Kalchbr.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Kalchbr.*\]
