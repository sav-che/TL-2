### Author

Puymaly, André Henri Laurent de (1883-?), French lichenologist; Dr. med. Bordeaux 1912, Dr. sci. nat. 1925; teaching at the University of Bordeaux. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Puym.*

#### Herbarium and types

in 1957 still in his private herbarium. – Letters at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 117; GR p. 294, cat. p. 70; Hirsch p. 240; LS suppl. 22323; Roon p. 91; SBC p. 129.
