### Author

Medicus, W. (± 1892), German naturalist at Kaiserslautern. (*W. Medicus*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – Issued "Etiketten für Pflanzen-Sammlungen, Kaiserslautern 1889 (Nat. Nov. Apr(2) 1889).

#### Bibliography and biography

CSP 4: 319, 17: 127; Kelly p. 147; LS 17635-17638; Rehder 5: 560; Tucker 1: 475.
