### Author

Boeuf, F. (fl. 1907-1936), French botanist, Chef de la Station expérimentale agricole de l'École coloniale d'agriculture, Tunis. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Boeuf*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 209; Kew 1: 281.
