### Author

Ráthay, Emerich (1845-1900), Hungarian botanist; from 1894-1900 professor and director of the oenological-pomological institute at Klosterneuburg near Wien. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Ráthay*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
