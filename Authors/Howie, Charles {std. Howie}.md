### Author

Howie, Charles (1811-1899), Scottish bryologist, nurseryman and seedsman, of St. Andrews. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Howie* \[standard form in IPNI: *Howie*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BM|BM]], [[Collection E|E]].
*Exsiccatae*: Mosses of Fifeshire* (e.g. at [[Collection OXF|OXF]]). Desmond states: "Herb, at St. Andrews" ([[Collection STA|STA]]).
