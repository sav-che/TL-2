### Author

\* Andrews, Henry Charles (fl. 1799-1830), British botanical painter and engraver of Knightbridge, London. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Andr.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Andrews*\]

#### Herbarium and types

None; see TL-2/1: 49.
