### Author

Dellien, Friedrich (fl. 1892), German botanist, born at Lübeck, studied at the Kgl. bayer. Friedrich-Alexanders-Universität zu Erlangen. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Dellien*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
