### Author

Klement, Oscar (1897-x), Bohemian lichenologist; pupil of Anders; in Czechoslovakia until 1945; later in Hannover and Kempten. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Klem.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Klem.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection PR|PR]] and [[Collection PRM|PRM]] (20.000, confiscated with his library in 1945 fide GR); IH also lists material at a regional museum at Chomutov (Komotau) where Klement was born and worked until he was forced to leave the country. Other material: [[Collection GOET|GOET]], [[Collection GZU|GZU]], [[Collection LD|LD]]. – Klement built up a new herbarium after 1945. This is now at the Institut für pharmazeutische Biologie, Bonn. Several types, designated after 1945, are at [[Collection M|M]].
