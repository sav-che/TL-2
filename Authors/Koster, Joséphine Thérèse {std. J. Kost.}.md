### Author

Koster, Joséphine Thérèse (1902-x), Dutch botanist at the Rijksherbarium, Leiden 1930-1967; Ph.D. Leiden 1935; worked on Malaysian Compositae, Cyanophyta and marine algae. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. Kost.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection L|L]].
