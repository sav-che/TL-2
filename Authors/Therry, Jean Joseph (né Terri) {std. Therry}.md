### Author

Therry, Jean Joseph \[né Terri\] (1833-1888), French merchant and banker, self-taught botanist at Lyon; one of the founders of the Société botanique de Lyon. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Therry* \[standard form in IPNI: *Therry*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection LY|LY]]. – Dupl. fungi [[Collection PAD|PAD]]; *Cryptogames du Lyonnais* [[Collection FH|FH]], [[Collection H|H]]-NYL (Lich.), [[Collection PAD|PAD]]. – See D.H. Pfister, Mycotaxon 23: 123. 1985.

Barnhart 3: 371 (b. 14 Feb 1833, d. 17 Sep 1888); Kelly p. 226; LS 6695, 26503-26541 (main bibl.); Morren ed. 10, p. 65.
