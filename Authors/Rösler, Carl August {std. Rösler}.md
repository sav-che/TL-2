### Author

Rösler, Carl August (1770-1858), German (Württemberg) botanist and "Hüttenamts-Buchhalter" in Ludwigsthal (iron-works administrator). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Rösler* \[standard form in IPNI: *Rösler*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection STU|STU]]; a number of lichens at [[Collection GZU|GZU]] and [[Collection M|M]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 170; BM 4: 1720; GR p. 125-126.

#### Eponymy

*Roesleria* Thüman et Passerini (1877) honors the Austrian, Leonard Roesler.
