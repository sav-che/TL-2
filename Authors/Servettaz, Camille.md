### Author

Servettaz, Camille (1870-1947), Swiss botanist; Dr. Sci. Paris 1909; high school teacher at Thonon. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Servett.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Servett.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – *Letters* at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 261; Hortus 3: 1204; MW p. 445; Tucker 1: 646; Zander ed. 10, p. 716, ed. 11, p. 817.
