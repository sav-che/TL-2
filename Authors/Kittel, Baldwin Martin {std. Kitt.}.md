### Author

Kittel, Baldwin Martin (1798-1885), German naturalist; from 1834-1869 Rector of the Landwirthschafts- und Gewerbsschule zu Aschaffenburg. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Kitt.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection B|B]] (destroyed).

**Notes**
