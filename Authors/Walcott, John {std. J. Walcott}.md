### Author

Walcott, John (1754-1831), British (Irish-born) naturalist at Bath and Highnam Court. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. Walcott* \[standard form in IPNI: *Walcott*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 450; BB p. 312; BM 5: 2253; Desmond p. 633; Dryander 3: 132; GF p. 328; Henrey 2: 136, 3: nos. 12, 1471-1472; Jackson p. 233; NI 2093; PR 9927, ed. 1: 10903; Rehder 1: 395; SO 145d, 1228; Tucker 1: 728.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Brit. Mus. gen. cat. print. books 251: 771-772. 1965.

#### Eponymy

*Walcottia* F. v. Mueller (1859) commemorates Pemberton Walcott, who collected plants in Australia in 1861.
