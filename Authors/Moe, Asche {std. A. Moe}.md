### Author

Moe, Asche (1867-1937), Norwegian botanist at Stavanger. 
**Abbreviated name**: *A. Moe*

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection BG|BG]] (inf. P. M. Jørgensen). Moe is not known to have made sizeable collections but sent material to Holmboe at Bergen for identification.

#### Bibliography and biography

BL 2: 454, 692.
