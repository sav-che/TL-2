### Author

Pool, Raymond John (1882-1967), American botanist; Dr. phil. Univ. Nebraska 1913; professor of botany and chairman, Department of Botany, University of Nebraska 1915-1948. (*Pool*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection NEB|NEB]]; other material at [[Collection MO|MO]], [[Collection NY|NY]], [[Collection WTU|WTU]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 98; BJI 2: 138; BL 1: 195, 313; Bossert p. 314 (b. 23 Apr 1884, d. 2 Feb 1967); Ewan ed. 1: 284, ed. 2: 177 (Ph.D. 1908); Hirsch p. 235; Kelly p. 179; Kew 4: 339; LS 38012, suppl. 22046; Pennell p. 616; PH 209.
