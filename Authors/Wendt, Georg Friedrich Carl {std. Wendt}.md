### Author

Wendt, Georg Friedrich Carl (*fl*. 1804), German forester at the Forestry Institute of Zillbach nr. Eisenach (Thüringen). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Wendt* \[standard form in IPNI: *Wendt*\]
