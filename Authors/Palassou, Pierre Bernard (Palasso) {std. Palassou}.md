### Author

Palassou, Pierre Bernard (Palasso) (1745-1830), French clergyman; naturalist and explorer of the Pyrenees. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Palassou*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MPU|MPU]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 43; BM 4: 1503; Colmeiro 1: cxci; Dryander 3: 142; IH 2: (in press); PR ed. 1: 7704; Quenstedt p. 326; Rehder 1: 535; Tucker 1: 535.
