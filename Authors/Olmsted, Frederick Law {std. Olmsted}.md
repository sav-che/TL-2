### Author

Olmsted, Frederick Law (1822-1903), American horticulturist and landscape architect; designed Central Park, Manhattan; superintendent Central Park 1857-1895. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Olmsted*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
