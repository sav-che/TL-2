### Author

McGibbon \[MacGibbon\], James (*fl*. 1848-1864), Scottish gardener; at the Cape of Good Hope for Kew from 1850. 
**Abbreviated name**: *McGibb.* \[standard form in IPNI: *McGibb.*\]
