### Author

Tode, Heinrich Julius (1733-1797), German (Mecklenburg) botanist and clergyman; "Consistorialrath", "Hof- und Domprediger" at Schwerin. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Tode*

#### Herbarium and types

Original collection of fungi destroyed.

#### Bibliography and biography

Ainsworth p. 65, 226, 312, 348; Backer p. 587; Barnhart 3: 388 (b. 31 Mai 1733, d. 30 Dec 1797); BM 5: 2118; Dryander 3: 224, 226, 343, 351, 352, 353; Frank 3 (Anh.): 104; GR p. 50, cat. p. 72; Hawksworth p. 186, 198; Herder p. 246; Kelly p. 228; LS 26866-26878; NI 1970; PR 9386, ed.1: 10350; Stevenson p. 1260.

#### Biofile

- Ainsworth, G.C., Dict. fungi ed. 6. 584. 1971.
- Anon., Bot. Zeit. Regensburg 3: 48. 1804 (mycol. collections dumped in a pool behind his house by his family).
- Boll, E., Fl. Mecklenburg 173-176. 1860.
- Killermann, S., Z. Pilzk. 8-10: 269-271. 1924-1926.
- Roemer, J.J., Arch. Bot. 2(1): 115. 1799 (d. 30 Dec 1797; Mecklenburg-Schwerinische Consistorialrath, Hof- und Domprediger).

#### Eponymy

*Mycotodea* Kirschstein (1936); *Todea* Willdenow ex Bernhardi (1801) and the derived genera names *Todeopsis* B. Renault (1896), *Todisporites* R.A. Couper (1958) and *Todites* A.C. Seward (1900).
