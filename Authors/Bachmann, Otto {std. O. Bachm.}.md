### Author

Bachmann, Otto (fl. 1886), German pharmacist; Dr. phil. Erlangen 1886, teacher at the Agricultural School, Landsberg (Bayern). (*O. Bachm.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 98; Morren ed. 10, p. 19; Rehder 1: 113.
