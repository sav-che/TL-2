### Author

Thévenau, Antonio Victor (1814/1815-1876), French physician and botanist at Béziers; from 1867 in Agde. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Thévenau* \[standard form in IPNI: *Thévenau*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Material at [[Collection BR|BR]], [[Collection DBN|DBN]], [[Collection MPU|MPU]], [[Collection MW|MW]], [[Collection OXF|OXF]]. – *Letters* at [[Collection UPS|UPS]]-UB.
