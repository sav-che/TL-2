### Author

Macchiati, Luigi (1852-1921), Italian cryptogamist; until 1905 at Savona; later scientific director and president of the R. Instituto Tecnico, Venezia. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Macch.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Macch.*\]
