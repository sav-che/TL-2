### Author

Unamuno Yrigoyen, Luis M. (1873-1943), Spanish clergyman (O.S.A.) and botanist; Dr. phil. Madrid 1906; professor of natural history at the college of the PP. Augustinos de Lianes, Oviedo; from 1927 in Madrid ultimately as director of the Mycological laboratory of the Jardin botanico, and professor of botany. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Unamuno* \[standard form in IPNI: *Unamuno*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MA|MA]]. – Further material at [[Collection H|H]] (fungi).

#### Bibliography and biography

Bossert p. 411; Hirsch p. 307; IH 2 (files); LS suppl. 28290-28306; Stevenson p. 1261.

#### Eponymy

*Unamunoa* Urries (1942).

#### Note

Unamuno was a prolific author of whom we treat here only a small number of publications. A good set of his mycological papers between 1920 and 1940 is at the Stevenson Library (BPI).
