### Author

Mueller, Karl ("Carl") (1817-1870), German botanist; continued the publication of Walpers Annales. 
**Abbreviated name**: *K. Muell. berol.* \[standard form in IPNI: *Müll.Berol.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 523; BM 3: 1365, 7: 877; Jackson p. 52, 225; Kew 4: 81; PR 6528; SO 2719; TL-2/1386-1387; TL-2/1: 570; Tucker 1: 505.
- Anon., Bonplandia 3: 300. 1855 (continues Walpers), 4: 305. 1856 (secr./libr. Ges. Gartenfr.); Bot. Not. 1871: 36; Bot. Zeit. 28: 600. 1870, 29: 240. 1871; Verh. bot. Ver. Brand. 12: xlvi. 1870 (d. 21 Jun 1870).
- Stafleu, F. A., Regn. veg. 71: 339. 1970.
- Wittrock, V. B., Acta Horti Berg. 3(3): 146. 1905.

#### Composite works

Continued Walpers' *Annales botanices systematicae* with vols. 4-7. 1857-1871; see under W. G. Walpers.

### Publications

##### n.6489. Annotationes quaedam de familia Elaeocarpeacearum

**Title**
*Annotationes quaedam de familia Elaeocarpeacearum* specierumque nonnullarum generum et elaeocarporum et monocerarum descriptiones, secundum specimina ex herbario wallichiano sumpta. Dissertatio inauguralis botanica quam consenu \[sic\] et auctoritate amplissimi philosophorum ordinis in alma litterarium universitate Friderica Guilelma ad summos in philosophia honores rite capessendos die xxiii m. Julii a. mdcccxlix h.l.q.s. publice defendet auctor Carolus Mueller berolinensis... Berlin (Typis G. Bernsteini) a.d. \[1849\]. Oct.
**Abbreviated title**: *Annot. Elaeocarp.*

**Notes**
*Publ*.: 23 Jul 1849, p. \[1\]-41, \[42, theses\], *1 pl*. (uncol. lith. by auct.). *Copies*: FI, G, MO. – Schlechtendal reports a t.p. with the erroneous date mcccxlix (rather than mdcccxlix), the copies at FI, G and MO have the correct date.
