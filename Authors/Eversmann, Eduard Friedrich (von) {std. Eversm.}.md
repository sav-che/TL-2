### Author

Eversmann, Eduard Friedrich \[von\] (1794-1860), German botanist at Kazan. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Eversm.*

Material at [[Collection B|B]], [[Collection LE|LE]], [[Collection MW|MW]] and [[Collection P|P]].
