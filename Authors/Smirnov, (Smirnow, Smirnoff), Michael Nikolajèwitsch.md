### Author

Smirnov, \[Smirnow, Smirnoff\], Michael Nikolajèwitsch (1849-1889), Russian botanist; school inspector at Tiflis. 
**Abbreviated name**: *M. Smirn.* \[standard form in IPNI: *M.Smirn.*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection TBI|TBI]]. – *Letters* at [[Collection S|S]].
