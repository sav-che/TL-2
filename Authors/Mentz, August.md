### Author

Mentz, August (1867-1944), Danish botanist; Dr. phil. 1912; professor of botany at an agricultural college at Copenhagen from 1923-1931. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Mentz* \[standard form in IPNI: *Mentz*\]

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection C|C]].

Barnhart 2: 476; BL 2: 6, 54, 477, 691; CSP 17: 163; GR cat. p. 68; Hirsch p. 195; IH 2: 527; Kew 3: 655; Kleppa p. 238, 239; KR p. 501 (b. 5 Dec 1867).

- Christensen, C, Dansk. bot. hist. 1: 881. 1926; Danske bot. Litt. 1880-1913, p. 157-159. 1913. 1912-1939, p. 36-39. 1940 (portr., bibl.).
