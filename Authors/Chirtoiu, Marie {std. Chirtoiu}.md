### Author

Chirtoiu, Marie (1892-x), Swiss botanist, studied with R. Chodat at Genève, Dr. ès. Sci. nat. 1917, married Henry Guyot. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Chirtoiu* \[standard form in IPNI: *Chirtoiu*\]

#### Herbarium and types
