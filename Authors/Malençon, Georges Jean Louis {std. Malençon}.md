### Author

Malençon, Georges Jean Louis (1898-x), French mycologist; long-time associate of the Institut scientifique Chérifien at Rabat. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Malençon*

#### Herbarium and types

private and [[Collection PC|PC]], [[Collection RAB|RAB]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 439; IH 2: 495; Kelly p. 141, 255; LS suppl. 17112-17119; Roon p. 73; Stevenson p. 1250.

#### Eponymy

*Malenconia* A. C. Batista & H. S. Maia (1960).
