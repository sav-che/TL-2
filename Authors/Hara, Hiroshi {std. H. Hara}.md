### Author

Hara, Hiroshi (1911-x), Japanese botanist at the University of Tokyo, explorer of the Himalaya and specialist on the Japanese phanerogamic flora. 
**Abbreviated name**: *H. Hara*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection TI|TI]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 124; Bossert p. 163; Hortus 3: 1194 ("Hara"); IF suppl. 4: 323; IH2: 255; Kew2: 577-579; Langman p. 348; MWp. 158, suppl. p. 84-88; Roon p. 48; Zander ed. 10, p. 667.
- Hara, H., Photo-album of plants of Eastern Himalaya. Tokyo 1968.
- Hara, H., et al., An enumeration of the flowering plants of Nepal, 1, 1978.

#### Note

H. Hara's *Enumeratio spermatophytarum japonicarum*, Tokyo 1948-1954, 3 parts, was reprinted in a single volume by Otto Koeltz (Koenigstein Ts.) in 1972; see Taxon 22: 307-308. 1973. For his *The flora of Eastern Himalaya* see Taxon 16: 337-338. 1967, 17: 434. 1968, 21: 186. 1972.
