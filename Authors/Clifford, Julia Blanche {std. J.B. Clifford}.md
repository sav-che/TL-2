### Author

Clifford, Julia Blanche (1864-1918), American botanist, born 1864, died 18 Apr 1918, Pasadena, California, studied and worked at the University of Michigan 1894-1896 (non-grad.). 
**Abbreviated name**: *J.B. Clifford*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
