### Author

Shecut, John \[Linnaeus Edward Whitridge\] (1770-1836), American botanist; Dr. med. Philadelphia 1791; practicing physician at Charleston, S.C. 1791-1836. (*Shecut*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
